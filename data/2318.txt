Haryana
May 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 58-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly raping his daughter-in-law at Bangadwa village.

Police said that the accused, identified as Mahabir Singh Yadav, was today produced in a local court which has remanded him in 14-days' judicial custody.

In her complaint, the victim alleged that her father-in-law sexually assaulted her yesterday when she was alone and then threatened her with dire consequences if she spoke of the incident to anyone.
