West Bengal
Aug 10, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her 68-year-old neighbour at Senpur-Shyamnagar area in Nadia district.

The incident, which took place on August 4th, came to light when the girl's mother lodged an FIR at Kotwali Women's police station.

The 13-year-old girl, a student of local Tarak Das High School, was raped by Kala Chand Mondal while she was going to an agricultural field near her residence.

A student of class VII, the girl had initially suppressed the matter out of fear, but narrated her ordeal to her grandmother.

The accused was arrested, who later admitted that he was in an inebriated condition when he had committed the offence.
