Mahbubnagar
Dec 02, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			The body of an unidentified woman was found on the outskirts of Shadnagar town. According to the police she was beaten up with sticks and boulders after being raped by her, as yet, unidentified assailants.

Police said it was the handiwork of more than three persons who came in a three-wheeler. The assailants dumped the body in some bushes after committing the crime.
