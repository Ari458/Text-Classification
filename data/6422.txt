Kolkata
Feb 13, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 17-year-old youth was arrested for allegedly sexually assaulting a four-year-old girl inside a BJP party office in Behala area of the city.

The incident happened between 3 PM and 4 PM in a small room of the party office, a senior police official of South West division of the Kolkata Police under which Behala falls.

The youth was arrested on the basis of a police complaint made by the parents of the girl.

Both the accused and the victim used to stay in the upper floor of the building that houses the party office, the official said.
