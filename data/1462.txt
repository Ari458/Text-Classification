GURGAON
Mar 12, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A security guard of Group 4 security agency was deployed in Vatika City in Gurgaon. A 10-year-old girl, daughter of one of the residents, was going to visit her friend in another flat and was taking the lift. It has been alleged that the security guard rushed inside the lift and started molesting the girl. When she returned home, the girl told about the incident to her father, who filed a police complaint.
