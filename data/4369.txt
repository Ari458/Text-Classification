Odisha
Mar 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 70-year-old woman was allegedly sexually assaulted in a remote village in Odisha's Kendrapara district.

The accused, Geneswar Jena (29) has been arrested.

The incident which occurred on March 10 in Haldiapatna village has triggered public outcry.

Both the victim and the accused were medically examined.

The accused was booked under Indian Penal Code Sections 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty), 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation)..
