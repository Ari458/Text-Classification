Maharashtra
Aug 10, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teacher attached to a Zilla Parishad school in the district was arrested for allegedly molesting a 13-year-old girl student on the school premises.

Ironically, Chandu Gotarkar was the recipient of ideal teacher award.

The alleged incident took place on the premises of the school in Shirala village in Patur tehsil.

The Class VI student claimed that Gotarkar had molested her several times.

Annoyed over the incident, villagers have demanded Gotarkar's dismissal from service.
