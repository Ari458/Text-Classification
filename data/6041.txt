West Bengal
Dec 22, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			One person was arrested today for allegedly raping a minor girl, who used to work as a domestic help in the house of the accused, the police said.

46-year-old Sudharshan Adhikari was taken into custody after the victim lodged a complaint with the police that Adhikari has been forcing him on her and used to call other too to do the same.

The 13-year-old victim, a resident of Bishnupur in South 24 Parganas district, was working as a domestic help at the house of the accused for the past few months.

The victim today fled from the house and lodged a police complaint.
