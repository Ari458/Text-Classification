Chandigarh
Jun 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A Faridabad court pronounced death sentence to a man for rape and murder of a nine-year-old girl.

Additional District and Session Judge Justice Ram Niwas Bharti also imposed a fine of Rs 25,000 on the convict, Inder Saini.

According to the prosecution, Saini raped and then murdered his neighbour's minor girl on January 15, 2012.
