Tamil Nadu
Aug 24, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			K. Murugesan (26) of Thazhakudy was arrested for allegedly abusing a woman head constable, Malathy, while she was regulating traffic at Chettikulam junction here on Saturday. According to police, the accused also used filthy words and attacked her when she prevented him from entering a one-way road. On a complaint lodged by her, Kottar police have registered a case.
