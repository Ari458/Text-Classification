Ranchi
Mar 20, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Police have arrested one of the accused in a gangrape case in which a mother-of-three was raped by two men known to her in Ranchi. 

The accused had allegedly raped the woman in an inebriated state at her house while she and here husband were sleeping at their house in Lakshmi Nagar area, Pandra, Ratu Road.

The persons were identified as Nitish Kumar Rai alias Lallu, 28 years , resident of Lakshmi Nagar area of Pandra , and his friend Amit Sharma, resident of same locality, abducted the victim from her house and gangraped her in their inebriated state.
