New Delhi
Aug 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her stepfather.

The incident took place when the girl was alone with her stepfather at their residence in West Delhi's Punjabi Bagh area.

The man raped the girl when her mother had gone to market to buy some groceries. When she returned, the victim narrated her ordeal to her, following which she filed a complaint against her husband.

The medical examination of the girl confirmed rape.


Source: Press Trust Of India
