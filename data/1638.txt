Rewari
Apr 26, 2012
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			An abandoned newborn girl was recovered by police from near a bus stand in Rewari.
 
The baby, lying wrapped up in a piece of cloth, was recovered on Monday night from a bushy area near the bus stand at Dharuhera, police said today.
 
The infant was admitted to a local civil hospital in critical condition.
