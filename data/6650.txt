Uttar Pradesh
Mar 19, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The director of a TV serial has been booked for allegedly trying to molest a girl for the past one month when she was called regarding a role in the serial at a hotel falling under Highway police station here.

"The girl was allegedly called by Director Mahendra Deo of a TV serial in the hotel between February 26 to March 12 on the allurement that she would be offered a role in the serial," an FIR filed by the girl's mother, said.

The details would be known after investigation.

"The address of the director would be traced from the hotel and then action would be taken," police said.
