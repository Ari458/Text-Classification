Uttar Pradesh
Jun 19, 2015
Molestation, Other
Description:
			A 30-year-old married woman tried to commit suicide by consuming poison after allegedly being sexually harassed by two youths at Sehat village in Shamli district, police said. 

Shila tried to commit suicide by consuming poison yesterday allegedly after two youths sexually harassed her in house on Wednesday, they said. 

She was admitted to a local hospital police said, adding a case has been registered against the two accused and hunt is on to nab them.
