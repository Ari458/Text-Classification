Uttar Pradesh
Feb 16, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 38-year-old woman was killed allegedly by her husband following a row over his drinking habits in Rae Bareli district, police said here today.

Dharmendra smashed his wife Manju's head against the wall, killing her after she objected to his drinking, police said.

The incident took place last night in Charhara Majra Rahi village under Mill Area police station area, police said, adding that both were married for the past 18 years and have four children.

A case has been registered and efforts were on to nab the culprit, police added.
