Madhya Pradesh
Dec 28, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her 13-year-old neighbour in Nagjhir village.

The incident took place when the girl was playing hide-and-seek in the courtyard with the neighbourhood children.

The boy allegedly hid the victim in his house while playing and sexually assaulted her.

The matter came to light when the girl complained of severe pain and was admitted to district hospital.

Medical tests confirmed rape following which a case was registered against the boy based on a complaint lodged by her parents.
