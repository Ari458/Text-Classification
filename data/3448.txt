Tamil Nadu
Oct 31, 2013
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A local court has sentenced a 63-year-old man to life imprisonment for raping a 13-year-old orphan girl last year.

According to the prosecution the man, Yovan, was the brother of the incharge of an orphanage at Greynagar village in Gobichettipalayam taluk and had forcibly taken the girl to another room and raped her on July 17 last year.

Delivering the judgment, Principal District Judge N Sathish Kumar described the crime as a barbaric act and also imposed a Rs one lakh fine.
