Rajasthan
Mar 27, 2014
Murder
Description:
			An ex-servicemen today shot dead his wife, a mother of three, allegedly after a quarrel over his extra-marital affair in Rajasthan's Bharatput district.

The 35-year-old victim, Baby, had a quarrel with her husband Mahipal last night over the issue.

She left the house this morning to go to her parents' house and was waiting for bus at Deeg when the accused opened fire at her with a pistol killing her on the spot.

"The woman's father has alleged in a complaint that the accused wanted to marry some other woman and Baby was opposing it," a police officer said, adding that the accused was absconding.
