New Delhi
Jun 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly raped in Bhajanpura, triggering protest in the area.

AAP leader Arvind Kejriwal alleged that police had earlier refused to file a case despite the victim approaching the cops.

He said that AAP activists were detained after they held a protest outside Bhajanpura police station.

Source: Press Trust Of India
