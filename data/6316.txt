Haryana
Jan 28, 2015
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman from Zakopur village was raped yesterday by two persons.

The incident took place when she was working in a field, police said, adding, Asif and Tohid, forcibly put her inside their car and took her to Kabulpur village.

Both the accused then allegedly raped her and escaped after leaving her in a forest area.

A case has been registered against the duo in this connection and efforts are on to nab the accused, police said.
