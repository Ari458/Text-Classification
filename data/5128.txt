Chandigarh
Jul 18, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 62-year-old Naya Gaon resident was arrested for raping a Sector 37 woman on Thursday.

The victim, a Punjab government employee in Sector 17, is a divorcee and stays with her son. Accused Manjit Singh Kamboj, a retired PWD employee, was remanded to judicial custody.

Sources in police said that the victim had complained that she was alone when Kamboj forcefully entered her house and committed the crime. She said that she resisted his entry and tried to close the door, but could not succeed.
