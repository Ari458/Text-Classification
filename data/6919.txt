Tamil Nadu
May 16, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The city police on Friday arrested Saminathan (45), a textile sector employee, on the charge of sexually abusing an eight-year-old girl.

Inspector R. Hema of All Woman Police Station here told The Hindu that the accused was arrested under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The accused had lured the girl, staying near his house, with chocolates and balloons on Thursday evening before abusing her. The girl was hospitalised during the early hours of Friday following epileptic seizures, according to the police sources.
