Jammu
Sep 05, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old deaf-mute girl was allegedly abducted and gangraped by two youths when she was on her way home from a shop in Bagh Kotli village of Reasi district.

According to the complaint filed by the victim's family members, two motorcycle-bourne youths intercepted her and dragged her to a nearby field where they raped her, before fleeing from the spot.

The girl narrated the incident to her family members who filed a complaint in this regard.
