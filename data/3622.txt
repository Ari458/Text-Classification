Haryana
Dec 01, 2013
Murder
Description:
			Haryana Police recovered a highly decomposed body of a woman near the hilly areas of Manger-Kot village in Faridabad district.

Upon getting information by a passerby about a decomposed body lying near the village, a police team went on the spot and recovered the body.

They suspect that the body had been lying there for a couple of days.
