Punjab
Mar 18, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old married woman was allegedly gang-raped by three youths at Gurchack village.

According to a complaint filed by the victim, the incident happened on Sunday night when she was going to the fields to answer the call of the nature.

The accused--Deepak Masih, Gora Masih and Vicky Masih-- forcibly took her to a secluded place and allegedly raped her.

The three accused also threatened her of dire consequences if she disclosed the incident to anyone.

The woman works as a labourer here along with her husband.
