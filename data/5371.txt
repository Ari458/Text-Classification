Uttar Pradesh
Aug 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a 40-year-old man at her house in Dharaye village under Kudwar police station of the district today, police said.

The 15-year-old girl was raped by one Rajaram Kori when she was alone at her house in the afternoon, Sub Inspector of Kudwar police station said.

The accused has been arrested, he said, adding the victim has been sent for medical examination.
