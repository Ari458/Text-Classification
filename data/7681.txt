Maharashtra
Aug 13, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			An electrician was arrested on Wednesday for molesting a Class X student on the staircase of the Kandivli high-rise where she lives. On August 10, she was chatting with a male friend on the staircase of the 25th floor when the accused, Rajendra Wakde (27), who has worked in several apartments of the high-rise, reprimanded them. After her friend left and she too started walking away, he followed her till the 20th floor and suddenly hugged her. She tried to flee in the elevator but he threatened to kill her if she told anyone about the incident. The traumatized victim confided in her governess and parents next day, who approached the police.
