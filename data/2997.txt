Rajasthan
Aug 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A youth was arrested on the charges of raping a 18-year-old girl at several instances in the last two years on the pretext of marriage.

The accused, Surendra Raigar, a daily wage earner, has been raping the woman, who was her neighbour at Gudhamansingh village in Kalwar area, for last two years on the false promise of marriage.

The girl later became pregnant and gave birth to a baby last month.

The victim lodged an FIR after her caste's panchayat failed to resolve the dispute when the accused refused to marry her and keep the baby with him.
