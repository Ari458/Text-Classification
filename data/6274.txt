Jammu
Jan 22, 2015
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Police today found a male foetus from a water canal in Jammu's posh Trikuta Nagar locality after receiving reports from locals.

"We noticed a dead baby in the water and informed the police about it following which the police team came and took it along with them," Mukul, a local resident said.

Police said they have registered a case and started investigations into the matter.

"The male foetus is three to four months old and was thrown in the water. We have registered a case and have started investigations into the case," Station House officer of Trikuta Nagar police station, Naveen Angral said.
