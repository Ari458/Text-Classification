Tamil Nadu
Oct 27, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The Principal District and Sessions Judge, P. Ravi, on Monday, convicted and sentenced a village panchayat president to one-year rigorous imprisonment for having abused and assaulted a Dalit girl.

According to Prosecution, N. Pazhanivel (45), president of Karukkakurichi village panchayat, had abused a Dalit girl . He had also attacked her with footwear, causing injuries.
