Maharashtra
Jul 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Three persons, identified as Vishal Burde, Sunil Wardile and Rani Chudasama, have been booked for their alleged role in raping a 17-year-old old girl between January in 2014 till this week.

Burde had befriended Sunita (name changed) around a year ago. Sunita worked as a salesgirl at a cloth shop. Burde, also working at a cloth shop at Sitabuldi, developed friendship with Sunita while meeting common friend Rani. Burde later took Sunita to different places where he forced himself upon her. He also started harassing her, leading to registration of an offence.
