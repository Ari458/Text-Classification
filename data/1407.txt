Chandanagar
Mar 04, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A case of dowry harassment was registered against TDP leader Muvva Satyanarayana, who contested from Serilingampally constituency during the Assembly elections in 2009 after his wife, Padma, 48, lodged complaint on February 17. A resident of Chandanagar, the woman charged that Satyanarayana was harassing her physically and mentally and demanding additional dowry. The couple got married 27 years ago. They have a son and a daughter.
