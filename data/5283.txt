Punjab
Aug 12, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old mute and hearing- impaired girl was allegedly kidnapped and gang-raped by two men in Shandewala village of the district, police said. 

The father of the victim in a complaint to the police alleged that two unidentified people abducted his daughter when the family members were asleep at their home last night. 

They took her to a secluded location and later gang-raped her. The girl returned home and gestured to her parents about the incident. Police have registered a case against two unidentified persons and launched a hunt to nab them.
