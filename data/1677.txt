Sahibabad
May 09, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 40 year old widow was gang-raped in Shalimar Garden area in Sahibabad on Monday afternoon.  The victim in her complaint alleged that one of the accused was known to her. 

"The victim alleged that on Monday one of the accused, Nitu, had come to her shop and invited her for a ceremony. The accused then took her to the Old Bus Stand in Ghaziabad where another man was waiting for them. The accused then bought cold drinks and samosas. They reportedly laced her cold drink with sedatives after which she fell unconscious. The accused took her to an under-construction site in Shalimar Garden area, where a third person was waiting for them. The trio then raped her and fled the spot," said SP (City) SS Yadav.
