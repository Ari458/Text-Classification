Nagpur
Nov 24, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Abhijeet Bansilal Chahande, a  police constable attached to Police Headquarters in Nagpur has been dismissed from service for facing rape charges twice within six months.

Accused has allegedly sexually attacked a neighbour's girl on June 14. Since then he was placed under suspension by the authorities and the case was filed in the court, they said. 
On Saturday, Chahande again assaulted the victim and clicked her objectionable photographs.
