Uttar Pradesh
Feb 19, 2015
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			The body of a newly-born child was today found in a garbage dump in the Loni area here, police said.

According to police, the body was discovered by locals today morning in a garbage dump close to a factory in the Arya Nagar locality.

The body has been sent for a post-mortem while further inquiries are on in the matter, police added.
