Haryana
Feb 23, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A youth allegedly attempted to rape a 15-year-old girl, resident of NIT area.

According to a complaint registered by the victim yesterday, the accused identified as Deepak called her to his house and then tried to rape her, police said.

The victim, however, fled from the spot and raised an alarm, he said.

Police said investigation is on in the matter.
