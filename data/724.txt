Vidisha
Nov 22, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A class 9th student, 14-year-old resident of Lohangi Mohalla, had gone to her ailing sister's residence at Mohangiri locality in Vidisha to help her in domestic chores. There, she was raped by her sister's brother-in-law and father-in-law's elder brother. The accused were identified as Lucky (27) and Prabhu Dayal (60)for several months.

When she visited doctor for a stomach ache, it was found that she was pregnant by seven months.
