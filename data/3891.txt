Uttar Pradesh
Jan 12, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her tutor in Islamnagar.

The 8-year-old girl was raped when she went to take tuition from the private teacher, aged around 28 years.

The matter came to light when the girl started shouting and passers-by heard. They caught the teacher and beat him badly before handing him to the police.
