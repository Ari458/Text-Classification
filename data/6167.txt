Uttar Pradesh
Jan 07, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A youth has been arrested here for allegedly molesting a minor girl in Tronica City Thana area here, police said today.

The youth Abhishek, who lived in the girl's neighbourhood, allegedly kidnapped and molested her, Station Officer Arti Tomar said.

After the incident, the girl informed her family members following which they lodged a complaint with the police, she said, adding the accused has been arrested and a case registered against him under various sections of the IPC. PTI COR
