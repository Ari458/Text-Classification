East Delhi
Jun 05, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was raped by a godman who claimed he could cure her epilepsy in east Delhi's Kalyanpuri area. The condition of the girl, who is admitted in hospital, is said to be serious. 

The family lives in a slum in Kalyanpuri area. Her mother works as a household help and her father died two years ago. 

A case has been registered against the godman under section 376 of the IPC and under provisions of the POCSO Act at Kalyanpuri police station. The accused, however, managed to flee. Medical examination of the girl confirmed rape. She was admitted in hospital after she began complaining of pain in her private parts a few days later.
