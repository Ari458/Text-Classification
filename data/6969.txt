Nainital
May 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A special POCSO court in Haldwani awarded 12 years' imprisonment to a 30-year-old man for sexually assaulting a six-year-old girl child. 

The court on Thursday also slapped a fine of Rs 10,000 on the man, Azam Iqbal. 

According to the prosecution, Iqbal sexually assaulted the girl in Nainital in July 4, 2014 when she was returning home from school. The court pronounced Iqbal guilty and observed that it was a "heinous" crime against the child. 

The case was registered by the girl's parents on July 6 under section 376 (rape) and other relevant sections of IPC, apart from the POCSO Act. The police nabbed the accused while he was trying to escape to Delhi. 

As per the Protection of Children against Sexual Offences Act 2012 (POCSO) the case related to the sexual offences on minors' should be decided within one year of the crime.
