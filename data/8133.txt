Delhi
Dec 08, 2015
Murder, Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A new born baby-girl's body was found dumped in the bushes on a roadside in Outer Delhi’s Narela on Monday morning. A passerby noticed some street dogs fiddling with a cloth bundle around 7 a.m. on Monday. He shooed away the dogs and found a baby girl’s body inside. He called the police who sent the body for post-mortem.

Police suspect it could either be a case of female infanticide or an unwanted child. A case under IPC 318, pertaining to concealment of birth by secret disposal of dead body, has been registered.
