Karnataka
Sep 02, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly assaulted sexually by her neighbour at Kattemalalavadi village in the taluk on Monday. 

The accused has been identified as Abhilash, 19, a resident of the village. He has absconded after the incident.

According to the victim's parents, the girl had been to Abhilash's house on Monday night to get some clarifications in her lessons.

The accused allegedly raped the girl when the power went off in the village due to load-shedding.

Victim in hospital

According to the police, the victim is being treated at a special unit at the government hospital in the town. 

The parents have lodged a complaint in this regard.
