Pune
Mar 17, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was raped by her lover on false promises of marriage. The victim lodged a complaint against Amol Shetty-Kusalkar (32) of Old Wadarwadi with Chatuhshrungi police station. 

According to the police, the suspect proposed to marry the victim at her house in December 2011 and demanded Rs 32,000 to purchase a flat to live together after marriage. He took the victim for a ride to Fergusson College ground and raped her in December 2011.

The victim was asked to remain silent as he promised to marry her. But he fled, prompting the victim to approach the police.
