Kerala
Feb 03, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The North Paravoor police on Tuesday held a 20-year old who raped a seven-year-old girl. The arrested has been identified as Manu of Kada makkudy near Varappuzha.The police said that the accused had lured the class II student to his house and abused her after showing porn videos on his computer. The girl narrated her ordeal to school authorities during counselling.

The accused had also threatened the victim of dare consequences if she disclosed the incident to anyone.
