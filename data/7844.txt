Telangana
Sep 14, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Dejected over alleged dowry harassment, a 23-year-old woman ended her life by hanging herself at her residence at Hanumana Nagar in Nalgonda. Two Town Sub-Inspector of Police, Naga Durgaprasad, said the deceased, a native of Suryanagar in Nalgonda, was married to Giri Prasad, a carpenter from Hanuman Nagar, a year ago. Since then, Gayathri's parents alleged that their daughter was harassed by Giri Prasad, his parents and sister.
