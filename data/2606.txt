Andhra Pradesh
Mar 24, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A jilted youth allegedly attacked a young woman with a sharp weapon and injured her, after she married another man despite getting engaged to the former.
	
Raju attacked the woman, a nurse, with a sickle on her back near a bus stop in Kandukur area on the outskirts of the city after noticing her there with her husband.
	
Raju and the woman had been engaged on February 6. However, the woman went on to marry her lover and this apparently angered Raju.
	
The woman was rushed to hospital and her condition was stable.
