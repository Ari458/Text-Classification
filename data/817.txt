Chandigarh
Dec 02, 2011
Crime, Street Harassment
Description:
			Varinder Singh, 24, of Kajheri village, Sector 61, was stabbed to death by a student (Sanju) near his residence in broad daylight. Police said a tussle between the two over eve-teasing led to the incident.

Sanju had allegedly misbehaved with the sister of Sohan Singh, who is Varinder's friend. To teach him a lesson, Varinder (Victim), an employee of a private company, and Sohan went to Sanju's place and slapped him. Enraged, Sanju went back to his house, returned with two knives and stabbed Varinder.
