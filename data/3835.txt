Arwal
Jan 04, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was raped by a man in a village under Karpi police station area in Bihar's Arwal district.

The incident took place in Pathak Bigaha village as the accused man Tipu Alam entered the house of a minor girl and raped her.

Upon hearing cries of the girl, the villagers overpowered Alam and beat him mercilessly before handing over to the police.
