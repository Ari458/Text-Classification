Maharashtra
Jul 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old man has been arrested allegedly for raping a minor in the Mumbra area in the month May. 

The accused Shabbir Mulani, a resident of Shazad tower raped the 17-year-old neigbour in May and threatened her with life and not to reveal the incident to anyone, the girlas mother mentioned in the complaint. 

However, as the girl got pregnant of two months, Mulani harried her to abort the foetus, failing which he force-fed her abortion tablets, a senior police officer informed.Mulani was produced in the court and has been remanded to police custody, the officer added. 

An offence has been registered at Mumbra police station under sections 376 (punishment for rape), 312 (causing miscarriage) and 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) of Indian Penal Code and section 4 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
