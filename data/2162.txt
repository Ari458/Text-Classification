Etah
May 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Etah district when she had gone to attend a marriage function, police said today.

In an FIR lodged yesterday, the minor had alleged that a 21-year-old youth, identified as Devender, took her to a nearby field and raped her, they said.

The victim was found by her family members in the fields, who later caught the accused and beat him up before handing him over to the police.
