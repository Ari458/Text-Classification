Kerala
Dec 13, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			City Police on Saturday arrested a 60-year person for sexually abusing a five-year-old girl.

According to the police, Kochi-native Vincent was arrested based on a complaint registered by the child's parents.

The parents of the victim in the complaint stated that Vincent took the minor girl, who is an LKG student, to his home and abused her. The incident came to light when the girl revealed the matter to her parents.

The police recorded the statement of the victim. Later, she was subjected to medical examination.

The arrest was recorded under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The accused was remanded in custody.
