Rajasthan
Sep 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman was allegedly gangraped by five people at a private hospital in Chomu, a sub-urban town of Jaipur.

The woman was gangraped on September 24.

In an FIR lodged with the Chomu police station, the victim has named five people; Cheetar Mal Kumawat, Dr Choudhary, Sagar, Atta Khan, and Mukesh Bagar as the main accused.

A woman cop with two policemen returned empty handed as the victim was not found at her rented residence near a hotel on Reengus Road in Chomu, unless the victim is found for recording her statement, no further action could be taken.
