Haryana
Dec 16, 2013
Molestation
Description:
			A 55-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly sexually harassing his daughter-in-law on several occasions.

According to the police, the victim filed a complaint in the Women's Cell against the accused alleging that her father-in-law forcibly tried to have physical relation with her for past few months and threatened to kill her if she refused it.

A FIR was lodged in this connection and the man was arrested by the police.
