Haryana
Sep 30, 2015
Domestic Violence, Molestation, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A Faridabad woman has accused her in-laws of torturing and harassing her over dowry. 

In a complaint filed at the women police station at Sector 16 yesterday, the woman said that since her marriage in 2008, her father-in-law and mother-in-law had been harassing and torturing her physically and mentally demanding more dowry. 

The complainant also accused her father-in-law of molesting her. 

On the basis of her complaint, a case has been registered under sections 498A, 354, 406 and 34 of IPC. Police have started investigations into the case, said Asha Rani, SHO of the women police station.
