Indore
Jun 24, 2012
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police have arrested three persons for allegedly abducting and gang-raping a 19-year-old girl in Juni Indore area on Thursday night. The accused allegedly filmed the incident on their mobile and threatened the victim of uploading the MMS on the internet.

In her complaint, the girl has mentioned that the accused beat her when she tried to shout for help. Within few minutes, they reached a deserted place in Piplihana where they reportedly locked the girl inside a house and took turns to rape her repeatedly.

The accused allegedly prepared an MMS of the incident and threatened the victim that they would upload it, if she did not respond to their advances further. They also threatened her of dire consequence, if she informed anybody about the incident.
