Uttar Pradesh
Mar 08, 2015
Murder
Description:
			The body of an unidentified woman was today recovered from a drain in Dadri area here, police said.

The body of the woman who appears to be around 35 years old, was in semi-nude condition and burnt too, police said, adding that the killers allegedly burnt her to make identification difficult.

Police has sent the body for post-mortem.
