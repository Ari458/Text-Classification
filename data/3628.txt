Haryana
Dec 03, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A Class XI student of Jacobpura Government Girls' Senior Secondary School allegedly committed suicide by consuming poison on the school premises after being teased by a neighbour.

The accused, Ravinder, sneaked into the school premises and teased her in front of others, leading her to consume poison. She was rushed to the civil hospital, then taken to a private hospital where she died.

Teachers and other students nabbed Ravinder and handed him over to police.

According to a police complaint filed by Hari Chand, the girl's father, Ravinder and his friend Sonu had been teasing his daughter for the past two years.

He had even complained to their fathers but the teasing continued.
