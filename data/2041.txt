New Delhi
Apr 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old boy allegedly raped his three-year-old cousin and upset over his misdeed, attempted suicide by consuming poison in Ramnagar village of the district, police said today.

Prempal, a class IX student, had taken the girl to a nearby place and raped her yesterday evening, police said.

Later, he attempted suicide by consuming poison, they said, adding, his relatives rushed him to a nearby hospital.

Source : Press Trust of India
