Jharkhand
Feb 18, 2014
Murder
Description:
			Three persons were today sentenced to 10 years rigorous imprisonment by a local court for killing a woman after branding her a witch two and a half years ago.

The court of Additional District Judge (First) Chandra Sekhar awarded the sentenced to Shankar Mahato, Babu Mahato and Amir Mahato.

The woman was killed at Giridhar Chak village in November, 2011.
