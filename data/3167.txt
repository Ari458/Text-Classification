Assam
Sep 21, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			The principal of a college in Assam's Lakhimpur district was arrested for allegedly molesting 10 girl students.

The principal of ST Junior Anthony College, Roydas Milaphil was arrested by Laluk police after a complaint was lodged by the local unit of All Assam Student's Union (AASU) at Harmut police station alleging that he had molested 10 girl students of the college.
