Faridabad
Mar 17, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A constable Virendra has been arrested for allegedly raping a 16 year old minor at gun-point in Faridabad.

In a complaint to police commissioner, the victim has alleged that Virendra, who was working as a computer operator in a police station in Ballabgarh, had raped her at gunpoint last year when he was posted in Old Thana and had been sexually exploiting her since on a false promise of marrying her.
