Telangana
May 14, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			She teams of Hyderabad City Police apprehended a person for sexually harassing a woman at Railway Reservation Complex, Secunderabad on Tuesday. A Pratap (32) a private employee from Warangal district misbehaved with the lady while she was purchasing a ticket.

She team personnel who were present at the place filmed the episode and caught him.
