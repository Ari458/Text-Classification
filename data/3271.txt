New Delhi
Oct 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in West Delhi's Ranhola area.

According to police, the accused, identified as 20-year-old Hemraj, alias Babu, has been arrested for the assault after a medical test on the victim confirmed rape.

The police received a PCR call about the incident. Hemraj had taken the girl to a secluded spot on the pretext of offering her snacks and then violated her. He fled the scene after the act.

The girl, whom the accused left behind in an unconscious state, was later noticed by passers-by who informed her family. She was then taken to hospital.

Source: Press Trust Of India
