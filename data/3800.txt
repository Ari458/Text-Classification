Uttar Pradesh
Dec 28, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly abducted and gang raped by four youths, including a local SP President, while she was returning home in Shamli district.

The 16-year-old victim was returning to her home in Banat town when the four accused abducted her and brought her in a car to a nearby house where she was gang raped by them.

The accused have been identified as Feroz, Pervaiz, Farman and Ikram, who is a Panchayat member and Samajwadi Party President in Banat.

Two of the accused, Feroz and Pervaiz have been arrested while police is searching for the remaining two. The victim has been sent for medical examination.
