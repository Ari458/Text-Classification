Chhattisgarh
Oct 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly gang-raped by two people, including a minor, at a railway station in Bilaspur district of Chhattisgarh.

The incident took place when the 30-year-old woman had gone to attend nature's call at an isolated place in Bilaspur railway station premises.

According to the victim, she had been staying with her friend in Bilaspur, after a quarrel with her husband a month ago.
