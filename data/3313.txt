Bihar
Oct 11, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A woman was beaten to death by her drunken husband at a village under Warsaliganj police station area of Bihar's Nawada district.

The incident took place at Balampur village when Bachchu Manjhi beat his wife Sintoo Devi in an inebriated condition.

Police caught him red-handed when he was trying to bury the body at an isolated place.
