Mulund
Nov 20, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A 74-year-old freelance photographer Nasri Katari was arrested in Mulund for luring several minor girls with chocolates, sexually assaulting them and shooting pornographic videos which he then sold abroad. 

The activist gathered a few residents of the building and barged into Katira's home, where he was found sexually abusing the girls. The activists recorded the incident on camera and called in the police.

According to Tiwari, Katira would attract young girls into his house by offering them chocolates, giving them money and ordering pizza and sandwiches from a nearby restaurant.
