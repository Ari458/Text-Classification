Tamil Nadu
Aug 04, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The husband of an armed reserve police constable was arrested for allegedly harassing her for money.

Vijayasekar,32, who married the constable in 2010 had pledged her jewels and spent the money. Her parents redeemed the jewels and kept them under their safe custody.

He started harassing her and asked her to get back all the jewels.
