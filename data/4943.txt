Uttar Pradesh
Jun 16, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly abducted and raped in a village in Dhanepur area.

The 15-year-old girl, who had gone missing on June 14, was found in an unconscious state near the village this morning, Superintendent of Police Bharat Singh said.

An FIR was registered against one Tularam and his accomplice on the complaint lodged by the girl's family members, he added.

The girl has been admitted to a hospital, where her condition was stated to be stable.

Source: Press Trust of India
