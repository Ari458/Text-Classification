Tamil Nadu
Oct 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A mahila court here on Wednesday sentenced a 65-year old man to 10-year imprisonment for sexually assaulting a 15-year old girl. 

Vellaiyan alias Maarimuthu of Melboorikal had got into the girl’s house, gagged her and sexually assaulted her in 2013. 

According to the prosecution, the girl was living with her paternal grandparents after her parents parted ways.

On the night of the crime, the girl was alone in the house. Marimuthu had threatened the girl that he would murder her grandparents if she told the incident to them. The accused had also assaulted the girl, four other times, when she went out to fetch firewood from the forest. The incident came to light after a distress call to ChildLine, when the girl was found four-month pregnant. ChildLine was the complainant in the case. 

Maarimuthu was found guilty under various sections of the IPC and under Section 6 of the POCSO Act.
