Uttar Pradesh
Sep 09, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			Three people were arrested on charges of eve-teasing.

According to police, four youths passed some lewd remarks on a woman, who had gone to a fair with her husband, following which an altercation broke out between the man and the youths.

Others soon joined in and caught hold of the three accused, while the fourth one managed to escape, and handed them over to police.
