New Delhi
Oct 11, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 23-year-old maid was allegedly drugged and gang-raped by a youth and three of his friends on the pretext of providing her with a better job in southeast Delhias Jamia Nagar. The victim, who is from Bijnor in Uttar Pradesh, has a six-year-old child and lives in Madanpur Khadar area.

The four youths have been on the run since the complaint was lodged. Police said the accused had offered her a soft drink laced with a sedative and took turns to rape her.
