Rajasthan
Jul 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth when she was returning home from a shop.

The incident took place at Manyana village and a case was registered with Nokha police station.

Three men, identified as Chagan, Omprakash and Rekharam, forcibly took her to a deserted government quarter where Rekharam raped her.
