Punjab
Jul 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly raped inside her house by a man in Jhandiana West village.

Accused Gora Singh on finding the victim alone in her house entered the building and committed the crime.

After registering a case under relevant sections of the IPC, the police got the woman medically examined at Moga civil hospital last evening, which confirmed the assault on her.
