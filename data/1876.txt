Barmer
Dec 28, 2012
Rape
Description:
			In Rajasthan's Barmer district, a 16-year-old girl was reportedly abducted and gangraped by three men inside a moving car on Thursday night.

The girl was returning home after attending a wedding when she was kidnapped. The three accused identified as Manohar, Narendar and Sanwaliya, who were in a jeep, forcibly picked her up in the vehicle and raped her, police said. She was later found abandoned in the desert.
