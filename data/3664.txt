Uttar Pradesh
Dec 09, 2013
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and gang raped by three men in Hayatnagar.

The victim was abducted by three youths on December 1 night and raped for next four to five days, the victim some how managed to escape.

An FIR has been registered against accused Aslam, Alam and Gulrez on a complaint by the victim's father, the accused are absconding.

Source: Press Trust Of India
