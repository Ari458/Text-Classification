Uttar Pradesh
Jan 22, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 30-year-old woman and her two minor children were today found murdered in Qila area of the district.

Aparna Mishra, daughter Samraddhi (3) and 18-month-old son Subhang, were killed with sharp-edged weapon near Ghari Chowki area this evening, Additional Superintendent of Police Rajiv Malhotra said.

He said that though the motive was not clear Aparna's husband Ankur Mishra has been detained.
