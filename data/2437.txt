Varanasi
Jun 18, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her 14-year-old neighbour, a class VIII student in Nevada village of the district.

The victim was playing in the fields yesterday along with other children when the accused convinced her to come with him to a nearby hut where he raped her.

On hearing the victim's cries, locals reached the spot and caught the boy.

The girl was rushed to a local hospital, while the boy was handed over to the police.

Source: Press Trust of India
