Maharashtra
May 24, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 45-year-old woman from Boisar in Palghar district, around 115 kms from Mumbai, was allegedly assaulted and disrobed by six men over a land dispute. The main suspect has been arrested. 

The incident took place on May 20 when the main accused identified as Naresh Dhodi, along with five others, barged into the woman's home in Dandipada, Boisar and assaulted her with sticks. The men allegedly dragged the woman out of her house, pulled her sari and blouse and hurled abuses at her. They later threatened her. 

According to the Boisar police, about six months ago the woman had bought a piece of land from Dhodi, also from the same village. Dhodi wanted developmental rights of the land, which the woman refused. On Wednesday, Dhodi along with his men assaulted her. They allegedly pulled her hair and dragged her out of her house. They stripped her, even as villagers looked on without interfering. 

She registered a complaint with the police. Dhodi and his men have been booked under sections 354 (B) (assault with intent to disrobe), 452 (house trespass), 143 (unlawful assembly), 147 (rioting), 148 (rioting with deadly weapon), 149 (unlawful assembly, 323 (voluntarily causing hurt), 504 (intentional insult), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC. While Dhodi has been arrested, the police are on the look out for the other accused, also from the same village.
