Tamil Nadu
Jul 19, 2016
Rape
Description:
			The Udumalpet police on Monday evening arrested three persons on the charges of sexually assaulting a 28-year-old woman.

Police sources said the arrested were Selvaraj (24), Muthukumar (31) and Sasi Kumar (32).

According to the police sources, the accused persons came to the woman's house in the pretext of buying a country fowl which she was rearing.

They then entered the house and sexually assaulted her, sources said.
