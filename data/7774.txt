Punjab
Sep 06, 2015
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly kidnapped and raped by a youth for one week before she was rescued by her family.

The victim in her complaint to the police alleged that she had come to her parents' home at Radala village on August 18. She said she had gone to take her nephew from his school bus when the accused came in a car and bundled her in the vehicle. She alleged that he put a cloth sprinkled with some liquid on her face following which she fell unconscious. The victim alleged that when she woke up, she found herself in a room where the accused, identified as Angrej Singh, son of Kulwant Singh, a resident of Sanghna village was also present. She alleged that the accused raped her for one week. The victim said she somehow managed to call her family yesterday from a phone, which belonged to the accused. She said her brothers came to the house and rescued her.

Investigating officer Bhupinder Kaur said following the victim's complaint the police have registered a case under Sections 376, 342, IPC. against the accused and started investigations. No arrest had been made so far, she added.
