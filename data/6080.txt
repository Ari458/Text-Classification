Uttar Pradesh
Dec 26, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			Two men were booked for attempting to rape two sisters when they were alone in their house at Unn town in Shamli district, police said.

"The youths- Roopak and Mohit, entered the girls' house one month ago and tried to rape them but the victims thwarted the attempt. Later, the accused fled from the scene," they said.

A case was registered yesterday by the police on the directive of the court, they said, adding efforts are on to nab the absconding accused.
