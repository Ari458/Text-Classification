Punjab
Dec 04, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A minor boy was allegedly sodomised by his own cousins at village Barrianwal.

Thirteen-year-old boy was alone at house, when his cousins Gurmeet Singh and Gurpreet Singh sodomised him at knife point.

Boy's parents had gone to pay obeisance at Gurdwara Ramdas in Amritsar district.
