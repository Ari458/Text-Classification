Chhattisgarh
Mar 01, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Police arrested five youths on charges of gang-raping a girl when she was returning from a local fair at Balod's Daundi region.

The girl was returning with her friend on a bicycle in the evening when the cycle's tyre got punctured.

As there were no repair shops on the way, they started walking and were attacked by five youths, who blocked their way and gang-raped her after dragging her to an isolated bridge nearby.

All five accused, identified as Ratan, Tejram, Ramgopal, Tarun and Ajay Yadav were arrested under relevant sections of IPC.
