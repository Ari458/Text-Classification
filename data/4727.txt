Maharashtra
May 13, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 24-year-old powerloom worker in Bhiwandi was arrested on the charges of killing his wife after he suspected her of having an extra-marital affair.

The accused, Ashfaq Ahmed Ansari had married 22-year-old Afsana in 2013 in Bhalrampur in Uttar Pradesh after which they moved to Shanti Nagar.

A few months later, Ansari heard rumours that Afsana was having an affair with two men.

On April 14, he sought divorce from Afsana but she refused and had also threatened to lodge a dowry harassment case against him, police said.

On Sunday night, the two had a fight again and Ansari strangled her to death.
