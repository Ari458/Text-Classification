Tamil Nadu
Nov 13, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A farm worker surrendered before a police station after killing his 40-year old wife suspecting her fidelity.

45-year old Rajendran was a habitual drunkard and used to pick up quarrel with his wife, suspecting that she had affairs with some one in the area.

After such a quarrel , he fatally stabbed her with a knife. He surrendered before police in the early hours and confessed to the crime.
