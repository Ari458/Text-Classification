Uttar Pradesh
Feb 05, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A woman, working in an NGO, was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by three youths here, police said today.

The incident occurred on February 3 when the woman (32) was returning to her home after working from the NGO. She was abducted by three car-borne youths, who took her to a secluded place, where they gang-raped her.

An FIR in this regard has been registered yesterday against Manoj Samaiya, Babloo and another unidentified person at Lalitpur police station.
