Maharashtra
Feb 22, 2014
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 27-year-old woman from Kisan Nagar has lodged a complaint against her in-laws for dowry harassment.

She was allegedly asked to get Rs 1 lakh within two to three days of marriage.

Anita Shrivastava, the victim, got married to Ambrish Shrivastava in May 2013 and was residing at Kisan Nagar with her in-laws.

Within a few days after her marriage, her in-laws including sister-in-law started demanding dowry and she left to her parent's place on June 21, 2013.

The victim stated that her family had already given gold jewellery as dowry to her in-laws but yet they had been asking for money to buy a four-wheeler.
