Delhi
Sep 02, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 45-year-old man has been arrested from central Delhi's Patel Nagar for allegedly sexually assaulting his four-year-old neighbour.

The accused has been identified as Pawan; he works as a rickshaw puller.

The incident came to fore when the girl rushed home crying from a nearby park. After much pursuance from her mother, the girl finally told her about an 'uncle' who would kiss her on the face and stomach. She also showed her mother the bite marks.

The girl's parents then took her to a police station and got a case registered against the accused. The girl was sent for medical checkup, and later she was counseled by Delhi Commission for Women's Shubhra Mendiratta and Sangeeta.

Police have registered an FIR under IPC section 376 (rape) and section of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act against Pawan.
