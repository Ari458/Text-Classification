Rajasthan
Jan 13, 2014
Molestation, Crime
Description:
			A girl student was allegedly molested at a university campus by some unidentified youths who had been stalking her for the past three days.

The girl, pursuing Master of Arts at Jai Narayan Vyas University of Jodhpur, has registered a complaint in this regard with the police.

According to the complaint, some unidentified youths had been stalking the girl for the past three day. They intercepted her while she was leaving the campus, made obscene gestures at her and touched her inappropriately.

Upon raising an alarm some students gathered but the miscreants managed to flee from the spot.

Even though the girl could not identify the youths, she could provide the registration numbers of their two-wheelers to the police.
