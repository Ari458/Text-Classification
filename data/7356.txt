Goa
Apr 25, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Calangute police have registered a complaint of sexual harassment against a general manager, a room attendant and a housekeeping department manager of a 4-star hotel. All three have been accused of sexual harassment by a staffer.

Calangute police have registered an offence under Sections 354 (use of criminal force to woman with an intent to outrage her modesty), 354 A (sexual harassment), 354D (stalking), 323 (causing hurt), 504 (intentional insult and provocation), 509 (act intended to insult the modesty of women) of the Indian Penal Code.

The complainant said she was in the hospitality industry for the past seven years and joined the Calangute hotel, her present place of employment, in 2013.

She alleged that her immediate departmental head on several occasions made sexual overtures to her and did not stop even after she told him that she is a married woman and not interested in his advances.

Her sexual harassment did not stop even after she approached the general manager. He too made sexual advances towards her promising promotion in her job, she alleged in her complaint.

When she did not oblige her seniors her services were terminated, she alleged.

Police said they are conducting investigation and they haven't arrested anyone as yet.
