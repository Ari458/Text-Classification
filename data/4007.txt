Uttar Pradesh
Jan 29, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by her husband and brother-in-law in Sahibabad.

Based on a complaint filed by the woman, a case was registered at Sahibabad police station. 

The accused, along with their two more brothers, reached the house late at night. While the duo took turn to rape the woman, two others tied her sister who had come to meet her. 

After the incident all four accused fled the spot. 

Police have registered a case against the woman's husband and her three brother-in-laws.
