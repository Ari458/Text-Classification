Maharashtra
Feb 28, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by three persons after they had beaten up her boyfriend.

Three persons were arrested last night from Nashik road's Rokadobawadi locality in connection with the alleged gang-rape of the woman on Wednesday evening, police said.

Allegedly, three people beat up her boyfriend and gang-raped her.

She went to the police station and lodged a complaint against three unidentified persons, giving their description following which they were detained last night, police said.

Police is further probing the issue.
