West Bengal
Apr 03, 2014
Rape, Other
Description:
			A married woman immolated herself in frustration after a kangaroo court asked a young man who allegedly raped her to seek her forgiveness instead of punishing him in Malda district. 

The woman, a mother of two children, died at the Malda Medical College and Hospital after battling for life. When she was allegedly raped by the young man, Rabindra Mondal, when her husband was away from home at Basantatola village. 

Earlier, her husband had approached the kangaroo court and demanded punishment for the alleged rapist, but it merely directed that he seek forgiveness from the victim. 

Frustrated, the woman doused herself with kerosene and immolated herself.
