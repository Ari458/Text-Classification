Uttar Pradesh
Jan 26, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 23-year-old married woman was today found dead under suspicious circumstances in Sangam Park colony in Khoda area of the district, police said.

Khoda police station in charge Ummed Yadav said Sona alias Neha was married to Sandip Jha, a taxi driver by profession, in 2009.

Today morning, Neha's in-laws informed police that she committed suicide by hanging herself from a ceiling fan in her room last night, Yadav said, adding that the police recovered the body lying on the bed.

Yadav said there were some marks visible on her neck.

Meanwhile, brother of the deceased, Dhirendra, told police that Neha's in-laws were in habit of torturing her sister with asking her to bring one car and Rs two lakh from her parents.

He charged that Neha has been intentionally killed by strangulation, police said, adding that a case of dowry death against her husband and in-laws has been lodged. The body has been sent for post mortem, Yadav said.
