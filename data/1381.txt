Nashik
Feb 25, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The Ambad police have registered a case of dowry against four on a complaint filed by Rupali Abhonkar (29) resident of Ganesh Chowk, Ambad that her husband Pritish Abhonkar and three of his kin others of her in-laws, have harassed her mentally and physically for demanding Rs 50,000 from her parents. 

The woman in her complaint has said that she was being tortured within a week of her marriage on January 16, 2005.
