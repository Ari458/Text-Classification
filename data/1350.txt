Bhavnagar
Feb 22, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Mehul Tamboli - A director of Tamboli Castings Limited (TCL)in Bhavnagar, has been accused of domestic violence and physical harassment by his wife.

Juli Tamboli filed a complaint against her husband alleging that he beat her up after a scuffle over a domestic issue. Juli, who lives in Dil Bahar Bungalows, alleged that Mehul has an extramarital affair for the last 10 years and she has been constantly telling him to call off the relationship. He allegedly harassed her mentally and physically, the complaint further states.
