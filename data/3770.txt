Bihar
Dec 24, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was gang raped in Bhaluwahi village of Bihar's Kaimur district.

One person identified as Ajay Yadav has been arrested in connection with the incident.

The girl who had come to her relative's house in the village was returning home in a vehicle arranged by her kin. A relative of the driver also sat in the vehicle midway and the two deliberately delayed the journey.

After darkness descended they parked the vehicle on roadside and gang raped the girl in the vehicle.

On arriving home the girl narrated the entire episode to her mother who lodged a police complaint.
