Rajasthan
Dec 16, 2013
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The director of a private school has been arrested for allegedly molesting an 18-year-old girl student in Jobner town.

An FIR was registered against the director of Tagore Public School, Bhanwar Lal Choudhary, following which he was arrested in the wee hours.

Protesters torched vehicles, the school office and its rooms demanding immediate arrest of the accused, no case of vandalism has been registered so far.

Police resorted to mild lathi charge to disperse the protesters.

The victim, a senior secondary student, lived in a hostel with 20 other girls. She alleged that Choudhary had molested and attempted to rape her on December 13 when other girls were elsewhere in the hostel.

The accused, who is said to be a government teacher in Macharkhani village, has been running the co-education senior secondary school in Jobner.
