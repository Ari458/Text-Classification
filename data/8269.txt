Maharashtra
Jan 10, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Ajni police arrested 22-year-old Deepak Gaurkhede for allegedly molesting an 8-year-old girl on Friday. 

The minor's parents told police that the girl was playing near her home when Gaurkhede lured her with chocolate and took her to his home. He then did indecent acts with the girl. The matter came to light when the girl immediately rushed home and informed her parents.
