Delhi
Jul 28, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by three neighbors, including a juvenile, in Punjabi Bagh area of west Delhi.

Police said the incident took place on Sunday evening when the victim was returning home.

"When she was going to her house, three accused, identified as Rajesh (22) and Mohammad Nazim (20) and their juvenile associate, took her to a vacant plot where they allegedly gang-raped her," said a police officer.

Source: Press Trust of India
