Maharashtra
Feb 14, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A housewife from Rabodi has alleged dowry harassment by her in-laws. The victim had bought household belongings worth Rs 3.5 lakh, which did not satisfy the in-law's demands. The in-laws would beat her up repeatedly demanding more dowry.

The victim's father had given household belongings of more than Rs 3.50 lakh which did not satisfy the in laws and demanded more from her father. The victim refused to bring and was beaten up time to time by her husband. Fed up with the harassment the victim lodged a police complaint at Rabodi police station under section 498 (a), 406 and 34 of Indian Penal Code.
