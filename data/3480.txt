Andhra Pradesh
Nov 05, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 27-year-old woman passenger was allegedly gang raped by four people in Vijayawada Railway yard.

The incident took place when the victim was on her way from Tirupati to Rajahmundry to reach her native place in East Godavari district.

The woman alighted from the train in the early hours and instead of boarding a train towards Rajahmundry, she entered a train meant for the railway yard by mistake.

As she got down from the train at the yard, she was sexually assaulted.

A case was filed against four people under Section 376 (D) of Nirbhaya Act following her complaint.

The victim has been admitted to a government hospital.
