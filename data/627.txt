Chandigarh
Nov 11, 2011
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 45-year-old widow was allegedly gang raped and looted by three persons at Sukhna Lake here on Wednesday night. 

The woman has informed police that she knew the accused for the past one year. she had paid Rs 1 lakh to the trio on the assurance of getting a job for her son in Australia.

On Wednesday one of the three persons called her to return her money near Sukhna Lake. However, the trio, the lady alleged, blindfolded her and bundled her in a car. She alleged that the trio gang raped her and snatched her jewelry too.
