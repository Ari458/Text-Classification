Hyderabad
Apr 12, 2015
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 20-year-old deaf & mute woman got pregnant after her employer's two sons allegedly raped her in the Bahadurpura area.

"The deaf & mute woman who worked as a house maid is now four months pregnant. We suspect that her employer's two sons allegedly sexually assaulted her and impregnated her," Deputy Commissioner of Police (South Zone) V Satyanarayana told PTI.

The woman complained, using sign language, that two people allegedly raped her, the DCP said, adding "We go by scientific evidence and DNA tests will be conducted on the accused and the victim".
