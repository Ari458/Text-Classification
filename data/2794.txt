Shergarh Ghat
Jul 23, 2013
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman, who is pursuing a court case against her husband, was thrown in the Yamuna river today by some unidentified people.

Gyan Singh (42), a resident of Konch area, was returning from a local court this afternoon when some people forcibly caught hold of her and threw her into the river at Shergarh Ghat.

The woman has lodged a case against her husband for harassing her for dowry.
