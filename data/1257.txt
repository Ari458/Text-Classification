Chandigarh
Feb 02, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Police on Tuesday registered a case of domestic violence against the in-laws of a woman from Sector 45. Tamana Puri alleged that her husband Ashish Sood and her in-laws including her father-in-law Rajesh Sood and mother-in-law Kamelsh who reside in Sector 45 have been harassing her for dowry.
