Rajasthan
Jul 01, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a teen-aged youth in the city last night.

The 17-year-old accused, who lives near the house of the victim, raped the girl after luring her to his house, police said, adding that the girl narrated the incident to her mother following which a case has been registered.
