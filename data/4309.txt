Bihar
Mar 04, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Three people were arrested for allegedly gang-raping a woman in a village under the Antichack Police Station area of Bihar's Bhagalpur district. 

The incident happened when the three accused took an Anganwadi worker to an isolated place and gang-raped her.

Based on the complaint lodged by the victim, the three accused identified as Komal Kumar(19), Akhilesh Yadav (20) and Babu Kumar (17) were arrested, the trio has confessed to their crime during an interrogation. 

The victim has been sent to a hospital for medical examination.
