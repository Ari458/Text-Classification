Kota
Nov 29, 2011
Murder
Description:
			A couple was shot dead by unknown assailants on Monday in Sidhartha Nagar colony of Kota city. The tragedy has made the three infants of the couple orphans.

In the morning, passers-by spotted two dead bodies in a pool of blood and informed the police. The deceased were identified as Deepak (25) and Suneeta (23) who hailed from Jhabua in Madhya Pradesh.
