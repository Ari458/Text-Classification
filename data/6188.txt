Maharashtra
Jan 12, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Manoj Nitnaware, 27, has been booked for allegedly raping his former girlfriend, aged around 18, luring her with a promise of marriage. The rape survivor, a first year Arts student, lodged a complaint with MIDC police. Police sources claimed that Nitnaware, a school bus driver, had befriended Nisha (name changed). She was living in his neighbourhood. Nisha claimed in her report that she was exploited on three occasions. Nitnaware is yet to be arrested.
