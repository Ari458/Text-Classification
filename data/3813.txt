Haryana
Jan 01, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A Delhi-based girl was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by eight people.

According to police, the victim had come to meet her friend and was waiting for bus at the Ballabhgarh bus-stand when an SUV stopped by and the five occupants offered to drop her. The occupants, instead, took her to a secluded place where three of their accomplices were waiting. All the eight people then took turns to rape her.

The accused after raping the victim dropped her back to the bus stand and fled. She, then, somehow managed to reach police station and registered her complaint.

Seven of the eight accused have been arrested and produced in a court, which sent them to judicial custody, all of the accused are residents of Hasanpur area in Palwal.
