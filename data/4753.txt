Maharashtra
May 15, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 35-year-old woman and her infant daughter were allegedly murdered in the district.

The unidentified bodies of the duo were found dumped on railway tracks near Pali village between Mumbai and Nashik on Monday evening.

"It was revealed in the postmortem report of the deceased that the infant was strangulated to death while the woman's head was smashed with stone," assistant inspector Bapusaheb Shinde of Shahapur police station said today.
