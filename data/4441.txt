Guwahati
Mar 22, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A nine-year old girl has been allegedly raped by a 65-year old man in Maligaon area of Guwahati, according to an FIR filed by the girl's parents.

The girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at his residence at Mangaljhara.

The girl has been sent for medical examination.
