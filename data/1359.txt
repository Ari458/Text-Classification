Andhra Pradesh
Feb 23, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A junior judge from Andhra Pradesh's Khammam district has been booked in an attempt to rape case here.

A 25 year old woman who worked as a sweeper in the residential quarter of Sattupalli additional Munsif magistrate K Balachander has lodged a police complaint alleging that he had tried to rape her. She  was sweeping the floor when the judge allegedly held her hand and tried to rape her.
