Nazirganj
Jun 12, 2013
Crime
Description:
			The body of a six-year-old girl who was missing since Monday was found today in Howrah district.

Six-year-old Sana Parveen went missing on Monday from Bastipara area of Shibpur in Howrah district.

Her family members lodged a missing complaint with the Shibpur police station alleging that she was kidnapped.

Today the body of Parveen was found near the Hanskhali khal area of Nazirganj in Howrah district.
