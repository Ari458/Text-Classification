Andhra Pradesh
Nov 16, 2011
Crime
Description:
			The mystery over the death of 10-year-old girl, Thedu Lakshmi, at Buchirajupalem here continues. A skull and some bones, alleged to be of the girl who had gone missing a month ago, were found along with the girl's books, school uniform and lunch-box near a burial ground in the same area.

After the Dasara vacation (October 1 to 10), Lakshmi attend the classes till October 13 and went missing on October 14. Some locals said they had seen three men follow the girl daily in the evenings.

Source: Express News Service 16 Nov 2011
