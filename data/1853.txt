Bokaro
Nov 29, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was raped by two youths in a running SUV at Tenughat area under Bermo sub-division of the district. 

The victim 15-year-old girl, Rani Kumari (name changed) was working as a maid at the residence of an advocate in Tenughat Colony. Police said there she struck a friendship with Ranjan who is a resident of the colony. Kumari said Ranjan and his friend raped her one by one in the backseat of a running car until she fainted. Later, she reached the police station and informed them about the incident. Bokaro SP Kuldeep Dwivedi said, "Ranjan has been arrested, while search for another accused is on."
