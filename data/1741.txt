Outer Delhi
Jun 25, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			The body of a teenaged girl was fished out of a canal in Outer Delhi on Sunday morning, police said.

The incident was reported from Bawana and the victim is aged around 15.

Investigators suspect that the girl was raped before she was murdered and dumped in the canal.
