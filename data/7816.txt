Punjab
Sep 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An incident of alleged gang rape of a minor girl by four persons has come to light. Four persons allegedly raped the girl from Jharkhand on Thursday. She was found by villagers in a semi-naked condition in bushes near Ghumanpura village the next day. 

Amritsar senior superintendent of police (rural) Jasdeep Singh said, "For now, rape has not been established. The girl was found semi-naked and we have admitted her to the Nari Niketan. Whether she was raped or not will be confirmed after medical examination."
