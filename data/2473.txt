Shillong
Jun 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A police constable was arrested for allegedly raping his step-daughter since nine years resulting in her bearing a child from him.

The 21-year-old victim, who is raising a nine-year-old child, said she did not dare to inform the incident as the constable would threaten her not to leak the information.

An under-graduate student of a college in the city, she however mustered courage to inform her ordeals to the members of the NGO after seeing other victims coming forward to lodge complaints with the police
