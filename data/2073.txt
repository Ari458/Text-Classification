Meerut
Apr 26, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A woman today alleged a man of sexually exploiting her for over two years, police said here today.

The woman has alleged that she met Amit at a training class and in 2011 they had physical relations, which he recorded in a video and later used it to sexually exploit her, they said.

The woman complained to police and later went to Amit's engagement function today and accused him of sexually exploiting her, they said.

Annoyed family members of Amit attacked the woman and chased her out of the engagement venue. They also attacked few media persons who had come there following the allegations by the woman, they said.
