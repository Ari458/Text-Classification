Telangana
Oct 15, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A money collection agent working with a private finance company was arrested by Balanagar police on Wednesday for allegedly harassing women by sending obscene pictures and videos.

The man, Brahmiah (27) of Ferozguda in Balanagar, was living alone after his wife left him owing to family disputes. Since the last few months, the man took to stealing mobile phones from women at different places in the city.

“After stealing the mobile phones, Brahmiah noted down the contact numbers of women in a diary . He then replaced the SIM card with the one he obtained through fraudulent methods and used it to send obscene pictures and videos and harass the women,” Inspector Balanagar, P Bikhshapathi Rao, said.
