Tamil Nadu
Nov 04, 2013
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped and murdered in nearby Tirupur and the manager of a finance company at Pollachi in the district has been arrested for the crime.

Arun Kumar raped the 17-year-old girl while she was alone at home in Velliyampalayam.

When the girl resisted the attempt and tried to scream, the accused gagged her due to which she suffocated.

While he was trying to flee, locals caught him and handed him over to police.
