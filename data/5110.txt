Uttar Pradesh
Jul 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A local court here has sentenced one person to ten years imprisonment for raping a 40-year-old woman.

Additional District Sessions Judge Harish Tripathi held Akhilesh guilty under Section 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC for raping the woman six years back.

The judge also imposed a fine of Rs 30,000 on him.

According to the prosecution, the victim was raped and threatened in her own house by her neighbour Akhilesh on October 8, 2011 at Sisoli town in the district. Akhilesh was held guilty under Section 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC on account of rape.
