Jaipur
Jun 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old married woman attending her ailing brother at a government hospital here was allegedly raped by a ward boy who has been arrested.

22-year-old Ramvtar Balai, employed with SMS hospital, told the woman's husband that he will help them get refund of the cottage ward fee where his brother-in-law was admitted.

Balai on the pretext of giving job to the man took the couple to their home where he sent the husband outside to bring cold drinks and snacks and raped the woman. The SMS hospital has suspended the ward boy with immediate effect.
