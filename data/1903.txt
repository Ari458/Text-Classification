Neredmet
Jan 07, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			An armyman was taken into custody by Neredmet Police in Hyderabad for trying to abduct and misbehave with a 19-year-old girl student, police said.

Pramod Reddy, working as a sepoy in Bollarum, allegedly misbehaved with the B Com student and attempted to abduct her in defence colony under Neredmet Police station limits, they said quoting the complaint lodged by her.
