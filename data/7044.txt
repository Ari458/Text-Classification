Kerala
May 31, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			The Nadakkavu police, on Saturday, arrested four persons accused in sexually abusing a Bangladeshi girl who was staying in an apartment at Eranhipalam with her husband. 

The arrest was made after the girl approached the police officials seeking legal action. The arrested four persons Suhail alias Bavakka, 44, and his wife Saajitha, 35, from Wayanad, K. Siddik, 25, and K.T. Abdul Kareem, 47, were later taken to the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court. 

When three of the men were charged under section 376 (punishment for rape) of the IPC, the woman was charged for helping them in the crime.
