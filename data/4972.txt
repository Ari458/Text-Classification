Uttar Pradesh
Jun 22, 2014
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			A woman consumed poison to end life following alleged torture by her in-laws over dowry in Domri village under Ramnagar police station.

According to reports, Sadhna (22) of Mirzapur was married with Babulal Sahni in 2013. She was allegedly tortured by her husband and in-laws for dowry. In the wee hours of Thursday, she consumed some poisonous substance. When her condition deteriorated, the in-laws informed her parents instead of taking her to the hospital. Later, she was admitted to a private hospital where she died on Friday morning. Her parents lodged an FIR alleging dowry killing against husband Babulal Sahni, father-in-law Phoolchand Sahni and mother-in-law Reeta Sahni. The police have detained Babulal for interrogation.
