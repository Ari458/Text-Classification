Punjab
Sep 22, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 27-year-old married woman allegedly committed suicide by electrocuting herself at Masitan village of the district.

The family of the deceased, identified as Charanjit Kaur, have lodged an FIR stating that her husband, Surinder Mohan, had been harassing her for dowry for quite some time.

It has been further alleged that the husband had illicit relations with other woman.
