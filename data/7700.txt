Andhra Pradesh
Aug 25, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The Fourth Additional Chief Metropolitan Magistrate (ACMM) Court on Monday awarded one-year imprisonment to M. Narasimha Rao, in a dowry harassment case.

Following a complaint lodged by M. Lakshmi of Vidyadharapuram in the city alleging harassment by her husband, the Bhavanipuram police registered a case in 2014, and filed a charge sheet in the court.

As the accused was found guilty, ACMM Court judge G. Satya Prabhakara Rao, sentenced him to undergo one-year imprisonment, besides imposing a fine of Rs. 1,000.
