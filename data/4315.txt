New Delhi
Mar 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old man was arrested for allegedly brutalizing a one-old girl in RK Puram, south Delhi.

Babu Ram, who belongs to West Bengal, has been sent to judicial custody for 14 days. Ram had also inserted a wooden stick into the child's body.

The incident was reported by a DU student who spotted the accused with the girl. He raised an alarm after which locals gathered at the spot and thrashed the accused before handing him over to the police. The child was taken to Safdarjung Hospital.
