Jammu & Kashmir
May 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 62-year-old librarian of a private school was arrested for allegedly molesting a nine-year-old girl in the institute in Baramulla district of north Kashmir, a police official said on Friday. 

Shabir Ahmad War was arrested on Thursday on the charges of molestation after a complaint was lodged in a local police station in Baramulla town, 55kms from here, the official said. 

He said War, a retired government employee, was working as a librarian in the school. 

He said police acted immediately after the mother of a student approached police with the molestation complaint against the accused.
