Andhra Pradesh
Nov 26, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A 45-year-old woman suffered burn injuries after an unidentified person threw acid on her in Khammam town of Andhra Pradesh.

The house wife was walking on the roadside at Kinnera Centre, when one person came from behind, suddenly threw acid on her and fled the spot.

It is suspected that the woman was attacked due to a family dispute.

The woman suffered around 30 per cent burn injuries in the incident and is undergoing treatment at a hospital, where her condition is stated to be out of danger.
