Punjab
Dec 06, 2013
Abduction
Description:
			A 22-year-old girl was allegedly abducted by a youth at gun point from Desh Bhagat ITI road.

Manpreet Kaur was allegedly abducted by Banni, son of Chaman Lal of local Hari Nagar.

She was walking along with her dog when the accused abducted her.

The accused took her away in a car.
