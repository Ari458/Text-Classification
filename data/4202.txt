Andhra Pradesh
Feb 19, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was sexually assaulted in Jahanuma Nawab Sahab Kunta locality of Falaknuma police limits. The accused, 22-year-old Mohd Javed is a tailor and neighbour of the victim. According to police, he lured the girl to his room by offering her biscuits and then raped her.
