Patna
Mar 22, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			A group of five men tried to molest a mother-daughter duo working in a pulse polio camp at the Sheetla Mata Mandir premises. A home guard posted there tried to intervene, but was beaten up by the goons.

A complaint was lodged by the home guard. One person was detained but verifications proved that he was not involved. An FIR has been lodged.
