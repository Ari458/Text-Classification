Jharkhand
Sep 23, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by a helper of a bus in Khunti district.

As soon the girl boarded the bus at Hutar, the helper of the bus took her to the last row seat of the bus and allegedly sexually assaulted her.

The cries of the child attracted the other passengers who caught the accused and handed him over to the police.

The girl was sent for medical examination.
