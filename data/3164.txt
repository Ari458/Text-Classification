West Bengal
Sep 21, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A daily labourer who tried to molest a minor girl in Cossipore area of the metropolis has been arrested.

The 35-year-old daily labourer, Mohammed Kausar, tried to molest an eight-year-old girl at Gopi Mandal Lane.

Kausar fled after a woman of the area raised an alarm.
