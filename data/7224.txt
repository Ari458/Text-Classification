New Delhi
Jun 23, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Indian police have said they have arrested the Bollywood director Mahmood Farooqui after an American woman accused him of rape.
Farooqui, best known for co-directing the 2010 satire “Peepli Live” about farmers’ suicides, was taken into custody in New Delhi on Saturday after the alleged victim went to the police.
Police spokesman Rajan Bhagat said the woman, a US citizen aged 30, was visiting India at the time of the alleged attack at Farooqui’s home in the capital.

“Based on her complaint, the accused has been arrested and sent to judicial custody,” he told AFP.
“Peepli Live”, which explores the divide between urban and rural India, was produced by Bollywood superstar Aamir Khan.
