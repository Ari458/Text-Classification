West Bengal
Feb 01, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A person was today arrested for allegedly trying to molest a woman inside a minibus in Kolkata.

Ashok Kumar Kejriwal was arrested today from a minibus plying along the Kalakar Street area after a woman, who was also in the bus informed the police over phone that a man was trying to molest her inside the vehicle.

On receiving the complaint over phone, the police swung to action and traced the bus to Kalakar Street area at around 8PM and arrested the accused.
