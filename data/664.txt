South West Delhi
Nov 15, 2011
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was raped allegedly by four youths in a car after they abducted her when she was returning from school in south-west Delhi, police said on Tuesday. 

On Monday afternoon the youths dragged the victim into the car and sexually assaulted the victim. The youths had initially asked the girl to accompany them, and later chased her and forced into the car after she refused their overtures. After raping her, the youths who are also suspected to be students, abandoned her.
