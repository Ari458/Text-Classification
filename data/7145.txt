Kerala
Jun 11, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			The Pozhiyoor police on Tuesday arrested Krishnan, 65, of Karode under sections of the Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act based on the complaint lodged by the parents of an 11-year-old girl.

The police said Krishnan had attempted to molest the girl on April 25.

The child had, however, revealed the incident to her parents only recently, following which they lodged the complaint. Krishnan was arrested on Tuesday and remanded in judicial custody on Wednesday.
