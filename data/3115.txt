Tamil Nadu
Sep 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 65-year-old man was arrested for allegedly sexually assaulting a minor girl.

Locals saw him taking the 10-year-old girl inside an under construction building, nabbed and beat him up before handing him over to police.

The girl has been hospitalised.

The man had allegedly sexually assaulted another minor girl on September 11 following which locals decided to monitor his movements.
