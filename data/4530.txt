Maharashtra
Apr 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Sangvi police arrested a 19-year-old youth for allegedly raping a 17-year-old girl on the pretext of marrying her somewhere between 2012 and February 2014 in Pimple-Gurav, Alandi and Solapur. The girl and the suspect knew each other. On the pretext of marrying her, the suspect had raped her at several spots in and around the city. He also used to beat her up. When the girl asked him about marriage, the suspect threatened to kill her. "We have arrested the suspect," said S V Gade, assistant inspector of Sangvi police station.
