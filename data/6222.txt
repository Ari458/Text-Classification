Uttar Pradesh
Jan 16, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			The Government Railway Police (GRP) has arrested a 35-year-old man for molesting a minor girl in a railway yard here.

GRP inspector Pankaj Lavania said the accused, Ramu, a resident of Madhopura, was arrested yesterday evening.

The accused lured the girl living in the neighbourhood to a forlorn spot in the railway cement yard on the pretext of getting a television repaired in Bazaria Market and molested her.

Hearing her screeches, a biker reached the spot and caught hold of Ramu. Soon several passersby assembled and informed the police, who reached the spot and arrested him.

A case under Section 354B (punishment for sexual harassment) of the IPC and relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act has been registered at GRP police station, police said, adding the accused has been sent to jail.

The girl was taken for medical examination by the police after which she was handed over to her parents.
