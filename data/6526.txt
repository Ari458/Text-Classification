Uttar Pradesh
Feb 28, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old woman was raped by her husband's friend under T P Nagar police station area, they said.

The incident took place yesterday inside her house when the accused had come to meet her. When the woman brought a cup of tea, the accused asked her to get eatables. In this period, he laced the tea with some intoxicant. After the woman consumed tea, she fell unconscious and in this condition, he raped her, police said.

Medical examination has confirmed rape and following victim's complaint, accused Rajesh Kumar has been arrested today and sent to jail, police said.
