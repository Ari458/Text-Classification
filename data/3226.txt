Andhra Pradesh
Sep 30, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A man, along with his parents, was arrested for allegedly murdering his 35-year-old wife at Chikkadpally.

J Bala Krishna, his father Chitranjan who is a local Congress activist, and mother Jagadamba, besides his two sisters and brother-in-law were arrested in connection with the murder of J Rajini.

Bala Krishna, who had a relationship with another woman, allegedly killed his wife by slitting her neck with a knife when she was asleep.

She had been given sleeping pills before the incident.
