Punjab
Jun 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old newly-married woman was allegedly raped by her distant relative at Daulatpura Neewan village in this district

According to the police, Anil Kumar (25) allegedly took the woman to a secluded place in his auto while she was waiting to catch a bus for Ferozepur last evening and raped her.

After registering the case in this regard, police raided the house of the accused and arrested him from Nurpur Sethan village in this district, an official said.
