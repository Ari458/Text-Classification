Karnataka
Aug 16, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 65-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly trying to molest a physically challenged minor girl.

Police said Hassanar, who runs a grocery shop at Bolenthur, lured the girl, a Class V student, to his house by offering sweets and attempted to molest her yesterday.

Locals gathered when the girl screamed for help.
