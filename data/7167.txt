Uttar Pradesh
Jun 15, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 21-year-old woman and her infant daughter were strangled to death allegedly by her husband and in-laws over dowry demand in Mehmoodabad area here, police said on Monday. 

Manisha and her five-month-old daughter were on Sunday strangulated by her husband Vimlesh and in-laws for dowry, police said. 

The victim's father has alleged that Vimlesh and his family have been demanding Rs 50,000 and a gold chain as dowry since the two got married three years back, they said. 

On his complaint, an FIR has been lodged against Vimlesh and five others, who were absconding. 

Bodies have been sent for post mortem, they said.
