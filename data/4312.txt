Rajasthan
Mar 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was allegedly raped and left bleeding by an unknown person in bushes at Mandan village in Alwar district.

Seriously wounded child was referred to Jaipur's SMS hospital.

The victim was picked up from the roadside shelter by a man while her parents (nomads) had gone to earn bread in the area around 3:30 pm.

Later, the parents found the child near the bushes with multiple injuries and bleeding.

The victim was taken at Behror hospital from where she was referred to Jaipur.
