Uttar Pradesh
Feb 06, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A local court has sentenced a woman to life imprisonment for killing her daughter-in-law for dowry in 2004.

Additional Sessions Judge Amar Pal Singh yesterday awarded life sentence to Lakwari Devi, mother-in-law of Nanda who was killed for dowry on February 25, 2004, counsel for the prosecution said.

The court also imposed a fine of Rs 10,000 on the convict.

Nanda was married on February 13, 2003 to Manoj Kumar Chaubey of Tewari ka Chapra village.
