Rajasthan
Dec 15, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A man allegedly axed his wife to death at Chak-5 MD in Nachana area of Jaisalmer district.

The body of 33-year-old Rukma was found in a pool of blood by her relatives at her home. The reason for the murder was not immediately known.

An FIR has been lodged in this connection.

Deceased's husband Peera Ram Bheel, who fled the scene after the murder, was caught by the cops near IGNP canal area later.
