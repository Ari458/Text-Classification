Uttar Pradesh
May 14, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was burnt alive allegedly by her husband for not fulfilling his dowry demands at Husainpur village in the district.

The woman identified as Deepa was murdered yesterday following which police registered a case against her husband Kallu and father-in-law Sukpal.

The body of the victim has been sent for postmortem, police said, adding the accused are on the run.
