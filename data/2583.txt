Nagpur
Mar 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A Nagpur Municipal Corporation (NMC) school teacher has been arrested for allegedly molesting a 15-year-old female student in school premises.
	
Allegedly, Pradeep Gupta (51) touched her body
inappropriately in an NMC school at the Dipti Signal area in a slum colony, in eastern part of the city.
	
The girl gathered some courage after an awareness
programme in her school and narrated this incident to a lady teacher, who informed the head of the school.
	
Allegedly, Gupta had also abused some other girls
whose parents approached the local corporator, who led them to lodge a police complaint.
