Madhya Pradesh
Jan 13, 2016
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The wife of a police constable has charged her husband with harassing her for dowry.

The Koh-e-Fiza police have registered an FIR against him. According to police, Constable Sunil Chouhan is posted at Bhopal police lines and lives in C-17, police line located in Kamla Nagar area. He got married with Babita Chouhan on 16 April 2015.
