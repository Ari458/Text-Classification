Rajasthan
Apr 17, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old Nepali woman, working at a road-side eatery, was allegedly raped by an auto rickshaw driver in Neemrana industrial area of Rajasthan's Alwar district.
The victim, working with her husband at the eatery, said in her complaint that she was raped by driver Sandeep Yadav, who used to carry tiffins to various clients in the industrial area, on March 24th.
The mother of two kids lodged an FIR against Yadav who is at large. Yadav raped the woman when her husband had gone to home for changing his cloths.

The victim's statement would be recorded, her medical test was conducted at a government hospital.
