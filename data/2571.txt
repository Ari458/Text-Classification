Uttar Pradesh
Mar 19, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly molested and a person was arrested in connection with this.
	
Ravi Agrawal, a shop owner in Sihani gate, area molested the child when she came to buy bread at his shop.
	
On the pretext of giving her bread, Ravi brought the girl inside his shop and tried to molest her, the minor's cry for help drew the attention of local residents.
