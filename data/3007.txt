Kerala
Aug 28, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a four-year-old girl was admitted to hospital with serious injuries caused by brutal beating by her father, who was arrested.

Sanofar (35) was arrested after neighbours complained of the cruelty to which girl had been frequently subjected to by her father.

According to police, on interrogation, the girl's mother admitted that her husband used to frequently beat the child.

The girl has suffered injuries all over her body including a deep cut near one of the eyes.

She was first taken to the District Hospital but later shifted to Medical College Hospital in Thiruvananthapuram as doctors suspected internal injuries.
