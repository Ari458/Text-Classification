Madhya Pradesh
Jul 27, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Accused in the case are Shanu, his father Saleem and two uncles, Kalu and Raja.

Minor was abducted by Shanu in March this year. He took her to his native place in Salamatpur that is close to Vidisha and raped her.

He kept her there promising to marry her. His father and uncles also assisted him in the crime.
Accused also tried to pour kerosene and set her ablaze when she got pregnant and insisted on marriage.

She somehow returned to Bhopal and approached Madhya Pradesh State Women's Commission after which police was ordered to register a case. However, the accused are yet to be nabbed.
