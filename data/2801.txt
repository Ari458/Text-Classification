New Delhi
Jul 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 55-year-old jeweller was arrested from Punjabi Bagh area for allegedly raping his two maids.

Ashok Arora was arrested after a complaint by the two women.

The victims, aged 23 and 21 years, alleged that Arora raped them after threatening of dire consequences.

One of them was employed by Arora three months back while the second maid was kept on work four days ago.

The matter came to light when both maids spoke to each other about the issue.

Thereafter, the two women filed their complaint against Arora at the Punjabi Bagh police station.

Source: Press Trust Of India
