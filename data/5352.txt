Punjab
Aug 22, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			In a shocking case, a seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a factory worker inside a room in a park at Kidwai Nagar on Wednesday evening. The accused, identified as Avinash Kumar, a factory worker in Janakpuri, was caught by Anil Kumar, who happened to hear the cries of the girl.

In his police complaint, Anil Kumar, a driver from Phagwara said the victim first came to him begging for some food. He said after that she went to the accused asking him to give her some money. The accused allegedly took her to a room near the tube well in the park where he raped her.

The complainant said when he heard the cries of the little girl, he rushed to the room, caught the accused and informed the police. Investigating officer Rajinder Prasad said a case has been registered against the accused under section 376 (rape), and 511 (Punishment for attempting to commit offences punishable with imprisonment for life or other imprisonment) of IPC and section 8 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act after medical examination of the victim. He said the accused was arrested immediately and would be produced in court on Friday.
