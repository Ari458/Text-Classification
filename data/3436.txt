Andhra Pradesh
Oct 29, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by an auto rickshaw driver at Chigurukota village of Mudinepalli area in Krishna district.

The auto rickshaw driver took the victim to an abandoned ONGC site and raped her.

She was admitted to a government hospital where her condition is stable

A case has been registered at the Mundinepalli police station, but no arrest has been made.
