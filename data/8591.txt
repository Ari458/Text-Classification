Kerala
Jul 08, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Chengamanad police on Thursday arrested a person on charges of abusing a minor girl.

The accused, identified as Manjush, had been working as a priest at a temple near Kalady and had abused the girl by taking her to his house. 

The police had earlier arrested another person on charges of assisting the accused. Both of them have been booked under provisions of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO Act) and remanded in custody.
