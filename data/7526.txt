Pondicherry
Jul 20, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			In an incident that highlights vulnerability of children at home, a case has emerged where a minor girl was sexually assaulted by her mother's lover a couple of years ago, and had been facing harassment by the accused since then.

The victim's parents are estranged and she had been living with her mother in Nettapakkam. The accused, Murunganandam (40), had sexually assaulted the girl, now 17, more than once some years ago, according to the Child Welfare Committee.
