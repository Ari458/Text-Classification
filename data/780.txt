Madhya Pradesh
Nov 28, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A sexual harassment case has been lodged against a retired deputy collector in the Shivpuri district of Madhya Pradesh on the complaint of his daughter-in-law. She has also made her brother-in-law Kaushal Gautam a co-accused in the case.

The victim has submitted a mobile phone video clip as a proof of her complaint.
