Tamil Nadu
Mar 11, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Mahila Court sentenced a 40-year-old man to life imprisonment for abducting, raping and impregnating a minor girl.

The court also imposed a fine of Rs 1.36 lakh on him.

Peer Mohammed had taken his friend's 14-year-old daughter to his house on March 7, 2007 and raped her. This continued for at least three months.

He also threatened the girl that he will kill her father if she complained about the incident to her parents or police.

However, the girl told her parents about the incident when she came to know that she was pregnant.

The parents lodged a complaint to police about the incident following which Peer Mohammed was arrested.

Mahila Court judge M P Subramanian sentenced Peer Mohammed to life imprisonment and ordered him to pay a fine of Rs 1.36 lakh.
