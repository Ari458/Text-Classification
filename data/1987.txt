Utter Pradesh
Apr 23, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped and gangraped by four persons in Bansdeeh area, police said here today.

The accused have been arrested.

The victim was kidnapped by four youths when she had gone out to call her mother from a nearby temple on Sunday night.

The youths later gangraped her at a secluded place, they said.

Perturbed with the incident, the girl attempted suicide, but was saved by the family members, police said.

On the complaint lodged by the victim's mother, police has lodged an FIR against Devendra Kumar, Shyam Lal, Manoj Kumar and Amresh Kumar last night.
