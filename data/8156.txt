Punjab
Dec 14, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Rampura police have registered a case against a Chandigarh resident for demanding dowry.

The accused has been identified as Vikram Batta, a resident of sector-39 D. Investigating Officer Balraj Singh at the women's police station said a resident of Mandir Wali Gali, Rampura, complained that the accused had been demanding dowry from her ever since her marriage. 

A case under sections 498-A and 406 of the Indian Penal Code has been registered against the accused in this connection and further investigation is on.
