Coimbatore
Mar 10, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			C. Abdul Kadhar Jailani, 30, of Karumbukadai and his family members were arrested for harassing his wife Sameemmunnisha.

The complaint parents gave a dowry of 20.5 sovereigns at the time of marriage in 2010. However, after marriage Abdul started harassing her for more dowry. Sameemmunnisha even miscarried after Abdul hit her on her abdomen,police said.
