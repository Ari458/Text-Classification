New Delhi
Mar 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 9-year-old girl was allegedly raped at knife point by her neighbour in New Ashok Nagar.

The accused, identified as Umesh, has been arrested.

The incident took place when the girl, a class 4 student, was playing near her house.

When she was playing, her neighbour Umesh (30) reached there and offered her a chocolate. Later, he took her in his room where he allegedly raped her at knife point. Umesh also threatened her of dire consequence if she revealed her ordeal to anyone.

The girl, however, told her mother about the incident. Thereafter, girl's parents registered a complaint against Umesh.
