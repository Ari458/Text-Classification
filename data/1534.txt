Madhya Pradesh
Mar 23, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Everyday at least four minors are raped in Madhya Pradesh. Replying to a question from Congress MLA Tulsi Silawat, state home minister Umashankar Gupta said in the last two years 3176 minor girls were raped in the state.

During the same period a total 3676 cases of rapes were registered and 1383 women/girls were killed, said the minister.
