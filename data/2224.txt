Madhya Pradesh
May 15, 2013
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A case of suspected female infanticide has come to light in Javi village near here after the recovery of a newborn baby girl's body from a pond, police said today.

Two days ago, the baby girl's body was found floating in the pond at Javi village, after which villagers informed the police, which seized the body and sent it for autopsy.

The postmortem report revealed that the girl survived only one day and she was strangulated, police said.

Police suspect that her parents might have thrown her into the pond after strangulating her.
