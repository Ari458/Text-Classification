Chandigarh
Jun 22, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Five youths were booked on Saturday for allegedly kidnapping and raping a minor girl in Jind town last month. The accused allegedly kidnapped the girl and her younger sister at gun-point on May 22 evening. Apart from violating the rape survivor, the accused also molested her younger sister. They also threatened the girl to upload videos of the sexual assault on porn websites.

Jind superintendent of police (SP) Anil Dhawan said that accused Surjeet and his four friends were for booked abduction, rape and molestation and under various sections of the IT Act. SP said the victim and sister had been medically examined. Meanwhile, police teams are conducting raids to round up the accused.
