Uttar Pradesh
Mar 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A case was lodged by a class XI student in Rabupura area, alleging that on March 11 while she was returning home after writing her paper, one local village youth Jaiprakash tried to kidnap her on motorcycle.

She, however, managed to escape from there and reached home.

On March 13, one Jagdish, along with three others came to her house when she was alone and tried to sexually assault her. She then raised alarm and the men fled away threatening her.

She has lodged an FIR against four persons. All accused were absconding.
