Andhra Pradesh
Jul 06, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A 26-year-old man was arrested for allegedly uploading obscene pictures of a woman, after she turned down his marriage proposal.

The accused has been identified as Bimal Kumar Patro, a resident of Marripalem area in the city.

According to police, he uploaded obscene photos of the woman and her husband on several social networking sites, including Facebook.

The accused and the victim were earlier friends. However, after she refused his marriage proposal, he started sending abusive and threatening SMSes to her mobile as the girl got married a few months back.
