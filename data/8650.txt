Maharashtra
Jul 30, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The Vakola police on Friday received an application from a civic-run school complaining that two male teachers have been repeatedly assaulting and sexually abusing its students.

Vakola police senior inspector Mahadev Wavale confirmed the complaint application received. "We are waiting for the parents to record statements. Then an inquiry will be done based on which further course of action will be decided. The complaint applications stated that the male teachers sexually abused the kids from class 1 to 10," said Wavale.

Former education committee chairman, Ritu Tawade registered the complaint on behalf of the students. Several girl students have corroborated to the complaint when asked by the police.
