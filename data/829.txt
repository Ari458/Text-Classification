Chennai
Dec 02, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old tele-caller was allegedly raped at knife-point. Victim, who worked in T Nagar, was walking home at around 11 pm when the accused Sugumaran, who worked in a brick kiln in a nearby village, was on a two-wheeler. He stopped her and threatened her with a knife. He dragged her to a burial ground in Vichur and allegedly raped her.
