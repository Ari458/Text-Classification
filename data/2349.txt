Ganjam
Jun 05, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 42-year-old man was arrested for allegedly raping a minor girl at a village in Odisha's Ganjam district.

Accused Panchu Sahu worked at Surat and had come home at Bhetani village in Aska area for his daughter's wedding when he committed the offence on Sunday. Sahu and the victim were sent to MKCG Medial college and Hospital at Berhampur for examination.
