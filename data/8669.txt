Karnataka
Aug 30, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police are on the lookout for two men who allegedly sexually assaulted a two-year-old girl at Manjunathnagar in east Bengaluru on Sunday. 

The girl's father, an auto rickshaw driver, told police he had gone out of the house when his daughter was assaulted. The girl was found near the house of the two men, who are absconding.
The child was the youngest among four children in the family.

On Sunday evening, the father returned home to find the girl crying. Suspecting someone had assaulted her, he filed a police complaint. Later, a medical test proved she was sexually assaulted.
