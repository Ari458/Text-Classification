Tamil Nadu
Sep 22, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			Two persons were arrested in connection with the attack on a woman during the Vinayaka Chathurthi festival celebration at the auditorium in South Manakkavilai in Eraniel on Sunday.

Police sources said that Janaki (60) of Manakkavilai was allegedly attacked by Suresh (25) and Suyambulingam (29) of the same area after a wordy quarrel.

On the basis of a complaint lodged by Ms. Janaki, Eraniel police have arrested the youths. Further investigations are on.
