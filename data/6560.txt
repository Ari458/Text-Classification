Haryana
Mar 05, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			Police have registered a case against a man for allegedly attempting to rape a 23-year-old married woman in the district.

The incident happened yesterday when the victim's neighbour Tippi entered the woman's house when she was alone and tried to rape her, police said.

Hearing the woman's screams, locals gathered at her house following which Tippi fled from the spot, police said, adding, the matter is being investigated.
