Chandigarh
Jun 10, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped inside a shop here late last night.

The woman, who hails from Punjab, in her police complaint has alleged that one Sunil Kumar, a resident of Sector 23 here, raped her at a shop in Sector 22.

The accused, known to the victim, had threatened the woman with dire consequences if she reported the matter, police said.
