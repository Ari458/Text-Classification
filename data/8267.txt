Punjab
Jan 09, 2016
Domestic Violence
Description:
			Police here on Thursday booked a man for allegedly forcing his wife to eat abortion pills and beating her up in December last year.

In her complaint to Daba police, Baba Deep Singh Nagar resident Harjeet Kaur has alleged that her husband, Gurpal Nagar resident Deepak Kumar, forced her to eat the pills on December 26, and also beat her up and kicked her in the abdomen because she was pregnant.

Harjeet alleged that she married Deepak four and half years ago, and he frequently fought with her during the course of their marriage. Daba police have started investigations into the matter and have booked the accused under sections 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty), 342 (punishment for wrongful confinement), 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code.

The police officer investigating the case, assistant sub-inspector Gurjeet Singh, said the accused was previously working with an iron factory, but after losing job he started fighting with his wife. He added that accused was on the run, but would be arrested soon.
