Tamil Nadu
Mar 13, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at Pallipalam.

The 17-year-old neighbour took the girl, a Class V student, to his residence and raped her.

The girl reported the matter to her mother, a labourer, when she returned home.

Following a complaint by the girl's mother, the accused was arrested.


Source: Press Trust of India
