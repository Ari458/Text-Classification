Assam
Jan 28, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped and murdered while her brother was seriously injured in Jorabat area along the Assam-Meghalaya inter-state border on Wednesday, police said.

The 10-year-old girl was allegedly raped when she was at home with her brother this morning, police said.

Neighbors rushed to the house after hearing screams and found the girl lying in a pool of blood.

The girl's mother then pointed out that her 14-year-old son was missing. Following which, the locals searched the nearby jungles and found him seriously injured. He was rushed to the Gauhati Medical College Hospital in a critical condition.
