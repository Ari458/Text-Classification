Tamil Nadu
Feb 22, 2014
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The All Woman Police (Tirupur North) arrested K. Nagendiran (24) and his father S. Krishnan (56) on the charges of demanding dowry from N. Rekha, wife of Nagendiran, and compelling her to opt for divorce.

Nagendiran, a resident of Chamundipuram, married Rekha, of The Nilgiris district, on December 11, 2013.

Soon after the marriage, the accused demanded dowry and when the woman refused, she was asked to settle for divorce.
