New Delhi
Jul 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A youth has been arrested for allegedly raping a four-year-old girl when she was alone at her house in Paschim Vihar area in West Delhi.

According to police, Hiral Lal (24), who worked as a labour and lived in the neighbourhood of the girl, barged into her house and raped her.

The girl has been admitted in Sanjay Gandhi hospital.

Source: Press Trust Of India
