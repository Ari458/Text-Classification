Haryana
Feb 29, 2016
Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old mobile shop owner was arrested on Sunday for allegedly raping a Delhi University student in a hotel in Civil Lines on Friday.
An FIR has been lodged against the accused and he will be produced in a city court on Monday, police said.

According to police, the 22-year-old girl used to recharge her phone at his shop. The girl said he had befriended her and on Friday night, asked her to meet him at a hotel in Civil Lines. When she reached the hotel, he allegedly raped her and threatened her.

She lodged a complaint at Civil Lines police station on Saturday night, based on which the accused was arrested. On Sunday, the girl underwent a medical examination after which her statement was recorded before the magistrate.
