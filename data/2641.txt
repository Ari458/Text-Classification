Odisha
Mar 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old man was arrested in Odisha's Ganjam district for allegedly raping a
teenager for the last six months by promising to marry her.
	
Medical test of the accused was conducted after he was arrested from Beguniapada area.
Medical examination of the 15-year-old victim was also carried out at MKCG Medical College and Hospital in Berhampur.
