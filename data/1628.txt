			Kavita (22) and her sister Poonam (20) were alone in their house when four men stormed in and tried to rape them. When the girls resisted, the men cut off Kavita's nose.

Kavita's nose was slashed and her younger sister seriously injured when they resisted a physical assault by the four men. The incident took place on Monday in Shahzadpur village under Mandawar police station.
