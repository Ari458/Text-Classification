Uttar Pradesh
May 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A youth was arrested for allegedly attempting to rape a 15-year-old girl in the district's Bopada village.

The accused, identified as Rizwan, last evening entered the girl's house when she was alone and tried to rape her, police said.

When the girl raised an alarm, her neighbours came to her rescue and nabbed the youth and handed him over to police.
