Maharashtra
Apr 28, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The airport police on Saturday arrested a 19-year-old Satara resident for raping a married woman in Dighi on April 8.

The suspect has been identified as Akash Ramesh More, who lives in Satara. More has been charged under section 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal code.

Police said that on April 8, More had allegedly raped the 23-year-old woman when she was on her way to take bath in a room adjacent to her house. "The woman informed her relatives about the incident and lodged a complaint on Saturday. More was arrested immediately," police added.
