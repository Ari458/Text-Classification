Andhra Pradesh
Jan 23, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A labourer allegedly killed his wife after she could not meet his dowry demands. 

The accused, D Govind (35), a daily wage labourer, had been harassing his wife Papa (29) for dowry since sometime.

Govind and Papa, who have two children, got married four years back. 

The accused fled after killing his wife with a sharp-edged weapon at their home in the city's Simachalam area, a search has been launched to nab him. 

The body has been sent to Rajahmundry Government general hospital for postmortem.
