Rajasthan
Dec 19, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			The body of a 16-year-old girl was recovered from a well near Sangariya village of Jhalawar district, with her parents claiming that she was raped and murdered.

The Class X student belonging to OBC was found dead.

On a complaint by her parents, a case of rape and murder has been lodged against a youth, Azad Mansuri, who was last seen with her, he is absconding after the incident.

The victim's body was sent for post mortem, following which it was handed over to her family for last rites.

It could be confirmed after receiving the post mortem report whether she was raped and the cause for her death.
