Punjab
Mar 02, 2014
Human Trafficking
Description:
			Police today claimed to have busted a sex racket with the arrest of four persons, including three women, after a raid at a locality here.

The raid took place at the residence of a Nirmal Kaur alias Neehan at Sardar Nagar, following a tip-off late last night.

Apart from Nirmal, the other arrested have been identified as Kulwinder Kaur alias Rosy, Karamjeet Kaur and Satwinder Singh.

Investigations have revealed that a flesh trade racket was operative from the house of Nirmal and the neighbours had made a complaint in this connection.
