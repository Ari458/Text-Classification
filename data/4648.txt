Maharashtra
Apr 30, 2014
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The Kondhwa police have booked a doctor of a private hospital for allegedly molesting a 19-year-old patient. The incident took place on Monday evening, when the woman had gone to the hospital for some medical treatment. "The suspect allegedly molested the woman on the pretext of conducting a check-up," said S S Sutar, sub-inspector of Kondhwa police station.
