Haryana
Sep 24, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth from Sirsa on the pretext of marrying her. Police filed an FIR against the youth after the girl approached them with a complaint. A medical examination of the girl was also conducted. According to the complaint, the girl was having an affair with the youth, who called her to his house on August 13 and raped her.

Seema Sodhi, SHO of women's police station, said a case has been registered against the youth and he will be arrested soon.
