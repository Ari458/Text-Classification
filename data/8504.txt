Telangana
Feb 16, 2016
Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman was raped by a pastor's son at Pahadisharif. Though the incident happened three weeks ago, it came to light only on Monday after the survivor approached the police.

The culprit was identified as Samuel, 25, son of a pastor in Pahadisharif. "On January 26, Samuel forcibly took his neighbour, a 26-year-old housewife, to an isolated place near his house in Sri Ram Colony of Pahadisharif and raped her," Pahadesharif inspector VV Chalapathi said.

On Monday, the survivor along with her family members lodged a complaint with the police, who registered a case of rape against Samuel.
