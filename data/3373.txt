Andhra Pradesh
Oct 20, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Medak district of Andhra Pradesh.

The incident occurred in Surampally village when the girl was on her way to a farm.

Oggu Illaiah allegedly dragged the girl to nearby bushes and raped her.

The victim was sent to a government hospital for medical tests.
