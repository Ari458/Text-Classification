Kerala
Jan 29, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Ernakulam rural police on Thursday arrested two persons on charges of abusing a 10-year-old girl.

The accused have been identified as Anil, 27, a native of Neriyamangalam and Haridas, a resident of Varappetty. The arrests followed a complaint by the school authorities, who had come to know about the incident during a counselling session.

Beginning last week, the accused abused the girl several times.
