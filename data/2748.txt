Andhra Pradesh
Jul 17, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was injured after a teenaged boy allegedly threw a chemical substance on her in Narayanapet of Mahaboobnagar district.

The 13-year-old boy, neighbour of the girl, threw the liquid (taken out from a battery) on her when she was playing in front of her house causing injuries on her thighs.

The juvenile threw the suspected chemical substance on the girl over a petty reason.
