Uttar Pradesh
Dec 19, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by three youths here, police said today.

The accused identified as Arif, Rashid and Noora, who live in the same locality as victim's, raped her on December 16 after giving her sedatives, they said.

The girl narrated the incident to her parents, who registered an FIR in this regard yesterday, they said, adding, the accused are absconding and efforts are on to nab them.
