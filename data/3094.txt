Jharkhand
Sep 11, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her uncle in Khalari block.

The accused Surendra Ganju (25) was arrested and produced before the judicial magistrate court which sent him to 14 days judicial custody.

The parents of the four-year-old girl lodged an FIR in which they complained that Ganju took the toddler to a paddy field and raped her, the victim sustained serious injuries during the incident.
