Chandigarh
Mar 31, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			An 18-year-old youth was arrested after he allegedly attempted to rape two six-year-old girls at his hut in Bapu Dham Colony on Wednesday morning. 

According to the police, the incident occurred around 11.30 am when the two girls were playing outside their hut. The accused lured the two to come inside his hut. Later, worried family members reached the spot and found the accused in a compromising position. The family raised the alarm after which neighbours gathered and thrashed the accused.
