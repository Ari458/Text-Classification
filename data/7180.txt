Andhra Pradesh
Jun 17, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old speech-impaired was allegedly sexually assaulted by a 23-year-old man at Tatavaripalem, near Kondepi in Prakasam district on Tuesday night. Kondepi Sub-Inspector V.Anjaneyulu said the accused took the girl to an isolated place and committed the offence, when she went to attend the nature's call .

Invoking provisions of the Nirbhaya Act and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO Act), Kondepi police launched a search for the accused, who fled the village after committing the crime.
