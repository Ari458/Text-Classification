Kerala
Jun 17, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by her drunken father at Thodiyoor near Karunagappally in Kerala. 

According to the police, Madusoodhanan, 43, is a habitual drunkard and is a problem maker at home and in the neighbourhood.

On the day of the incident, the accused beat his wife brutally in the evening after coming home drunk. To escape from the attack, she fled to a nearby house leaving her daughter alone in the house. It was at this time that he allegedly raped the girl.
