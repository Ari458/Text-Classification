Jammu
Sep 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two people were arrested for allegedly raping a teenaged deaf and mute girl in Reasi district of Jammu and Kashmir.

A Special Investigation Team (SIT) led by SP Reasi, M S Chouhan, apprehended two people- Mohan Singh and Qubla Singh- in connection with the alleged rape of a 16-year-old deaf and mute girl in Bagh Kotli village of Reasi district on September 3.

The two miscreants confessed to their crime after they were identified by the minor during an identification parade along with others.

On September 3, the victim was intercepted by the motorbike-borne accused when she was on her way back home from a shop. The duo dragged her to a nearby field and raped her before fleeing from the spot.
