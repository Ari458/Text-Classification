Uttar Pradesh
Aug 07, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman was allegedly killed by her husband for dowry in Kotwali area here, police said today.

The victim identified as Sarita was attacked with a hammer by her husband Ramendra Verma in Koyalbagh locality yesterday.

Sarita's family members have lodged an FIR against seven people, including Ramendra, in this regard.

Her father Kanhaiyalal has alleged that the accused was an alcoholic and used to torture his daughter for dowry, they said.

Sarita got married to Ramendra in 2008 and the couple has a four-year-old son, said police.
