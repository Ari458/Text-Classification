Kerala
Nov 18, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Two people, including the father of a minor girl, were arrested on charges of sexually molesting her at Pooyapally in the district.

The accused were identified as Sethunath (60), father of the seventh standard student of a local school and his friend Rajesh (33).

They were produced before a court in Kottaraka which remanded them to 14 days judicial custody.

Source: Press Trust Of India
