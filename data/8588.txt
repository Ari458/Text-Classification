Kerala
Jul 13, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Kozhikode Additional Sessions Court Judge on Tuesday sentenced a 50-year-old man hailing from Beypore to 30 years of rigorous imprisonment and fined Rs.15,000 for sexually abusing three boys studying in Class V and VI.

Judge A. Sankaran Nair found the accused Dasan of Thonichira Mundayar Vayal Colony in north Beypore guilty under Section 3 (penetrative sexual assault) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act and Section 377 (unnatural offences) of the Indian Penal Code.

Based on the complaints of the victims parents, the police had registered three cases against Dasan who had been living alone from his estranged wife.

He had sexually abused the children who were residing in the neighbourhood, according to the prosecution.

If the accused refuses to remit the penalty, he will have to undergo another six more months in prison.
