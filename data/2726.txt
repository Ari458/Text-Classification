Haryana
Jul 15, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A man was arrested for allegedly abetting his wife's suicide by ill treating her for dowry.

Satish Kumar of Lilodh village, about 42 km from Rewari, was arrested after his wife Poonam Devi (28) burnt herself to death following persistent ill-treatment by him for dowry.

He was produced in a court at Kosli, which remanded him to 14-day judicial custody.

Kumar was arrested on a complaint filed by Poonam Devi's brother Mahesh Kumar.

Mahesh alleged Satish, who married Poonam on December 4, 2004, ill-treated her for dowry.
