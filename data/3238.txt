Uttar Pradesh
Oct 02, 2013
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A school teacher was arrested for allegedly trying to molest a 10-year-old girl inside the school premises in Nanpara.

According to a complaint filed by the victim's father, Micheal Yeshu alias Yashudas Baba, teacher and manager of St. Peter Inter college, tried to molest the child but failed as some people suddenly arrived there.
