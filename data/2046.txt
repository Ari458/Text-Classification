Hyderabad
Apr 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A middle-aged man was today picked up on charges of attempting to sexually assault a minor girl in Moosapet here, police said.

Chalapathi Reddy, who is the neighbour of the five-year-old girl, an LKG student, allegedly tried to rape the minor after taking her inside his house on April 23, they said based on a complaint lodged by the girl's parents.
