Ramanathapuram
Jun 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			June 22 (PTI) A 22-year-old man was arrested today at Vithanur for allegedly raping a 10th standard girl student.

The 16-year-old girl had come from Tiruchendur to participate in a local festival and was staying at her elder sister's house. On June 19, she was sleeping at home after her sister went to a hospital when the man barged in and raped her. The girl raised an alarm following which he fled from the spot.
