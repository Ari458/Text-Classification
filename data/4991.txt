Uttar Pradesh
Jun 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by two youths at Riyawli village in Muzaffarnagar district, police said.
The incident took place on Sunday in a mango orchard in the village.  The girl narrated the incident to her family members who lodged an FIR against the two youths.
Police have arrested one of the accused identified as Mehmood while the other accused is absconding.
