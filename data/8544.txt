Haryana
Feb 26, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A Rohtak court on Friday sentenced a man to death for raping and killing a three-year-old girl. The additional sessions judge also awarded a sentence of four years for his accomplice who had helped the rapist in escaping from the spot.

The incident dates back to September 11, 2013, when the kid had gone missing from her house. Her parents had lodged a complaint and the girl's body was found abandoned behind bushes of a green belt in Rohtak.

According to information, victim's family had suspected the foul play and named Sandeep who used to work in an under-construction house near the residence of victim's family. He lured the girl and took her to a secluded place where he abused and murdered her. Later, he abandoned the body. He escaped to Nazafgarh after killing the girl.

Soon after arrest, Sandeep confessed to his crime.
