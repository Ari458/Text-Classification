Rajasthan
Sep 11, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in a deserted place in Bharatpur city.

The incident happened in Mathuragate.

A 25-year-old man, running a shop in the area, was arrested after the victim's father lodged an FIR in this connection.

The girl was playing outside her house when the man took her to a deserted place and allegedly raped her.

The incident sparked tension with locals damaging the house of the alleged rapist.
