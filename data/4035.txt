Haryana
Feb 02, 2014
Murder
Description:
			The body of a teenage girl, who had gone missing a week ago, was today recovered from a well in Hasanpur village in the district.

"Some villagers got a foul smell emanating from the well and later they informed us," police said, adding they took out the body from the well with the help of villagers.

Later, a team of forensic experts also visited the spot and lifted some samples from there.

The body has been sent to Civil Hospital for post mortem.

The 14-year-old class VII student had gone missing under mysterious circumstances from her village a week ago.
