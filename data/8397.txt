Madhya Pradesh
Jan 27, 2016
Domestic Violence, Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman was gang-raped by four persons including her husband and his brothers in Dewas after she refused to enter into a settlement in a domestic violence case.

She was stashed in a sack and thrown by the side of a road in front of Nandan Kanan hotel in Dewas on Wednesday morning.

According to locals, the woman's hands were tied behind her back and her lower body was stashed in a jute sack, she was unconscious.

The locals immediately informed the police and rescued the woman. She was rushed to district hospital for treatment by the police.
