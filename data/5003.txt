Maharashtra
Jun 26, 2014
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Former chief medical health officer of the Vasai-Virar civic body, Dr Sunil Wadkar (38), has been arrested for allegedly raping a female doctor, who met him for a job at his private nursing home, at a secluded spot on the highway in Vasai.
He was allegedly removed from the civic body after woman employees had complained of harassment.
