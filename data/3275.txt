Rajasthan
Oct 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old married woman was allegedly raped by an agricultural labourer in Bhankrota.

Mangilal Balaee (42) was arrested after the victim lodged an FIR against him in the Bhankrota police station.

The woman, a vegetable vendor, was kidnapped and taken to a deserted place where she was raped.
