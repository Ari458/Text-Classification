Noida
Apr 27, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a neighbour last night in the capital city while another teenager claimed that she was kidnapped and sexually assaulted.

Police said a Bilaspur resident lodged an FIR on Saturday that his 15-year-old daughter was raped by his neighour Shajid.

He had forcibly taken her to his room and raped her at gunpoint, according to the FIR lodged at Dankaur police station.

Shajid allegedly threatened to kill her if she revealed the incident to anyone. However, the girl confided to her parents who took her to police station.
