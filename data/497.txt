Abuse
Description:
			A husband poured acid on his wife outside a beauty parlour, where she was working. The woman, according to police, was in critical condition and succumbed to her burns injuries. The man, who was brutally beaten by the public after the incident, was admitted at the SSG Hospital. 

Jignasa Soni, who got married to Virendra Soni, was facing harassment for dowry from her in-laws. Her husband Virendra was also torturing her alleging that she was having an affair with someone else.

City Police Station officials said there were family problems which resulted into the incident.
