West Bengal
May 14, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A girl was forced to return to her parents' house a day after she was married for refusing to pay extra dowry. 

Police said soon after the wedding feast was over, Samir (husband) asked his wife Anna to arrange Rs 4,000 as dowry in addition to Rs 6,000 already paid under an agreement. 

As the girl refused to pay the amount, the husband and his relatives forced her to leave for her parents' house, they said. Samir is employed with a local hotel as a casual worker, police said.
