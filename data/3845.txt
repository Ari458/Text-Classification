Howrah
Jan 06, 2014
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The headmaster of a primary school in Howrah district was arrested today for allegedly sexually harassing a woman teacher of the same school for three years.

The woman in her FIR alleged that the headmaster of Gayespur Primary School in Domjur had been sexually harassing her for the past three years, additional SP Sukhendu Hira said.

It was learnt, that the duo had an altercation over the issue on Saturday, following which the teacher lodged the FIR.
