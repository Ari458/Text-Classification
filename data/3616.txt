Kashmir
Nov 29, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was found dead under suspicious circumstances in Kupwara district of Kashmir Valley.

She was found dead in her house at Poruhpat village of Handwara.

A police party rushed to the scene on getting information about the mysterious death of the girl and started inquest proceedings to ascertain the cause of her death.

Her body was handed over to her relatives for last rites after completion of all medico-legal formalities.
