Tamil Nadu
Jun 11, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 39-year-old woman of Arunthathiar Street, Middle Chekkarakudi, was allegedly assaulted and raped by a 16-year-old boy of South Street, Keezha Chekkarakudi on Tuesday evening. 

According to police, the woman was grazing goats at Chekkarakudi when the incident occurred. She was admitted to Tuticorin Government Medical College Hospital.
