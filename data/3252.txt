Karnataka
Oct 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The Fourth Additional District and Sessions Judge B.K. Naik  found Roopesh, a resident of Munchchoor, guilty of raping a 17-year-old girl in Munchchoor in Bajpe on December 10, 2010.

The 17-year-old girl was raped when she was returning to her house on December 10 morning. Roopesh dragged her to a bush and sexually assaulted her. She reported about the incident to her mother. Four days later a complaint was filed.

Among the witnesses examined by Prosecutor Shivaprasad Alva included the girl, her mother, her uncle and the doctor.

In his statement before the court the doctor said though the girl did not have any injuries on her body, sexual assault could not be ruled out.
