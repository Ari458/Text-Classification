New Delhi
Jul 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two youths were arrested for allegedly raping a 19-year-old Nepali girl.

According to police, the girl, who works as a maid at a businessman's house in Geetanjali area of Malviya Nagar, was raped by Shripal and Suresh who work at a nearby under construction building.

The girl told that when she was sleeping on the terrace, the two youths climbed up the building and raped her. They threatened her of dire consequences if she revealed about the incident to anyone.

The girl informed about the incident to the businessman who took her to police and lodged a complaint after which the two youths were arrested.

Source: Press Trust Of India
