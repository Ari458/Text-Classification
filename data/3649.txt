Rajasthan
Dec 06, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor Dalit girl was allegedly raped on the pretext of marriage in Pushkar town in Ajmer district.

The 14-year-old girl was married earlier but staying with her parents in Harijan basti of Adarsh Nagar after leaving her husband where the accused, 17, also lived.

The accused developed friendship with her and took her to Pushkar after allegedly assuring marriage and raped her.

The girl returned and a case was lodged against the accused, also a Dalit.
