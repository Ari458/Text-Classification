Delhi
Aug 21, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 14-year-old blind girl was allegedly molested by her landlord's son in south Delhi's Katwaria Sarai area here this evening.

The accused has been apprehended, police said.

According to the complaint, the accused entered the victim's house when her parents were away for work, and tried to touch her in an inappropriate manner and also misbehaved with her around 5 pm today.

The girl raised an alarm after which the accused fled. She later narrated the ordeal to her parents who informed police.
