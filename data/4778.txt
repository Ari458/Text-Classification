Tamil Nadu
May 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenaged boy was arrested here today for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl at her residence at Puduchetram in the district.

Police said the girl and the 14-year-old boy were watching television at her house on May 20 when the incident occurred.

The girl's mother caught the teenager raping the child and filed a complaint today, following which he was arrested.

Source: Press Trust of India
