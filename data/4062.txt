Andhra Pradesh
Feb 04, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old mentally unstable and dumb woman was allegedly gang-raped by two men here, police said today.

The incident occurred last night when the accused, identified as Gauri Shankar and his aide Shaikh Subani, both 25 and drivers by profession, allegedly dragged the destitute woman to an isolated area in Black Tank locality and sexually assaulted her, they said.
