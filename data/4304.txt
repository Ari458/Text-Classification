Rajasthan
Mar 04, 2014
Dowry Harassment, Rape
Description:
			In a dowry-cum-sexual exploitation case, a 19-year-old newly-wed was allegedly raped by her father-in-law two days after she got married in Sanganer. 

The incident took place on February 10 and FIR in this connection was lodged only recently. 

According to the complaint, the victim said she was physically and mentally tortured by her in-laws for dowry.
