Maharashtra
Feb 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Pimpri police arrested a 27-year-old man for allegedly raping 24-year-old college student on the pretext of marrying her. The police have identified the suspect as Ritesh Navani, a resident of Pimpri.

The incident took place between June 2013 and February this year.

In June 2013, the student was admitted in a Pune based private hospital for some medical treatment. She came into contact with the suspect in the hospital. The suspect told her that he was a student of medical college and would help her in getting proper treatment.

The student got acquainted with the suspect. The suspect then allegedly raped her at her residence and in a lodge in Chikhali on the pretext of marrying her. He also had threatened to kill her parents and repeatedly raped her.

The woman approached the police when she came to know that the suspect was not going to marry her.

The police have booked the suspect Navani under section 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code.
