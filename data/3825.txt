West Bengal
Jan 03, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped in Birbhum district while she was returning from a village fair.

The victim's father today lodged a complaint with the Nalhati police station alleging that his daughter has been raped by Ekramuddin while she was returning from a fair at Bhelian village.

As his daughter was a little behind her friends, Ekramuddin suddenly pounced on the girl and dragged her to a nearby bush and raped her.
