Delhi
Mar 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old man was arrested for allegedly raping his 11-year-old daughter for last three months in South Delhi's Malviya Nagar.

The police said the accused had threatened his daughter of dire consequences if she revealed anything to her mother.

"The victim finally told her mother yesterday that her father used to rape her when she used to go out for work. The mother did not immediately confront her husband and instead waited through the night and approached the police today," a senior police officer said.

Police arrested the father and have sent the victim for medical examination.
