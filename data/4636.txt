Tamil Nadu
Apr 28, 2014
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			Government railway police on Saturday arrested a private company employee and his parents on charges of dowry harassment and abetting the suicide of his wife.

Kanchana Devi, 25, killed herself and her three-year-old son, Karthikeyan, by jumping in front of a train near Pattabiram on February 17. Inquiries revealed that she had taken the extreme step due to dowry harassment.

Based on a complaint filed by Kanchana's father, police registered a case and arrested her husband, Karuna Murthy, his father, Kannaiah, 53, and mother, Maheswari, 50.

They were remanded in judicial custody after being produced before a magistrate court in Chennai.
