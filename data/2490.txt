Ushait
Jun 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was sentenced to life imprisonment by a local court for raping a minor girl and later forcing her to abort.

Additional District Judge S N Tripathi also imposed a penalty of Rs 1.2 lakh on the convict Ahbaran Singh.

70 per cent of the penalty amount will be given to the victim.

Singh had sexually assaulted the 15-year-old, a resident of Ushait area of the district, on pretext of marrying her and when she became pregnant, he forced her to abort.

A complaint in this regard was lodged on the directives of National Human Rights Commission a complaint was lodged in 2011.
