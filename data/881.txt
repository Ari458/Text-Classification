Srinagar
Dec 07, 2011
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Shazia, a house wife was found hanging at her husband’s home on 6th November 2011.

Her relatives claimed that she was murdered by her husband, who they say, was beating and harassing her for dowry. They say that the tumult in their relationship had started from day one of their marriage but she was bearing it because of their daughter, until it finally took a toll with her life.

Since her death, her family and friends have been demanding justice for Shazia.
