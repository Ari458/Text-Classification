Noida
May 21, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man near an under-construction building at Roza Jalalpur village falling under Bisrakh police station.

The girl was raped by one Anwar last night, who had forcibly took her to a lonely place near an under-construction building after finding her alone, they said, adding he also threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed the matter to anybody.

Later, the accused was arrested.

The victim has been sent for medical examination.

Source: Press Trust of India
