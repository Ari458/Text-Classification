Goa
Aug 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A sub-inspector of Goa Police was arrested for allegedly raping his 19-year-old niece at several occasions since January this year.

Sub-inspector Razashad Sheikh was arrested by Mapusa police where he has been deputed, following a complaint filed by the mother of the victim.

The accused, who is in his late twenties, is married and the victim was staying at his place.
