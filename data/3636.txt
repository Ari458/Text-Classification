Haryana
Dec 03, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man was booked for allegedly raping a minor.

According to a complaint lodged by the victim's father, Hari Singh, a resident of Mathura, raped the 12-year-old girl in Sanjay Colony.

Singh used to stay at the house of a relative of the victim where her family, including her brother, had moved after her mother's death six months ago.

The father, who works as a scrap dealer, was away on work when Singh called the girl for some reason and raped her.

Source: Press Trust Of India
