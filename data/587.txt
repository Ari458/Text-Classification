Rajasthan
Nov 06, 2011
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Some residents spotted a foetus floating in Nandsamand Dam in Nathdwara police station area of Rajsamand district, some 340 km from state capital Jaipur. They informed the police.

The foetus of a girl child was five-month old.  Cases of female foetus being recovered from the dam have been reported in the past too.
