Manimajra
Dec 03, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her step-father in Manimajra town near here, police said on Monday.

According to the complaint filed by the victim, the 14-year-old girl was raped on several occasions during the past few months by Sushil Kumar(32) in the absence of her mother, they said.

The girl's mother was married to Kumar after she had divorced her first husband about four years ago, police said.
