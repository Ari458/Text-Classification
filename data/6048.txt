Amethi
Dec 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was today allegedly raped by her relative in Sonpur Mankanth area in the district, police said.

Amethi police station SHO Mohammad Hamid said a case has been registered by the girl's mother against Ram Baran Tiwari (65), who is elder brother of the girl's father.

According to the SHO, there were some family disputes between both the families.
