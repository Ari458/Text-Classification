Tamil Nadu
Jul 01, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl student was allegedly raped by father of her classmate today at Attur.

The seventh standard student went to her classmate's house this morning as they go to school together daily. When the girl reached the house, her classmate's father took her to an isolated place and raped her, Attur All Women Police Inspector Kannan said.

The girl returned her house and narrated about the incident to her parents, who lodged a complaint to police.

The accused was arrested under section 4 of the Child Sexual Harassment Act and produced before a local court and sent to 15 days judicial custody, Kannan said.

Source: Press Trust of India
