Bathinda
Feb 04, 2016
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The police have registered a case against four persons for demanding dowry.

In a complaint lodged with the police, Sukhpal Kaur said she was being harassed by her in-laws after her marriage.

Investigating Officer Balwinder Singh said a case under Section 498-A of the IPC had been registered against the accused, identified as Sandeep Singh, Baldev Singh, Manjit Kaur and Charanjit Kaur of Sirsa district in Haryana.
