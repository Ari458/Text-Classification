Tamil Nadu
Aug 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Locals rescued a minor girl who shouted for help after a 20-year-old youth allegedly tried to sexually harass her at his house.

The youth lured the nine-year-old girl to his house,saying her father was at home in an inebriated state. When she came in, he closed the door and allegedly removed her dress and tried to sexually harass her.

The girl started shouting and as locals rushed to her rescue, the youth escaped in the cover of darkness.
