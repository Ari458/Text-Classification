Uttar Pradesh
Jun 18, 2014
Molestation, Crime
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl allegedly committed suicide by setting herself on fire in Shahjahanpur district soon after a youth molested her when she had ventured out of home to relieve herself.

According to Inspector General of Police (Law and Order) Amrendra Sengar, the incident occurred on Monday when the victim had stepped out of her home to attend nature's call where the accused, Pawan waylaid and allegedly outraged her modesty.

Immensely disturbed by the incident, the girl took the extreme step, poured kerosene and set herself on fire, Sengar said.

According to the World Health Organisation, around 65 per cent of people in villages defecate in the open in the country.
