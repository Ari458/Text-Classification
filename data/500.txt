Orissa
Oct 22, 2011
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by three men while she was waiting to catch a bus on Wednesday night. The matter came to light after the girl was rescued and subsequently handed over to a shelter house on Thursday.

"I was waiting to catch a bus to go to my uncle's house at Bhanjanagar in Ganjam district. A man came to me and offered me cold drink. He took me to his house and raped me. Two of his friends also forced themselves on me," she said.

The girl said she had fled from her house in Cuttack. "My parents used to torture me. So I fled," she said.
