Chhattisgarh
Sep 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor tribal girl was allegedly raped by two men in Jashpur district of northern Chhattisgarh.

The alleged incident took place a week ago, but the complaint was lodged recently.

The 16- year-old girl was raped when she was returning to her village, Bahama, on the night of September 15.

According to the victim, she and her friend were returning from Dumarbhawana village after watching a dance programme when the accused Jageshwar and Tihali, from the same village, accosted them at a lonely spot.

The victim's friend managed to flee.

A case under section 376 (2) (g) (gangrape) and other sections of Indian Penal Code and sections 3 and 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 had been registered against the accused.
