Tamil Nadu
Jul 06, 2016
Domestic Violence
Description:
			A constable of 6 Battalion of Tamil Nadu Special Police, M. Kannan (28) was arrested and sent to judicial custody on charge of harassing his wife under the influence of liquor.

The police said that Kannan used to torture his wife, Dhanalakshmi (23), in a drunken mood. Though a case was earlier registered in All Women Police Station in Tirupparankundram in the past, it was withdrawn following a compromise.

However, he again abused her in filthy language, beat her up and threatened her with dire consequences. Based on a complaint by Dhanalakshmi, the Tallakulam police arrested the policeman.
