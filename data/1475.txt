Hyderabad
Mar 15, 2012
Rape
Description:
			The police has arrested six people in connection with an alleged rape that was perpetrated under the Dhabeerpura police station limits on Sunday night.

The accused have been identified as Syed Mohammed, 31, Imran Ali Khan, 29, Mohd. Naser Khan, 31, Mohd Azam, 30, Mohd Hashmath, 31, and Mohd. Chand, 42.

The victim who works as a house maid said that on Sunday at about 10.30 pm, she was raped by Syed Mohammed, with the help of his associates at an abandoned house at Chanchalguda. After the incident, she said, she was threatened by the accused that if she lodged a complaint they would kill her and her children.
