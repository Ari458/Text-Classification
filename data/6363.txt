Jharkhand
Feb 05, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A person was today sentenced to life imprisonment for killing his wife in 2011.

The Court of Principal District Judge here Pradeep Kumar Srivastav, convicted Kerele Koda for killing his wife Sunita Diggi and sentenced him to life imprisonment.

The judge also slapped a fine of Rs 10,000 on the convict.

In default, he will have to serve an additional period of imprisonment and both the sentences will run concurrently.

As per the FIR lodged by the victim's father Maheti Diggi on October 7, 2011 with the Sonua Police station of West Singhbhum district, his son-in-law killed daughter Sunita following a domestic feud.

He had stated that Sunita went missing mysteriously following the quarrel but her body was found hanging from a tree near her village Chinikuti under Sonua police station.
