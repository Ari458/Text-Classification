Uttarakhand
Oct 07, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A man allegedly killed his wife with a sharp-edged weapon after a tiff with her at Laksar in Haridwar district.

50-year-old Snehlata Devi died on the spot after being hit in the head by her husband Punjab Singh with a sharp-edged weapon at Kuankheda village near Laksar in Haridwar district.

Singh attacked his wife in a fit of rage after an argument with her.
