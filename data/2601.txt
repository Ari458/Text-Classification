Andhra pradesh
Jul 05, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in Bheemunipatnam area.

According to the complaint, the girl had gone to a market at Bheemunipatnam to buy medicines. When she was returning, K Yellaji (22), resident of the same area as the girl, offered her lift on his motorbike.

However, instead of taking her to her house, he took her to a secluded spot four km away and raped her. Afterwards he fled.

According to police, the girl's parents first approached the elders in the community to seek justice against the culprit, but to no avail. Thereafter they went to the police.
