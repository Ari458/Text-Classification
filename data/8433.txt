Andhra Pradesh
Feb 05, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was sexually abused at Palasa in Srikakulam district on Thursday. On hearing her cries, the local people rushed to rescue her and nabbed the accused.

The accused, Dharma Rao (40), was taken into custody and sent to Palasa police station.

Dharma Rao belonged to Vajrapukotturu mandal of Srikakulam district.

The police said that the girl was sent to a nearby hospital for treatment.
