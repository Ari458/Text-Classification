Shivala Kalan
May 11, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was gang raped by three persons in Shivala Kalan area in Bijnor district yesterday, police said.

An FIR was lodged against the accused Ritesh, Reenu and Arjun, they said.

Station House Officer Sunil Kumar Yadav said the girl was sent to the district hospital for medical examination where in the rape was confirmed.

He said that one of the accused Ritesh had been arrested.
