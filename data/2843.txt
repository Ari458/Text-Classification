Sikkim
Jul 30, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Three taxi drivers were taken into custody in East Sikkim on charges of wrongful confinement, kidnapping and rape of two minors who were reported missing.

The two sisters, aged 16 and 12 years, who had gone missing from Singtam in East Sikkim were also traced.

The sisters had been taken away by a taxi driver who allegedly sexually assaulted the younger sister while returning from Siliguri on July 22.

The sisters were reported to have been lodged in a hotel at Lall Bazaar in Singtam by two other taxi drivers.
