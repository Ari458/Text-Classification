New Delhi
Aug 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 29-year-old Iraqi national has been arrested for allegedly raping a girl in South Delhi's Kalkaji area.

According to the police, Saif Mohammed was arrested for sexually assaulting a woman who resides in his neighbourhood.

Saif Mohammed was a student at Jamia Millia Islamia. He was pursuing M.Sc and had been living in Delhi since the last several years.

Source: Press Trust Of India
