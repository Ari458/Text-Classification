Maharashtra
Apr 30, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Khadak police on Monday arrested a 25-year-old man for allegedly raping a 19-year-old girl from Bhavani Peth area after promising to marry her. The police said the suspect Firoz Arge is a resident of Tadiwala Road. The suspect and the woman knew each other since the last three years. When she became pregnant, the suspect refused to marry her, police said.
