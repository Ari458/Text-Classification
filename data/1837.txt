Haryana
Nov 18, 2012
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl, who was allegedly abducted by a truck driver a few days ago from Bihar, was gangraped by him and other drivers and later dumped here, police said on Sunday. A local resident found the girl lying in a semi-conscious state near the Milk Plant on Saturday and informed the
authorities, they said.

The girl told the police that she was allegedly abducted by the truck driver from her village in Bihar and was subsequently raped by him and some other drivers before being dumped here. The state's Women and Child Development Department has taken the girl under their custody and shifted her to a local hospital for treatment and medical examination, police said.
