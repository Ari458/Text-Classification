Rajasthan
Dec 31, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman complained that since her marriage in June 2013, her husband Asif and in-laws were harassing her for dowry and threatened to kill her.

On her complaint, Asif, his father Islam, mother Harmani and his sister, residents of Bharatpur in Rajasthan, have been booked, the police said.
