Andhra Pradesh
Dec 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			(PTI) An autorickshaw driver was arrested for allegedly raping a 19-year-old girl student in Sunkollu village of Nuzvid mandal in Krishna district.

Autorickshaw driver P Srinivasa Rao, who frequently dropped a 19-year-old girl native of Sunkollu village to college, became very close to her.

The girl and the autorickshaw driver, who belong to the same caste and same village, were in a live-in relationship.

The driver had promised to marry the girl, but went back on his word later.

The girl lodged a complaint in Nuzvid Rural police station.
