New Delhi
May 05, 2015
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A security guard was sentenced to 10 years imprisonment by a trial court for abducting and raping a 31-year-old woman. 

The court awarded the rigorous jail term to 32-year-old Paramjeet, a Haryana resident, while relying on the woman's s testimony. 

A fine of Rs 40,000 was also imposed on Paramjeet for raping the woman on the pretext of giving her a job. 

"Testimony of the woman on careful perusal and analysis is found to be clear, natural, cogent, convincing, trustworthy and inspiring confidence," additional sessions judge Mahesh Chander Gupta said.
