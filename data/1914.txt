Delhi
Jan 10, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A 25 year old home guard was molested inside a DTC bus. The accused was thrashed by a mob and later booked. The incident took place in the Madhu Vihar area. 

The woman was going to a police station where she was posted when the accused started misbehaving with her and threatened to abduct her.

Source: 09th January 2013 New Indian Express via PTI
