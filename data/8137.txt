Tamil Nadu
Dec 07, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old youth, M. Andi, was accused of having sexually abused a 5-year-old girl in a place under Sholavandan police station limits on Friday.

Police said that the accused, a tractor driver, had taken the girl, who was studying in a local school, to a farm and committed the crime. The girl was being brought up by her aunt. After the girl’s parents got wind of the incident, the aunt lodged a complaint with the police.
