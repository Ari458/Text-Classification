Parli
Apr 24, 2013
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A two-day-old baby girl was found abandoned near Mahavitaran office in Parli in the district this evening, police said.

The girl was in a hole made for water pipeline valve and two labourers found her when they heard crying.

She was covered in a cloth. Doctors sent her to Ambajogai Hospital for further treatment.
