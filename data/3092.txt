Uttar Pradesh
Sep 10, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her cousin brother at Thana Medical area.

The incident took place when Ravi (22) raped his eight-year-old cousin sister in an inebriated condition.

The victim's parents were not present at home when the incident took place.

After listening to the girl's plight, her parents informed the police and lodged a complaint, following which the accused was arrested and sent to a jail today.
