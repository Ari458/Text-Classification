Haryana
Apr 24, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl has filed a case against a youth for allegedly harassing her, police said on Thursday.

In her complaint, the girl alleged that the accused Amit teased and followed her while she was going to the market on Wednesday, they said.

A case has been registered against the youth under Section 12 of POCSO (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences) Act, police said.
