Haryana
Feb 18, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was raped by her neighbour at her house in Dharuhera area here.

Ishwar, aged 21, entered his neighbour's house in Bhim Basti colony last night and allegedly raped the married woman.

The woman, aged 28, was alone in her house when the alleged incident happened.

When the woman's husband came home, she narrated the incident to him and the couple filed a complaint in a police station in this regard.
