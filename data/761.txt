Tehri
Nov 26, 2011
Crime
Description:
			Dead body of a woman Guddi Devi (40), wife of Prabhu Das of Kansali village was found in Tehri lake. Police said that the woman was missing from her house for the last two days. The body was sighted by some villagers this morning in the Tehri dam.

Source: The Pioneer 25 November 2011
