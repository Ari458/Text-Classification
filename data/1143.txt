Abuse
Description:
			The victim had married Mohammed Bayas on December 5, 2010 with seven sovereigns of gold ornaments and one lakh rupees as dowry to the groom. 

However, Mohammed Bayas did not have a job and sold off the jewels. According to the FIR, he compelled her to have sexual intercourse with his friends. He threatened her that he would upload the videos on the internet, if she resisted. Eventually, Shemeena escaped his custody and came to her brother's house at Nehru Nagar at Kalapatti. Bayas visited her here as well and threatened her. He demanded 10 sovereigns of gold and one lakh rupees from her family and threatened to upload the video on internet if they failed to accede to his demand.
