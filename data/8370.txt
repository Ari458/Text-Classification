Kerala
Jan 23, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			The Poojappura police have registered two separate cases under provisions of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act in connection with complaints received from the Child Welfare Committee unit here. The police said the complaints pertained to the sexual harassment of two minor girls, aged 17 and 15, with one of them hailing from the Malayinkeezhu police station limits, and the other, from the Kallambalam police station limits.
