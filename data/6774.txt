Andhra Pradesh
Apr 23, 2015
Rape
Description:
			An elderly man was today arrested after he allegedly tried to rape his daughter-in-law in Andhra Pradesh's Nellore district.

The accused, aged around 65, attempted to rape his 30-year-old daughter-in-law at their home in Gudur town of the district last night, they said.

Based on a complaint by the woman, a case was registered against Rao and he was subsequently arrested, Gudur sub-inspector Hanif said.
