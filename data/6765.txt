Haryana
Apr 18, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman, Renu, resident of Trikha Colony, has filed a case yesterday at police Station Ballabgarh City accusing her in-laws of harassing her for dowry. 

She alleged her in-laws were demanding money from her for buying a new house and used to torture her, while her husband used to beat her up.
