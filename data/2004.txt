Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old girl was allegedly gangraped by five youths in Chhattisgarh's Raigarh district, police said today.
"The incident took place when the girl was returning along with her boyfriend on his motorbike after visiting Chandrapur temple on Sunday (April 14) evening to Raigarh
town," he added.
The duo stopped at a place in Tetla village near Pusaur police station due to bad weather conditions.
Later, a few men beat up the girl's boyfriend and threatened him to leave the place.
The five accused then allegedly outraged her modesty and fled from the spot, the ASP said.
They also threatened the victim with dire consequences if she informed about the crime to anyone, he said.
