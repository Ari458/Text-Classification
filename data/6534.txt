Uttar Pradesh
Mar 02, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A man, his brother and father were today awarded 7 years rigorous imprisonment by a district court in a dowry death case.

The victim woman Deepti died under mysterious circumstances in December, 2009 about seven months after her marriage and a dowry death case was registered against her husband and in-laws.

The court convicted the three persons while acquitting four others, the woman's mother-in-law, sister-in-law, uncle and uncle's son.

The convicted are victims husband Umesh his brother Mukesh and his father Bhagwan who were handed out 7 years rigorous imprisonment.

Deepti was married on April 27, 2009 and died under mysterious circumstances on December 8 the same year.
