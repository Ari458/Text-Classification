Kanjhawla
Feb 11, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was kidnapped from Kanjhawla in north-west Delhi by two men who sexually abused her for hours in a car roaming around Delhi streets. One of the accused - Amit is a friend of victim's relative and she had met him on earlier occasions.  According to police, the duo allegedly raped her in the car and then dumped her in Kanjhawla after which she went home and narrated the incident to family. The matter was reported and the police arrested the men based on the victim's statement.
