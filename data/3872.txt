Tamil Nadu
Jan 10, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old boy was arrested for allegedly sexually assaulting a six-year-old girl at nearby Mettutherunam.

The incident occurred when the girl was returning home from school. The teenager took her to a nearby field and allegedly raped her.

The girl went home crying to her parents who then filed a complaint at an all-woman police station.

The child has been admitted to the government hospital here.

Source: Press Trust of India
