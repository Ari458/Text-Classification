Mapusa Market
Nov 21, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The 22-year-old victim of commercial sex trade rescued by Crime branch from Mapusa market is a Bangladeshi.As per the victim, she was trafficked from Bangladesh to West Bengal and then brought to Mumbai.

The police caught the traffickers when the girl was being taken to the prospective customer. Police arrested two persons for trafficking her to Goa for prostitution. The accused were identified as Nagesh (full name not known), Chandrakala Dixit alias Annapurna and Chintamani Krishnappa Potdar were arrested from Mapusa bus stand.
