Uttar Pradesh
Aug 08, 2014
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The principal of a primary school was beaten by villagers before the police arrested him on charges of sexually harassing a woman education motivator in Azamgarh district on Thursday.
Nilu Yadav (name changed) is deployed as education motivator at a primary school in Badhardih in Maharajganj in Azamgarh district. Around 10 am, principal Iqbal Ahmed called her inside his office where he allegedly started indulging in objectionable activities. The woman raised an alarm.

On hearing it, the school staff, students and large number of villagers gathered there and thrashed the principal. Later, the police reached there and took Iqbal into custody.
