Uttar Pradesh
Dec 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old girl was allegedly raped by an army officer on the pretext of getting her a teaching job in an army school.

The incident occurred on October 22 when accused Rohita Sharma (29), a Lieutenant in army and resident of Dabthuva village, called the victim to the hotel room on the pretext of giving her a job and raped her.

The medical examination of the girl is being conducted, while her statement has been recorded.

Army and police officials have been been contacted to look into the matter.
