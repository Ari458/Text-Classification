Uttar Pradesh
Jan 05, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly kidnapped and raped by her neighbour in Hayatnagar area here, police said today.

The incident took place on January 3 when the 16-year-old girl was kidnapped and raped by one Mukesh, they said.

The victim's family members filed an FIR yesterday, following which, the accused was arrested.
