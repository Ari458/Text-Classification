Telangana
Nov 04, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Five members of a family have been arrested on charges of causing death of a 28-year-old woman in Vanasthalipuram police limits following dowry-related harassment.

The deceased, Swathy, attempted suicide by hanging herself on October 15 and  died on October 28. According to police, the woman said in her suicide note that she had been harassed for dowry by her in-laws. Based on a complaint lodged by her father, police arrested five members of the womans in-laws, husband Anirudh, his mother Rajalakshmi, his father Ramachandra Reddy, and his brother Kiran and sister-in-law Sumabindu.

Chandraiah said his daughter had been harassed for dowry for two years, said B Bhaskar, assistant commissioner of police, Vanasthalipuram. The couple have two children, a 4-year-old-son and an 11-month-old daughter.
