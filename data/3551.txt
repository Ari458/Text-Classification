Uttar Pradesh
Nov 19, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two people were arrested for allegedly making obscene MMS of a teenage mentally-ill girl and sexually exploiting her using the video.

The youths were arrested from Faithfulganj area following a complaint by the 16-year-old's father, hunt is on to nab the other six accused.

The father alleged that few days back, the accused made the girl's video after feeding her with some sedative-laced food item.

The accused also exploited her sexually, threatening that they would made the video public if she disclosed this to anyone.

The matter came to light when the girl told about the matter to her elder sister.

She has been sent for medical examination.

The girl was also receiving psychiatric treatment.
