Assam
Jul 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly molested by a policeman leading to mob violence and police firing in Assam's Kamrup district.

A girl, who was going alone on a bicycle, was stopped by a police constable, identified as Kamal Krishna Nath, deployed at the Gaachlia police camp in Rangia subdivision of the district, and was allegedly molested by him.

The girl went and informed the people of her village following which an angry mob gheraoed the camp and pelted stones demanding that the policeman be handed over to them.

The police resorted to lathicharge and burst tear gas shells to disperse the mob but they refused to go following which the police opened six rounds of blank fire.

Some policemen and members of the public were injured when the mob clashed with the policemen.

Senior civil and police officials, including Sub- divisional Officer Manisha Chelleng and Sub-divisional Police Officer (SDPO), rushed to the spot and brought the situation under control.

The police constable who allegedly molested the girl was arrested and produced before the Sub-Divisional Judicial Magistrate who remanded him to jail custody while the camp was also disbanded from the spot.

Security has been tightened with police and paramilitary forces deployed in the area.

Meanwhile, a magisterial inquiry has been ordered into the incident to be conducted by magistrate Mamoni Parme and the report must be submitted within six months.

Source: Press Trust Of India
