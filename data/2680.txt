Nagpur
Mar 31, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped by a mechanic under the pretext of marrying her.
	
On the complaint of the victim against the accused Yogesh Machirke, police booked him under relevant sections of IPC including rape.
	
According to police, Machirke and the victim had been into sexual relationship over a period of time, but he later went back on his word.
	
Police said the victim foiled the accused's plan to marry another woman when she filed a complaint against him.
