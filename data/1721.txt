Rewari
Jun 17, 2012
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl was allegedly raped by three youths in Haryana's Rewari district on Saturday. She had returned by a late night train from Sirsa and was going to her residence in Bacchod village when she was forcibly picked up by the youths, police said.

The youths, riding a motor-cycle, abducted the girl from Rao Tula Ram Park here and took her to a deserted place where they allegedly criminally assaulted her, police said.
