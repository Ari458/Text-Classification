Maharastra
Jan 12, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Rekha Chavan (42), a Dalit widow, was assaulted and allegedly paraded naked and as her 22-year-old son eloped with a girl from a high-caste family.  The police however refused to confirm if the victim had been paraded naked. victim has been admitted to a hospital at Karad in the district.
