Karnataka
Aug 06, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl committed suicide by setting herself ablaze at Paccharlahalli under the Vemagal police limits in Kolar taluk.

The police have arrested Pramod (21), a first year B.Com student, on the charge of kidnap and rape. Cases have been booked against him under provisions of the POCSO Act, Vemagal sub-inspector B.S. Yashwanth told The Hindu .

According to the police, the girl used to work in a field belonging to Pramod's family at Aleri. The two fell in love and eloped from the village on July 31.

Pramod then sexually abused the girl and left her back at her house on August 3.

Upset over this, she doused herself with kerosene and set herself on fire when the family members were out. The neighbours, who heard her screams, doused the fire and admitted her to Victoria Hospital in Bengaluru. She died on Tuesday.
