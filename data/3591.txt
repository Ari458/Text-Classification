West Bengal
Nov 27, 2013
Murder
Description:
			The husband, father-in-law and mother-in-law of a housewife were arrested in North 24 Parganas district after she was found dead under mysterious condition.

The housewife was found dead under mysterious condition at her house in Sahebcolony of Naihati town.

The parents of the victim lodged a police complaint alleging that she was killed by her husband and in-laws.
