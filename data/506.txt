Delhi
Oct 23, 2011
Molestation
Description:
			A travel agency's salesman was arrested for sexually assaulting a 26-year-old woman at a hotel in south Delhi Sunday afternoon, police said. The incident was reported around 2.10 p.m. from Vasant Kunj, when police received a call from the woman who claimed that she had been raped. 

The victim, a resident of Buddh Vihar in west Delhi who works as a sales executive, has alleged that the man called her to his hotel room and then raped her, said police officer.
