Kanaknagar
Feb 22, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Police said that on February 12, A 13-year-old girl from Ramnathpara was lured away from a wedding function in Kanaknagar area and raped for two days in confinement. She surfaced two days later and confided in her parents.

Accused Alpesh Bavaji (23) had taken the girl to his relative's home near Kaisar-e-Hind bridge, where he raped her. He lured her away from the wedding by giving her a joyride on his bike.
