Goa
Aug 18, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman from Karaswada, Mapusa, on Sunday, filed a complaint that she was raped by a Pernem resident under the pretext of marrying her.

The girl, in her complaint filed at the Anjuna police station, said that the alleged accused, Richard Trindade, resident of Korgao, in Pernem, befriended her while she was working at a shop in Panaji.

She added that the incident took place in March 2014, when the alleged accused took the her to an isolated place at Anjuna beach and "sexually exploited" her with a false promise to marry her.

Police registered an offence under Section 376 ( rape) of the Indian Penal Code. PSI Vijay Kawlekar is further investigatigating the case.
