Andhra Pradesh
Aug 13, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A school teacher was found abusing girl students at Podalakur ZP High School. The girl students complained to their parents about the misbehaviour of the teacher Ramesh and refused to go to school. Ramesh had reportedly threatened the children of dire consequences if they complained to their parents.

Parents reached the school and searched for Ramesh, who, however, managed to escape. upon sensing the threat

Parents then lodged a complaint with the local police.

Local CI Hyma Rao said that they would nab the absconding teacher. Joint collector G Rekha Rani also directed the district education officer for a report on the incident and ordered strict action against the errant teachers.
