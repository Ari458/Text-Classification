Khunpura
May 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old deaf and mute girl was allegedly raped by unidentified persons in Khunpura village in the district, police said today.

The girl was raped last evening when she had gone out to play in the village in Kud Fatehgarh area here, they said.

She has been sent for a medical examination to Moradabad district hospital, they said.
