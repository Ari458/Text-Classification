New Delhi
Jun 27, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Four placement agency owners, including a woman, have been arrested for an alleged involvement in human trafficking. Eight minor girls from Assam, Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh have also been rescued, police said on Tuesday.

"We received information that placement agencies  Babita Enterprises, India Maid Bureau, Deepika Placement Agency and Mission Welfare Society  were involved in trafficking of minor girls from Assam, Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh, said Sanjay Kumar Jain, deputy commissioner of police (crime and railways).

The placement agencies had been placing the rescued girls as domestic helps in the city. Of the eight minor girls rescued, four belong to Assam, one is from Chhattisgarh and three from Jharkhand,Jain added.
