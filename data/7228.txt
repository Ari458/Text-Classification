Haryana
Jun 22, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 7-year-old girl was allegedly raped and murdered by her uncle in a village of Hisar district on Sunday. The body of the victim was found in the fields. Police have sent the body for autopsy. A police source said the victim died due to multiple neck bone fractures.

A case of rape and murder has been registered against the accused and police have started investigation into the matter. The investigating officer said, "We got information from the parents of the victim that she was raped and murdered. A case has been registered against one person, on a complaint by the victim's mother. Police teams have been formed to nab the accused."
