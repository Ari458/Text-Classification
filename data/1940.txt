Dhanapur
Jan 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was raped allegedly by two persons in Dhanapur area, police said here today.

The victim was dragged to a canal by the accused and raped on Wednesday.

An FIR has been registered against Sintu Yadav and an unidentified person, police said.

The girl has been sent for medical examination at district government hospital, they said.
