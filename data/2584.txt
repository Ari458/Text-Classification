Gujarat
Mar 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A woman has lodged a complaint accusing her husband of sexually abusing the couple's 15-year-old daughter.
     
The family lives in Bhardasar village of Tharad taluka in north Gujarat district.
     
According to the police complaint lodged, the
woman said she resisted from approaching the police so far fearing bad name for the family.
     
The girl was being sexually assaulted by her father over the last six months. She was sent a for
medical examination.

Source: Press Trust Of India
