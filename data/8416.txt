Punjab
Jan 29, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Meherban police booked a man for trying to sexually abuse his minor daughter after getting drunk. In his police complaint, brother of 17-year-old survivor alleged that his sister and father were at home when he left for work on Wednesday. He added that after some time, he got a call from his sister telling him that father had got heavily drunk and is trying to sexually abuse her.

The complainant said his sister also told him that father is not allowing her to go out. He added that as he reached home, his father threatened to kill them both after which he informed the police. Cops have booked the accused under Sections 342 (punishment for wrongful confinement), 354-B (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) and 506 (criminal conspiracy).
