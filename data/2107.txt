Safdurjung Enclave
May 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old sweeper today alleged that she was gangraped by her two brothers-in-law and their three friends at her home, police said today.

The incident was reported from Safdurjung Enclave area.

All the five accused have been arrested, police said.

The victim, a mother of two, alleged that the five had come to meet her husband.

She left home for work and when she returned, she found the men having alcohol at her house though her husband was not there.

She claimed that she came out of the house, but was overpowered and raped.

Source: Press Trust of India
