Rape
Description:
			Two people have been arrested after a woman accused them of attempting to rape her.

The victim, who lives in Baraula area of Sector-49, alleged that Naresh, Satish and Chhotey sneaked into her house on Saturday night and tried to rape her.

They, however, fled from the spot as the neighbours came after she raised an alarm.
