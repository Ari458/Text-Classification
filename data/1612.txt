Jaipur
Apr 15, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old mentally challenged girl was allegedly raped by a witch doctor who was approached by her family members for a cure for her ailment.

According to police, the girl, had developed some mental illness and her family members thought that it was due to some evil spirit. 

They approached a witch doctor named Mahesh Kumar and requested him to visit their house to see the girl," Surendra Singh, officer in-charge of Amber police station, told. 

He added that on the pretext of casting some spells, Mahesh asked the girl's family members to leave the house. "He said that for the spells to work the girl needed to be isolated. When the family members left, Mahesh allegedly raped the girl," said the officer.
