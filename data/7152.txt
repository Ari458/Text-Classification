Tamil Nadu
Jun 11, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The district police have registered a case against a software engineer from Thudiyalur, who is presently working in the USA, for allegedly harassing his wife for dowry.

The accused, J Bathri Narayanan (29) from TVS Nagar in Idayarpalayam near Thudiyalur married the complainant B Sri Aruna (23) on July 9 last year. The couple went to USA after marriage, where Aruna was allegedly harassed by her husband and his family members.

Recently Aruna returned to Coimbatore and lodged a complaint with the Thudiyalur All Women Police. In her complaint, she also claimed that her jewellery has been retained by her husband's family.

Police registered a case against Bathri Narayanan, his father Jayabarathi Babu (69), mother Kalavathy (67), sister S Sujithra Priya (38) and her husband P Sureshkumar (41). Only the accused's father resides in the city, while the others are in USA.

They were booked under sections 498(A) (husband or relative of husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty) and 406 (punishment for criminal breach of trust) of the IPC.

Police will be approaching the passport authorities to confiscate the passport of Bathri Narayanan.
