Maharashtra
May 29, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old labourer, Prakash Rathod, was arrested on Wednesday for allegedly raping a four-year-old girl. The police said the incident took place around 10 am when the child was playing outside her hut in a slum near Cuffe Parade.

The accused, who was the girl's father's friend, lured her to a secluded hut and allegedly raped her, the police said. Hearing the child's cries, locals rushed to the spot and caught Rathod. The child has been sent for medical test.
