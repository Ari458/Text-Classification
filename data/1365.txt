Sisu Mandir
Feb 23, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A software engineer, Asit Kumar Padhi of Ramharinagar area of the city, was arrested by the personnel of women's police station in the city on the charge of dowry harassment. He had married Suchismita of Kalhandi district in January last. Suchismita is also an employee.

According to a compliant filed by her in the women's police station, her husband and in-laws were torturing her for more dowry. While Asit was arrested, his father and mother are absconding.
