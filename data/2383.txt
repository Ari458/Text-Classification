Morena
Jun 10, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			Madhya Pradesh Mahila Congress president Archana Jaiswal today alleged that Congress leader Urmila Gurjar was tortured in the police custody.

After meeting Gurjar in the hospital, Jaiswal while talking to media alleged that not only was Gurjar beaten, but she was also tortured otherwise in an inhuman manner.

Gurjar, Mahila Congress chief from Morena district led an anti-encroachment agitation here on June 6.

Police had registered a case of obstructing official work against Gurjar, Jaiswal added.
