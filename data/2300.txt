Uttar Pradesh
May 30, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			An eight-year-old boy was battered to death allegedly by three youths after his sister refused the advances of one of them in Haldi area of the district.

Sudhir Kumar's body was recovered from a brick kiln this morning.

Kumar was allegedly battered to death by Nakul, Raja Babu and Rajkumar last night when he had gone to attend a tilak function in Sonwani village.

During interrogation, Nakul admitted that he had asked Sudhir's sister to have an affair with him and had threatened to kill the boy if she refused.

When the girl refused, Nakul and aides killed Sudhir.
