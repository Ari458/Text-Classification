Punjab
Aug 31, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A local court awarded 10 years rigorous imprisonment to a man in a rape case.

The court of additional sessions judge held Manpreet Singh guilty in the case and pronounced the verdict on him.

Singh, a resident of Bhadalthuha village, had barged into the house of the victim on the night of December 21 and raped her.

The convict had even threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed about the incident to anybody.
