Anupparpalayam
Jan 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 65 year old man was arrested at a village in nearby Tirupur district for allegedly trying to molest a nine year-old girl, police said.

They said the man, manning a public toilet at Anupparpalayam village, tried to molest her after she went inside. The girl ran out and informed her father, who lodged a complaint with police.
