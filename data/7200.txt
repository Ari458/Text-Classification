Hyderabad
Jun 19, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Kamatipura police on Thursday arrested Mohd Shamsheer Khan (56), an auto driver, for allegedly sexually assaulting a minor.  According to police, the child's mother had lodged a complaint on June 10. Shamsheer kidnapped the girl in his rickshaw and assaulted her in his house. He then dropped her back later. She was taken to a hospital by her mother. Medical evidence proved that she was subjected to sexual assault, said DCP. Police nabbed the accused and he reportedly confessed during interrogation.
