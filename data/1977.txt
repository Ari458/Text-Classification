Rajasthan
Apr 22, 2013
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Two foetuses, one female and the other male, were today found in separate places in the district today, police said.

The male foetus was found behind a hospital in Nokha town, they said.

Locals spotted the foetus and informed the police following which it was shifted to hospital.

In Rasisar village, a female foetus was found at an isolated area, the police said, adding that separate cases were registered against unknown persons in this regard.
