Mandanapalle
May 13, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			An MBA student was allegedly raped and murdered by a home guard at Madanapalle, 120 km from here, police said today.

The first year student of a private college at Madanapalle was admitted to a Government hospital under mysterious circumstances by the home guard last night and she died at midnight, police said, adding, he was taken into custody for questioning today.

Local politicians and the victim's parents suspected that she was raped and seriously injured by the home guard and tension prevailed for some time after they demanded the arrest of the guilty.
