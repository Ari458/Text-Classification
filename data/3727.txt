Jharkhand
Dec 19, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor tribal girl was allegedly raped at Bariatu locality and an accused was arrested in this connection.

The 11-year-old girl was caught by Goldi Khan and two of his associates forcibly took her to a hilly area and allegedly raped her.

The accused were in a drunken state, Goldi Khan was arrested after the girl identified him while the other two accused were absconding.

The girl informed her family members, who lodged a complaint with the police, she was sent to the Rajendra Institute of Medical Sciences where medical tests confirmed rape.

Agitated local people staged demonstration, demanding security to the family and adequate compensation.
