Rajasthan
Dec 20, 2014
Foeticide/Infanticide, Rape
Description:
			A girl has filed an FIR against three persons, accusing them of rape and forced abortion.

The girl, aged around 22 years, filed the complaint last Thursday against Khem Singh Bhati, Lokesh Bhati, and Vikki Bhati at Pratapnagar police station for allegedly raping her on June 26 in Pratapnagar area.

She also accused the trio for forcing her to undergo an abortion after the incident, police said.

Police has started the investigation after lodging a case against the three accused under relevant sections of IPC.
