Punjab
Jan 28, 2016
Domestic Violence, Molestation, Murder
Description:
			One man has been arrested for killing a seven-year-old-boy after his attempt to rape the victim's mother failed. 

The accused, who hails from Uttar Pradesh, has been identified as Raj Kumar. He was on a night shift in a factory when he barged into her room after scaling the outer wall of the house and allegedly tried to rape the woman. When she fought back, he strangulated the woman's son. The man has been booked for murder. Residents of the colony assembled and nabbed the accused.

The matter was then reported to the police who later arrested Kumar. Bathinda Canal Colony station house officer Harpreet Singh said, "We have arrested Raj Kumar and a case of murder and house trespass has been registered against him."
