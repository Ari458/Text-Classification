Punjab
Jan 10, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Two unidentified men allegedly kidnapped and raped a 13-year-old girl in Kharar on January 7 and dropped her in front of her house on the night of January 8. 

The girl was returning home from her tuition classes when the accused struck. At 1 am on Friday, her mother found her standing outside the house. Initially, the girl did not reveal much but later narrated her ordeal. She said the two youths had covered their face. They allegedly took her to an isolated area and raped her. 

A medical examination of the girl confirmed rape. A case was lodged under sections 366A and 376 of the IPC and relevant sections of POCSO act.
