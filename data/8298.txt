Kerala
Dec 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Naruvamoodu police on Tuesday arrested Shameer, 26, of Paroorkuzhi, near Pallichal, on charges of sexually abusing a minor girl.

According to the police, Shameer, who has a similar case registered against him in the Varkala police station, had sexually abused the 17-year-old girl repeatedly from November 2014.
