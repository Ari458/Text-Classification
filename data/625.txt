Mumbai
Nov 11, 2011
Street Harassment
Description:
			The woman, a resident of Titwala, works in Atagaon and used to travel by train to work. The accused used to molest and tease her on Atagaon station premises when she used to return home. She was scared to lodge a complaint. After some women commuters urged her to complain, she filed a complaint at the Kalyan railway police station on Wednesday. Policemen in plainclothes laid a trap at Atagaon station and nabbed the trio.
