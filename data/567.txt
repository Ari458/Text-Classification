Jaipur
Nov 03, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A woman was gang raped by 3 men, including her 'male companion' at Chandwaji village in Jaipur on Wednesday.

Police Complaint:
The victim, a divorcee was promised marriage by the accused Prakash Gurjar. She agreed to accompany him. Both had worked together for a catering firm. After reaching, Chandwaji, the accused invited two of his friends to the place where the trio, after getting drunk, raped the woman in turns. Later, they left the woman in the jungle and fled.
