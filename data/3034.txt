Bihar
Sep 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at a village in Samastipur district.

A man identified as Md Munna took the 12 year-old girl to an isolated place at Jitwarpur village and allegedly raped her.

The incident took place when the girl was returning to her house after visiting a fair in a nearby village. 

The accused was later arrested from the same village after the girl's family lodged an FIR in this connection.

The girl has been admitted to a hospital for medical examination.
