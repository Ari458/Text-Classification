Hingni Budruk
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a 70-year-old man in Hingni Budruk village here, police said today.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl, playing outside her house in Dharur tehsil, was lured by the accused Damodhar Himtaji Dongare to a nearby farm, where he sexually assaulted her, Police Sub-Inspector R D Panchal said.
