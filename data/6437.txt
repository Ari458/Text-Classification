Bangalore
Feb 14, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			One person was today arrested for allegedly trying to molest a minor girl in R T Nagar Police station limits here.

"This person tried to undress a 10-year-old girl.

Immediately, he was caught and police were informed," DCP North (Bengaluru) T R Suresh said.

Suresh said he has been booked under the POCSO (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences) Act.

Police said the man was known to the girl.
