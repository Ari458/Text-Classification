Palamau
May 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested today on the charge of raping a seven-year-old girl at Lori village in Palamau district, police said.

The grandfather of the girl lodged an FIR accusing their neighbour, Mithelesh Choudhary, of raping the girl when she was sleeping in the house last night.

Choudhary, hailing from Bihar's Gaya district, was staying in the locality.
