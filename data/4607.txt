Jaipur
Apr 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a temple priest in the premises of Madhukeswar temple, a Shiv shrine at Chakroda village in Jajpur district, THE police said today.

The accused, identified as Rabindra Panda alias Rabinana (45), is absconding, the police said.

The incident took place when the girl, a class 10 student, went to the temple on Monday noon to collect 'prasad'.

The priest took her to the kitchen adjacent to the temple on the pretext of giving her prasad and allegedly raped her there. The accused also warned her against telling anyone about the act, the police said.

After returning home, the girl told her parents about the incident. The family members accompanied by the villagers rushed the minor girl to government hospital at Dharmasala.

"After preliminary medical examination, doctors informed us that it was a case of sexual assault and accordingly a police team went to the hospital," Sudarshan Dash, inspector-in-charge (IIC) of Dharmasala police station.
