Khagaria
May 12, 2013
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A newborn girl child was found dead today in the Sadar hospital premises in Bihar's Khagaria district, a hospital official said.

The child was found dead in a heap of garbage in the Sadar hospital, the Deputy Superintendent Krishna Mohan said.

It appears that some outsiders threw the body in the hospital premises from outside its premises, he said.
