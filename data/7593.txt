Punjab
Aug 05, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			A Zirakpur resident, Neeraj Raina, was booked for teasing a girl at the stairs of a showroom in Sector 22 on Tuesday. Raina has obtained anticipatory bail from a local court.

Police said the accused would be arrested after a probe. Police said the victim hails from Nada village near Nayagaon and came to Sector 22 along with her cousin.
