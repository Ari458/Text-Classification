Assam
Aug 18, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 59-year-old woman was severely beaten up allegedly by four persons who accused her of practising witchcraft at no. 2 Ramnathpur village in Sonitpur district.

The woman was beaten when she was alone in her house on Saturday and was found by the villagers in an unconscious state near her house, Superintendent of Police Sanjukta Parashar said. The woman was admitted to Tezpur Medical College Hospital, Parashar said.

One of the accused, Ajoy Orang named by the woman, was arrested and search for three others was on, the SP said.
