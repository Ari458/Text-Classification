Jaipur
Jan 30, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor Dalit girl studying in class nine was allegedly raped in the backyard of her house by a labourer at Teetarvarda, 25 km away from Dausa district headquarters, police said today.

The 20-year-old accused, belonging to Uttar Pradesh, was arrested this morning from the area, Circle Officer of Dausa police Rajendra Singh said.

The incident occurred yesterday when the 14-year-old girl was going home from school.

The girl was admitted to a hospital in her village by parents and the police recorded her statement there.
