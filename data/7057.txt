Kerala
May 31, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			Baiju, 35, a resident of Pattom, has been arrested on charges of harassing women pedestrians near the Medical College area on Saturday. Sheen Tharayil, Circle Inspector, Medical College, said the accused, in a drunken state, used to harass women during evening hours.

The arrest was made following a complaint from a women employee with a bank in the city, the press release said.
