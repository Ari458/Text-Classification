Uttar Pradesh
Mar 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 60-year-old man allegedly attempted to rape an underage girl in Dadri. Police have lodged an FIR against the accused and sent the girl for medical examination.

The accused, Maamchand, is the victim's neighbour. He lured the girl to his house and attempted to rape her. The victim's screams alerted neighbours.
