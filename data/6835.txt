Bangalore
May 04, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman set herself ablaze over alleged dowry harassment in D.J. Halli, on Saturday evening.

The victim, Shabeena, had been married to Irfan, an autorikshaw driver, for the past six years and the couple had two children.

Inquiries revealed that Irfan, an alcoholic, allegedly harassed Shabeena for dowry. Shabeena's parents had bought an autorickshaw for him but Irfan harassed Shabeena for more money. According to the police, on Friday, the couple fought over this and Shabeena set herself on fire the next day. She was rushed to Victoria Hospital but did not survive.
