Andhra Pradesh
Sep 02, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two police constables were arrested for allegedly sexually exploiting a woman for over a month.

Upon the complaint lodged by the victim's husband, the police arrested the constables K V J Vara Prasad (30) and Praveen (26).

The victim, a 30 years old woman, is the wife of an auto driver, who also deals in gold.

In July, he was arrested for buying stolen gold and lodged in central jail at Adivivaram.

According to the complaint, when his wife sought to meet him, Prasad demanded sexual favours from her. The victim agreed; however Prasad, allegedly, also forced her to sleep with Praveen, and the two began to blackmail her.

After the husband got bail last month, he and the victim approached the police.
