Delhi
Feb 18, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A minor girl who was molested while returning from school committed suicide here by taking poison, police said Saturday.

The victim was reportedly molested by a minor boy in west Delhi's Bhalswa Dairy area when she was with one of her friends. The accused abducted both the girls. 

But they molested only the victim, who reached home around 8.30 p.m. Friday and committed suicide within an hour.
