Maharashtra
Nov 03, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old man was arrested on Tuesday for allegedly sexually assaulting a four-year-old girl who lives in his neighbourhood in Shivaji Nagar.

The girl was playing outside her house when the incident took place, said the police. A police source said that the accused had lured the girl by promising her sweets and had taken her to his house.

The police source added that the girl later informed her mother about what had happened after which the child's mother approached officials of Shivaji Nagar police station where a complaint has been lodged.

"We have arrested the accused. He was produced in a court which has remanded him in police custody till November 7," said the source. The child was sent to hospital for a medical examination and a report of the same is awaited.

The accused has been booked for molestation besides relevant sections of the Protection Of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO).
The police refused to reveal much information. "It is a sensitive case and we have invoked sections of POCSO. So we cannot disclose much information about the case," said a police officer.
