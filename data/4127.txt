Tamil Nadu
Feb 10, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A nine-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at a village in the district.

The girl, a Class IV student of Nandakarai, was alone at her house yesterday evening when 45-year-old Kaliyamurthy took her to his power loom factory and committed the crime.

The girl returned home crying to her parents, who filed a police complaint.

A case was registered against Kaliyamurthy under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act-2012 and he was arrested last night.

The child has been admitted to the government hospital here.

Source: Press Trust of India
