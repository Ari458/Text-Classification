Jharkhand
Apr 30, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Three people were arrested and sent to jail on Tuesday from Bhawnathpur in Garhwa for allegedly raping a teenaged Dalit girl, a resident of Ranpura village, on Monday night. The accused, Ashok Ram, Chandan Ram and Raja Ram, were residents of Ranpura. Nagar Untari sub-divisional police officer Surjit Kumar said further action will be taken against the accused after reports of the girl's medical tests are out.

According to the FIR lodged by the victim with the Bhawnathpur police station, Ashok Ram, Chandan Ram and Raja Ram of the same village repeatedly raped her.
