Muzaffarnagar
Jan 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A doctor and his associate allegedly raped a 21-year-old girl at Haibatpur village in the Muzaffarnagar district, police said on Thursday. The victim in her complaint claimed that doctor Praveen Saini and his associate Pradeep raped her when she was returning home from his clinic on Wednesday.

The victim also alleged that she was given some medicine that made her unconscious during the ordeal. A case has been registered against both the accused, police said, adding that a search was on to nab the two who were absconding.

According to the police, the victim has also been sent for a medical examination.
