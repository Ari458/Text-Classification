Haryana
Sep 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old BCA student, who along with his friend allegedly raped a 15-year-old Class XI student, was arrested on Monday. The suspects friend is still at large. 

The rape survivor complained at the womens police station on Monday against the two. She alleged the two boys first sedated her with a drug before raping her in a field on September 4. They left her in an unconscious state and fled, she said in her complaint. 

When she regained consciousness, the girl reached her home but did not reveal her ordeal to her family out of fear, according to the police. 

Her family members, in whom she confided on Monday morning, took her to the police station. 

After getting the girls medical examination done, an FIR was registered under Section 376-G (gang rape) of the IPC. The 21-year- old was arrested in the evening. During interrogation, he confessed to having used a drug to sedate her, a police officer said. 

Later in the evening, police got the girls statement recorded before a magistrate.
