Batala
Mar 26, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was arrested for allegedly demanding dowry from her daughter-in-law.
	
Police registered a case against husband Sanjeev Kumar, father-in-law Des Raj and mother-in-law Neelam for allegedly demanding dowry from Nisha who got married four years ago.
	
She was being tortured and harassed by her in-laws for dowry, Nisha alleged in her FIR.
