New Delhi
Jan 15, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Harsh Vihar area of East Delhi.

The incident took place when the neighbour took the girl to Anand Vihar for some work. After completing the work, he took her to Harsh Vihar on some pretext where he raped her.

The accused then threatened her of dire consequences if she reported the incident and fled from the spot.

The girl somehow managed to reach home where she described her ordeal to her parents, who then took her to police station.

An FIR was registered in this regard after the medical examination of the girl confirmed rape.

Source: Press Trust of India
