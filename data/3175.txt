Andhra Pradesh
Sep 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An eleven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in a village in Kasimkota mandal of the district.

The victim, a class VII student, had gone to a farm to fetch some sugarcanes. Spotting her alone, the accused, who resides in the same village, raped her.

The accused, identified as one N Appa Rao (30), is absconding.

Though the village elders tried to hush up the matter, the victim's family members lodged a complaint with Kasimkota station.

The girl has been admitted to a government hospital in Anakapalle and her condition is stable.
