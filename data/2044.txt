New Delhi
Apr 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Police today arrested an accused for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl in Rasulpur village under Motipur police station of Bihar's Muzaffarpur district, sources said.

Kamrul Hoda (45), a mechanic was nabbed from Motipur bus stand while trying to flee on bus, Deputy Superintendent of Police (Muzaffarpur west) Anuj Kumar said.

The six-year-old girl fell pray to Hoda while she had gone to field to relieve herself yesterday.

Source: Press Trust of India
