Uttarakhand
Oct 16, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A local court has awarded life imprisonment to four tantriks for gang raping a college girl, who had met them after seeing an advertisement in newspaper.

Holding them guilty, Special Judge (SC/ST Act) Amit Kumar Sirohi also imposed a fine of Rs 5,000 each on the convicts Motilal, Subhash Kumar, Rajkumar ans Makkhan Lal.

All the four convicts hailed from Rajasthan's Jhunjhunu district.

In 2005, the woman, after seeing a newspaper advertisement, had met the tantriks seeking a solution to some of her personal problems.
