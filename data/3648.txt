Punjab
Dec 05, 2013
Abduction, Crime
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl from Maujgarh village in the district was allegedly kidnapped by a youth after luring her with marriage promise.

A case has been lodged against the youth Palwinder Singh and his mother and sisters, who allegedly helped him in kidnapping the girl.

Singh allegedly abducted the minor girl on December 3 on the pretext of marriage.

The victim's family has also alleged that the accused stole a gold chain and Rs 70,000 cash from their residence.
