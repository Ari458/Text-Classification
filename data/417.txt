East Godavari
Oct 09, 2011
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A housewife allegedly committed suicide due to the harassment of her husband for dowry. Veeralakshmi, 25, of East Godavari district was married Koteswara Rao of Dondaparty in the city six months ago. Her parents reportedly agreed to give Rs.1 lakh as dowry at the time of marriage. 

As days passed by, Koteswara Rao began harassing his wife to get the dowry from her parental house as promised, it was alleged.

The couple used to quarrel on the issue frequently. Vexed with her husband's behaviour, Veeralakshmi allegedly hanged herself from the ceiling fan hook when there was no one in the house.
