New Delhi
Dec 29, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A five-year-old girl, who had allegedly gone missing from near her residence, was found dead inside a drain in Haiderpur.

The girl has injury marks on her forehead. We are investigating the case from all angles. The body has been sent for post mortem. A case of kidnapping and murder has been registered.

The parents of the girl, Meenakshi, had informed police that she had gone missing when she was playing outside their home in Mongolpuri.

The mother had gone out to look for the girl and on not finding her, had raised an alarm.

Immediately, her family members and neighbours had started looking for her and later in the night, her parents had lodged a case of kidnapping at Mongolpuri police station .
