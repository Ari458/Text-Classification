Haryana
Mar 13, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A man has been arrested here for allegedly raping a 27-year-old married woman, police said today.

The 29-year-old man identified as Roshan Lal, a resident of Mahendergarh district and presently living in Uttam Nagar colony here, was arrested yesterday, they said.

He intruded into the rented accommodation of the victim on the night of February 4 when she was alone and allegedly raped her. He also threatened her of dire consequences if she disclosed the incident to anyone, police said.

Following the woman's complaint yesterday, a case of rape and criminal intimidation was registered against the accused, police said, adding that the woman's medical examination was conducted at the local civil hospital last evening.

The accused was today produced in a local court which remanded him to 14 days judicial custody.
