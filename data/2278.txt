Mumbra
May 27, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A man from Mumbra in the district was today arrested for allegedly raping his 12-year-old daughter.

The accused had been sexually abusing his daughter since the past four months.

The victim's mother said her husband also used to force their daughter to watch obscene videos.

"The arrested accused lived in the township with three children (two daughters and a son) and his wife," a police inspector said.
