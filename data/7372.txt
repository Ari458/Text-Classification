Maharashtra
Jul 10, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			A 30-year-old woman levelled molestation charges against Kamlesh Tripathi, his son Sahil and Ghanshyam Tiwari. Police said that the trio had gained entry in her home over an old dispute and behaved indecently with the complainant and her sister-in-law. Kalamna police have arrested Tripathi and Tiwari in the case.
