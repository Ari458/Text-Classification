Haryana
Dec 24, 2014
Abduction
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped by three youths in the district here, police said today. 

According to a complaint filed by the girl's uncle Israel, the trio kidnapped the girl yesterday at sector 25 Ballabhgarh here when she came to visit her elder sister, they said. 

The accused, identified as Samsuddin, Devendra and Aasma, are absconding, police said, adding a case has been registered against them.
