Faridabad
May 12, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Seven people have been booked for allegedly harassing and threatening to kill a woman over dowry demands. The people who were arrested on Friday from Faridabad, include the woman's husband Bhagat.

"Jyoti had registered a complaint that her in-laws were demanding Rs. 3 lakh cash and a car as dowry since her marriage in May 2009," a police spokesman said.
