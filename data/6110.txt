Rajasthan
Jan 01, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old man was arrested today for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl on the pretext of dropping her home in Harmara area, police said.

The accused, identified as Gopal Sharma, took the victim with him on a motorcycle and raped her in inebriated condition at an isolated area on December 27, they said.

The girl was found crying at the spot and was hospitalised, police said.

"The girl was on her way to home when the accused said to her that he will drop her to home so she accompanied him. He then purchased liquor and later took her to an isolated area and raped her," Jaipur Police Commissioner Srinivas Rao Janga said today.

He said that the accused was later identified and arrested today.
