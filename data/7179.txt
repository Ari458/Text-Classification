New Delhi
Jun 17, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman has alleged molestation charges on a doctor in west Delhi's Uttam Nagar area on Tuesday. 

Around 1.30pm, the woman reached the police station and reported about the matter. The woman works with an NGO and lives in a JJ Colony in west Delhi, police said. 

The woman in her complaint told police that she had a persistent backache after which she went to the doctor for consultation. However, during check-up, the doctor had touched her inappropriately. 

Police have registered an FIR under IPC sections 354 (molestation) but the doctor has not yet been arrested.
