Bihar
Mar 29, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A man allegedly raped his aunt and assaulted his uncle and cousin over a property dispute in Bihar's Nawada district.
	
Manoj Yadav, the accused, allegedly committed the crimes.
	
He was arrested from Parahia village under Nadiganj police station.
	
A pistol and 11 ammunition were seized from his possession.
	
Raids were being carried out to nab Manoj's father Rameshwar Yadav, who, too has been named as an accused in the assault case.
	
The rape victim has been taken to a government hospital for medical test.
