Maharashtra
Dec 20, 2013
Murder, Crime
Description:
			A 59-year-old woman was bludgeoned to death allegedly by her drunken nephew after she refused to give him money to buy alcohol.

The victim Rosemary Chettiar was found lying in a pool of blood by her daughter in their Neeraj Park flat.

A close examination revealed that gold ornaments worn by the woman, valued over Rs two lakh, were also missing.

Accused Mocha M Chettiar (34) had approached Rosemary for money to buy liquor. After being refused, he attacked her with a hard and blunt object due to which she died on the spot. He then fled the scene with her jewellery.
