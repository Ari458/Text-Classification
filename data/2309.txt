Mupidathi Amman temple
May 24, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A Special Sub inspector has been transferred to Palayamkottai Armed reserve police after reports that he misbehaved with women devotees at Mupidathi Amman temple festival at nearby Kadayanallur last night.

Police said the inebirated SSI had allegedly misbehaved with somewomen during the temple car festival and continued to do so, despite being warned by members of the public.

Superintendent of Police Vijayendra Bidari, acting on complaints ordered his transfer.
