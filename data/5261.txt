Maharashtra
Aug 09, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Additional sessions judge V N Tambi has sentenced a 25-year-old married man to rigorous imprisonment of ten years and fined Rs. 2,000 after convicting him for rape on a nine-year-old girl.

Kallu alias Karim Khan, a resident of Nazuk Nagar on Mohta Mill road here, allegedly raped the girl, who was his neighbour, at a brick kiln near river Morna on January 31, 2012. The complaint was filed by the survivor's aunt.
