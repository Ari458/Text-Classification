Tamil Nadu
Dec 18, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Allegedly upset after telling her mother that she was raped by a 78-year-old man, a 15-year-old girl hung herself to death, at Vellakovil. Later, Vellavkovil police arrested the septuagenarian.

According to police, Simi (name changed) (15) had been behaving abnormally for the past few days. On questioning, Simi told her mother that over a month ago a local had raped her.  When her mother went out for help, she hung herself from the ceiling and died.

Later, police arrested Gnanasigamani (78).
