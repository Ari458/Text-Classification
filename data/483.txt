Description:
			Hospital on Tuesday suspended a doctor, accused of 'molestation' by a female dentist, till an internal inquiry was completed. The police, however, claimed that that the 35-year-old woman, who had lodged the complaint, was now seeking quashing of the FIR.

Earlier, the hospital said in a statement that "post the internal fact-finding inquiry, we have suspended the doctor. As a responsible healthcare provider, we have already set into motion anti-sexual harassment proceedings in accordance with the principles of corporate governance. Max Healthcare is fully cooperating with the law enforcement authorities. We have reiterated our 'zero tolerance' towards policy violations pertaining to medical ethics amongst our physicians"
