Uttar Pradesh
Jun 24, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A man along with his brother has been arrested while dumping the dead body of his wife here, in an alleged incident of dowry death, police said on Monday.
Vijendra Singh, husband of the deceased woman and his brother Rajendra Singh, were arrested on Sunday by the Tanda city police while the duo was looking for a safe place to dump the dead body, SP Sadhna Goswami said.
The accused husband, who works as a teacher, had been demanding an Alto car and Rs 1 lakh as dowry and killed her by giving poison to her after his demands were not met, she said.  Probe is on in the matter, added Goswami.
