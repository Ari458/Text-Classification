India
May 11, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenaged girl was allegedly abducted and raped by two youths near Tanda village.

Ranjit Singh of Bhatnura, Bholath in Kapurthala district, and Buta Singh of Talwandi Sallan, allegedly abducted the 15-year-old victim on a motorcycle from near her house in Tanda village two days back and took her to an unknown location where they raped her.

After committing the crime, the accused dropped the girl near her village and threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed the matter to anyone, the victim has alleged in her complaint to the police.
