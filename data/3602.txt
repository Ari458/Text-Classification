Andhra Pradesh
Nov 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two people from Bihar were arrested by the Cyberabad police on charges of raping a 18-year-old girl at Sheriguda village on the city outskirts.

Accused Dinesh Mathod and Bade Lal Mathod, both in their twenties, working as labourers in a rice mill, allegedly gang raped the victim who worked as a labourer in the same rice mill on November 15 and fled to their native Sitamarhi district of Bihar.

Ibrahimpatnam police had booked a case and a special team was sent to Bihar to nab the accused who were arrested and brought to the city, the two confessed to gang raping the victim, during interrogation.
