Chandigarh
Aug 04, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The police on Sunday night arrested a 19-year-old Mohali resident for allegedly raping a Class VII student, who is suspected to be pregnant, in Sector 45.

The girl was sent to hospital for medical examination. The accused was identified as Ranjit Singh, a resident of Jhujhar Nagar in Mohali. "Families of both the accused and victim were known to each other and were earlier residing together. The accused raped her in the house. We received information on Sunday and arrested the accused the same day", said inspector Rajesh Shukla, SHO, Sector 34 police station.

The accused worked as a labourer. A police official said that the girl appeared to be pregnant.

A criminal case of rape under charges of Section 360 was registered at Sector 34 police station.
