Delhi
Dec 04, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			On the day of the incident, Chand (Accused) returned back from work picked up a fight and started abusing her and set her ablaze before she could realise. Accused fled from the spot after the incident.

Though, accused contended that the victim set herself on fire and he was not a part of it. the court still held him guilty because he did not try to save his own sister in law and did not do anything to extinguish the fire.

"This conduct of the accused is highly unnatural that he would see the wife of his brother burning and would not save her" Court
