Tamil Nadu
Dec 23, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The All Woman Police (East) have arrested a man and registered a case against his four brothers for alleged dowry harassment. Ajeetha (24) of Thuckalay in Kanyakumari district married P. Ranganathan (34) of Irugur in December 2014.

According to the sources, her husband and in-laws harassed her for dowry, sent her home and forced her to sign the divorce papers.
