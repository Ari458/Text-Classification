Maharashtra
Apr 09, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl, who works at a brick kiln at Talonda of Bhiwandi tehsil, was allegedly sexually assaulted by Kamlakar Jadhav, 30.

Jadhav offered toddy to the victim and then took her to a nearby farm, where he raped her last night.

The accused is absconding and an offence has been registered with Ganeshpuri police station in this connection.
