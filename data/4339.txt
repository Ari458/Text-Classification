New Delhi
Mar 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 33-year-old married woman was allegedly raped by a property dealer in the Rohini area of Outer district on the pretext of providing her with a job.

Based on the woman's complaint, a case has been registered against the accused, identified as Gulshan, at the KN Katju Marg police station.

The accused works in the Sector 16 Rohini area and also has an office there.

Gulshan had taken the victim, a resident of north Delhi, to his office on the pretext of giving her a job.

At his office, Gulshan allegedly raped her and also threatened her with dire consequences if she spoke about the matter to anyone.

It was after the woman told her husband about the incident that the two registered an FIR against the accused, a medical test on the woman has confirmed rape.

Source: Press Trust of India
