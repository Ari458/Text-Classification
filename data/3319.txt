Andhra Pradesh
Oct 12, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was allegedly raped by her relative at Akkapalli village of the district.

The incident took place at the Bugga Rajeshwara Thanda locality of the village.

The victim, who is married, lives with her parents at Akkapalli village.

A distant relative, identified as A Ramesh (35), who resides in the same village, entered her house and sexually assaulted her after gagging her.

The victim was later taken to a hospital.

A complaint was lodged against Ramesh by the victim's father.
