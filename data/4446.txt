Jaipur
Mar 23, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 50-year-old woman was allegedly raped by three men known to her.

The woman living in Bhankrota area filed an FIR yesterday, alleging that she was raped by Ratnya, Raju and Narayana, all known to her, police said.

She claimed that on March 15, the men called her to Mahapura Road and sexually exploited her one after another, they said.

The victim, who has eight children, was sent for medical examination and her statement had been recorded, they said, adding that the accused, all daily wagers, were being traced.
