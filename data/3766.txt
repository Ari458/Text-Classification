Uttar Pradesh
Dec 23, 2013
Molestation
Description:
			Two men allegedly attempted to rape a woman while she was alone at her house.

The two accused, Yogendra and Bablu entered the house of the victim in Morna village and attempted to rape her but soon ran away as the neighbours hearing the cries of the woman came for help.

The offenders, who are absconding, have been booked on the charges of attempt to rape. The investigations are going on.
