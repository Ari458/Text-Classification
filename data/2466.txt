Bihar
Jun 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested today for raping a minor girl in a locality in Town police station in Bihar's Bhojpur district.

Acting on a complaint, Md Ainal Haque was arrested for raping a three-year-old girl in Qazi Tola.

The accused took the girl to a deserted place and raped her.

The family members took the victim to a hospital earlier in the day after the local people had found her lying unconscious.
