West Bengal
Mar 12, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman of Taltala Bazar in central Kolkata has alleged that a 32-year-old man tried to rape her on a staircase of a house located on Gardener Lane.

The complaint was received around 8pm. Though the accused has been identified as Suleiman Pasha, he is yet to be arrested. Police are investigating the case.

The woman visited a flat in that building and was returning home when she was accosted on the stairs by Pasha, who allegedly tried to rape her. The woman managed to free herself and raised an alarm. The accused fled soon after.
