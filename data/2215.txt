Jaitpur
May 14, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman was found dead under suspicious circumstances in her in-laws' house in Jaitpur village of the district today.

Delhi resident Anshu was married to Virendra around six months back, police said.

The body of Anshu was found hanging from the ceiling this morning, they said, adding there was an injury mark on her hand.

Her relatives alleged that Virendra and his family murdered Anshu for dowry.
