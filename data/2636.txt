Madhya Pradesh
Mar 21, 2013
Rape
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a 60-year-old woman was allegedly raped by her sons-in-law at
a village.
	
The two accused, identified as - Santosh Basod (40) and Manjan Basod (29) - were inebriated when they sexually assaulted their mother-in-law at her house in Ghuvara village of the district.
	
Later, the woman filed a complaint with police against the two.
	
She was sent for medical examination which confirmed rape.
