Katihar
Mar 22, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Nuzhat Parween(25), a school teacher in Bihar was allegedly beaten to death by her husband and parents-in-law who attacked her with sticks for not bringing in enough dowry.

The harassment over dowry began soon after she got married two years ago. 

"Her husband was reportedly demanding more dowry and wanted to grab her monthly salary, which she refused," a police officer said.
