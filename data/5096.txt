New Delhi
Jul 11, 2014
Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old DU student has alleged that her boyfriend had raped her on the pretext of marriage. The victim is a first year BCom student at a south Delhi college. The girl alleged that she was raped in his house a few weeks ago. She became pregnant but her friend refused to marry her. The girl then went to LBS Hospital for an abortion on Wednesday. Doctors then informed police and her parents following which her statement was recorded. The friend has been arrested.
