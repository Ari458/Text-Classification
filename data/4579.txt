Uttar Pradesh
Apr 17, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A woman was killed and her husband seriously injured allegedly in an attack with an iron rod.

Usha(60) was found dead with head injuries and her husband, Jai Bhagwan Bansal (63) seriously injured at their residence in Patel Nagar.

An iron rod was recovered from the site, police said adding, it is suspected to be a case of murder.

Bansal was rushed to a hospital where his condition was stated to be critical.

The reason behind the attack is yet to be ascertained and investigations are on.
