Assam
Nov 13, 2011
Crime, Other
Description:
			A woman was killed by locals in the name of witch-hunting in the Gelapukhuri area of Sonitpur district on Saturday.

Though police did not confirm if the murder was related to 'witch hunting', arrests have been made.

Earlier an elderly married couple, 65-year-old Jogen Boro and wife Akhri, was killed by locals at Hadunguri village in the Majbat area in Assam's Udalguri district suspecting that the couple was spreading diseases in the hamlet
