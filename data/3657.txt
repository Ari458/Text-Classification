Rajasthan
Dec 07, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her maternal uncle in Dadabari police station area of the city.

The accused, Taj Singh alias Tinku (23,) was arrested from his native village in the Jhalawar district and would be produced in the court.

The victim's mother filed a complaint against Tinku, stating the accused would visit her home frequently and on the night of December 4, he had stayed at her home.

The incident could be exposed only when the victim complained of aching in her private parts, according to the complaint filed by the victim's mother.
