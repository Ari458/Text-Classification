Tamil Nadu
May 13, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Mahila Court judge N. Vasanthaleela sentenced M. Ganesan (37), a farmer, to three years rigorous imprisonment for harassing wife Uma Maheswari seeking dowry. She had committed suicide at Dhali on January 19, 2010.

V. Parimala, the Special Public Prosecutor, told reporters that a fine of Rs. 50,000 was also imposed on Ganesan. The amount would go as compensation to his four-year-old daughter who is now staying with the father of Uma Maheswari.
