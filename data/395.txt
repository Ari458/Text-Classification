Bhatta
Oct 06, 2011
Rape
Description:
			Seven more women of Noida's Bhatta village claim rape by PAC men Farmers protest at Bhatta Parsaul. Counsel for the victims, V. P. Sharma, said: "A woman from Bhatta had submitted before the court that she was assaulted by the PAC men posted at her house following violence in the village in May. Encouraged by the court's intervention in her case, seven more women will move court soon." 

On September 20, a Noida court had ordered the police to lodge a case against 16 PAC personnel, but the police have not still filed any FIR. The court on Wednesday ordered the police to explain why its orders had not been complied with.
