Jaipur
Jul 31, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly raped by a man, who also recorded the act on his mobile phone and had been blackmailing her since then in Gandhi Nagar area of the district.

"The accused, Dilip Jaiman, called the woman at a place two months ago on the pretext of giving her a job and raped her.

"He also made a clipping and later showed it to her and raped her again," a police officer said.
