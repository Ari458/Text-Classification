Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The accused Abhay Thakkur (32) owns Thakkurji Construction Company. The victim on Friday cleared her interview for an 8-hour receptionist job at a monthly salary of Rs5,000. On Monday at around 1.30pm, the victim was called by the suspect inside his cabin to give her dictation. But the suspect came near her chair and started talking and doing objectionable things that made the victim to run out of the office and approach the police station.
