Jharkhand
Jan 07, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A woman, whose children accused some people of calling her a witch, has been killed by unknown people in Gumla district.

The body, bearing stab signs, of 45-year-old Bitni Devi at Birkera-tongritoli village under Raidih police station was recovered. It bore stabbings.

In their complaint to the police, the women's sons, Arjun and Bhairon, alleged that their mother was a victim of superstition as some called her a witch in the village.
