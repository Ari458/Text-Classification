Madhepura
May 08, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Man sentenced to life imprisonment for strangulating a minor girl to death and attempt to rape her mother in Madhepura district last month.

The Ad hoc court -IV Additional district judge Yogesh Narayan Singh passed the order after convicting Ajay Chauhan as guilty for an attempt to rape a woman and strangulating her one-year old daughter to death in a village of Madhepura district on April six, 2013.

According to prosecution, the convict and his associate Mukesh Chauhan had barged into the house of the 22 year-old woman in a drunken state. The associate fled away even as Chauhan tried to rape the woman and strangulated her daughter to death.
