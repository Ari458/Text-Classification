Delhi
Jul 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour for over a year in R K Puram area in the city.

The incident came to light last night when accused, identified as Tasleem (40), tried to molest the victim, but she somehow managed to escape and informed her parents who later registered complaint against him.

According to police, girl alleged in her complaint that she was allegedly raped by Tasleem on different occasions whenever he found her alone.

"After finding victim alone, he would take her to his room and commit crime. Tasleem also threatened her of dire consequence if she revealed her ordeal to anyone," said a senior police officer.

Officer also said that medical examination has confirmed rape, adding that further investigation is on.

Source: Press Trust of India
