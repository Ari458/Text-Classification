Samaipur Badli
Jun 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was raped allegedly by her neighbour at an isolated place near her house, police said today.

The incident took place in Samaipur Badli yesterday when the girl was playing outside her house.

Malkhan Singh (25), her neighbour, lured the child to an isolated place near the house and allegedly raped her, police said.

Source: Press Trust of India
