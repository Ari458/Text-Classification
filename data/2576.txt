Uttar Pradesh
Jul 04, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped in Babu Ka Pura village.

On the complaint by the victim's family, an FIR has been registered against a man who also belongs to the same village and is absconding.

The girl has been sent to district hospital for medical examination, police said, adding hunt has been launched to nab the accused.
