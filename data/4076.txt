Madhya Pradesh
Feb 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old youth was arrested here today on charges of allegedly raping a two-years-old girl in Sama area here on Monday night.

Tarsinh Ninama, a resident of Jhabua in Madhya Pradesh, allegedly lured the two-year-old girl with a chocolate, took her away to a nearby field and raped her, after which he returned her to her parents.

The victim sustained severe bruises and her private parts were bleeding, and has been admitted to hospital, police said, adding that the FSL team also collected evidence from the spot.

Ninama, a casual labourer, is a relative of the girl's parents, who have been working as casual labourers in Vadodara.
