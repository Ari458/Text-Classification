Kolkata
Feb 13, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 17-year-old youth was arrested for sexually assaulting a four-year-old girl in the city's Behala.

The crime took place inside the local office of a political party, the police said.

The youth was arrested on the basis of a police complaint made by the parents of the girl.

The police has a launched an investigation into the incident.
