Haryana
Feb 16, 2015
Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth on the pretext of getting her a job in Ballabhgarh area in the district, police said today.

The victim, a resident of Noida, in her complaint said that she knew the accused, identified as Gopal, who had called her yesterday to Ballabgarh, saying he would get her a job in a good company, a police spokesperson said.

When the girl reached there, the accused came on a motorcycle and took her away to a nearby house where he allegedly raped her, police said.

A case has been registered against the accused, who is absconding, police said, adding, further investigation is on to nab him.
