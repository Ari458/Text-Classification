Pune
Mar 20, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old labourer was arrested for allegedly raping his neighbour's three-year-old daughter. The incident took place at the victim's room in Shivaji Housing society on Senapati Bapat Road. 

The incident took place around 8.30 pm on Sunday, when the victim's father was outside his house. The suspect, Ranjeet Vashishta Ram, was known to the girl. "On Sunday night, the victim's mother asked the suspect to look after her, as she wanted to go out for some work. Ram took the victim inside the house and allegedly raped her. The incident came to light when the victim's father heard her scream," Sakhare said.
