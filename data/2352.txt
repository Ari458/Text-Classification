Karimnagar
Jun 06, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A dental surgeon's assistant has been arrested for molesting a girl at Sircilla.

Circle Inspector S Chary said the accused Y Suresh (30) told the victim girl, when she came to the hospital, that he himself was the doctor, and sent her aunt away to bring ice-cream.

When she was alone with him, he allegedly attempted to rape her, Chary said. When she raised alarm, people came to her rescue and Suresh was arrested.
