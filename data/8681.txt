Andhra Pradesh
Aug 28, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Parents of a 9th class girl studying at a residential school in Atmakur filed a complaint with the local police seeking action against the principal concerned for allegedly sexually harassing their daughter.

Following the incident, Andhra Pradesh Rajaka Vruthidarula Sangham president M. Bhaskaraiah said the police had registered a case based on the complaint and justice should be done to the girl's parents by carrying out the investigation in a transparent manner.

The parents along with their relatives visited the CPI(M) office where they sought stringent punishment to the principal.
