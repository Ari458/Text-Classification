Chandigarh
Aug 04, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A tempo driver has been booked for raping his 12-year-old niece in Kharar on Sunday morning.

Police said that the minor was studying with her friend when the accused, who is married, approached her. He allegedly lured the girls to accompany him on a drive. After dropping the friend at her house, he took his niece to the highway, drugged her and raped her at an isolated place. Later, he dropped her home and fled.

A police officer said that medical report of the survivor has confirmed rape.

The accused has been booked under sections 363, 366, 376 and 586 of IPC and under relevant sections of POCSO act at Kharar police station. He is still at large.
