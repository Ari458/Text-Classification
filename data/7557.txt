Karnataka
Jul 29, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old man from a village near Bantwal has been accused of sexually assaulting a 15-year-old girl from the same village, who delivered a child in a government hospital. According to Superintendent of Police Sharanappa S.D., family members of the girl filed a complaint after she gave birth to the child in a government hospital on Tuesday morning.

Mr. Sharanappa said the police have registered a case of sexual assault under Section 376 of Indian Penal Code and also under provisions of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act. He said they are questioning the accused person.
