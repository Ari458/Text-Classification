Maharashtra
Jun 30, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A new rape case has shocked most of India, making it to the newspapers across major titles of the nation.

As the nation struggles to contain sexual attacks against women, a report has emerged that a father has been arrested for raping his daughter.

The reports go on to state that the crime only emerged after the girl got pregnant and delivered a child.

The father, who has been arrested has been accused of raping the girl since she was an 8-year-old.

When he discovered the girl, now 17, was pregnant he first forced her to blame the conception on her boyfriend.

However, the girl narrated her ordeal to the counselors of a women's welfare organisation.
