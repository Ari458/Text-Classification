Kanpur
Oct 24, 2011
Molestation
Description:
			On April 24, 2011, while the victim was alone at the house, the accused and his three aides forced their entry into the house and molested her. Sarnam, thereafter, threatened her with dire consequences. However, she informed her parents who approached the Chakeri police, who have registered a molestation case against a man, identified as Sarnam, and three others on Saturday. 

The accused had allegedly raped Rekha (name changed) of Lal Bangla locality on April 24, 2011. Police are conducting raids to nab the accused. The victim, had on several occasions informed her family members regarding the harassment that she was continuously facing at the hands of Sarnam and his aides.
