Uttar Pradesh
Mar 25, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A police constable has been arrested for eve teasing a minor Dalit girl.
	
The accused cop, Sarfaraz has been arrested for  eve teasing a Dalit girl student of class X in Miranpur town of the district.
	
He has been remanded to 14 days judicial custody by a local court in connection with the case.
	
Locals handed over the accused to police after beating him up.
