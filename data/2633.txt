Assam
Mar 21, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was hacked to death and her grandmother critically injured by
unidentified persons at Aahatguri in Bongaigaon district of Assam.
      
The assailants broke into the house of an elderly woman Mohila Barman and injured her critically before hacking to death her granddaughter Kiran with sharp weapons.
      
The injured woman was admitted to hospital.
