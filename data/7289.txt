Tamil Nadu
Jul 01, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The All Women Police here have arrested a Grade I constable, attached to the Armed Reserve (AR) police, on a charge of harassing his wife, demanding dowry. The police said that the constable, K. Ramachandran (40), was arrested under Sections 498(a) and 323 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC), and released on bail.

Based on the complaint lodged by Mageswari, Inspector of Police Josephin Amalarani arrested him.
