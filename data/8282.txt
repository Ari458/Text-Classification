Haryana
Jan 14, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a car driver in Badkhal, police said.

The incident was reported on Tuesday when the victim and her grandmother were waiting at Sohanna bus depot to go to Badkhal. The duo had to board the car of the accused after the elderly woman's health started to deteriorate, police said.

The driver, Shaheed, a resident of Mewat, drugged the elderly woman in the car, drove around for a while, before dropping her back at Badkhal depot again.

He then took the girl to a secluded place where he raped her, beat her up and later, threw her off the car, they said.

A case has been registered. The accused is absconding, police said.
