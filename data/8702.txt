Madhya Pradesh
Oct 12, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at her home in Gujarkheda area on Tuesday. He had been harassing the girl for the past few months.The girl reached Central Kotwali police station along with her parents to register a case against the accused.

The girl said that the youth had been harassing her for the past few months following which she had attempted suicide at her home by ingesting acid a month ago. She, however, recuperated at the hospital and came back home. She had stopped venturing outside her home because of the accused and would remain at home all the time. She had not informed her parents about the same.

On Tuesday, the girl was alone at her home when she was raped by the accused. Later her parents got the complaint registered.
