Poonch
May 09, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A police constable allegedly raped his step-daughter in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir. Constable Mohammad Tahir allegedly raped his 13-year-old step-daughter at his residential quarters in District Police Lines in Poonch when his wife had gone to Rajouri district for some work on Sunday night, a police officer said.

The girl narrated the entire happening to her mother, who returned in the morning and a complaint was lodged against the constable in Poonch, he said. Special Police Officer (SPO) Sunita Devi had married Tahir last year and was living with him along with her 13-year-old daughter and 8-year-old son.
