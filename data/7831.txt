New Delhi
Sep 12, 2015
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 28-year-old tantrik was arrested after a woman accused him of repeatedly raping her for over a year. Around two years ago, she lost her daughter and soon after fell ill. Her husband took her to a tantric, who asked her to visit him for five consecutive Saturdays, said a senior police officer.

One of the Saturdays, the tantrik offered to visit her home. Finding her alone, he allegedly raped her. The woman finally revealed about the abuse to her husband who took her to Narela police station to file a complaint.
