Bihar
Dec 16, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A woman was strangulated to death by unidentified people in a village in Bihar's Bhojpur district.

The perpetrators barged into the house of the Mukhiya of Belaur Panchayat, Champa Devi and strangulated her to death.

The battle for supremacy between rival groups over getting a pie in panchayat works could be the motive behind the murder of 40 year-old mukhiya.

An FIR has been lodged against two villagers Hemant Chaudhary and Ranjit Chaudhary, besides eight unidentified people in connection with the murder case, all of the accused people were absconding.

Angry with the murder of the woman Mukhiya, local residents blocked the Ara-Sahar road for hours before senior administrative and police officials rushed to the spot and pacified the protesters assuring actions against perpetrators.

The body has been sent for post-mortem.
