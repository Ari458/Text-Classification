Kiphire
Jun 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour on the pretext of giving her a lift in his vehicle in Kiphire district of Nagaland today.

The 25-year-old accused, identified as Taliba Sangtam of Kiphire village, a driver by profession, has been arrested.

Sangtam offered a ride to the girl, who was returning from school after dropping her little brother, on the pretext of taking her home.

He then allegedly took her to the outskirts of Kiphire town and brutally raped her twice.
