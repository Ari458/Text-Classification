Andhra Pradesh
Jul 09, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Police on Wednesday booked a case under the provisions of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act against a private school principal for sexually assaulting a 14-year-old girl at Darsi in Prakasam district. 

Police took the step after the girl was examined by doctors at the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Medical Sciences here, Darsi Deputy Superintendent of Police R.V. Ram Babu said. We will proceed further against the principal after a thorough probe, he added. 

The incident came to light after the girl was found to be pregnant by her parents, the DSP said.
