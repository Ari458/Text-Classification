Uttar Pradesh
Jun 13, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A mother of two children was allegedly gang-raped by three men in Dadri area here, police said on Friday.

The incident took place on June 9 and a case of rape has been lodged against the three accused at Dadri police station today, police said.

The woman, a resident of Baghpat, alleged that on June 9 she had come to Dadri and Deepak along with Vikas and Bittoo asked her to join them in their car on the pretext of shopping, following which they took her to a house in Greater Noida where she was raped by the three accused.

They threatened to kill her if she reported the matter to anyone, police said, adding, they later left her in Dadri area.

Efforts are on to nab the three who are absconding.
