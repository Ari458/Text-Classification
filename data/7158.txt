Karnataka
Jun 13, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The Principal District and Sessions Judge N. Sharanappa on Thursday convicted and sentenced three persons including the husband and in-laws of a woman who committed suicide by jumping into a canal due to dowry harassment in May, 2011.

Suvarna's husband Shivasharanappa Angadi was sentenced to five years of imprisonment and in-laws Sadashiva Angadi and Bhagarathi Angadi to three years of imprisonment each.
