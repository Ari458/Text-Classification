Punjab
Aug 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A class 10th student was raped allegedly by her tuition teacher, following which he has been arrested today.

According to a complaint lodged by the girl, the accused, identified as Pankaj Arora, was a private tuition teacher, Parmjit Singh Pannu, Additional DCP, Ludhiana said, adding, he coaxed the girl into seeking coaching from him on the pretext of getting good marks in the examination.

Arora later raped her and also prepared an objectionable video film, following which he started blackmailing her, Pannu said.

He also hurled sexual abuses at her, he said.
