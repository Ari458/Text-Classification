Madhya Pradesh
May 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was today arrested from Jaitapur area of the city for allegedly raping his 21-year-old daughter, police said.

The accused, identified as Omkarlal, had been sexually assaulting his daughter since last eight years by forcing her to consume sedatives, they said.

He also used to give sleeping pills to his late wife.

According to the police, the victim lodged a complaint yesterday on the advice of her relatives following her mother's death recently.

Though the victim had earlier told her mother about the incident, police said, she had advised her not to reveal it to anybody saying it would bring ignominy.

The victim finally spoke to her maternal grandmother and aunt.
