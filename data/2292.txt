Bangalore
May 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A local court has sentenced a man to seven years rigorous imprisonment and imposed a fine of Rs 10,000 after convicting of kidnapping and raping a minor girl two years ago.

On May 22, the 11th Fast Track Court (FTC) convicted Arun Kumar for kidnapping and raping a 15 year-old girl on Aug 6, 2011.
