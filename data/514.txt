Thane
Oct 24, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			An NRI man, along with his parents and brother, has been arrested for allegedly harassing his wife, police said today. 

According P P Gugle of the Naupada police station, Sudipto's wife Samboti had moved to the United States after their marriage. However, soon, her in-laws started demanding more dowry. Later, when the couple came to India on a vacation, the in-laws started harassing her. She was allegedly abused and tortured physically by her in-laws. According to police, her in laws even snatched her ornaments and passport and drove her out of their house along with her 5-year old daughter.
