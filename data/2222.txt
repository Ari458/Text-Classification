Kaila Bhatta
May 15, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A woman was allegedly gangraped by two persons, who made a video of the crime after giving her sedatives in Kaila Bhatta area here, police said today.

The woman, a resident of Meerut, for the past three years was living with a person named Naushad, who had promised to marry her, SP Crime Ram Abhilash Tripathi said.

The woman in her complaint has alleged that Naushad and his friend Omprakash raped her after giving her sedatives.

They also took lewd pictures of her and made a MMS of the crime, police said.

The woman, who is a divorcee and has a four-year-old child, has alleged that the accused threatened her of dire consequences if she made a police complaint.
