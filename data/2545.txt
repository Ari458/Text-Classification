Siddharthnagar
Jul 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An eight-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her maternal uncle.

The crime took place on Saturday night when the minor was lured to an isolated place in Siddharthnagar slum area by accused Ravindra Manohar Sonwane, on the pretext of giving her some snack.

The girl was allegedly beaten and then sexually assaulted, a complaint was lodged against the 35-year-old at Tophakhana police station by her mother.

The girl has been admitted to a government hospital and her condition is stable, Civil Surgeon Dr Ravindra Niturkar said.

Source: Press Trust Of India
