Delhi
Jun 18, 2014
Murder
Description:
			An elderly woman was found dead and her residence ransacked in West Patel Nagar area of Central Delhi this evening.

66-year-old Savitri Devi was allegedly murdered when she was alone at home, they said, adding, robbery could be the motive behind the crime.

The incident came to light around 8 PM when Devi found dead by her daughter-in-law upon her return from the market.

It seems that the woman was killed around 7 PM when she was alone at home. The entry of the accused into the house did not seem forced, a police official said.

Police is questioning family members and neighbours and examining CCTV footage of the area.
