Uttarakhand
Apr 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by her school teacher in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand.

The teacher has been arrested and sent to judicial custody for 14 days following a complaint by the victim's guardians.

The accused identified as Anusuya Prasad Tiwari sexually exploited the class four student over a period of time.
