Haryana
Dec 17, 2014
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman from the North-East was allegedly molested by a fashion boutique staffer in a shopping centre on Sohna Road, police said today.

The incident took place last evening when the woman, hailing from Manipur, had gone to the fashion boutique on Sohna Road to pick up her clothes.

In her complaint, she alleged that the staff at the boutique molested her and threatened her with dire consequences.

A case has been registered at Sadar police station under sections 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC, the spokesman said, adding police are probing into the case.
