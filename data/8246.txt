Tamil Nadu
Dec 30, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 20-year-old youth who was on bail was arrested by the police on charges of harassing a school girl here on Tuesday.

K. Jeyakumar of Bodamalai was arrested in June on charges of sexually assaulting a Standard IX student and was lodged at Borstal in Paramathi Velur. He was released on bail a month ago. But after his release, he continued to follow the girl and harassed her. The girl informed her father who in turn lodged a complaint with the Namagiripettai police.
