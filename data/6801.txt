Akola
Apr 29, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Mangesh Palaspagar (25) has been convicted and sentenced to 10 year rigorous imprisonment for raping a minor girl in a village near Dahihanda police station in Akola district on March 5, 2014. The verdict was delivered by the sessions court at Akot tehsil town in Akola district. Handing down the sentence on Monday, additional sessions judge SW Chavan also sentenced the accused to three year RI for criminal intimidation and threatening. The judge imposed a fine of Rs 11,000 on two counts and directed Rs 7,000 be paid to the father of the rape survivor if the convicted accused paid the fine amount. Assistant government pleader Jaikrishna Gawande led the evidence in court for the state.
