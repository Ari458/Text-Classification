Kanakakunnu
Jan 02, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Kayamkulam deputy superintendent of police S Devamanohar said Chandramohan of Muthukulam North, Haripad, had been arrested for raping the 14-year-old daughter of his domestic help, who is his neighbour. The victim is a school dropout. Chandramohan is a clerk with a private gas agency at Cheppad.
