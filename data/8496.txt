Abuse
Description:
			Two men booked for assaulting women
Yashodhara police booked two men in separate incidents for allegedly assaulting women.

In first incident, 49-year-old Shekhar Tarnekar was booked for allegedly hitting his wife, Priti, 40 with a grinding stone at their Shahu Mohalla residence on Sunday early morning.

In another incident, 25-year-old Mohammad Afzal, a resident of Hameed Nagar was booked for allegedly assaulting Shabana Khan, 32.

Khan told police that she had given Rs25,000 to Afzal a month back. Khan had gone to take her money back when Afzal abused her and hit her with bricks.
