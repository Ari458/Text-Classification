Arwal
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a man at a village in Bihar's Arwal district, a police officer said today.

The incident took place at Sachai Mathia village last night as the man identified as Sudhir Kumar allegedly raped the 14-year-old girl after finding the girl alone in her house, Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) Mamata Kalyani said.

The neighbours informed the police about the incident following which the girl was taken to a government hospital for medical check up, she said.
