Punjab
Nov 19, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A local court sentenced a man to life for the murder of a 65-year-old woman.

The District and Sessions judge also imposed a fine of Rs 15,000 on Satwinder Singh.

Karamjit Kaur, wife of Gurmail Singh, a retired revenue official living in Dadheri village near Mandi Gobindgarh, was murdered in November 2012.

Police arrested Satwinder, a neighbour of the victim, in connection with the murder.
