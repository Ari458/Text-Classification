MP
May 19, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A software engineer here was arrested today for allegedly uploading an obscene video of a nursing student on the Internet.

Kapil Gadhwal (24), a resident of Mandsaur, is employed at a call center in the city, police said.

Gadhwal befriended the 21-year-old nursing student who studied at a private college, and about ten days ago he allegedly shot an obscene video of her which he uploaded on the Internet and began to blackmail her.
