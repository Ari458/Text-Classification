Punjab
Dec 31, 2013
Abduction
Description:
			A 20-year-old girl student was kidnapped by some people while she was returning home with her mother from Dhudike village.

The girl and her mother were returning home after buying medicines from a hospital in Dhudike to their native village Madoke when the incident happened.

Two accused identified as Amarjit and Harpreet Singh arrived there in a car and kidnapped the girl after assaulting the mother. Police investigations revealed that the accused and victim's family knew each other.
