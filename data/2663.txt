Odisha
Mar 29, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her uncle near Paradip in the the state.
	
The incident took place at Taradapada village in Jagatsinghpur Police Station. The accused Sibacharan Das was arrested and forwarded to judicial custody.
	
The minor girl was lured to a secluded place and raped there by her uncle during Holi.
