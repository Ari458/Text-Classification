Andhra Pradesh
Jan 12, 2014
Crime
Description:
			A Telugu movie producer has been booked by the police for allegedly cheating a 24-year-old woman after allegedly promising to marry her.

According to police, Kolagatla Pratap, hailing from Vizianagaram district, had introduced himself to a young woman from Karimnagar town over mobile phone, about two years ago.

The woman complained to the Karimnagar II Town police station, accusing the producer of being in regular touch with her over this period and promising to marry her.

However, he (producer) suddenly stopped meeting her and answering her phone calls, she alleged in the complaint.
