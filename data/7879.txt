Delhi
Sep 18, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			Five youths have been put on trial by a city court for allegedly gang-raping and assaulting a 26-year-old woman after pulling her out of an autorickshaw in south Delhi's Defence Colony area. The court paved the way for trial of the accused-Balwinder, Deepak, Aakash, Aman and Sundar-after framing charges under Sections 376 (D) (gang rape), 365 (kidnapping), 366 (kidnapping a woman for marriage), 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) and 506/34 (criminal intimidation with common intention) of the IPC against them.

"Prima facie case under Section 376 (D) (gang rape) of the IPC is made out against all of them except accused Rohit," additional sessions judge Sanjiv Jain said, while noting that Rohit got down from the car after abducting the woman. The accused pleaded not guilty and claimed trial. The court has now fixed November 3 for start of the trial.
