Uttar Pradesh
Jul 13, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and gang raped by two youths in Chanda area.

The incident took place last night when the girl had ventured out of home for some work when the two accused-- Rinku Pal and Pankaj Kumar abducted her and gang raped her in a nearby field, Station House Officer Jitendra Giri said.

The girl was spotted in the field by a passerby this morning after which her family members informed the police about the incident.

Source: Press Trust of India
