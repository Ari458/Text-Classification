Dibrugarh
Jun 23, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A man was arrested for allegedly killing his wife in Assam's Dibrugarh district.

The man, Taranga Narayan Duwarah, brought his wife to a local hospital this morning but when the doctors examined they found that she had died earlier.

The woman's family members filed a case against the accused in Dibrugarh police station alleging that he had killed her following which the police arrested him.

Dibrugarh Additional Superintendent of Police Pranjit Bora said there was circumstantial evidence against the accused.
