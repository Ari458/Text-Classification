Jammu
Jul 27, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two people were arrested in connection with the alleged gang-rape of a minor girl in Kishtwar district of Jammu and Kashmir.

The incident took place on the intervening night of July 24 and 25 when the 15-year-old girl was abducted and gang-raped by four youths in Batkot village of the district.

Later, after the girl returned home and informed her family members about her ordeal, they lodged a complaint against the accused.
