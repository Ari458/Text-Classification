Madhya Pradesh
Jan 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Premlal Wanjari (55), who works at a hotel, was arrested on Tuesday for allegedly sexually abusing his neighbour's a five-year-old daughter at Ramai Nagar. The incident came to light after the girl informed her mother.

Sonu (name changed) was alone at home with her four-year-old sister while their parents had gone to work. Police said Wanjari had gone to the girl's home on pretext of some work. He started making undue sexual advances towards Sonu who raised an alarm forcing Wanjari to leave the home in a huff. Later, Sonu told the matter to her mother who decided to approach the cops with a complaint.
