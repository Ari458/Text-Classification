Bihar
Mar 04, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A man was sentenced to life imprisonment for the rape and murder of a girl two years ago by a court in Bihar's Kishanganj district. 

District and sessions judge Rajeshwar Tiwari passed the order convicting Md. Farooque alias Vinod Yadav after finding him guilty of the rape and murder of a 12 year-old girl. 

According to prosecution, Farooque had raped the girl and later murdered her in Dangi basti locality under Town police station area on September 21, 2011.
