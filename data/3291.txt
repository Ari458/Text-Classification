Odisha
Oct 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor school girl was allegedly gang raped by two drunken youths in Odisha's Kendrapara district.

The incident took place when the 14-year-old girl had gone to fetch water from a tubewell at Naraharipur village.

The police have arrested both the accused after the father of the victim girl registered an FIR alleging that his minor daughter was raped by them.

The accused were identified as Nirmal Pradhan (26) and Abhimanyu Swain (25). They committed the crime in an inebriated state.

The duo raped the ninth standard school girl at a deserted spot of the village.

Both, the arrested duo and the victim girl, were medically examined.
