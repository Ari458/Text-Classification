Vadakara
Jun 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A criminal was today arrested for allegedly trying to molest a girl travelling by the Mangalore-Nagercoil Parasuram Express Train at Vadakara station in the district today.

The girl was going to Thiruvananthapuram for an examination when the incident took place.

Fellow passengers caught the criminal Biju and handed him over to the railway protection force personnel.

The accused in an earlier case, Biju has been evading police.
