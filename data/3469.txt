Bihar
Nov 05, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two sisters were allegedly raped in a village in Bihar's Banka district.

The sisters, aged 15 and 17 respectively, had gone to attend nature's call in a field in Asado village where they were allegedly raped by two men.

After the girls did not return home, the kins launched a search for them this morning and found two of them lying unconscious in the field.

After regaining consciousness, they narrated the brutal incident following which the villagers launched a search for the perpetrators and nabbed one of them, identified as Manzoor Ansari and mercilessly beat him up before handing him over to the police.

The other accused, Kalam Ansari managed to escape, the girls have been admitted to a government hospital for medical examination.
