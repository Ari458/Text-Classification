Maharashtra
Nov 28, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 27-year-old housewife lodged a complaint with the Mumbra police against her in-laws for demanding dowry. Nilofer Merchant, a resident of Mumbra got married to Moin in 2006 and was residing in Dabholkar road Mumbra. The victim alleged that her in-laws, husband and his second wife often pressurize her for dowry. The victim had already given nearly 23 tola gold during marriage and other items worth more than a lakh.
