Maharashtra
Jun 28, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old has been sent behind the bars after he allegedly kidnapped a 17-year-old and took her to Valdhunis area in Kalyan and raped her. A complaint has been lodged with the Tilaknagar police station in Dombivali. The accused has been produced in court and is in police custody till July 1. Further investigation is on.

The accused is identified as Dinesh Sharma, works on temporary basis and is booked under section 363 (Punishment for kidnapping), 366 (Kidnapping, abducting or inducing woman to compel her marriage, etc), 376 (Punishment for rape) and several sections of The Prevention Of Children from Sexual Offences act 2012 (POCSO).

It was day time on June 25 when the accused lure the girl that he will marry her and she has to come with him to a place where they will get married as she is already pregnant so he wants to clarify certain things. The accused took her to a lodge and raped her. The girls parents were aware about the boy, so they rushed to his place but he was not there. Later to which the girl's family lodged a complaint with the Tilaknagar police station.

The accused was arrested over the tip-off from one of the police source and he was brought to Dombivali. He will be send for a medical test.

B Dhekle Investigation police officer from Tilaknagar police station says, "We have arrested the accused and he works on temporary basis. The girl is minor and in her statement it is mentioned that she is five months pregnant to which we will take her to the hospital. Further probe is on"
