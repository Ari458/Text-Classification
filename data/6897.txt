Andhra Pradesh
May 15, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			Ponnur police on Thursday arrested three persons on charges of attempting to molest an unidentified woman near the Government Hospital in the late hours of Wednesday. Ponnur Station House Officer Billauddin said cases under Section 376 of the IPC, read with Section 5/11, had been registered against the accused, K. Manikya Rao (25), Yesu Das (23) and P. Srinivas (20). The woman, in her mid-20s, had been found moving near the railway station for the last two days, police said. Police, however, said there had not been conclusive evidence pointing to the molestation of the woman, who was also found mentally unstable and not able to speak Telugu. Police also tried to extract a statement from her with the help of some persons, but the woman did not speak to them.
