Hyderabad
May 14, 2014
Murder
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl was found murdered at her house in New Shahapurnagar area on the city outskirts.

"The girl's father G Rao found her at around 5 am. She was strangulated by unidentified persons," Jeedimetla Police Inspector M Sudarshan said.

The parents of the victim were away when the incident occurred.
