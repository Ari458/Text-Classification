Maharashtra
Jan 24, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 40-year-old man from Jedule Colony Pada in Dahanu here has been arrested for allegedly bludgeoning his mother-in-law to death, police said today.

Ravindra Thackeray was arrested last night after he attacked his mother-in-law Jaidu Babu Pardhi (55) with a huge stone in a fit of rage under the influence of liquor, Police Inspector AP Fegde of Wangaon police station said.

The drunk accused quarreled with the woman and hit her with stones, leaving her dead on the spot.
