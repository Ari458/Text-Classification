Haryana
Jan 03, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Berli Kalan village here following which he was arrested, police said today.

The accused identified as Sonu Yadav, was booked for allegedly raping a girl in a hotel on December 8, they said.

The victim in her complaint filed with the police on December 28 had alleged that Sonu, who was known to her, gave her a lift on his bike when she was coming from Rewari railway station.

Instead of taking her to college, he took her to a local hotel and allegedly raped her, police said.

Sonu was today produced in a local court which remanded him in police custody for two days, they said.
