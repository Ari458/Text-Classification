Haryana
Dec 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year old girl was allegedly raped by her brother several times for the last seven years in Sant Nagar area of the district, police said today.

The victim in a police complaint said she had been living with her brother since her mother passed away seven years ago.

She alleged that her brother harassed and raped her several times during this period and threatened her with dire consequences if she told about it to anyone.
