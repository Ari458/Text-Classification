Andhra Pradesh
Nov 01, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 23-year-old tribal woman was allegedly raped by two unidentified men under Kancharapalem police station limits in the city.

According to police, the woman and her husband came to the city in search of livelihood around a year back.

They live at an old building on railway land. The couple works at a construction site in the city.

Two unidentified men entered the couple's room, thrashed the husband and tied him with a rope, and raped the woman.
