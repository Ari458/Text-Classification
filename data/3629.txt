Odisha
Dec 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old dalit woman, mother of two children, was raped at Patalipanka village in Marshaghai police station of Odisha's Kendrapara district.

The accused who has been arrested committed the sexual assault by trespassing into the house and tying the victim.

The offence was committed in the absence of male members of the family.

Jayaram Mallick, the accused has confessed to the crime.

Both the victim and the accused were medically examined.

The accused would be forwarded to the court.
