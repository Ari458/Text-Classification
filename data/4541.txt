Karnataka
Apr 09, 2014
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			Before she could celebrate her first marriage anniversary, she fell victim to alleged dowry harassment which forced her to commit suicide.

Savita Raju, 25, who was married to a private company employee NS Raju on April 21, 2013, set herself ablaze in her Tunganagar residence in Byadarahalli, Tavarekere, Ramanagaram Rural district. Byadarahalli police arrested Raju, a non-technical staff with a software company and resident of Sadashivnagar for dowry harassment.

Raju had asked Savita not to return home till she brought the dowry he demanded. She was living with her aunt in Tunganagar and took the extreme step after locking herself in the bathroom.
