Punjab
Oct 12, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A local court on Tuesday sentenced a man to 12-year jail term for attempting to rape a minor.

Mohammad Niyamuddin, a resident of Dhakauli (originally hailing from Uttar Pradesh) was also fined Rs 10,000. The accused was arrested by Zirakpur police on the complaint of the mother of the victim. She alleged the accused had attempted to rape the child after taking her in his house. 

The police had registered a case under relevant sections of the IPC and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences in March this year.
