Rajasthan
Dec 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Alleging that she had been raped by president of the students' union of her college, a girl set herself on fire and is in a critical condition.

The girl, an undergraduate student at a college in Bijolia town, poured kerosene and set herself on fire.

She was taken to a local hospital from where she was referred to Kota government hospital, her condition is critical.

The girl has alleged that she was raped by the president of the students union Ajit Singh on the pretext of marriage.

Meanwhile, several students protested in Mandal town and in colleges in Bhilwara, demanding arrest of the accused.
