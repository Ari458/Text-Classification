Rape
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her teenage brother and brother-in-law on separate occasions in North Delhi's Burari area.

The victim was raped by her 25-year-old brother-in-law. The agony of the girl continued after she met the same fate at the hands of her 14-year-old brother.
