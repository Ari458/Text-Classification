Puducherry
Oct 13, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police booked a youth on charges of raping a 13-year-old girl in Puducherry. 

Police said the girl was alone at home in Veerampattinam after her parents went for work. Sankar, 21, living in the same locality approached her and requested her to help him in sweeping his house. The girl reached his house on his request and the youth raped her. 

The girl narrated the incident to her parents when they returned home later in the evening. 

The couple lodged a complaint with the Airyankuppam police. They have who booked the youth under Section 4 (punishment for penetrative sexual assault) of POCSO Act and have launched a hunt for the youth.
