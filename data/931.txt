Bhandup
Dec 16, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Mumbai, Dec 16 : In a shocking incident, two 3-year-old girls were allegedly stripped and molested by a 35-year-old man at Bhandup in north-east Mumbai last evening, police said.
 
The accused Shivprasad Kurini, who was arrested last night, abducted neighbour's children and molested them.
