Puducherry
Oct 13, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police booked a minor boy on charges of raping his 10-year-old cousin, a differently-abled girl, in Puducherry. Police said the boy visited his uncle's house in Nathamedu in Nettapakkam on the outskirts on October 9. 

He took advantage of the fact that his cousin was alone in the house and sexually assaulted her. 

Their grandmother, who reached home was shocked to find him abusing the girl. She raised an alarm and the boy fled. She lodged a complaint with the child welfare committee. A protection officer from the committee made preliminary inquiries. 

The Nettapakkam police registered a case against the boy under Section 6 (punishment for aggravated penetrative sexual assault) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO), 2012 and begun inquiries. Police have initiated efforts to record the statement of the girl, who according to an investigation officer, has not recovered fully from the assault. 

"We have booked the minor boy and have sent the child for medical examination. We will secure the boy as soon as we receive the report of the medical examination and produce him before a juvenile court," said an investigation officer.
