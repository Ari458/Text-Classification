Andhra Pradesh
May 29, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A lorry driver tried to molest an 11-year-old girl at Ponnur in the wee hours of Thursday. The accused, Gopi, was nabbed by police. 

The victim was sleeping in the verandah of her house when Gopi tried to force himself upon her at 1 am. The alarmed girl shouted out loudly. Alerted by this, parents and neighbours woke up. However, the accused fled from the spot. "He tried to molest the girl," Ponnur circle inspector Sheik Bilaluddin said. Gopi was later caught by the police.
