Uttar Pradesh
Oct 08, 2011
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A two-and-half-year old girl was abducted, raped and abandoned beside a railway track in Kalyanpur area here by an unknown person, the police said.

Meena Devi, the mother of the victim, was waiting for a train at Rawatpur railway station when a stranger took the girl from her and then disappeared. She lodged an FIR of kidnapping at Farrokhabad police station, the police said.

The girl was found this morning lying in a serious condition near a railway track in Kalyanpur area, said Shailendra Lal, Circle Officer, Kalyanpur police station.
