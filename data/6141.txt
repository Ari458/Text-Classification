Uttar Pradesh
Jan 04, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and gangraped by three youths at Bhagwanpur village here, police said today.

The minor girl was abducted by three accused - Gulab, Gyyur and Lal - near her residence on December 29 and then gangraped her at Bhagwanpur village under Sikhera police station, they said.

The victim's mother alleged her daughter was found in an semi-conscious state when she returned home, they said.

Police have yesterday registered a case against the accused, they said, adding that the hunt is on to nab them.
