Sangli
Dec 25, 2011
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			On August 31, 2007, social activist and lawyer Varsha Deshpande had conducted a sting operation by sending two pregnant women as decoy patients to Shinde's hospital. Shinde took the woman to Raval's hospital in his car. After confirming the sex of the foetus, Shinde advised the decoy patient to get an abortion done.

The convictions were as per the provisions of the Pre-conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques Act, 2003.
