Haryana
Dec 17, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A youth was arrested for the murder of a dalit woman three months ago in Julana area here, police said today.

Anil alias Sonu was in a relationship with the woman, Manju, and when she refused to elope with him, he slit her throat and murdered her at her residence in Julana on September 4, Julana police station SHO Sahdev said.

Manju, married to a mason Ajit with whom she had a one-and-a-half-year-old son, was staying on rent in a one-room house where the incident happened, the officer said.
