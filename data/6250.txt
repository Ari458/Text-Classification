Uttar Pradesh
Jan 19, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A youth allegedly tried to rape a 14-year-old girl after finding alone at her residence in Budina Kalan village here, police said today.

According to the complaint filed by victim's father, accused Munna entered the house yesterday when the girl's parents were away, they said.

He allegedly tried to rape the minor but she resisted and raised an alarm, following which the accused ran away, the police said.

A case has been registered against absconding Munna, they said, adding that efforts are on to nab him.
