Odisha
Mar 02, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old deaf and dumb woman has allegedly been raped by a youth. 

The 19-year-old accused Pabitra Bal, was arrested and forwarded to judicial custody. 

The victim lodged a complaint accusing him of raping her in Badabaranga locality of Kendrapara Town, after which the police swung into action. The victim has been taken for a medical test.
