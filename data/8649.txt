Tamil Nadu
Jul 29, 2016
Domestic Violence
Description:
			The Chennai police have arrested a 23-year-old man for allegedly assaulting and harassing his wife.

Police said Lakshmi Kanth, a resident of Lakshmiamman Koil Street in Puzhal, married Tamilarasi in 2010. On Tuesday, a quarrel broke out between them when he asked her to demand Rs 1 lakh as dowry from her parents. Following the quarrel, Tamilarasi went to her parents' house.

On Thursday, she returned to collect her clothes. On spotting her, Lakshmi Kanth quarrelled with her and allegedly assaulted her. Based on Tamilarasi's complaint, police registered a case and arrested him. He was remanded in judicial custody.
