Bangalore
Nov 05, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A 34-year-old woman allegedly hanged herself in Kempegowda Nagar on Thursday night.

The deceased, Manjula, was married to Kumara, 40, for 14 years. The couple was childless. Manjula and Kumara, an alcoholic, had frequent arguments over the matter. The police suspect that this could have driven Manjula to commit suicide.

On Thursday night, Kumara came home in an inebriated state and went to sleep. Manjula hanged herself to the metal rod supporting the ceiling.
