Jammu
Aug 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A person was arrested for allegedly molesting a woman at Leri village in Udhampur district.

Fareed Mohammad, a habitual offender, molested the victim on August 23 following which a complaint was lodged with the Udhampur police station.

Taking prompt action, police teams were dispatched to various places in Kashmir, Jammu and Punjab to apprehend the Mohammad.

The accused was arrested from Qazigund area of Kashmir.
