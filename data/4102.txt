Bihar
Feb 07, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The Fast Track Court (FTC) IV judge Arun Kumar Jha passed the order convicting the accused, identified as Kanhaiya Kumar, to lifer and imposed a fine of Rs 2000 for murdering his wife Manka Devi and 18-month-old son Rakesh Kumar in Pulma village under the Akbarpur Police Station area on October 10, 2007.

Kumar took the step after his in-laws failed to meet his demand of a motorcycle and Rs 50,000 cash in dowry.
