Barwani
Sep 23, 2012
Murder, Rape
Description:
			Barwani: A 24-year-old woman was allegedly murdered by a man for resisting his advances at her house in Kela Amba village in the district, police said on Sunday. 

The accused identified as Jamdaar entered Kamli Bai's house on the intervening night of Friday and Saturday where she was sleeping with her children and tried to rape her to which she resisted, they said. 
 
Jamdaar then stabbed her in stomach and neck before fleeing from the spot, police said. 

Kamli was rushed to district hospital in serious condition where she succumbed to injuries last evening, they said.
