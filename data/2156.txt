Raisen
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Panjara village here, police said today.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl was playing outside and accused Chhotu alias Dharmendra from the same village sexually assaulted her, they said.

Police has launched a hunt to nab the accused, who fled from the scene, after villagers gathered there, hearing the cries of the girl.
