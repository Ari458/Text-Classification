Jharkhand
Jan 16, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old youth was arrested for allegedly abducting and raping a minor girl in Pakur district, police today said.

The accused identified as Md Kalimuddin had allegedly lured the 14-year-old girl while she was on her way to school couple of days ago and raped her.

He then dropped her in Hiranpur Bazar yesterday, police said.

On reaching home, the girl, a resident of Chilto village under Littipara Police Station, narrated the incident to her parents. The girl belonged to Pahariya community.

An FIR has been lodged based on the statement of the girl's father and Kalimuddin was arrested.
