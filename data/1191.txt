Kharghar
Jan 21, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			The accused sub-jailor of Taloja jail Ashok Malwad contacted the 41-year-old wife of undertrial Riyaz Siddiqui and told her that he wanted to meet and talk to her about something. This was when she invited him to her residence,said senior inspector Kiran Patil of the Kharghar police, adding, Once at her house, Malwad allegedly held her hand and told her that she could call him anytime she wanted for anything, even at late nights or early hours. Saying so, the accused further outraged her modesty, the complainant has said.
