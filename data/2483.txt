Begusarai
Jun 26, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A college girl was allegedly raped and murdered by unidentified persons in Bihar's Begusarai district.

The 17 year-old B.Sc first year student was allegedly raped last night and later stabbed to death. Her tormentors then dumped her body on the railway track near Danauli Phulwaria station.

The police recovered the victim's body from the
railway tracks after being informed by locals during the day.
