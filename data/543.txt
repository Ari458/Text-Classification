Patna
Oct 29, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A railway employee was on Saturday forwarded to jail for allegedly raping his only daughter. 

The 14-year-old victim alleged her father, a yard master posted at Garhara in Begusarai district, had been sexually assaulting her for quite some time. On Friday, when she returned from school, he entered her room and attempted to rape her again. When she resisted, he allegedly hit her with a stick and forced himself upon her, the Class IX student told police. The girl's mother, a housewife, and two brothers were not at home at the time of the incident.
