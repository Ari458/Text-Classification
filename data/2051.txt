New Delhi
Apr 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The medical report of a three-year-old girl, who was allegedly sexually assaulted by her cousin, has confirmed rape, police said today.

17-year-old Prempal had raped the girl in Ramnagar village of the district on Wednesday, police said.

Later, upset over his deed, he had attempted suicide by consuming poison. He was immediately rushed to the hospital by his relatives.

Source: Press Trust of India
