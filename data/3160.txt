Uttarakhand
Sep 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A school principal was arrested in Pauri for allegedly molesting a girl student.

Madan Prasad Bahuguna was arrested after students of the school demonstrated in protest against the incident accusing him of molesting the girl inside the institution premises.

According to the victim, who studies in class eight, the principal of the school molested her during lunch recess but she somehow managed to escape.
