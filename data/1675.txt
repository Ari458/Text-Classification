Sohiong
May 09, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 12 yr-old girl was raped by her father at Mawkneng near Sohiong in East Khasi Hills on April 23.

When the FIR was filed, the Women's organisation did not know that the culprit was the father of the girl and later got the confirmation from the police.

The culprit was arrested and the girl was given medical attention including counseling, police informed while refusing to provide the name of the father stating that this would affect the mental psyche of the child.

Meanwhile, the CSWO President also appreciated the work of the Mawngap Police outpost who have made instant arrest and also the good work of the doctor at the CHC Sohiong, who has informed authorities of such an act.
