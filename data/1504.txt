Mysore
Mar 19, 2012
Rape
Description:
			21 year old girl from Anchaiya village of Mysore was kidnapped and raped by Parmesh, 26, who is residing in the same village, on March 12.   

She was allegedly raped by him in Bangalore and Goa. Police said Parmesh is living with his wife and one-year-old daughter in the same village. The girl, who came to the village on Sunday afternoon, filed a case against Parmesh's father Sannaswamy and his sister Rajalakshmi.
