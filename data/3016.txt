West Bengal
Aug 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			One person was arrested from Burrabazar area of the city for allegedly molesting a teenaged school girl.

Sambhu Mandal was arrested after he tried to molest a Class 12 student when she was returning from school.

Sambhu allegedly blocked her way and tried to molest her. When she raised an alarm, locals nabbed Sambhu and handed him over to police.

It has been revealed during investigation that the girl was known to him.
