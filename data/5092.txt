Uttar Pradesh
Jul 13, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly kidnapped and gangraped for three days by three persons in Nawabganj area of the district.

The 13-year-old girl was kidnapped on July 10 and allegedly gangraped by Vinod, Vijay and an unidentified person, Superintendent of Police Bharat Singh Yadav said today.

He said that the accused left the girl in a village last evening and escaped.

Source: Press Trust of India
