Punjab
May 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old dalit girl was allegedly raped by two brothers, also related to the victim, police said today.

Joginder Singh and Swaran Singh, both residents of Rauwal village here, allegedly raped the girl on the evening of May 10 when she was alone in her house, they said.

The duo escaped after the victim's mother arrived in the house and caught them in the act, police said, adding that the accused were relatives of the girl.
