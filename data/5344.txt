Rajasthan
Aug 21, 2014
Abduction
Description:
			A 16-year-old tribal girl studying in a government school was allegedly abducted by four men in a vehicle in Dungarpur district.

The ninth class student was abducted yesterday in Overi village.

The girl was going home after school hours when the four men, who came in a tempo, forcibly took her away, police said.

Other students, who witnessed the incident, informed her parents about it. Later, they registered a case with the Sagwara police station.

So far no arrest has been made and the police is searching for the accused.
