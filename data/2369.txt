Rajkot
Jun 08, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman was branded severely by her husband on both legs after he grew suspicious about her character.

Varsha Munna Solanki was rushed to Rajkot civil hospital in a critical condition yesterday by her aunt Chandaben, as she fell unconscious after being branded severly by her husband with hot pincers on Thursday.

Chandaben told police that Munna was suspicious about Varsha's character and allegedly harassed and beat her regularly.

Munna came to home and started beating Varsha with a stick and then branded her with hot pincers on her both legs.
