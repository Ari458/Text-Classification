Odisha
Jan 26, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A minor tribal girl had to pay the price of resisting rape with her life in Jajpur district, near Kendrapada of Odisha. The eight-year-old tribal girl was killed after she resisted rape.
 
Police have arrested a 16-year-old boy on charges of throttling the girl of Rangiguda village. The father of the victim filed an FIR stating his daughter was missing. During investigation, police found the body at a hillock in Bagasadei village. The accused tried to rape the girl and in the struggle that followed, she was killed.
 
Police have seized the body and sent it to a local government hospital for autopsy.
