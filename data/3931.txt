Tamil Nadu
Jan 21, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 30-year-old lady college professor was found dead with stab injuries at her residence at Subramaiyanagar here last night, police said today.

They said police personnel rushed to the spot on receiving information and found her dead. The jewelry she was wearing was intact.

Inquiries revealed that the women was a professor at a private college at Tiruchengodu. Her husband is a medical representative.
