hyderabad
Oct 29, 2011
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Priyanka lodged a complaint against her husband A Srikanth and father-in-law Narsing Rao, both employees of a vernacular media organization, for demanding additional dowry of Rs three lakh. 

According to the police, the couple got married in 2003 and have a one-year-old daughter. The relation between the couple got strained over time following alleged demands for money. The accused, who are residents of Chaitanyapuri, at the time of marriage, reportedly took Rs three lakh dowry and 10 tola gold
