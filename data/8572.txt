Karnataka
Jul 01, 2016
Molestation
Description:
			Gangoli police on Thursday arrested a youth from Bengeri for allegedly forcefully undressing and sexually assaulting a girl.

According to police, the accused is Bavikatte Suraj, 19. The incident happened around 7am on Wednesday, when the 19-year-girl was on her way to Gujjadi bus stop to go to her tailoring shop, where she works. When she reached an isolated place, the accused blocked her way and allegedly undressed her. When she shouted, he attacked her and fled.

The girl informed about the incident to her family and a complaint was lodged at Gangoli station. Police arrested him and produced him before Kundapur court. He has been remanded to judicial custody till July 12.
