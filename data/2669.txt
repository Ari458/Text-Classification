Bihar
Mar 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A nurse, returning home from the market was gang raped allegedly by three men in Bihar's Saharsa district.
	
The 35-year-old nurse, working at a private nursing home, was returning home after buying vegetables from the market when the three allegedly assaulted her and dragged her to an
isolated place where they gang raped her.
	
The victim was admitted to a hospital for medical tests.
