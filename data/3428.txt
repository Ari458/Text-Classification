Andhra Pradesh
Oct 28, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl succumbed to injuries after she was set afire allegedly by a youth for resisting his advances in Khammam district of Andhra Pradesh.

The eighth class student, who had suffered 90 per cent burns in the incident on October 17 at Thirlapuram village of Garla mandal,succumbed to injuries.

The accused, identified as V Babu Rao, had allegedly doused the victim in kerosene and set her ablaze after she warded off his attempt to get intimate with her.
