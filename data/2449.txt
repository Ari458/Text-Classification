Amolapatty
Jun 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A five-year old girl was sexually assaulted by a 40-year old man in upper Assam's Dibrugarh town.

The girl had gone near a neighbourhood shop in a slum in Amolapatty when its owner Nekib Ali brought her in and allegedly sexually assaulted her.

Hearing the girl shouting on being molested, nearby people came to her rescue and handed the culprit to police.
