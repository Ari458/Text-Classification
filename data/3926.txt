Maharashtra
Jan 18, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth at Badlapur over a period of one month.

The accused, identified as Sonu Khan, 23, a resident of Nirmaladevi Gate in Kalwa, was arrested from his residence in this case.

The accused used to threaten the girl and ask her to accompany him to Badlapur. He would threaten to defame her, if she failed to follow what.

The accused repeatedly raped the girl between December 18 and January 17.

However, the victim finally told about her ordeal to her family members, following which her father registered a complaint with the police and the accused was immediately arrested.

Further investigation into the case is on.
