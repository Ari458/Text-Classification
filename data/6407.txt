Uttar Pradesh
Feb 11, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 35-year-old woman was stabbed to death allegedly by her husband in Khalilpur village in Chitbadagaon area here, police said today.

The deceased identified as Kashmiri was killed by her husband Hareram Rajbhar after they had an altercation over selling land yesterday, Superintendent of Police K C Goswami said.

The accused then fled from the spot, leaving his dead wife in the house, the SP said, adding that the incident came to the fore when his son reached home.
