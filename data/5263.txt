Rajasthan
Aug 07, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her stepfather in Rajasthan's Rajsamand district, police said.

The accused, Hiralal (35), was arrested yesterday after the principal of the school the girl studies at lodged an FIR in the matter.

The girl told of her ordeal to her teacher who then informed the principal about it, SP Sweta Dhankar said today, adding that a medical test on the girl has confirmed rape.
