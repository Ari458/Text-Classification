Maharashtra
Apr 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old trader from powerloom town of Bhiwandi here has been arrested for allegedly raping a teenage girl for nearly a year.

According to A M Saikar of Nizampura police station, accused Sheram Abdul Kalam, a resident of Kidwai Nagar, Nagaon, in Bhiwandi has arrested for sexually abusing the 15-year-old since June 2013.

The victim, a tenth grade student from the same locality is six months pregnant.

Source: Press Trust of India
