Madhya Pradesh
Jun 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was gang-raped allegedly by relatives of her brother-in-law in Rampura village near here.

The incident occurred on the intervening night of June 2-3, when Ramakant Kushwaha (19) and Sube Kushwaha (20) asked the 11-year-old girl to come to their place under the pretext of charging her mobile phone.

The duo then took her to a nearby agriculture field and raped her, they also switched off her mobile phone so that she remained untraceable.

The girl's father could contact her only after she switched on her mobile phone after she escaped from the clutches of the accused.

The family members of the girl initially decided to keep the matter under wraps but later gathered courage to approach police and lodge complaint.
