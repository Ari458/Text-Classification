Punjab
Jun 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by four youths at gun-point at Mahatam Nagar village in Fazilka district, police said on Wednesday. On a statement of the victim, Fazilka police have booked brothers Pamma and Rinku, Pritam and Hansa, all residents of Mahatam Nagar, on charges of kidnapping and raping an unmarried girl. No arrests have been made so far.

In her statement to police, the girl alleged that Pamma and Rinku, along with their friends Pritam and Hansa, broke into her house on Tuesday night while she and her brothers were sleeping in the courtyard. The victim's mother had gone on a pilgrimage on the night of the incident.

The accused took her away after gagging her and then allegedly took turns to rape her at gun-point. They threatened her with dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anyone, the victim alleged.

She narrated her plight to her mother who took her to the civil hospital and police were informed about the incident. When contacted, the station house officer (SHO) of Fazilka sadar police station, Baljinder Singh, said they were probing the matter.
