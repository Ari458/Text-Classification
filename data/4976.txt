Madhya Pradesh
Jun 22, 2014
Molestation, Crime, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman who was traveling with her kid on a bus was sexually harassed and beaten by the bus conductor here on Friday night. A case in this regard was later registered at Jehangirabad police station.

Accused Ranjeet has been taken into custody. The woman, a resident of Kaachi Chola, was traveling from a place near Sagar to Bhopal.
