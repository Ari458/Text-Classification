Tamil Nadu
Mar 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old polytechnic student has been arrested for allegedly raping a minor girl at Periya Agraharam area in the city.
 	
Police said the sixth standard girl, staying with her grandmother, had gone to her relative's house to
attend a festival.
	
The boy, who was their neighbour, took her to an isolated spot nearby on his bike on the pretext of giving her a cold drink and biscuits and raped her.
 	
The girl made her way back and narrated her plight to her relatives, who immediately informed police. The girl has been hospitalized.
