Kuruvikkarankulam village near Pulivalam
Feb 18, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Dhanabal, 32, of Kuruvikkarankulam village near Pulivalam, married Gomathi, 22, of Aevoor near Musiri in 2008. The couple was living with Dhanabal's parents in the same house. However, Dhanabal and his parents reportedly harassed Gomathi demanding dowry and this led to frequent quarrels between them.

A similar quarrel broke out between Dhanabal and Gomathi on the night of November 21, 2010. The charred body of Gomathi was recovered from near their house the next morning.
