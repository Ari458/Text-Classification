Haryana
Jun 14, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Kalka police on Friday arrested a rape accused, a resident of Himachal Pradesh, and produced him in a local court that sent him to judicial custody.

Police had registered a rape case against the accused on Thursday after receiving a complaint.

SHO Mohender Singh said, "The accused was arrested from Kalka and presented in the district court that sent him to judicial custody."
