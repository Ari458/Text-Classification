Tezpur
Apr 17, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped and killed by unidentified persons in a paddy field near Cheralia village in Tezpur, Sonitpur district, today, police said today.
     The body of the girl, missing since last night, was spotted by villagers this morning and they informed the police.
     Police, however, are yet to confirm the rape and said details would be released after post-mortem.
Various organisations, including the All Adivasi Students Association of Assam (AASAA,) have condemned the incident and demanded immediate arrest and punishment of the culprits.
Gakul Orang (26) arrested. Dipen Orang (22), Sam Orang (27) and August Orang (28) detained. Identity of 4 others not disclosed.
