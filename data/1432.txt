Jalna
Mar 08, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Jalna: A two year-old girl was allegedly raped and murdered by her 20-year-old neighbour at Indira Nagar in Jalna.
  
The accused, Ravi Ghumare, lured the child, who was playing outside her house, to his room on the pretext of offering her ice cream and sexually assaulted her. He then strangulated the victim, they said.
