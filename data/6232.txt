Maharashtra
Jan 17, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 30-year-old school teacher has been arrested for allegedly molesting a Class III girl student, police said today.

Premsingh Shravan Chavan, employed with a Zilla Parishad-run school in Vikramgadh town of the district, was arrested last evening after the nine-year-old girl's parents lodged a complaint, they said.

Sub-Inspector P S Naik of Vikramgadh Police Station said Chavan, who was the class teacher of Standard III, used to molest the complainant and other girls on the pretext of checking their homework.
