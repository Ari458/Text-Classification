Delhi
Jan 28, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl alleged that she was raped after being abducted by her neighbour in west Delhi's Nihal Vihar.

The victim lives with her family in Lakshmi Park. In her complaint to police, she claimed that she was abducted by a man named Gaurav. The accused took her to a friends house on January 17 and kept her confined for three days, a police officer said.

It is alleged that she was raped several times by Gaurav during that period. 
The girl somehow managed to escape from Gaurav clutches and revealed the ordeal to her family. Police have also conducted her medical examination which confirmed sexual assault.
