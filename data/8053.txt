Kerala
Oct 29, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Aluva police on Wednesday arrested a 24-year-old person on charges of sexually abusing a mentally ill girl after promising to marry her. The accused has been identified as Ashif K M of Mannarkadu. According to police, the accused had been in a relationship with the girl, an inmate of the Aluva SOS Village, and he abused her a couple of days ago promising to marry her.

The incident came to light when the accused left for Palakkad after switching off his phone soon after the abuse. The police took him into custody on Tuesday night while returning to Kalamassery by train. The police also said accused, who is also married man and father of a kid, was wanted in several cases in Mannarkadu, his native place.
