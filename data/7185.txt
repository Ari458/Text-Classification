Tamil Nadu
Jun 20, 2015
Domestic Violence, Murder
Description:
			Thangamani (27), a salt pan worker from Kunjaiyapuram, was murdered by her husband Sudalaimadasamy (29) at Periyasamypuram at Soorangudi on Friday. According to police, Sudalaimadasamy, a painter, used to harass his wife in an inebriated condition. On Friday, he attacked her with an iron rod while she was at work in the salt pan at Periyasamypuram. Based on a complaint lodged by M. Kottaipandi (52), father of the deceased, Soorangudi police have registered a case.
