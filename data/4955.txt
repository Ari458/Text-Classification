Maharashtra
Jun 16, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			Fed up of being sexual harassed by a youth, a 15-year-old Thane girl tried to commit suicide by slashing her wrists on Saturday, said the police.

The Kasarvadavli police have registered an offence against an unidentified person as the Class X student was not in a position to name her tormentor. Reportedly, the girl initially ignored the accused's lewd remarks and harassment. But the youth is believed to have proposed marriage to the girl, following which she tried commit suicide.
