Tamil Nadu
Sep 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 39-year-old man was arrested for allegedly attempting to rape a teenaged girl, his neighbour.

Gopal of Poondurai had a couple of days ago taken the 16-year old girl on a two-wheeler to the railway colony area and attempted to rape her.

The girl who suffered minor injuries managed to escape from him and returned home.

She later told her parents about the incident.

They lodged a complaint with All Women Police Station today following which Gopal was arrested.
