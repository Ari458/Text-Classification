Rajasthan
Jan 04, 2014
Street Harassment
Description:
			A 44-year-old man was arrested for allegedly teasing a girl in Durgapur.

Deendayal Chaudhary, who was in an inebriated condition, stopped his car and misbehaved with a 17-year-girl, who was waiting for a bus at Durgapura bus stand.

The girl noted the number of his car and informed police, following which the accused was arrested.
