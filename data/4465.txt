Tamil Nadu
Mar 26, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 35-year-old labourer was arrested today on charges of sexually abusing a six-year-old girl, police said.

Police said Valapadi took the girl to an isolated place when she was standing near a riverbed here and sexually abused her. When she made loud cries, public caught hold of him and handed over to police.
