New Delhi
Sep 04, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl, who ran away from her house, was allegedly molested by two juveniles.

According to the police, the girl was found lying in a semi-conscious state near Abhimanyu Apartments in East Delhi's New Ashok Nagar area.

The girl was seen lying in a semi-conscious state by passersby who, in turn, informed the police. The girl has said that she is a resident of Khoda Colony in Noida. She ran away from her house as her mother forced her to work at home.

She has claimed that when she reached near New Ashok Nagar area, two juveniles tried to molest her. The girl has been sent back home.

Source: Press Trust Of India
