New Delhi
Aug 10, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by two men, one of whom she claimed to be a Delhi Metro official, on the pretext of getting her a job at Prashant Vihar in outer Delhi. A case has been registered on the basis of the complaint, police said.
The complainant, who stays at Shakurpur in northwest Delhi, said she had approached one of the men, a contractor looking for work with DMRC. He introduced her to a corporation official who promised to get her a job in one of the Metro projects. The contractor took her to a house in Haryana a few days ago promising her an interview and training under a senior official, she added. She was confined for a few hours.
After a complaint was received, the woman was sent for a medical examination, which confirmed rape. A case was then registered at Prashant Vihar police station. Her statement was recorded and the two men, one of whom lives in Sector 11 of Rohini and the other in Noida, were called.
They are questioning them. A team will be sent to retrieve clues.
