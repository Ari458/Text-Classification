Gujarat
Nov 20, 2013
Abduction, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A Dalit woman was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by three people while she was returning home with her brother-in-law.

The incident took place near Morbi Road, when the woman was returning home along with her brother-in-law on a scooter.

The accused allegedly intercepted them and beat up the victim's brother-in-law.

They took the woman to a nearby abandoned house and raped her at knife point, the victim lodged an FIR against the three.

Primary investigation revealed that the victim and one of the accused were residents of same locality and she had close relations with the latter's sister-in-law.

Police have not disclosed the identity of the accused.
