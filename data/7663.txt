Uttar Pradesh
Aug 19, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Additional district and sessions judge Udai Pratap Singh on Tuesday convicted a man named as Ramakant Mishra for dowry death and sentenced him for life imprisonment. He was also ordered to pay a fine of Rs 25,000. 

There were four other accused in the case, all family members of Ramakant Mishra. The judge acquitted them. 

Ramakant, a resident of Juhi Baradevi, was married to Pratibha Devi, daughter of Jagat Narayan Pandey, a resident of Naubasta, on March 8, 2007. He was not satisfied with the gifts and presents given by his in-laws at the time of marriage and kept on demanding Rs 1 lakh and a motorcycle. 

On May 25, 2010, Jagat Narayan Pandey got information that his daughter had been killed by her in-laws. He lodged an FIR against Ramakant Mishra and other members of the family. 

The post-mortem report revealed that cause of death was head injuries.
