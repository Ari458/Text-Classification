Central Delhi
Dec 19, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 23-year-old woman from Bangalore has been rescued from a brothel in central Delhi G B Road, police said on Tuesday.

The victim was lured to Delhi by a woman from Bangalore with promises of better jobs.
She was rescued with the help of an anti-human trafficking NGO Shakti Vahini,said Devesh Chandra Srivastva, Additional Commissioner of Police (central). Action was taken after we were informed that a woman was confined in kotha number 5211, against her wishes, he added.
