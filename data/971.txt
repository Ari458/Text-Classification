Fergusson College
Dec 21, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Students and Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) activists on Monday staged a protest at Fergusson College (FC) demanding action against a faculty member who allegedly sexually harassed a student. 

On October 12, the student had complained against the faculty member to the head of her department who in turn went to the principal.
