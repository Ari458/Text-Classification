Bihar
Oct 12, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly gang raped and later killed by unidentified people at a village in Bihar's Rohtas district.

The nine-year-old girl had gone to a fair in Dharhara village on the occasion of Dusshera festival with her mother.

Her mother returned home early leaving the girl alone in the fair.

Taking advantage of the situation, three unknown people lured her and took the girl to an isolated place where they allegedly raped her.

Her body was traced from a tank nearby the village.
