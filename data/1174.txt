Maharastra
Jan 19, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			The victim, Najma Lulania(35), immolated herself at her paternal home at Jawhar on January 8 and succumbed to her injuries on January 15. She accused The deputy tehsildar of Mokhada taluka in Thane, Nandkumar Khade of sending lewd SMSs and forwarding intimate MMS of their affair prior to her wedding, to her husband. In her suicide note, Najma accused Khade of blackmailing her into a relationship even after marriage. Fearing her marriage would collapse, Najma set herself on fire.
