Attur
Feb 16, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A secondary grade teacher, A Anbazhagan (46) works in Vasanthapuram primary school. During class hours, Anbazhagan allegedly beat up students and allegedly molested a class 5 student at an elementary school in Manivizhundan colony in Attur taluk.

On Tuesday, the girl's parents and villagers laid siege to the school demanding action against the teacher. When assistant elementary education officers Nedumaran and Martin rushed to the school the villagers told them that the teacher had come to the school drunk.
