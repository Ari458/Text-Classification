Chandigarh
Dec 15, 2013
Molestation, Murder
Description:
			A man allegedly tried to outrage the modesty of a woman, and stabbed her brother-in-law to death when he protested.

An FIR has been registered on the complaint of the woman, who alleged that Shankar alias Judi tried to outraged her modesty and also stabbed Pappu at Bapu Dham Colony, Sector 26.

Pappu, who was fatally injured, succumbed to his injuries at PGIMER.
