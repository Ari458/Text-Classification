Goregaon railway station
Jan 31, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			22-year-old Aarti Thakur (a resident of Nallasopara) was attacked by an unidentified assailant who threw acid on her face, outside Goregaon railway station. 

"This is the third time that Aarti has been attacked by an unknown person" said the girl's mother Seema Thakur. Her mother added that she was first attacked in November last year as she was walking in Malwani. The second attack occurred in December.
