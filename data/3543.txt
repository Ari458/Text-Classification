Andhra Pradesh
Nov 18, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Three people were taken into custody on charge of raping a woman in neighbouring Ranga Reddy district.

On the pretext of offering lift to the 40-year-old woman, accused Ramesh took her on his bike and was later joined in by Mahesh and Anji who allegedly raped her near a forest area on the outskirts of Chowdapur village of Pargi mandal.
