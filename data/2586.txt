Odisha
Mar 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old mentally retarded girl was allegedly gang-raped in a remote village in Odisha's Kendrapara district.
	
The mentally unstable girl was dragged from near her house in Nuapada village under Patkura police station and raped by two offenders on March 19, according to the complaint lodged by victim's elder sister.
  
The complaint did not name the offenders who sexually abused the victim. The victim has been sent for medical examination.
