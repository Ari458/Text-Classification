Maharashtra
Feb 25, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Murder
Description:
			A 26-year-old man was arrested today for allegedly raping and murdering a seven-year-old girl in Wada taluka of the district.

The accused Atul Rama Lote allegedly abducted the girl outside her house in Kudus village on the pretext of giving sweets last evening.

He then took her to a forest area and allegedly sexually assaulted her before strangulating her to death as she was crying.

The girl's body was found in the jungle today, following which police launched a hunt for the accused and traced him in afternoon on a tip-off.

Police said Lote is the resident of Chinchghar village.

The girl's body is sent for postmortem.
