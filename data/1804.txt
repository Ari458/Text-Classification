Madhya Pradesh
Oct 28, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A man, who had earlier served ten years in jail for raping a minor, has been arrested again for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl in Sunera village about 10 km away from the district headquarters, police said today.

The incident took place yesterday, when the minor girl was sleeping with her parents in a hut on their farm.

After committing the crime, he dumped the victim near her hut and when her parents spotted the girl crying with pain they informed the police about the incident.

Earlier in 2000, Bagri was jailed for ten years for raping a four-year-old girl and was jailed for ten years, police said adding that he had been released from jail in 2010. Further investigation is on.
