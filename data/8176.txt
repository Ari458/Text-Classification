Punjab
Dec 20, 2015
Domestic Violence
Description:
			Harish Verma was arrested for harassment on the complaint of his wife Nisha, a resident of Model House.

The woman had alleged that her husband was a drug addict and he used to thrash her. Harish did not appear before the police despite repeated reminders following which he was arrested. She alleged that she got married to Harish in 2013 and they have a six-month-old girl child.
