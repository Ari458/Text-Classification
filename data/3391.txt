Andhra Pradesh
Oct 21, 2013
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 26-year-old housewife committed suicide in Alwal over harassment by her husband for dowry. G Padma had married Bhikshapati seven years ago. Bikshapati had asked her to get money from her parents when she visited them during Dasara. However, when she returned empty handed, he began harassing her. Padma set herself ablaze with kerosene at her house. She succumbed at the hospital.
