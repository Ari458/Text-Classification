Maharashtra
Oct 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The Hadapsar police arrested Ravi Sonba Dhanawade (45), a resident of Hadapsar, for allegedly raping a 10-year-old girl. A special court sent Dhanawade to police custody.

Dhanawade is a carpenter. Dhanawade had called the girl, a class V student, to his house and allegedly raped her.

The rape survivor told her mother about the incident. The mother lodged a complaint against Dhanawade under sections of the Indian Penal Code and Protection to Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The police will record the parents' statement to find out why they delayed registering the complaint.
