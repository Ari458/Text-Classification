India
Jun 10, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor boy allegedly raped a five-year-old girl on the pretext of giving her candy in a sub-urban village Nangal-Jassa-Bohara, following which he was arrested, police said.

The incident occurred on Tuesday when the 14-year-old boy lured the girl on the pretext of giving her candy (toffy) to the terrace of his house and raped her there, police said. The victim's father on Wednesday filed a complaint with the Kardhani police station against the boy, who is a student of ninth class, they said.

A case has been registered under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, they said, adding the accused was arrested and sent to 'Bal Sudhar Grah'.
