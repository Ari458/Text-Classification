Haryana
Dec 04, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly raped by a 24-year-old youth at Berli Khurd village.

The victim yesteray lodged a complaint alleging that when she was going to a field in the village to answer the nature's call, Dharmender allegedly sexually assaulted her.

She further alleged that when she tried to raise the alarm, he shut her mouth with a piece of cloth and threatened her with dire consequences.

He was produced in a local court, which remanded him to 14-day judicial custody.

Source: Press Trust Of India
