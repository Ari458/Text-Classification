Tamil Nadu
Jan 29, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 75-year-old man was arrested today on charges of sexually harassing a six year old girl, his neighbour, police said here.

Muthuswamy took the girl to his house when her parents were away and sexually harassed her, All Women Police Inspector Ezhilarasi said.

She narrated the incident to her parents when they returned home.

Following a complaint from the parents, the man was arrested and a local court remanded him to 15 days judicial custody.


Source: Press Trust of India
