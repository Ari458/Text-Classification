New Delhi
Apr 14, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 23-year-old woman has accused her father-in-law and her husband's friend of raping her in south Delhi's Govindpuri.

According to a complaint filed by the woman today, the father-in-law raped her over a year ago while her husband's 32-year-old friend, Sonu raped her in March.

The woman, who had gotten married two years ago, said in her complaint, that she was raped by her father-in-law last year, after which she left for her parents' home. After a compromise, she returned to her husband's place this year.

After this, her husband took her on a holiday during which she was introduced to Sonu, who, according to her, raped her in Delhi last month.

Source: Press Trust of India
