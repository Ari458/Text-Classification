Karnataka
Jul 07, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A dowry harassment case has been filed against a person and his family members in town police station here.

The victim woman has been identified as Soumya (22). She had married Lingaswamy alias Kumar, a resident of Athur cross here on February 20, 2013. The couple has a child. 

It is said that 15 sovereign gold was given during the marriage as dowry. But Kumar along with his family members Suresh, Santamani, Ambika, Geetha and Vijayakumar tortured Soumya to bring additional dowry. They also threatened her it is stated in the complaint.
