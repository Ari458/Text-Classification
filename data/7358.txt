Andhra Pradesh
Apr 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was sexually assaulted by a 60-year-old man in an inebriated condition at Cumbum in Prakasam district.

Cumbum police said the man, K. Venkateswarulu, took the girl coming from school in the night to an isolated place and committed the offence. The girls parents, after a frantic search, found her near a bunk in the wee hours of Thursday. The man was booked under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, police said.
