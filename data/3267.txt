Punjab
Oct 04, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Police booked a 21-year-old man for allegedly raping a teenager at Rehali village in the district.

The 19-year-old girl's mother filed a complaint that Surjit Singh committed the crime around three months ago.

Surjit, who used to visit the girl's house, got her engaged with his brother-in-law.

But around three months ago Surjit came to their house and raped the girl after spiking her drink.

After a month of the incident, the girl's mother got her abortion done.
