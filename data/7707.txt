Tamil Nadu
Aug 27, 2015
Rape
Description:
			The Mahila Court here has awarded seven-year imprisonment to an autorickshaw driver for sexually assaulting a girl.

According to prosecution, M. Petchimuthu (24), an auto driver from South Aauththikulam near Kayathar, sexually assaulted a 19-year-old girl from the same area on June 15, 2013 and refused to marry her.

When the victim told Petchimuthu's father Mariappan, mother Sudalai and grandfather Velu about the incident, they verbally abused her. Based on a complaint from the victim, the police arrested all the four.

Mahila Court Judge J. Flora while sentencing Petchimuthu to imprisonment slapped a fine of Rs. 2,000 on him on Wednesday. However, the judge acquitted others.
