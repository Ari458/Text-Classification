West Bengal
Jul 28, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A high school teacher near here was arrested today for allegedly trying to rape a Class XII student on Saturday.

A source said Abdul Latif, the teacher, is a resident of Bongaon in North 24-Paraganas. He has been teaching Bengali at a co-educational higher secondary school for the past three years.

The girls' family filed a police complaint yesterday. Latif was produced in the chief judicial magistrate's court here today and remanded in judicial custody for 14 days.
