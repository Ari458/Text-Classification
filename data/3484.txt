Kashmir
Nov 06, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two youths were arrested for allegedly raping a 16-year-old girl in Pulwama RPT Pulwama district of Kashmir.

Aadil Wani and Shahid Manzoor Wani, both residents of Mandakpal in Khrew area of Pulwama RPT Pulwama, were arrested after the minor girl lodged a complaint against them.

According to the complaint, the men allegedly took the victim behind some bushes in nearby paddy fields where they took turns to rape her.

The victim has been sent for a medical examination.
