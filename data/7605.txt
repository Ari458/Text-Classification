Maharashtra
Aug 10, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Panvel police registered a rape case against an unidentified youth after a 14-year-old girl conceived a child. The class IX girl confessed that she did not know the youth who raped her. "Her parents took her to a clinic where the doctor confirmed she was pregnant. Her parents took her to Sion hospital where she aborted the child," said Bajirao Bhosale, senior police inspector.
