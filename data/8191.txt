Haryana
Dec 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Sonipat court on Wednesday sentenced a man to 14 years of rigorous imprisonment for raping his minor stepdaughter. Additional sessions judge Gagandeep Kaur also slapped a fine of Rs 12,000 on the convict, Jagminder Singh.

Mother of the 13-year-old victim had lodged a complaint of rape against Jagminder in March. She had informed the police that Jagminder was her second husband whom she married in 2005. The complainant said she and her daughter from earlier marriage started living with Jagminder.

She further stated that her daughter complained of illness and stomach ache. A medical examination revealed she was pregnant. The girl told her mother her stepfather used to sexually assault her, following which a police complaint was filed.
