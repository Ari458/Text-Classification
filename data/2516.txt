Coimbatore
Mar 16, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 63-year-old priest of a church here was arrested on charges of attempting to outrage the modesty of a woman during prayers.
	
John Mark, the priest of CSI Church, was arrested on a complaint from the 48-year-old woman.
	
The woman, in her complaint lodged with the City
Police Commissioner's office, alleged that the priest made advances towards her and touched her when she was attending prayers on March 10.
