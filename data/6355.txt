Uttar Pradesh
Feb 03, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her cousin in Bansdeeh police station area here, police said today.

On Saturday, the victim 17-year-old cousin lured her to a secluded spot and raped her, Assistant Superintendent of Police K C Goswami said.

The parents were under pressure from the family members not to report the matter to police, but they yesterday got the FIR registered, he added.

The medical examination of the girl was conducted today.

The accused, his father and an uncle were arrested and investigations are on, police said.
