Odisha
Dec 19, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A 32-year old woman was burnt alive, allegedly by her husband, in Pattamundai area of Odisha's Kendrapara district apparently for her failure to bear an offspring.

The incident occurred in Baulanga village and the victim identified as Pramila Dash.

Gopabandhu Dash, husband of the deceased, was arrested  on charges of murder.

The arrest was made after the woman's father, Gopinath Dash lodged an FIR in this connection.
