Andhra Pradesh
Jul 19, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by an auto driver at Chopparametla village in Krishna district on Monday. The girl was sent to the government hospital for medical examination and her condition is stable, said Nuzvid Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) V. Srinivas.

The auto driver picked up the girl while she was waiting at Agiripalli centre on the pretext of dropping at her village. He took the victim into the fields and allegedly committed the crime.

Following a complaint, the Agiripalli police registered a case and are making efforts to trace the auto driver, the DSP said.
