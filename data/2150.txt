Thane
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A youth has been arrested on the charges of raping a four-year-old girl from a slum colony here, police said today.

Rahul Chowdhary, a resident of Nalla Sopara area, and the victim were neighbours, they said.

On Saturday evening, when the girl was playing with her friends in front of her colony, the 20-year-old accused lured her under the pretext of giving a chocolate, Mukund Maharajan, Inspector of the Nalla Sopara Police Station, said.

Chowdhary took her to an isolated place and raped her, he said.
