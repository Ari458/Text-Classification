Haryana
Jun 20, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth at Sunderpur village here, police said today.

The accused identified as Bunty, hailing from the same village, entered victim's house when she was alone today and committed the crime, they said.

A case has been registered in this connection, police said, adding the accused has been arrested.
