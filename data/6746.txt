Jharkhand
Apr 09, 2015
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by three youths at Badgarha village in Palamau distirct, police said.

An FIR, naming the accused in the April 7 incident at night, was lodged today, police said adding the 25-year-old was sent for medical check-up.

Officer-in-charge of Medininagar police station Akhilesh Kumar said police launched a man-hunt to apprehend the absconding culprits identified as Anil Mistry, Dilip Ram and Dhananjay Bhuiyan.

The victim had gone to watch a marriage procession in the village when the accused trio abducted and allegedly raped her.
