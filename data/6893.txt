Punjab
May 15, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			A 23-year old girl was allegedly harassed by a Phagwara-based college student Sumeet Kumar (21) in a private bus at Laddowal in Ludhiana district on Wednesday, police said.

Police have arrested the student on the basis of a complaint filed by the girl, who was on her way to Jalandhar for family work.
