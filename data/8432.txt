Tamil Nadu
Feb 05, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An 8-year-old girl was sexually assaulted by a 17 year-old-boy here in Mathur.

The incident that occurred on January 30 came to light on Wednesday. According to the police, the victim, a Standard Four student, was alone in the house, after her parents had left for work. The accused of the same neighbourhood had lured the girl to his house where he sexually assaulted her. The girl had told her parents of the crime a day later.

The girl’s father assaulted him, following which the accused was admitted to Uthangarai Government hospital. It was only then that the crime of sexual assault came to light.
