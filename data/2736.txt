Rajasthan
Jul 16, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A property dealer was booked for allegedly raping a 25-year-old woman on several occasions in the last nine months on the pretext of marriage and forcing her to undergo a pregnancy termination at Vidhyadhar Nagar.

The accused Anil Tanwar, who had helped the victim selling one of her property last year, gradually developed a relationship with her. He raped the girl on several occasions in the last nine months.

She was even forced to get her pregnancy terminated by the accused.
