Punjab
Jun 23, 2015
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			Harassed by her husband and his family for bringing less dowry, a newlywed woman allegedly committed suicide by consuming poison at her in-laws' house in Nihalewala village in Ferozepur on Sunday night.

In his complaint to the police, victim's father Gurjant Singh, a resident of Sohansadar village of Fazilka district, said his daughter Vandana got married to Mangat Singh in December last year and sufficient dowry was given at the time of wedding. He claimed that Mangat, a drug addict, and his parents allegedly drove his daughter to take the extreme step.

Police have registered a case of abetment to suicide under Section 306 of the IPC against Mangat, his two family members. No arrest had been made so far.
