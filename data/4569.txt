Uttar Pradesh
Apr 15, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman has been allegedly killed by her husband and in-laws over dowry in Bhokaheri village area.

According to a case registered against the accused, the victim identified as Pooja, was strangulated to death by her husband Kapil Kumar and in-laws yesterday over dowry.

Further investigation in going on in the case.
