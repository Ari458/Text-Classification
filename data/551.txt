Delhi
Oct 31, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a BSF constable in Najafgarh area of south-west Delhi. A senior police official said that the victim, Nupur (name changed) was on friendly terms with the accused Anuj for the past eight months. They met each other through a common friend and became close. 
She was called by Anuj on the pretext of a meeting. It was a tent house where Anuj took her along. Nupur has alleged that in the tent house the accused raped her and threatened her of dire consequences. The official added that Anuj went to drop her off at the nearest Metro station from where she called the police.
