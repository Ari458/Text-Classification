New Delhi
Jan 10, 2015
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old woman's body was found from a nursery in Delhi's Vasant Kunj area with police suspecting that she was raped and then killed. 

According to media reports, in the morning the body was spotted by a security guard with hands and legs tied. Her clothes were dishevelled and she bore injury marks in her body, prompting police to suspect that she was gang-raped and then killed. 
The woman had left for work the day before in the morning and then went missing. Her relatives later informed police about her disappearance.
