Tamil Nadu
Dec 23, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year old boy and two others were arrested by Kancheepuram police on charges of raping a 21-year-old woman in Cheyyur. Police said Kamal, Nagaraj and the boy working in a nearby hotel, sexually assaulted a woman in Tandalam on December 11 and she was abandoned near a lake.
