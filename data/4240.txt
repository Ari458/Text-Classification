Maharashtra
Feb 25, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The 23-year-old son of a railway employee has been arrested for allegedly raping a minor girl on Valentine's Day. A case was lodged after the survivor, a Class IX student, told her mother about the incident. 

The girl alleged the accused, Santosh Pawara, who used to live in the same building where she attends her tuition, befriended her recently. On February 14, he took her to the top floor of the building and sexually assaulted her.
