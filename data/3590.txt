Uttar Pradesh
Nov 27, 2013
Molestation, Crime
Description:
			Seven members including six women of a family were attacked allegedly by youths while they resisted their teasing bid in a local bus in the district.

The incident took place when the victims were traveling in a bus from Khatoli to Jansath to attend a wedding, when six youths started passing lewd remarks at them.

On being resisted by the victims, hooligans hit them.

A case has been registered against the six absconding youths, one of them identified as Sushil Kumar.

Later, irate people protested against the incident outside the Khatoli police station.
