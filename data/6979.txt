Haryana
May 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour for the past several months here following which she became pregnant, police said on Saturday .

The victim's father, a resident of sector 6 in Dharuhera, complained that one Mithun, a worker who used to stay in same building as a tenant and hailed from Bihar, frequently sexually exploited her for the past several months, police said. The matter was revealed when the girl complained of pain in abdomen and on examining she was found pregnant, they said.

The accused is on the run, police added.
