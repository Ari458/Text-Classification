Agartala
Jul 15, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 18-year-old girl was raped and murdered at Kalsi, a tribal hamlet of south Tripura district.

The tribal girl went with a youth at about 3:30 AM when the later called her and after few hours the body of the woman was recovered from a dense jungle in the area.

Preliminary investigation revealed that she was murdered after rape.
