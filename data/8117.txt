Bihar
Dec 04, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			One Md Rayeesh of Mahajali under Mahalgaon police station area of Jokihat block in Araria district was arrested on Wednesday for allegedly raping and beating to death his 13-year-old daughter from his first wife on December 1.

The deceased was a Class V student. Rayeesh wanted to bury the body in his courtyard but his mother informed the villagers, who surrounded him and handed him over to the police. An FIR has been lodged against the Rayesh while the victim's body was sent to Araria sadar hospital for postmortem.

Araria SP Sudhir Kumar Porika said the case would be put on speedy trial.

Rayeesh had returned home from Delhi six days back. After the death of his first wife, he had married Bibi Shayeeda, who has demanded strongest punishment for her husband. She, too, was badly assaulted by Rayeesh recently after which she had gone to her parent's house.

On hearing the news about her step- daughter, she returned home on Thursday. The victim's maternal grandfather wants Rayeesh to be hanged.

Mahalgaon PS SHO Akhileshwer Kumar said Rayeesh had confessed his crime.
