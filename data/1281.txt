Jyoti Nagar
Feb 11, 2012
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was forced into a car allegedly by three people, including a minor boy, in Jyoti Nagar and raped by one of them after she snubbed his moves to strike a friendship with her.  The incident came to light after the victim walked in to a police station despite the accused  Gaurav (30), Harish (21) and a minor boy threatening her and her family with dire consequences if they approached police. The victim was on her way to the market when she was abducted allegedly by the men who forced her into a car and took her to Gaurav's room where he allegedly sexually assaulted her.  According to the girl's claims, the other two boys did not assault her.
