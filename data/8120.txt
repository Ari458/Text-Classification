Uttar Pradesh
Dec 07, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman on Sunday received burn injuries when three bike-borne men allegedly threw acid on her over old enmity in Dolatpur village here, the police said.

The victim Pooja was returning to her village when the unidentified accused threw acid and escaped on a motorcycle, they said.

She was rushed to the medical college in Begrajpur here where her condition is stated to be stable, the police said.

Police are suspecting that the three allegedly attacked Pooja as she is fighting a case for her husband who is in jail in connection with a murder.

An investigation is under way in the matter, the police said.
