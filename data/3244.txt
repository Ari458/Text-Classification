Tamil Nadu
Oct 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			In an incident that took place at Vaniyambadi a class IX student was raped by a 60 year old belonging to the same village. The Puthanatham police who registered the case said that the victim along with another friend had been grazing the goats wandering in the fields where 60 year old Mukkan was with his cattle. The victim had come to her grandmother's house for the pooja holidays where the incident took place.

On seeing the two girls, he got himself engaged in a conversation and raped one of them. The victim then returned home and has told her grandmother who filed a complaint.
