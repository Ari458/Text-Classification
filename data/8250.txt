Haryana
Dec 31, 2015
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was allegedly burnt to death by her husband and in-laws for dowry in Ballabhgarh here, the police said on Wednesday.

A U.P. resident Sandeep complained that his sister was on Tuesday killed by her husband Udham Singh and in-laws, residents of Subhash Nagar in Ballabhgarh, who set her afire over dowry demand, they said.

The victim got married to Udham in October 2013 and since then her husband and in-laws were allegedly harassing her demanding dowry, the police said.

The victim and her family raised the matter in Panchayat meetings, but her harassment did not stop, they said.

On the basis of Sandeep's complaint, an FIR was lodged against Udham Singh, his father Shishpal, mother Dharmvati and his sister.
