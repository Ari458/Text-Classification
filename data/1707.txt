Shyampukur
May 21, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A youth was arrested on Sunday for allegedly raping a woman in Shyampukur a month ago. His brother, who had stood guard during the crime, was also picked up.

The woman said in her complaint that Sushovan Kundu had raped while his brother Sudip stood guard in the staircase, said an officer of Shyampukur police station, where the victim filed a complaint on Saturday. The law says that if more than one person is involved in a rape, the accused can be slapped with gangrape charges. So, the brothers have been charged with this section of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).

According to the complaint, the woman was not only raped but also threatened by the brothers. They threatened to kill me if I dared to disclose the incident to my husband (who was away during the crime) or police. I was scared, she said.
