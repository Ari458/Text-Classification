Tamil Nadu
Dec 21, 2014
Murder
Description:
			The wife of a customs employee and their adopted minor daughter were found murdered in the compound of a their house at Velladam Raju Nagar in the district, police said.

The bodies of Vasanthi (53) and Abu Sri (12) were found among bushes in the compound of their house late last night, they said.

Police said the customs employee Subbaiah was missing and suspect he might have been kidnapped.

They said Subbaiah's cell phone was found switched off.

Enquiries with his brother revealed that he had spoken to him.

Subbiah had recently made out a will, apportioning equal share of his property to his adopted daughter and his niece.

Eight teams had been formed to find out the culprits behind the murder, police said.
