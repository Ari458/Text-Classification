Lunglei
Dec 15, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The 14-year-old was raped by a 27-year-old youth on the night of December 10. The accused, Zaithanpuia was arrested later.

Meanwhile, following suspicion, when the girl was asked how many times his father had slept with her, she said ‘four times’. When the girl’s father, a 37-year-old divorcee, was taken to the police station, he immediately confessed to raping his own daughter.
