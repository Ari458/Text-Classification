Punjab
Jan 07, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and raped by three youths in Una district of Himachal Pradesh.

The accused, identified as Gurpreet Singh, Gopi and Soni, are native of Ghal Kalan village.

The victim was allegedly abducted from her village Bahona a few days back and taken to Una in Himachal Pradesh where she was raped.

She, however, managed to escape from the clutches of the accused and reached her village where she told her father about the incident.

The victim's father is a labourer in a brick kiln.

Three accused were booked following the medical examination of the girl which confirmed that she was raped.
