Uttar Pradesh
Apr 07, 2014
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A German journalist staying at a hotel in Agra was assaulted by the hotel's manager. He allegedly tried to rape her while she was taking a bath. When she protested, he made a video clip of her.
Hotel Relax in Baluganj is located just opposite the residence of Deputy Inspector General of Police V.K. Meena

The woman filed a complaint at Rakabganj police station, located barely 200 meters from the hotel and the manager was arrested and booked under Section 353/354 of the IPC.
