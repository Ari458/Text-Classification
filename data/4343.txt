Madhya Pradesh
Mar 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her 22-year-old neighbour in Jehangirabad area.

Moon Bahadur, who lived near the girl's house, lured the victim by offering to buy her a toffee and raped her at an isolated place. 

The accused has been arrested while the girl was admitted to a hospital in a serious condition.
