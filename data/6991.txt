Haryana
May 26, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			40-year-old Surjeet was arrested for allegedly raping his 13-year-old daughter at Nathupur village under DLF II Police Station, police said.

An FIR was lodged on the complaint of the wife of the accused, following which he was arrested, police said, adding, Surjeet was produced before a Judicial Magistrate who sent him to 14 days of judicial custody.
