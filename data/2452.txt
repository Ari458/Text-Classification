Somrei
Jun 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 60-year-old woman was allegedly raped by a 25-year-old youth at Yairipok area in Imphal East district of Manipur.

The youth identified as Md Sanayai allegedly raped the woman while she was working in an isolated paddy field at Somrei village, about 30 km south east.

Soon after the incident, enraged local residents set afire and destroyed the house of the youth.
