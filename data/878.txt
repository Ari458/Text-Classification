DHARAMSALA
Dec 07, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			About 70 girls live in the nursing college hostel of Dronacharya College are living in a state of fear for the last two days as some anti-social elements in the locality.

Incident 1
A person banged his car repeatedly at the hostel gate at night.

Incident 2
A group of 8-10 miscreants had attacked the hostel with hockey sticks and thrown stones at the girls' room, breaking the window panes. Vice-principal of the college was also thrashed by miscreants with hockey sticks, who received serious injuries in the head.
