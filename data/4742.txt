Maharashtra
May 14, 2014
Rape
Description:
			In a sensational incident, the police arrested a 46-year-old man for allegedly raping his two daughters for several years.

The police said that the incident came to light after the wife of the accused filed a complaint with the Parbhani police and the case was transferred to the City Chowk police station in Aurangabad.

The police said that the accused made video clips of his henious crimes and stored them on his computer at home.

Allegedly, the accused also got his daughters aborted twice, fearing social stigma.

Sources said that the accused is an engineer working for a reputed company.
