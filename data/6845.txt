Andhra Pradesh
May 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A youth allegedly sexually assaulted a minor girl in the Machavaram area in the city. The accused, a neighbour of the victim, allegedly asked the girl to come to his house and abused her.

Following a complaint, the police registered a case under the Protection of Children from Sexually Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012, and took up investigation.
