Tamil Nadu
Oct 28, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			Principal District Judge S. Ramathilagam sentenced a 69-year-old man to undergo 22 years of imprisonment for sexually assaulting a five-year-old girl in 2011.

The prosecution case is that the girl was residing with her parents, both labourers, at a village in Tiruchengode. On November 7, 2011, when her parents were away for work, her neighbour Kalimuthu sexually assaulted her. Based on her mother's complaint, the Tiruchengode Rural police registered a case and arrested Kalimuthu. The judge found Kalimuthu guilty and handed over 10 years imprisonment and a fine of Rs. 15,000 for sexual assault, another 10 years for atrocity committed against a girl belonging to SC/ST community and additional two years for threatening her.
