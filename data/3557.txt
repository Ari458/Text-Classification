Kashmir
Nov 20, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A fast track court has sentenced a man to five years of rigorous imprisonment for raping a minor girl.

Additional Sessions Judge Shazia Tabasum announced the sentenced to Mohammad Yasin Bhat, who was convicted on charges of raping the 15-year-old girl after eloping with her in December 1997.

The parents of the girl had lodged an FIR against Bhat in December 1997, claiming that their minor daughter has been enticed by him.

The police immediately swung into action and arrested the accused along with the girl from Jammu and during the course of investigation it was established that the accused had raped the girl.

The police filed the charge sheet against the accused on September 25, 1999.
