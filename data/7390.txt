Karnataka
May 02, 2015
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was found hanging at her house in Mahalakshmi Layout on Friday. The parents of the woman, identified as Nandini, claim she killed herself unable to bear dowry harassment from her husband.

Nandini, a native of Mandya, married Muniyappa about three years ago. The couple were living in Ashokapuram with their one-year-old son. Police said the incident took place late on Thursday night when Muniyappa and the child were asleep. He found the body upon waking up and informed the police on Friday. Muniyappa was arrested following a complaint.
