Bhubaneswar railway station
Nov 30, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The victims are from Muturugadia village in Tihidi police station of Bhadrak district.
A co-passenger of the girls in the Baripada-Bhubaneswar Superfast Express alerted the Police suspecting trafficking. "When we entered the train, two men accompanying them fled the spot raising our doubts further," said the officer.

The victims and their families were told that they were being taken to Madurai in Tamil Nadu for jobs in a factory at a monthly salary of Rs 4,500 each.
