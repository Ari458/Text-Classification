Bihar
Jan 03, 2015
Murder
Description:
			Body of an unidentified woman was today found in Kiul-Gaya passenger train at Nawada Railway station, police said.

"A few persons informed us about the body. The woman seems to have been killed in the train. She was wearing gold ornaments but the criminals did not take them away," Nawada GRP in-charge Inspector Raj Kishore Singh said.

He said the body had several blood stains and an FIR has been registered in this connection.

Police are trying to ascertain her identity.

"We will be able to find out more about her killers once we are able to know her identity and trace her residence," Singh said.
