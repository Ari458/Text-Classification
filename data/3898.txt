Andhra Pradesh
Jan 14, 2014
Rape
Description:
			An intermediate first-year student was allegedly raped by two teenagers (aged around 17) with the assistance of one man at Bonguluru Gate under Vanasthalipuram police station limits on December 21.

Though the incident took place last month, a complaint was lodged in this regard by the victim's parents only recently when she informed them about the incident.
