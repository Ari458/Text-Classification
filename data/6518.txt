West Bengal
Feb 27, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly gang-raped by at least four men in front of her husband at Mourigram station premises in this district, GRP officials said today.

The incident happened when a couple missed the last train to their village in West Midnapore and were sleeping at the station last night, they said.

About four men lifted the woman to a corner and raped her after tying her husband with ropes, the officials said, adding, the woman was admitted to hospital for medical examination.
