Jaitpur
Feb 24, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A married woman Jyoti allegedly ended her life by hanging in her Jaitpur residence this morning. No suicide note was recovered from her possession. The victim was married to Devinder for the past 18 months.

The couple did not have any children. Devinder runs a shop in Jaitpur village and it is not clear what prompted the suicide bid since no suicide note was recovered, a senior police official said.
