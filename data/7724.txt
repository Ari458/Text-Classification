Bihar
Aug 28, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl has accused her father, a policeman, of sexual harassment.

The victim, a resident of Begusarai district, met additional director-general of police (headquarters) Sunil Kumar in Patna on Thursday and demanded the arrest of her father, a personnel with the Bihar Military Police.

In her petition to the ADG (headquarters), the girl alleged that when she narrated her woes to her mother, she suggested her not to report the matter to the police.

ADG Kumar is learnt to have asked the station house officer of Begusarai Women's police station, Neel Mani Kumari, to look into the girl's complaint.
