Gurdaspur
Jan 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The Punjab police on Saturday arrested five men in connection with the alleged gangrape of a 24-year-old woman in Gurdaspur district. According to the police, seven men had raped the woman on Friday night after abducting her from a bus in which they were all travelling.

The police said that the woman, who is married, was on her way from her family home in Pathankot to her husband's house in Gurdaspur and had boarded a minibus. The accused were on the bus. According to sources, the men took the woman to an isolated spot and raped her.
