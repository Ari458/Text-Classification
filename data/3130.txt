Rajasthan
Sep 16, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was allegedly raped by a welder at an engineering college in Kukus.

An FIR was registered on a complaint filed by the victim.

The victim has alleged that while she was sweeping rooms on the second floor of Shankara Engineering College on August 17, welder Ramphool Meena (25) raped her.

She alleged that Ramphool threatened her of dire consequences if she told the incidnet to any one.

The woman has been sent for medical examination and the police are looking for Ramphool, who is absconding after he heard about the FIR.
