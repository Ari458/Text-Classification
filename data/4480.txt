Maharashtra
Mar 27, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Nigdi police arrested a 25-year-old man for allegedly raping a four-year-old girl in Kudalwadi.

The suspect, identified as Mithilesh Harijan of Kudalwadi, hails from Uttar Pradesh. The girl's mother has lodged the complaint with police.

The incident took place on February 13 in Bhairavnathnagar in Kudalwadi.

The girl's parents, who didn't approach police initially due to fear of disrepute, nabbed Harijan after the girl noticed him near her house later.

During interrogation, Harijan admitted to his involvement in the crime.
