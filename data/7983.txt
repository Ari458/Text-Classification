Puducherry
Oct 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Street Harassment
Description:
			A case was registered against two youth under Section 8 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act at the Ariyankuppam police station following a case of sexual harassment.

The victim, aged 14, and her sister were returning home on Sunday evening in Kakkayanthoppu when the accused, Tamizh and Selvam harassed them. The accused pulled the victim's hand, said Inspector Palanivel of Ariyankuppam police station.

The victims parents filed a complaint at the police station. The accused are from Veerampattinam and the police are conducting enquiries, said Mr. Palanivel.

They are yet to be arrested. The victim was produced before the Child Welfare Committee on Wednesday and her statement recorded.
