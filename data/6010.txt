Jharkhand
Dec 18, 2014
Street Harassment, Other
Description:
			Unable to bear the brunt of eve-teasing, a college girl today died after jumping into Damodar in Dhanbad district.

Deputy Superintendent of Police (Sindri) Rama Shankar Singh said that the body was fished out later in the day.

He said the girl, student of a college in Sindri, was reportedly teased by a group of boys acording to prima facie findings.

An FIR has been lodged against seven named accused in connection with the incident, he said.
