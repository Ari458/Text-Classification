Dadar Railway Station
Dec 17, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The victim's family had alighted from a Churchgate fast local at Dadar and was crossing the foot overbridge of platform 4 when Bhoir, who was in civilian clothes, touched the girl from behind. The victim screamed and her father, a sandwich vendor, confronted him. Before Bhoir could flee, the father and some home guards caught him and reported the matter to the railway police at Dadar.
