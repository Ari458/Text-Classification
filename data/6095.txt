Uttar Pradesh
Dec 29, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by four youths at gun point in Daurala area here, police said today.

The victim, who had gone to attend the nature's call in a nearby jungle, was abducted at gun point and taken to a nearby vacant house where the four youths gang raped her on the night of December 25, said Daurala SHO Janak Singh Pundir.

The accused released her the next morning, threatening to kill her entire family if she told about the incident to anyone.

The woman, however, told her family who then complained to the police.

A case was today registered against absconding accused-- Amit, Vikas, Balkar and Ankit, the SHO said, adding that the girl has been sent for medical examination.

Source: Press Trust of India
