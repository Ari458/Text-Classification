Maharashtra
Jul 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old man has been arrested on charge of raping a housewife.

Tarachand N Mukne, a resident of Dombivili, entered the house of the victim, who stays in the same area under the pretext of drinking water.

Realising that the woman was alone in the house, Mukne allegedly sexually assaulted her and fled.
