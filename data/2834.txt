Tripura
Jul 29, 2013
Crime, Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A man strangulated his 18-month-old daughter and threw her into a well at a village because she was a girl child.

The incident took place at Nehalchandranagar village.

Tapan Bhuyan (35) sent his wife and seven-year-old elder daughter to their neighbour's house and went out with the girl child. He strangulated her and threw the body into a well.

After the incident, Bhuyan, a driver by profession, tried to commit suicide by consuming poison.

Meanwhile, the wife and daughter returned and started searching for Bhuyan and the baby. With the help of villagers, they found Bhuyan.

Bhuyan used to beat his wife for giving birth to the second girl child.
