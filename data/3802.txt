Maharashtra
Dec 28, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A 50-year-old tribal woman was beaten to death by her son as she refused to give him money for buying liquor.

The accused, Shantaram Kakdya Wagh, a resident of Kakdyachiwadi village under Jahwar taluka, is absconding after committing the crime.

The 28-year-old, a habitual drinker, would often ask for money from his mother Sakrubai Wagh to buy booze and harass her if she did not oblige.

When she refused to provide him money, enraged, Shantaram kicked her several times and beat her with a stick, leading to her death instantly.

He fled from the house and a search is on for him, a murder case has been registered against Shantaram.
