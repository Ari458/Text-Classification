Nagaland
Sep 04, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A mentally challenged girl was allegedly gangraped by two youths in Khonoma village.

The two youths, identified as Viriezolie Meyase and Kepevilie Savino, hailing from the same village, raped the teenaged girl.

Both the youths were caught by villagers.

A police team had gone to the village to take custody of the culprits.
