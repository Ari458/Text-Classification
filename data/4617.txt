Punjab
Apr 25, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old home guard volunteer of the Chandigarh police, Gagan Kumar, was arrested for thrashing and raping a woman at Sunny Enclave in Kharar on Thursday. Gagan, a resident of Dadumajra village, was attached with the Sector 39 police station. He has been remanded in judicial custody.

The victim was alone at home when he sneaked in and committed the crime around 10pm on Wednesday. Police sources said the accused knew the victim's relatives and thrashed her when she grew suspicious about his behaviour and asked him to leave the house. The victim is married and her medical examination confirmed rape allegations. Inspector Surjeet Singh Dhanoa said a report has been sent to UT police. A case was registered.
