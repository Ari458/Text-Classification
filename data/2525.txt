Andhra Pradesh
Mar 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A homeguard Srinivas was booked on the charge of raping a 20-year-old nurse under Pargi police
station limits of Ranga Reddy district.
	
The alleged incident occurred in a stationary car on
the road side between Pargi and Kalapura.
	
The homeguard was known to the victim for the past few months.
