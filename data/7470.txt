Tamil Nadu
Jul 28, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Alangulam All Women Police have arrested a labourer for sexually assaulting a six-year-old girl.

Police said that Ramar (40) of Muppidathi Amman Temple Street in Ooththumalai near Alangulam abducted the girl while she was playing in front of her house on Sunday night.

He took her to a secluded place and sexually assaulted her.

When the victim screamed for help, the girl’s mother rushed to the spot.

On seeing the woman, Ramar took to his heels.

Based on a complaint from the victim’s mother, the police arrested Ramar on Monday.
