Punjab
Dec 20, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Manjit Kaur had complained to the police that her husband Sukhdev Singh of Machhiwara had been harassing her for dowry.

She alleged that her husband had been manhandling her for bringing less dowry. She said initially her parents kept on satisfying all his financial demands but when she refused to cooperate further, her husband's harassment escalated.

The police said investigation was on and a remand for the accused would be sought in the case.
