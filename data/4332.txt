Chandigarh
Mar 07, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Rape
Description:
			A local court framed charges against five dismissed cops of Chandigarh police for gang-rape and molestation of a minor girl.

The court has framed the charges of committing rape, molestation, illegal confinement and under Section 6 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act against two accused -- Akshay and Sunil. While the three other accused -- Jagtar, Himmat and Anil -- have been charged with molestation, threatening, criminal conspiracy and under Section 12 of POCSO Act.

All the accused were present before the court at the time of framing of charges.

The report from the Central Forensic Science Laboratory is still awaited, for which the investigating agency will file a supplementary chargesheet.
