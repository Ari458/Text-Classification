Tamil Nadu
Jul 07, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Tuticorin Fast Track Mahila Court Judge J. Flora on Monday sentenced three elderly men, accused of sexually assaulting two minor girls, to undergo 10 years of rigorous imprisonment.

According to prosecution, the victims were aged 10 and studied in a child labour special school at Thalamuthunagar here.

The three accused – P. Mookaiya (62), A. Palraj (65) and A. Sakkarai (65) – all residents of Thalamuthunagar, watched the movements of the girls, who studied class V, and picked them to their houses in August 2013 and sexually assaulted them.

Tuticorin All Women Police registered a case following the incident, which came to light when the teacher of the class grew suspicious over the victims who were in possession of money.

The girls revealed that the money was given to them by the accused and narrated the incident.

After the issue was brought to the notice of Child Welfare Committee here, the children were sent for medical examination.

The medical test proved that they were victims of sexual assault.

Hence, the accused were punished under Section 5 (aggravated penetrative sexual assault) of the Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012. The judge also imposed a fine of Rs. 2,000 each on the accused.
