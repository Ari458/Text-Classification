Tamil Nadu
Sep 19, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Fast Track Mahila Court Judge J. Flora sentenced a youth accused of sexually assaulting a minor girl to undergo seven-year rigorous imprisonment on Friday.

The accused was also found guilty of kidnapping the minor girl before committing the crime. According to the prosecution, M. Muthuraj (23) of East Kamarajar Nagar, worker in a salt packaging company, made false promise of marriage to a 16-year-old minor girl of Ilavelangal, a co-worker, kidnapped the victim girl and sexually assaulted her.

The incident occurred on January 1, 2015 and Kadambur police filed a case.

The case was registered under Section 366 of IPC (kidnapping) and Section 4 of Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 (sexual assault).

The accused was sentenced to undergo one year rigorous imprisonment and a fine of Rs.1,000 was imposed for kidnapping besides a sentence of seven-year rigorous imprisonment under the POSCO Act and a fine of Rs.2,000 was also slapped. The Judge ordered the sentences to run concurrently.
