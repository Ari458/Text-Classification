Uttar Pradesh
Feb 20, 2015
Rape
Description:
			An FIR has been registered against a sub-inspector for allegedly trying to rape a woman in Barua Sagar area, police said today.

An FIR has been registered against SI B L Yadav for allegedly entering the house of a woman and trying to rape her on February 18, Superintendent of Police, Kiran S said.

The SI fled from the spot when the woman started shouting and villagers gathered there.
