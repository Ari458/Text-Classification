Jammu
Jul 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped by her cousin in Udhampur district of Jammu and Kashmir.

The 18-year-old was returning home when the cousin, who was accompanying her, took her to a secluded place and sexually assaulted her.

Girl's family members have lodged an FIR in this regard.

Her medical examination is conducted and the report is awaited.
