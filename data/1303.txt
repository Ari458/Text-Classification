Sushant Lok.
Feb 18, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Complainant Hena Dhupad filed a case against husband Vikas Dhupad, who is working with Mahindra Auto in Kirti Nagar, brother-in-law Vijay and mother-in-law Prabha. The three accused are residents of Block C, Sushant Lok.

According to Hena, her husband often physically abused her over petty issues and harassed her for dowry since they got married.
