Haryana
Feb 07, 2015
Abduction
Description:
			A man has been charged for kidnapping a minor girl who had gone missing from a village here, police said today.

The girl,14, was missing since February 4 from Fatehpur Chandela village of the district and the cops were informed yesterday by the family, police said.

In their complaint, the family has alleged that one Ramkishan, a resident of Budana village lured the girl to go with him on the pretext of marriage.

Investigating Officer Jagdish Chand said, efforts are on to trace the girl.
