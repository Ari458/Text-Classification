Bolangir
Nov 25, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			The miscreants were in an inebriated condition forcibly entered into the hut when the 45-year-old victim was sleeping with her five minor children and husband. Her youngest son who was one-and-a-half-years-old was sleeping close to her. The miscreants beat up her husband when he protested.

The family who had migrated here from neighbouring chattisgarh jointly prevented the rape but during the scuffle the baby suffered injuries and died.
