Karnataka
Sep 04, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			A man was beaten by the residents of Shravanur in Holenarsipur taluk on Thursday for allegedly misbehaving with a woman. Later, the accused was handed over to the Holenarsipur police. The police gave the name of the accused as Praveena of Arehalli in Holenarsipur taluk.

According to the police, Mr. Praveen had passed a lewd remark against a woman in the village. Later, her husband Manju and others assaulted him and took him to the police. They have filed a complaint with the Holenarsipur Rural police.
