Bihar
Jan 31, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped at Kawalsia village under Falka police station in Katihar district.

Falka SHO Satyanarayan Rai said a 35-year-old man Bihari Mahto of the same village was named an accused in the case.

The girl had gone out to the agricultural field on the outskirts of the village, when the accused raped her.

"Villagers rushed to the place after hearing her screams, but Mahto escaped from the spot," Rai added.

The SHO said that an FIR has been registered and the girl has been sent to government hospital for medical examination. Raids are on to nab Mahto.
