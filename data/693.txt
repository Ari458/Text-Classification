Ambala Cantonment
Nov 18, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			According to the information, a Chandigarh-based couple had visited Jugdish (tantrik) for the treatment of their daughter at Sarsehri in Mahesh Nagar area of Ambala Cantt. Their 15-year-old girl was in depression since last few days and was not able to sleep. 

The tantrik started checking the girl and sent her parents to market to bring seven packets of sweet. When they came back, the girl was crying and told her parents about tantrik’s bad intentions. The mother said that her daughter was very upset and disclosed that the tantrik tried to undress her and kiss her. 

Source: The Pioneer 19 November 2011
