Jaipur
Apr 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A middle aged woman was allegedly gangraped by three persons in Kanota area of the district, police said.

The incident took place yesterday when she was going to Jagatpura area to meet her son. Two men identified as Ramchandra and Bhimaram, who were known to her, met her and offered a lift on their motorcycle. The accused then took her to an isolated area where a third unidentified man joined them and they took turns to rape her, the police said today.

The woman, aged around 40 years, hails from Dausa district, they said.
