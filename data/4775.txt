Nashik
May 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 55-year-old man who has been absconding for more than a year after allegedly raping his minor daughter, was arrested.

The accused Rajendra Shivprasad Kharat, a resident of Phule Nagar locality, was arrested yesterday from his sister's house in Devlali camp area here by team of Panchavati police station officials.

Kharat was on the run for last one-and-a-half year after his wife lodged a police complaint of alleged rape of their daughter.
