Andhra Pradesh
Jul 01, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The city police took a 24-year-old man into custody here for sexually assaulting a 16-year-old girl.
The accused lured the orphaned girl, took her to various places and sexually assaulted her before leaving her in the lurch, Ongole Deputy Superintendent of Police G. Srinivasa Rao said.
Childline India Foundation NGO partner HELP rescued the girl from Srigiri hill late on Monday night.
Case booked
After booking a case against the man, Shyam (24), who works at a petrol pump here under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and IPC Section 363 (kidnapping), I Town police launched a probe.
Circle Inspector K.V. Subashini said the girl was sent to the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Medical Sciences for medical examination.
