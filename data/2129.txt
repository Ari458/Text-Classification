Rajasthan
May 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman accused her uncle of rape. The woman filed a complaint in a local court seeking direction to police to lodge the case against her uncle Kamal Kumar and the FIR was registered with Gandhi Nagar police station yesterday.

"She alleged that her uncle raped her in 2010 and filmed it. She said that he her uncle was blackmailing her on the basis of the video clip," investigating officer said.
