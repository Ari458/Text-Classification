Maharashtra
Aug 07, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by some persons in suburban Mankhurd today after she turned down marriage proposal of one of the accused, police said.

The girl, employed as domestic help, lives with her younger brother in a suburban shanty, the Bandra police said.

The victim had spurned marriage proposal of one of the accused. Today, at about 1 am, five persons abducted her in a vehicle and took her to a slum in Mankhurd, they said.

Three of the five accused raped the girl taking turns, police said, adding the accused threatened to kill her if she revealed the incident to police.

The victim somehow managed to escape and reached Bandra police station around 10 am.

"A gangrape case has been registered and a manhunt has been launched to nab the accused. The victim has undergone a medical test. She is in a stable condition," senior police inspector Ramchandra Dhawale said.
