New Delhi
Mar 11, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Four juveniles have been apprehended for allegedly molesting and attempting to rape a 13-year-old girl at her residence in central Delhi's Patel Nagar area.

The incident took place when the girl, a student of Std VII, was alone at her home.

The four accused, who live in same neighbourhood as the victim, went to her residence on the pretext of taking a textbook from her. However, once inside, they molested her.

The victim said in her complaint that they even gagged her and attempted to rape her. She, however, fought off the four, who then fled.

Based on the girl's complaint, a case has been registered against the four juveniles.

Source: Press Trust of India
