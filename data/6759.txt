Kerala
Apr 19, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			The police on Saturday arrested a 43-year-old man on the charge of raping and sexually harassing a minor for the past two years.

The police said the Upputhara Chappathu resident had been sexually exploiting his 14-year-old relative for long. He married her mother six years back after the death of her father. The lady was in the Gulf leaving the victim and her brother with the accused. The police on getting a tip-off arrested the accused from Pottankad, near Rajakkad.
