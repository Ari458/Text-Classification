Haryana
Mar 12, 2014
Abduction
Description:
			Two youths were arrested for allegedly kidnapping a 20-year-old girl from near her college in Guraora village.

Sandeep of Jhajjar district and his accomplice Manish Sharma of Gurgaon district, had allegedly abducted the girl, a student of BA first year, on March 6.

The girl, a resident of the Pataudi area of Gurgaon district, has been restored to her parents.

Her statement was recorded in a court. She refused to go in for a medical examination while stating that no wrong had been done to her by the duo.

Earlier, the police had registered a case of abduction against the youths on a complaint filed by the girl's father.
