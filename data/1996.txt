pandesar
Apr 15, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly
raped in Pandesar area of Surat city, police said today.
	A complaint has been registered against - Sonukumar Shah(21) - by father of the victim, Pandesar police sub-inspector A B Mori said.
	The victim, whose father is a vegetable vendor, and the accused's niece were playmates and used to visit his house in Aavirbhav colony of Pandesar area, police said.
	Last evening, when the victim went to play, the accused, who had lost his job 15 days back, confined her in a room and allegedly raped her.
	The offence came to light when the victim's mother came there to find her, police added.
