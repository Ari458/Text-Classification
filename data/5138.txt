Karnataka
Jul 18, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Malur police arrested Nagaraju, 25, resident of Kumbarapet in Malur who allegedly raped a 21-year-old mentally challenged girl on Thursday afternoon. Sub-inspector R Dayanand told TOI that Nagaraju, a mason, was arrested within hours of the incident and produced before the magistrate.

The sub-inspector said Nagaraju allegedly entered the girl's house when she was alone and raped her. When she raised an alarm, he fled. Dayanand said the girl was living with her father and grandmother.
