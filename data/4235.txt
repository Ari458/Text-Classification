Madhya Pradesh
Feb 24, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Murder
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped and strangled near Bhanpur. Police, however, are waiting for post mortem report to confirm rape. The half-naked body was found near a ground adjoining People's Medical College.

The girl, a resident of Sukhi Sewania, was missing from her house. Her father had lodged a missing person report. The minor was a rag picker and had left home with her two sisters. The police called her family to identify the body after it was recovered.

Senior officials and forensic team members also visited the spot.

The girl's father, a labourer, has two sons and four daughters. The family hails from Chhindhwara.

A case was later registered at Nishatpura police station.
