Paravoor
Dec 02, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Human Trafficking, Rape
Description:
			The case came to light in June last after the girl from Paravoor complained to her aunt about her plight and she lodged a police complaint. In her complaint, the girl had said her father, who used to do small roles in Malayalam films, was the first to rape her. He then took her to various places and 'offered' her to at least 100 men.
