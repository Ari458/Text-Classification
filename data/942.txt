Ramanathapuram
Dec 17, 2011
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			N Sivadass working as a design engineer in a private firm in Kuwait, married S Sathyapriya (30) in 2004. Victim claimed that her mother-in-law N Kanniga, 55, started harassing her, demanding Rs 5 lakh as dowry. She alleges her husband of inflicting mental trauma by threatening to divorce if her family failed to give him the money.

Police said that the victim's parents had already given 40 sovereigns of gold jewels and Rs 50,000 as dowry. and they had spent Rs 2 lakh for conducting the marriage.
