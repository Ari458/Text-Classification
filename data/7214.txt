Haryana
Jun 22, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was killed allegedly by her in-laws over dowry at Mewla Maharajpur area here, police said today.

The victim identified as Asha was being harassed by her in-laws over dowry and they forced her to drink acid, they said.

The victim's family members alleged that Asha's in-laws demanded Rs ten lakh as dowry.

A case has been registered against eight people including the victim's husband under relevant sections of the IPC, Investigating officer Shermohammad said, adding, efforts were on to nab them.
