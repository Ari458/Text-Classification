Maharastra
Dec 20, 2011
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Chargesheets have been filed against the five doctors, who were issued show cause notices under the PCPNDT Act by the MMC. When the doctors did not provide satisfactory explanations, they were removed from the MMC registry and debarred from practising in the state a decision that can only be revoked only after the court's final verdict, said Dr Shivkumar Utture, an MMC member.
