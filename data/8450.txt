Haryana
Feb 07, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Sonipat police on Saturday arrested a person for allegedly sexually abusing a 16-year-old girl for 24 days. Identified as Om Parkash alias Kala, the accused was arrested from the outskirts of Sonipat district. A case of rape and abduction under sections 363, 366 and 376 of the IPC and various sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act has been registered against him at Gannaur police station. He was produced before the court that remanded him to judicial custody.

As per the police, the victim had gone missing from outside her house on January 13. She had returned home on Friday evening and narrated the sequence of events leading to her kidnapping and rape by the accused. Police said the accused had confessed to his involvement and had identified the places where he had kept the girl.
