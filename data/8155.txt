Tamil Nadu
Dec 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Seventeen-year-old girl was repeatedly raped over the pretext of marriage. The accused took her to Chennai with false promises. When the accused denied to marry her, she lodged a complaint against him with Tilaknagar police. The accused is absconding.

The accused has been identified as Rafik Akbar Shiakh, 25, a Dombivali resident. A case has been registered with the Tilaknagar police station under sections 376 (punishment for rape), 363 (punishment for kidnapping), 366 (kidnapping, abducting or inducing woman to compel her marriage, etc) of the Indian Penal Code and The Prevention of Children from Sexual offences Act, 2012 (POCSO).

A police official from the Tilaknagar police station said, "We have registered the case. We are on a lookout for the accused and are hopeful of his arrest soon. Further probe is on."

The accused is 25 years old and the victim is minor.
