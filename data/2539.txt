Muzaffarnagar
Jul 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped at gunpoint by a youth at her house in Kitas village of the district.

According to police, Sudhir along with two of his associates -- Dharmender and Chotu -- barged into the victim's house when she was alone at home.

Sudhir raped her while his two accomplices guarded the door.

They had also allegedly threatened the girl to withdraw her case.
