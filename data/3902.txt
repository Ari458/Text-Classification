Jharkhand
Jan 14, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Class X girl student has been allegedly gang raped in Ranchi city.

The 16-year-old girl, who had left home to attend a coaching class, went to a temple situated at Ratu road with a person. Suddenly eight men, aged around 24-25 years, surfaced and forcibly lifted her to a nearby place where they committed the crime.

The girl also alleged that the accused had made MMS clips.
