Assam
Sep 09, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Police today arrested a 53-year-old grade IV employee of Gauhati Medical College and Hospital (GMCH) for allegedly molesting an 11-year-old girl in the Birubari area of the city.

The police said the accused, Jadav Chandra Thakuria, lured the girl to his house last Saturday and committed the crime.

"Thakuria, called her to his house on Saturday night and tried to molest her. She, however, managed to escape. The accused also threatened the victim because of which she didn't reveal the incident to anybody until today," a police source said.

"This morning when Thakuria went to the victim's house again and invited her to come over to his house, the girl complained to her parents," the source said.

After being alerted by the girl's parents, the residents of the area caught Thakuria from his house and handed him over to the police.

A case (802/15) has been registered against the accused under Section 8 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.

Thakuria was produced in court this afternoon and remanded in judicial custody for 14 days.

The police are currently looking into the antecedents of Thakuria, who has been living in the city for the last 35 years, to find if he has committed any such crime earlier.
