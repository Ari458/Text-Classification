Phugana Area
Dec 28, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by four youths in Phugana area in the district, police said today.

The 14-year-old victim was kidnapped by the youths from her residence in the absence of her parents, who had gone outside for some work, they said.

Later, the accused brought the girl to panchayat office, where she was raped by them.
