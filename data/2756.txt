Andhra Pradesh
Jul 18, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old female construction worker was allegedly gangraped in Medak district of Andhra Pradesh.

A 20-year-old married woman resident of Posanpet village of Tekmal mandal was allegedly gangraped three days ago.

According to the police, the victim was divorcee staying with her parents at her native village of Posanpet.

On July 16 her co-worker, Srinivas, took her to Sadasivapet in Medak district near a chemical factory and raped her along with his two accomplices Sandeep and Ashok.

Her parents took her to the government hospital at Jogipet and after a check-up, the doctors informed the police.

Since she was in a state of shock, the Jogipet police could take her statement and complaint only later.

A case has been registered against Srinivas, Sandeep and Ashok for gangrape, under Section 376 of the Indian Penal Code.
