Tamil Nadu
May 11, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 19-year-old youth was arrested and lodged in the Borstal School at Pollachi on the charge of attempting to rape a four-year-old neighbour at Velandipalayam on Saturday.

The All-Woman Police (Central) of the Coimbatore City Police said that the girl was staying in her aunt’s house and was playing near the house when accused M. Arunkumar (19) took her with him with a promise of getting some fruits.

He tried to assault her amidst some bushes near her house. A neighbour noticing this raised an alarm and Arunkumar fled the place.
