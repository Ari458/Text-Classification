Uttar Pradesh
May 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Four youths allegedly gangraped a minor girl when she was going to relieve herself in a jungle near a village.

The girl has been sent for medical examination.

Locals have alleged that efforts are being made to hush up the matter.

They have claimed that the victim's relatives are being forced to withdraw the gangrape case and lodge a complaint of rape only against one accused.
