Uttar Pradesh
Apr 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married man has been arrested for allegedly trying to rape two young girls in Loni area of the
district.
  Narendra, a resident of Ankur Vihar Colony in Loni, was arrested for allegedly trying to rape two girls, aged 10
and 11 years.

The victims were playing outside when Narendra called them to his house on the pretext of making them watch TV and allegedly tried to rape them.

 The girls started crying. Hearing their cries, locals rescued them and nabbed Narendra.
His wife is pregnant and she and their children are living with her parents these days.
	
Narendra was alone at home at the time of the incident.
