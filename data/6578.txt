Odisha
Mar 08, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A class IX student was allegedly gangraped by three persons in a moving car after they kidnapped her from Chhamunda village in Odisha's Ganjam district, police said today.

Though the incident took place on February 18, the victim lodged an FIR on Friday, police said.

"The 14-year-old girl was sent for medical test yesterday," said Sub-Divisional Police Officer of Bhanjanagar R C Sethi.

The accused have been identified, however, no arrest has been made so far and efforts are on to trace them, police said.
