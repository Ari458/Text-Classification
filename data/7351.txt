Jharkhand
Apr 25, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by his father in Nildih area here, police said on Saturday.

D Janardhan Rao was arrested on his wife's complaint alleging the incident occurred when she was not at home and the girl narrated to her about Rao's act yesterday, police officials said.

The complainant further said her husband had also committed similar offence with her daughter earlier, but she did not had the courage to lodge a complaint against Rao in view of social stigma.

Following the complaint, police arrested Rao yesterday itself.
