Uttar Pradesh
Mar 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			A minor has lodged a case at Dankaur police station against one Monu alleging that while she was going to market, he passed some obscene remarks. When she objected then Monu assaulted her.

The minor, in her FIR, said that she suffered injuries during the incident and later was freed from his clutches by some villagers who admitted her at a local clinic.

The man was in inebriated condition, police said, adding that the accused was arrested today.
