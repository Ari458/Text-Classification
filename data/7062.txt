Kerala
May 30, 2015
Rape
Description:
			The Chirayinkeezhu police have arrested a 22-year-old man for allegedly sexually abusing a mentally challenged woman.

The accused has been identified as Aneesh, a resident of Thunduvila. According to the police, the arrest was recorded following a complaint lodged by the mother of the 24-year-old victim.

The victim was under the keen observation of the accused. When her mother left for buying vegetables, the accused allegedly entered her house and sexually abused her. She told the matter to her mother, who informed the police, police said.
