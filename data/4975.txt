Madhya Pradesh
Jun 22, 2014
Molestation, Murder
Description:
			A woman was axed to death on resisting rape attempt in front of her 12-year old daughter in Shivpuri district on Friday night. Incident took place at Kairau village.

Kin of the victim, including her husband, father in law and son had gone to attend a marriage ceremony in nearby Chhitri village leaving behind deceased Komal Bai and her daughter Anjali. Both mother and daughter were sleeping together when accused barged into their house and tried to rape the deceased. Hearing her mother's cries, the girl got up. Seeing this, accused, who was carrying an axe, attacked Komal Bai leading to her death, police sources said.
