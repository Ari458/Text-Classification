Uttar Pradesh
Feb 16, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl has allegedly been abducted and raped by a youth in a religious area in Muzaffarnagar district.

The incident took place when the victim had gone to shop at the religious place Shukertal yesterday.

The girl after regaining her consciousness, found herself in a house and in her complaint said, 22-year-old Ankur raped her there after forcibly taking her away with him last evening.

Source: Press Trust of India
