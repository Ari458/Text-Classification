Bihar
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An eleven-year-old girl was raped by a man in Naurumal village in Bihar's Jehanabad district last night, a senior police official said.

The victim had gone to a field near her house for attending nature's call when Akhilesh Yadav, hailing from a neighbouring village, overpowered her before committing the crime, Deputy Superintendent of Police Bachcha Singh said.
