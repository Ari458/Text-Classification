Tamil Nadu
Jul 23, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police on Thursday arrested three minor boys for sexually abusing a six-year-old girl at Kalunguvilai in the Tuticorin district of Tamil Nadu. 

The Sattankulam police arrested a 17-year-old boy studying at a polytechnic college, a Class 11 student and a Class 7 boy in connection with the incident. 

The police said the boys took the Class 1 girls to a farm belonging to one of their families on Wednesday and sexually abused her. 

The girl sustained injuries in her private parts. Her parents came to know about the incident when she returned home, and she was hospitalized. 

Based on a complaint filed by the girl's father, the police picked up the boys on Wednesday night and formally arrested them on Thursday. The boys confessed to committing the crime. 

The police registered case under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
