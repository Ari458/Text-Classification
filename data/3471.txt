Andhra Pradesh
Nov 05, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Three army personnel were arrested for allegedly attempting to rape a teenage girl at the Mahindra Hills area in Hyderabad.

Hawaldar Lok Bahadur Chatri and sepoys Tapas M and Solan N posted at the Army Ordnance Corps (AOC) Centre at Secunderabad were arrested after a 17-year-old girl accused them of misbehaving with her after taking her to a secluded place in the military area.

Before that the men had allegedly snatched her two-wheeler and cellphone.

In her complaint, the girl also alleged that the men beat up her boyfriend accompanying her. The boyfriend then told the matter to locals who rushed to the spot and caught hold of the three army personnel.
