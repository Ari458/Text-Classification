Haryana
May 27, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A local court on Tuesday sentenced a 30-year-old man to seven-year jail term for raping a 15-year-old girl. Shakti, who hailed from Tamil Nadu and was residing in Tamil Colony, Sector 21, Panchkula, was booked under charges of rape on September 10, 2014. According to police, Shakti, who was married, lured the girl into his house when she was playing outside and raped her.
