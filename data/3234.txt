Tamil Nadu
Oct 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A local court awarded 11 years rigorous imprisonment to 28-year-old man for raping a girl in 2010.

According to prosecution, Munusamy was in love with the gril working in a private spinning mill at Gobichettipalayam.

When she became pregnant from her affairs from the man, she asked him to marry her in 2010, which he refused, following which she gave a complaint with the All Women Police.

Additional Sessions Judge Ramamurthy awarded seven years RI to Munusamy under various sections of IPC including for rape besides imposing a fine of Rs 25,000 on him.
