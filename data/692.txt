Dehradun
Nov 18, 2011
Crime
Description:
			Victim Shakuntala Devi has been in confrontation with her in-laws over family dispute for long time. An altercation between victim and her father-in-law Bhagawat Prasad, mother-in law Sama Devi and sister-in-law, Bhampi Devi took place on Thursday evening over an issue which immediately took an ugly turn and said accused had beat her mercilessly with baton.

She had sustained serious injuries after the incident and was taken to Doon hospital by an ambulance. and later succumbed to her injuries .

A case was registered against the said accused by brother of victim Shalikram Prasad, a resident of Kirti Nagar Tehri under Section 304 of Indian Penal Code.
