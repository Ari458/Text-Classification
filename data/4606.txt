Haryana
Apr 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly strangulated to death after being raped by unidentified persons at Chelawas village.

Family members of the victim, who had gone to the fields, returned home in afternoon yesterday and found her lying dead while her hands and feet were tied.

The body also bore strangulation marks. The family has alleged that she was raped before being killed, police said adding the body was sent for post mortem.

Source: Press Trust of India
