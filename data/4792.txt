New Delhi
May 27, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old Class IX student was allegedly gang-raped by her neighbour and two others who abducted and took her to a house in the national Capital and committed the crime.

According to the complaint, the accused, identified as Guddu, his maternal uncle Rajan and Lalit kidnapped the girl when she was returning to her home in Ghaziabad.

On Saturday, they were arrested and booked under Sections 363/366 (abduction), 386 (rape), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC and under the provisions of the Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The incident took place when the 14-year-old was returning home. The accused stopped and kidnapped her in their car. They took her to a house in Delhi where they took turns to rape her.

The girl reached her home and informed her family who in turn approached the police. When police raided the crime scene, they recovered beer bottles and the car used during the crime, police official said. "We have recorded the girl's statements and sent her for a medical examination," a police officer said.
