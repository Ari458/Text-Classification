New Delhi
Jul 15, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was raped allegedly by her neighbour in North Delhi's Keshavpuram area.

The neighbour, Umashankar, was arrested.

According to the police, Umashankar sexually assaulted the girl when she was alone at her residence.

Umashankar was arrested after the victim's medical report confirmed rape.

Source: Press Trust Of India
