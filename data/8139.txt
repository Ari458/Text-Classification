Kerala
Dec 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Ernakualm Rural police on Saturday arrested a 22-year-old on charges of sexually exploiting a minor girl after offering to marry her.

The accused has been identified as Suhail, a native of Edathala. Officials said the accused had taken the girl to a lodge in Munnar and raped her couple of times.

The victim had been in a relationship with the accused and he took the girl from her home near Nedumbassery to Munnar on a motorcycle on the night of December 2,officials said.

Investigations revealed that he was wanted in a couple of other rape cases as well. The accused was produced at a local court and remanded in custody.
