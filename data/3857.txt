Andhra Pradesh
Jan 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A man allegedly raped a 45-year-old woman with the help of his wife in the neighbouring Medak district.
The alleged incident took place last night.

M Narasimhalu allegedly raped the woman with the help of his wife Sushila at the woman's house in Chinnakanjrla village of Patancheru mandal of Medak.

The victim owed Rs 30,000 to the couple for the last six years.

The woman had been sent for medical examination.
