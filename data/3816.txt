Madhya Pradesh
Jan 02, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A complaint was registered against two youths for allegedly attempting to molest a girl inside a car on the New Year's eve.

Two youths Santosh Jaiswal and Nitin Dwivedi offered lift to a 22-year-old girl at the Mhow naka area when she was waiting for a bus for Pithampur.

The girl got into the car as they were known to her, but soon they started making indecent advances.

As the girl started screaming, the duo dropped her and fled, after threatening her with dire consequences.
