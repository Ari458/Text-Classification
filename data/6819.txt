Bangalore
May 01, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Upset over domestic problems, 23-year-old woman committed suicide in Mahalakshmi Layout of Bengaluru on Thursday.

The deceased, identified as Nandini, was a resident of 7th Main and married for the past three years to Muniyappa, a fabricator. The couple had a two-year-old son.

According to the police, Nandini committed suicide by hanging herself once Muniyappa left for work. In a complaint, Nandini's parents accused Muniyappa of harassing her for dowry which lead her to take the extreme step.

The Mahalakshmi Layout police have detained Muniyappa for questioning.
