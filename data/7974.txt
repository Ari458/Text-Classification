Uttar Pradesh
Oct 04, 2015
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman was allegedly killed by her husband and in-laws over dowry in Barnahal area here, police said today    

Rinki, who got married recently, was killed yesterday by her in-laws. Subsequently, they fled from the scene, police said.  

Based on complaint of victim's brother Neeraj Kumar, an FIR has been lodged against husband Yatindra and two others, including victim's mother-in-law, they said.

The body has been sent for post mortem.
