Odisha
Nov 22, 2013
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A girl student of a private college at Pattmundai in Odisha's Kendrapara district was allegedly molested by the managing director of the institution.

A case has been registered after the girl lodged an FIR alleging that the accused had outraged her modesty in the college premises on November 16.

The accused is absconding. The matter is being investigated.
