Gujarat
Nov 14, 2016
Domestic Violence, Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old woman lodged a complaint of rape and physical and mental torture against her husband at the women's police station, on Saturday. According to the complaint, she came into contact with the accused, Chirag Padhiyar, a resident of Vastral, in August 2015.

"After they fell in love, Chirag took the girl to different guesthouses and also to his residence, where he forced the girl into a physical relationship," said a woman police official. "Tired of the harassment, the girl approached us on Saturday afternoon and lodged a complaint against Chirag," said a senior woman police official.
