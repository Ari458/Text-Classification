Haryana
Jan 28, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old resident of Sector-22, Noida was raped by Haryana police constable Ramlakhan, police said.

As per the complaint lodged by the woman, the constable and the woman were residing together in a room in Ballabhgarh, following which they got married in a temple.

Later, the victim became pregnant and was asked by the accused to abort the child, police said.

He also told her that he was marrying another girl and even threatened to kill her, the complaint alleged.

Police have registered a case in this connection and further investigations are on, they said.
