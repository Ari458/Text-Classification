Haryana
Mar 12, 2014
Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			A person has been arrested for allegedly molesting a married woman.

The incident took place when the victim was on her way to pick up her son up from his school.

The accused grabbed her hands from behind.

The accused ran away when the woman raised an alarm, but was later nabbed by the victim's husband, who handed him over to police.

The man identified himself as Birpal and is a resident of Bharat colony.
