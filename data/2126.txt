Uttar Pradesh
May 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly gang-raped and murdered by some unidentified men in Pitambarpura village in the district, police said today.

Soni (17), a student of class IX, was sleeping on the terrace with her family members last night when she was abducted by the miscreants, who took her to a field outside the village and gang-raped her, they said.

Later, the accused hanged her to death using her clothes and disposed off the body in a village well where it was spotted by the villagers this morning.
