Uttar Pradesh
Mar 15, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped by two men after being confined in a house here, police said today.

An FIR in this regard was registered on court order yesterday, they said.

The victim, who hails from Merapur area in Farrukabad district, had come here on February 22. She was waiting for a bus when Suresh of Chachaina village and his two accomplices in a vehicle approached her.

They offered to drop her to the desired destination. The woman agreed and went with them.

But the men, took her to a house where two of them raped her, police said.

The victim approached the court after police allegedly did not register her complaint.
