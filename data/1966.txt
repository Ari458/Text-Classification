Jammu
Apr 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly raped by a youth in border belt of Akhnoor in Jammu district, police today said here.

The woman has lodged a complaint at Khour Police Station that a youth identified as Sanjay Kumar of Narayana-Pandwal village entered into her house when she was alone and raped her yesterday, police said.
