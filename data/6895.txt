Uttar Pradesh
May 15, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			Three women were injured while jumping off a moving vehicle here after the driver allegedly misbehaved with them, police said today.

Praveen, Sonia and Moni sustained injuries after they resisted the eve-teasing by the driver and jumped off the moving vehicle in Baghra village here yesterday, they said.

The women were offered a lift by the accused. The driver started misbehaving with the women and refused to stop the vehicle despite repeated pleas by the victims, police said.

The accused has been arrested, they said.The injured have been admitted to the hospital, police added.
