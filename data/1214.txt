Mumbai
Jan 25, 2012
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The girl (7), a resident of Baiganwadi in Shivaji Nagar, had accompanied her grandmother to fetch water, when the unidentified person abducted her on January 22 at around 10 pm. Yesterday at around 6.30 am, the girl was seen walking in to the area crying and her clothes were torn and stained, in a state of shock and unable to communicate.

A medical test has confirmed that the girl was raped.
