Tamil Nadu
Jan 22, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 22-year old woman, whose wedding has been fixed, was today stabbed to death by her cousin after she rejected his proposal to marry him on the ground that marriages of first cousins are not correct.

Saranya, a school teacher, was attacked by 28-year old Shiva, son of her father's younger brother, with a knife in the neck and stomach in the presence of her mother and sister at her house in Kondanayakanpatti near Andipatti.

She was rushed to a hospital but died without responding to treatment.

Shiva, who fled the scene, was later arrested, police said.

She had explained to him that since he was her brother, by relation, it was not possible to marry him, but he entered into an argument with her and then suddenly stabbed her, police said.
