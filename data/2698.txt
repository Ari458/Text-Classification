Andhra Pradesh
Jul 10, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			An unidentified woman was found murdered on a roadside in Nalgonda district of the state, with the police suspecting she was raped before being killed.

The woman's body was found on the outskirts of Balemla village of the district.

Liquor bottles were found close to her body and it is suspected that unidentified people might have smothered her to death after raping her.

The body has been sent for post-mortem.
