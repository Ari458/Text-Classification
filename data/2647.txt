Uttar Pradesh
Mar 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly gangraped by three people in Urwan village, triggering protests by locals who blocked traffic and torched the nearby Sakaldiha Tehsil office.
	
Agitated over the incident, locals set afire the Sakaldiha Tehsil office and blocked traffic movement on Sakaldiha-Chandauli road.
	
The situation was brought under control by the police and the blaze doused by the fire brigade team.
	
Adequate force has been deployed in the area to prevent any untoward incident.

Source: Press Trust Of India
