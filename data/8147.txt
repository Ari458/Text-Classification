Madhya Pradesh
Dec 12, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Mahila police registered cases under Dowry Act against a man and his parents, on Thursday.

According to Jyoti Sharma, TI of Mahila thana, cases are registered against Deepak Mandloi, his father Ramcharan and mother Santoshibai on the complaint from Reena (19), a resident of Limboda, under the Dowry Act and IPC.

In her complaint, Reena stated that her husband Deepak and in-laws harassed her demanding Rs 2 lakh in dowry. She was beaten and mentally tortured by them and sent to her parents. Further investigation in the matter is on.
