New Delhi
Sep 16, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by an unidentified person in Naraina area of South West Delhi.

According to police, the victim is in critical condition and recuperating at Deen Dayal Upadhyay Hospital. Her family alleged that when the girl was playing outside, an unidentified person took her to an isolated place on the pretext of giving her some eatable and sexually assaulted her.

An FIR has been filed on the basis of the allegation made by family members of the victim.

The victim's family said the child returned home crying, while her clothes were torn.

Source: Press Trust Of India
