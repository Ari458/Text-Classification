Tamil Nadu
Dec 25, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A teenaged boy allegedly raped a six-year-old girl and seriously injured her following which he was thrashed by the public.

The 17-year old boy of R N Kottai suffered injuries in the assault by the public and had been admitted to the Government hospital.

The girl, daughter of a mason, has also been hospitalised and the condition of both was stated to be serious.

The boy had taken the girl, his neighbour, to a secluded place by luring her with a chocolate and sexually assaulted her.

As the girl started bleeding, her parents rushed her to a hospital where she told doctors about the incident.

As the news spread, people of locality beat up the boy, till he fainted. 

Source: Press Trust of India
