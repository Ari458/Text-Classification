Uttar Pradesh
Aug 24, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			In yet another incident of sexual offence against a minor, a seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a neighbour in Fatehpur district on Saturday.
 
Following the complaint, a case of rape under relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act was registered against 21-year-old accused Ram Babu, who was later arrested, the police said.
 
The alleged incident took place on Saturday afternoon under the limits of Asothar police station of the district, when the victim was playing outside her home.
 
"As per the victim's family, the accused lured the girl over some pretext and took her to his home. Once inside his residence, he gagged and raped her," said a police official.
 
The incident came to light after the girl returned home and narrated her ordeal to her parents.
 
The girl's father later apprised the Asothar police station. The cops sent the girl for medical examination.
 
She was also provided medical aid there. "A case of rape has been registered with Asothar police station under relevant sections of law. The accused has been arrested and sent to jail," said station in-charge Pradeep Kumar.
