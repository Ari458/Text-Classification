Uttar Pradesh
Nov 29, 2013
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			An Assistant General Manager of a nationalised bank has been accused of sexually harassing a woman manager.

The victim, manager at the bank's Chakeri branch, filed a complaint at Collectorganj police station, alleging that on November 8 the AGM called her in his chamber at the bank's office at Birhana road and scolded her in front of a customer.

After the customer left the chamber, AGM Lakshmikanth Chandana tried to molest her and when she resisted, he threatened her, the woman alleged in the complaint.

When contacted, the AGM rubbished the allegations made by the woman manager and said he is ready to face any investigation into the matter.
