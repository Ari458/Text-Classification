Madhya Pradesh
Jun 05, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly gangraped by four youths near Madhya Pradesh's Sagar district and later set ablaze by them when she told her family about the incident, police said.

The victim somehow reached her house and narrated the crime to her family, who rushed to the forest in search of the youths.

When the accused came to know that she had informed her parents about the incident, they reached the girl's house and set her on fire by pouring kerosene.

On the basis of the victim's statement, a case has been registered against Mukesh Kurmi, Deepak Vishwakarma, Murli Sharma and Vivek Kurmi, they said.
