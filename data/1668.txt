Uttar Pradesh
May 04, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A local court here has awarded life sentence to a constable for raping a Dalit woman four years ago.

Constable Akhilesh Yadav has been awarded life imprisonment for the rape of a Dalit woman in Samrakhapur village under Asandra police station area of February 5, 2008.

The constable in an inebriated condition barged into the house of the victim when she was alone with her two minor kids while on patrol duty and raped her at gun point, sources said.
