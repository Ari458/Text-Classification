Tamil Nadu
Oct 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Judge of the Mahila Court, Lingeswaran, on Wednesday, convicted and sentenced a youth Rajendran (35) to life imprisonment in a case of sexual assault on a mentally challenged minor girl.

According to prosecution, Rajendran committed the offence at her residence in November last year. Based on a complaint by the girl’s parents, the All Women Police registered a case under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, and arrested him. The Judge who found Rajendran guilty, sentenced him to life imprisonment.
