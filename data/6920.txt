Tamil Nadu
May 16, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Mahila Court Judge N. Vasanthaleela on Friday sentenced T. Eswaramoorthy (41), a casual labourer, to 51 years of imprisonment for sexually abusing three minor girls at Mangalam.

According to V. Parimala, the Special Public Prosecutor, the judge had convicted the accused under Section 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act in each of the three sexual offences he committed.

Under Section 376, the accused was sentenced to 30 years of imprisonment and for the POCSO Act he was sentenced to 21 years of imprisonment.

The accused was also to pay compensation to the three victims.
