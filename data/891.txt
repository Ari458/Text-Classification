Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Deputy director S.S. Misra  an officer at the planning department of the Urban Development Directorate, who had been brought over on deputation  had been allegedly making advances towards the woman, Sunita Debbarma (34), ever since he joined office in May 2011.

Victim Alleges that Misra allegedly pounced on her from behind and started molesting her, while she was looking for some files in the cabin. Employees who heard her scream rushed in and saw her crying, though by then, Misra had returned to his chair.
