Senapati
Jan 01, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Victim's relative Paolenonang alleged that he along with his sister-in-law stayed at a hotel in Imphal, after they could not catch bus to reach their village. While searching for lodging, they met Md Fakruddin, who led them to Tomba Hotel, Governor Road. 
While he checked-in in one of the rooms, Fakruddin took away his sister-in-law, saying she would be lodged in another room. After which, Fakruddin confined the victim in a secret room and raped her, he said.
