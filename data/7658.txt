Maharashtra
Aug 18, 2015
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			The NRI police arrested Parmeshwar Sanjay Chandrup alias Vicky (25), an auto driver from Ulwe on Saturday for allegedly abducting and raping an 18-year old girl from Diwale. 

Vicky abducted the girl on Friday, took her to his home and allegedly raped her, as she had rejected his proposal, said the cops. He left her on Palm Beach Road on Saturday after which a complaint was filed. 

Vicky's mother, sister and brother-in-law have been booked as accessories. Vicky has been remanded to police custody till August 21.
