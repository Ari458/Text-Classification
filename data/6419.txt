Uttar Pradesh
Feb 12, 2015
Murder
Description:
			In a case of honour killing, a 20-year-old girl was burnt alive by her mother and brother in Amirpur village, police said today.

Mohar Devi, her son Pramod and one Ajay have been arrested in this connection, SSP Manjil Saini said.

Both mother and son have confessed that they had burnt Neeraj Kumari because they did not approve of her relationship with Ajay, Saini said.

The duo along with Ajay has been arrested, he said.
