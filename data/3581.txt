Andhra Pradesh
Nov 25, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a 16-year-old boy on the terrace of a building in a village.

The boy, a neighbour of the 12-year-old girl, took her to the terrace of the building in the district's Nagaram village late night and allegedly committed the offence.

Following the complaint, Nizamabad V Town police booked a case against the teenage boy under relevant sections of the IPC and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
