Uttar Pradesh

Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in Khairighat area.

In a complaint to the police, the victim's father alleged that the minor girl was raped by Maiku (45) when she had gone to graze cattle late last evening, Circle Officer Mahsi Mohammad Akmal said.
