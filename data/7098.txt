Tamil Nadu
Jun 04, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			Police arrested a teenage boy for allegedly harassing a Class IX student sexually in Namagiripet near Rasipuram.

Police said, K Jayakumar (19), of Chithannaperumal Nagar, Ayyampalayam, had allegedly been harassing the girl of the same area sexually for a while.

Along with his friend, a minor boy of Class XII who is absconding, he had taken the girl’s picture on his mobile phone recently and forwarded it through WhatsApp to their friends.
