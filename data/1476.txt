Sikkim
Mar 15, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			In Gagtok, A man was arrested today for allegedly torturing his wife for dowry which led to her committing suicide here. Rajen Kumar Gupta was arrested for inflicting mental and physical torture on his wife, the police said. Gupta's wife was found hanging from the ceiling of their home at Deorali here on 8 March. The police said the woman's brother Rajib Kalowar, a resident of Cooch Behar in West Bengal, alleged that the victim had suffered persistent mental and physical torture by Gupta. PTI COR AMD
