Uttar Pradesh
Dec 07, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			An 8-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Ghodi Bachheda village.

In a complaint filed by the victim's kin, the accused Manoj kidnapped the girl and took her to a lonely stretch, where he raped her and later threatened to kill her if she told anyone about the incident.

The girl has been sent for medical examination.

Source: Press Trust Of India
