Jharkhand
Jun 12, 2015
Murder
Description:
			An elderly woman was beaten to death by her neighbours on charge of practicing witchcraft at Dubudih village in Seraikela-Kharswan district, police said on Thursday. 

Rajni Kumharin, aged over 70, was sitting in her courtyard when her neighbor Mangal Kumhar and his father Chuku Kumhar barged into her house and attacked her with a lathi after accusing her of practicing black magic, police said. 

Rajni succumbed to her injuries on the way to the hospital, police said. The accused father-son duo fled on a motorcycle after committing the crime. 

Referring to the incident, police said Mangal's five-year-old son was not keeping well for last few days despite medical treatment and held Rajni responsible for it. 

Police said a case has been registered against the absconding duo. The incident was a third-of-its-kind in the last three weeks in Kolhan region comprising East and West Singhbhum and Seraikela-Kharswan districts. Earlier, three of a family, including a 95-year-old woman, had been axed to death by unidentified miscreants in Khadgu village under Potka police station of East Singhbhum district on June 1 .
