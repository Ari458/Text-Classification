Rewari
Nov 16, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old married woman of Dulhera Khurd village of Rewari district was raped then burnt to death on Tuesday night.

Report:
The woman was asleep when the accused barged into the house and committed the crime. Her husband was reportedly not at home at the time. He set the woman afire by pouring kerosene on her when she tried to raise the alarm.
