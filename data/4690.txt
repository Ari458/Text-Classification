Jaipur
May 08, 2014
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 28-year-old nurse in a private hospital here has alleged that her colleague sexually exploited her for one year on the pretext of marriage.

In her FIR lodged with the Kardhani police yesterday, the woman alleged that one Dara Singh, a male nurse, dodged her marriage proposal and kept physical relations for one year.
