dharauli
Dec 30, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly raped and held captive for 15 days by three men in Dharauli village here.

The girl went missing on December 14 while going to school, the police said on Sunday, adding that she was freed on Saturday after which the matter came to the light and an FIR was registered against three persons.

While Raju has been charged with rape, Basantlal and Sahabdeen have been booked for criminal conspiracy.
