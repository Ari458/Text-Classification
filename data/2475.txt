Rajasthan
Jun 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Three people were handed down 10 years in jail by a local court for repeatedly raping a 20-year-old for over four years.

Jahir Khan, Naim Ahemad and Ikramuddin had been blackmailing the victim using video clip of the act, after which she had lodged a complaint in the Sadar police station in 2011.
