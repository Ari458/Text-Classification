Telangana
Jul 29, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			Two persons who were caught by the She teams for sexually harassing women at public places were sentenced to two-day imprisonment by a city court.

The accused Sravan Yadav, 19, a student from Falaknuma, and Mohd Ziauddin, 27, of Banjara Hills, were caught in two different cases reported at Falaknuma and Bowenpally.

The court also imposed a fine on them.
