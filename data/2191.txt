Balepur
May 12, 2013
Crime, Other
Description:
			Two teenaged cousins were allegedly abducted by their relative in Balepura village, police said today.

The accused Raju of Karkoli village and his two associated were named in the FIR filed by the 18-year-old victim's father yesterday.

The victim and the 18-year-old cousin were abducted two days back, police said.
