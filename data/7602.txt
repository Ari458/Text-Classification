Haryana
Aug 09, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Police here on Saturday booked a 21-year-old man for allegedly raping his aunt.

The youth, who is on the run now, allegedly committed the crime at his aunt's house and had bolted the door of her room from the inside.

A case of rape has been registered at the Sector 20 police station. Though the alleged crime was committed on Friday afternoon, the victim approached the police on Saturday evening. Police are on the lookout for the accused and have started an investigation. The station house officer (SHO) of Sector 20 police station, Satbir Singh, confirmed that they had registered a case. "The accused has been charged with rape and the accused will be arrested soon," he said.
