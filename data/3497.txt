Uttar Pradesh
Nov 08, 2013
Abduction, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A ten-year-old girl was allegedly raped in Lavakheda Badriprasad village under Nawabganj police station.

The incident happened when the victim, sleeping outside her house, was picked up by a masked man who took her to the jungles outside the village and raped her.

Though the villagers started searching for the victim after hearing her cries they could not trace her at night and the incident came to light only after she returned home in the morning.

The six-year-old sister of the victim, who had followed her while she was being taken was made to sit nearby and witness the crime.

The medical examination was being conducted in the district hospital.
