Vemulakurthi
Mar 16, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old married woman was allegedly raped by a person in Karimnagar district.
	
The incident took place when the woman was on the way to Vemulakurthi village under Ibrahimpatnam police limits, after collecting firewood from
nearby forest.
	
The accused, identified as A Gangaram (50) fled from
the spot soon after committing the crime.
	
The victim has been admitted to Government Hospital at Metpally Mandal head quarters.
