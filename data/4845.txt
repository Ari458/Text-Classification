Kerala
Jun 06, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Alakkode police have arrested two men on charges of sexual abuse and rape, in two separate cases. In the first incident, police arrested a 35-year-old man on charges of sexually abusing his nine-year-old daughter.

The victim, a resident of Kudiyanmala in Alakkode, was abused by her father on the evening of April 25 when her mother was away. Police said the accused is an alcoholic.
