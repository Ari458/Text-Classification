Delhi
Jan 28, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old married woman claimed that she was raped by her neighbour in outer Delhi’s Mangolpuri on Sunday.

The victim lives with her husband in Buddh Vihar. 

She told police that Suresh had called her to a friend’s house, where he proposed her for marriage. When she refused, Suresh allegedly raped her.
