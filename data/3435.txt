Andhra Pradesh
Oct 29, 2013
Crime
Description:
			An auto rikshaw driver allegedly attempted to kidnap a college girl at Chilakaluripet town in Guntur district.

The girl boarded the three-wheeler at Ganapavaram village to go to Chilakaluripet town.

When the auto driver did not stop at her destination, she shouted for help and she jumped from the moving vehicle.
