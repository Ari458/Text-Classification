Maharashtra
Aug 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old man was arrested for allegedly raping his 10-year-old daughter in Mankhurd. The police said the incident took place on June 15 when the girl was alone at home. After forcing himself on her, the accused warned her against speaking about the crime. "The girl's mother approached us with a complaint following which we registered a case and arrested the accused," said Senior Inspector Vilas Kanade of Mankhurd police station.
