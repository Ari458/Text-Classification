Jharkhand
Feb 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 35-year -old paharia ( primitive tribe) woman was raped at Littipara in Pakur district while she was home with her two children. An FIR was lodged at the Littipara police station on the basis of victim's statement. The accused, identified as Shiva Mandal, broke into the victim's house in the afternoon, locked both the children in a room and raped her and fled.

The matter was brought before the local panchayat, but the accused, who hails from Katihar district of Bihar and resides in Littipara for work, did not turn up. The victim then approached the police.
