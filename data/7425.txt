Jammu
Jul 06, 2015
Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			The Jammu & Kashmir police have arrested a person and were looking for another for allegedly stripping a woman in Udhampur near here.

"The accused persons intercepted a couple at an isolated place in Jaganoo on Udhampur outskirts and misbehaved with the woman," a police source said.

"When she resisted, the accused allegedly overpowered the woman and stripped her. The accused had also reportedly video-graphed the woman on their mobile."
