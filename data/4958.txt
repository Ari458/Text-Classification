Chandigarh
Jun 20, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police have failed to arrest Shubham, 22, absconding in case of attempt to rape of a minor girl, who was also forced to consume phenyl.

The victim, a Burail resident, was attacked when she was alone at her house following a property dispute involving her family. She has been admitted to GMCH-32 and her statement was recorded by a duty magistrate on Thursday.

A team for forensic experts visited the house of victim and collected some blood samples from the spot. A half empty bottle of phenyl was also seized from the room. A case was registered at Sector 34 police station.
