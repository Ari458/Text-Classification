Andhra Pradesh
Dec 11, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenager was allegedly raped by a youth on the pretext of marriage in Hayathnagar on city outskirts.

The accused Prashant, a degree student, allegedly developed physical relationship with the 16-year-old girl over the past five months after promising that he would marry her.

However, he started avoiding her recently.

Following the complaint, the Hayathnagar police booked a case of rape against Prashant and launched a hunt to nab him.
