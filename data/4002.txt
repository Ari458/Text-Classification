Jharkhand
Jan 29, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			One man, who along with two others had allegedly gang raped a minor girl in Panki block of Jharkhand's Palamau district three days ago, was arrested today, police said. 

The girl, a class IX student, was sent for medical examination, he said. 

The CPI (ML-Liberation) observed a bandh today in protest against the gang-rape and demanded arrest of the perpetrators. 

The police dispersed bandh supporters during protests and arrested party's district president R S Dinko for enforcing the bandh in Panki block, Ramesh said. 

Left parties demanded immediate release of Dinko.
