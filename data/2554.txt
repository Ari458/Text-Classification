Uttar Pradesh
Jul 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two teachers of a private school were arrested for allegedly molesting a class VII student in the institute's premises.

The 13-year-old girl's mother filed a complaint stating that the accused teachers had molested her daughter on different occasions. The incident took place in Vaishali area of the city.

Gaurav molested the girl in the school's washroom while the other accused, Naveen did the same on Monday.

Meanwhile, school authorities have defended the accused teachers, saying that the allegations made by the girl's mother are false and they are trying to harm the reputation of school.
