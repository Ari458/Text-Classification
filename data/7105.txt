West Bengal
Jun 05, 2015
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A Trinamool Chhatra Parishad leader, Tajamul Haque, allegedly threatened to rape a lady teacher on being asked to leave Samsi College campus. The youth came to college drunk to "supervise" the BA second-year examination on Wednesday. 

Teachers lodged a police complaint and sought security. Police said Tajamul is absc-onding.
