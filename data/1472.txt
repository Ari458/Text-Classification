Kosad
Mar 15, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Kosad area of Surat.The girl's parents had gone out without locking the door on Saturday. 

The accused, an autorickshaw driver Akram (24) entered the house at around 10 am and raped the girl. Akram even threatened to kill the girl if she complaint about the incident to her parents. Fearing for life, she did not reveal the trauma till Wednesday.
