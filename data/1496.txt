Bairagarh
Mar 18, 2012
Dowry Harassment, Abuse
Description:
			Tarun Jaiswal (26)working was booked under the Section 306 of the IPC and 3/2 of the Dowry Prohibition Act for compelling his wife to commit suicide. The victim's relatives lodged a complaint with the police stating that the deceased was also tortured for dowry.  

The accused for the last few months had developed an affair with a woman in Balaghat where he was working. His wife Prerna was living in stress after she came to know about the matter. She tried many ways to restrain Tarun from the relationship but he continued to maintain it which provoked his wife to commit suicide by consuming poisonous substance in Bairagarh suburb on February 5.
