Uttar Pradesh
Feb 07, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The principal of a public school here was today arrested for allegedly attempting to rape a 30-year-old teacher of his school, police said.

Raj Deep Tyagi, 40, lured the teacher to come to Raj Nagar Extention in the city on the pretext of teaching school kids and tried to rape her, SHO Sihani gate Ashok Sisodia said.
