West Bengal
Jan 25, 2014
Molestation, Crime
Description:
			Two people have been arrested for allegedly attempting to rape two tribal women. 

Ramjan Sheikh (40) and Hablu Sheikh (35) entered the farm house of Rafiq Sheikh and tried to rape two tribal women who have come from other states for field work. 

As the women fought back and raised an alarm, the accused ran out of the house, but one of them was caught by Additional Superintendent of Police Tarun Haldar, who was on patrol in the area. 

His interrogation led to the arrest of the other man, both the women have lodged written complaint to the police.
