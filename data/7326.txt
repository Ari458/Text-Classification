Tamil Nadu
Jul 11, 2015
Abduction, Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			M. Lakshmi of Thisayanvilai in Tirunelveli district was arrested by Asaripallam police on Friday for kidnapping and killing a baby girl.

According to police, Lakshmi kidnapped the baby from Kanyakumari Government Medical College Hospital on Thursday evening. She took the infant to Thisayanvilai, murdered and cremated it. The baby was born out of wedlock to her daughter who was working as a nurse in a private hospital in Nagercoil. The girl was born on July 7. 

A case of female infanticide was registered against the woman. She was produced before a court which remanded her in judicial custody, police sources said.
