Madhya Pradesh
Apr 13, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly gangraped by three minors in Madar Challa area here in Jabalpur, police said today.
      The victim was sexually assaulted by the trio two-days back when she was going to a nearby shop for purchasing milk, they said.
      They pulled her in an auto-rickshaw and took turns to rape her. When the victim failed to return home for long, her worried mother looked for her in the area and found her lying in the vehicle and crying in pain.
     She narrated her ordeal to her mother and also told her that when she tried to escape from the spot they beat her.
