Karnataka
Jul 19, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police on Saturday arrested a carpenter identified as Muniraju (25) on charge of sexually abusing a five-year-old girl in his neighbourhood on Friday.

The victim, daughter of a watchman at an under-construction building, complained of pain in her abdomen on Friday evening. On being questioned by parents, she narrated how the carpenter took her to an empty room luring her with chocolates and sexually abused her. The parents immediately approached the police, who registered a case of rape and under POCSO Act, 2012 and sent the child for medical examination. According to sources, medical examination confirmed sexual abuse.
