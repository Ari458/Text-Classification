Rajouri Garden
Mar 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 50-year-old labourer was arrested for allegedly raping a minor girl in west Delhi.
    
The incident was reported from Rajouri Garden
and the arrested has been identified as Surender Singh, who was the victim's neighbour.
    
The 11-year-old girl told police that she was being
sexually assaulted for the past few months and was threatened.

Source: Press Trust Of India
