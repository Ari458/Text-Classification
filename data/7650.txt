Tamil Nadu
Aug 17, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Neyyattinkara police have arrested a 26-year-old man for allegedly raping a minor girl after promising to marry her.

The accused has been identified as Kiran Kumar, a resident of Olathanni. According to police, Kiran was nabbed following the complaint lodged by the victim’s family after she was found to be pregnant. The accused had sexually abused the victim many several instances after giving false promises of marrying her.

After detecting her pregnancy, the accused tried to flee the scene and subsequently the parents of the victim lodged a complaint with us, police said. “We registered a rape case under section 375 of Indian Penal Code against the accused. The Judicial First class Magistrate Neyyattinkara remanded him in judicial custody.
