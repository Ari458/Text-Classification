Rape

			A 22-year-old woman was allegedly gangraped by seven people at a guesthouse in Gurgaon on Saturday.
The victim, who hails from West Bengal, has alleged that she was raped by her acquaintance Kapil and his associates at a guesthouse in Sushant Lok area.
A medical examination of the complainant has confirmed rape, he added.

According to police, the victim met Kapil at a nearby market on the day of the crime, who then accompanied her to the guesthouse. 
At the guesthouse, she alleged, six of Kapil's acquaintances entered the room and took turns to repeatedly rape her. They were also carrying a firearm, she said.
An FIR has been registered under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and the Arms Act, and the neddful action is being taken to identify the other accused, the ACP said. No arrests have been made in the case so far.
