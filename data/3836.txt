Shillong
Jan 04, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Three policemen were arrested and suspended from service for allegedly molesting a 12-year-old girl in Meghalaya's West Garo Hills district.

Police have arrested the police officers based on an FIR lodged against them by the girl's uncle.

Sub-Inspector Bankerlang Kharshanoh and two other sub-inspectors on probation - Ronald Nongrum and Sylvester Chyne were arrested from Tura.

According to the FIR, the alleged incident took place on December 28 when the girl and her uncle were returning from their shop. They were stopped and allegedly harassed by the three policemen who were in an inebriated condition.

The trio had allegedly assaulted the uncle after accusing him of being a militant and molested the girl inside the auto rickshaw in which they were travelling home.

The policemen later took the two to Chandmari police beat house in Tura town and forced them to sign some papers and took them for a medical examination before being let off late in the night.

A woman police officer in the rank of sub-inspector was appointed to probe the incident after which punitive actions would be taken accordingly against the three.
