Uttar Pradesh
Jan 09, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			An Army Man has been arrested at Mughalsarai railway station for allegedly "misbehaving" with a woman co-passenger who was travelling with him in Howrah-New Delhi Rajdhani Express, a railway official said here on Saturday.

The accused has been identified as Lance Naik Diler Singh, who is posted at Agartala, was arrested by the Government Railway Police (GRP) of Mughalsarai late last night for allegedly "misbehaving" with the woman travelling in the same B3 coach of Howrah-New Delhi Rajdhani Express, the Eastern Railway (ER) officer said. An FIR was lodged against Singh under IPC Section 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty).
