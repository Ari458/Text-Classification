Gujarat
Apr 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old housemaid has registered a complaint of rape against her employer, a diamond worker, alleging that he had repeatedly raped her over a period of eight months in Vijaynagar Society in Dindoli. The woman, a single mother who had separated from her husband sometime ago, lodged the complaint against Suresh Singh M Solanki, 42.

The rape survivor also alleged that he used to beat her up and threatened to kill her if she told anyone about her sexual exploitation by him. The accused has been booked under Indian Penal Code (IPC) sections 376, 406, 420, 323, 504 and 506 (2) by Dindoli police. Solanki could be arrested anytime.

The woman came in contact with the accused in 2013 when he had just separated from his wife. The accused lured the woman, who stayed near his house, with the promise of marriage. He told her that he has initiated divorcee proceedings against his wife and once they are completed, he would marry her. The rape survivor used to also manage his household chores.

SM Vala, police sub-inspector, said, "We are collecting evidences in the case and would soon be arresting the accused."
