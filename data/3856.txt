New Delhi
Jan 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old boy, his father and one of their associates have been apprehended for allegedly raping their 37-year-old relative in Mehrauli area of South Delhi.

According to the police, Gopal (32), Sunil (31) and his juvenile son were nabbed from their residences.

The victim made a PCR call and reported the incident. She has alleged that the trio barged into her home when she was alone at her home and then allegedly gang-raped her.

Before fleeing, they threatened her that she would have to face consequences if she reported the incident.

On her complaint, an FIR was registered at Mehrauli police station after which the trio were apprehended.

Source: Press Trust of India
