Karnataka
Aug 05, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 24-year-old newly married woman died after she was reportedly forced by her husband and in-laws to consume poison at Narnal village in Chincholi taluk in Kalaburagi district on Tuesday.

According to a complaint lodged by her father, Renuka was married to Ganesh two months ago and was forced to consume poison when she failed to bring more dowry. The complaint said that Renuka was harassed by her in-laws and husband seeking more dowry. The police have arrested Ganesh.
