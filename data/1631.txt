Uttarkashi
Apr 25, 2012
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A woman Army officer has lodged a case of sexual harassment against a Lieutenant Colonel. In her complaint, she has alleged that the officer sexually harassed her on the pretext of marrying her.

She also alleged that he cheated and sexually harassed her over a period of time after promising to marry her.

The woman, a divorcee, also claimed that the two had got married in a temple, but when she asked him to get their wedding registered, he refused, saying that he was already married, police said.
