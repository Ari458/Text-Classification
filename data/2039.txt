Hisar
Apr 25, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A taxi driver has been booked for allegedly molesting two minor girls during their visit to Gurgaon recently, police said here today.

The father of the minor girls had hired a taxi from Hansi to Gurgaon to visit Sheetla Mata Mandir on April 22 for 'Mundan' (hair-cutting ceremony) of his son, police said.

During the visit, the driver allegedly molested the two girls, who narrated what happened with them to their parents after the family returned to Hansi.
