Gujarat
Mar 23, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 23-year-old girl hailing from Surat in Gujarat was allegedly raped by a 30-year-old travel agent known to her.

Ramesh Meena allegedly raped her at his residence at Balaji Tower on the intervening night of Friday and Saturday in Jawahar Circle area, police said.

An FIR in this regard was registered yesterday.

The victim's statement would be recorded under Section 164 of IPC, they said, adding that a search was on for the travel agent at his office and residence.

Source: Press Trust of India
