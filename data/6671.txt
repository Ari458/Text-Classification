Uttar Pradesh
Mar 23, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Farenda area, police said here today.

The incident happened when the victim was playing outside her house yesterday. The accused Prasidh Nath Chaudhary took her to his house and allegedly raped her, they added.

Chaudhary has been arrested and FIR has been lodged in this connection, police said, adding that the girl has been sent for medical examination.
