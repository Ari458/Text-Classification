Punjab
Oct 01, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The police on Wednesday arrested a 26-year-old constable posted in women and child support unit of the Chandigarh Police for allegedly raping a minor girl in Manimajra.

The incident happened in the wee hours of Wednesday when father of the victim found that the cop had entered the second floor of his house, where his daughter was living.

Inspector Jaswinder Singh of Manimajra police station said the girl's parents were in the first floor section of the house when her mother complained of chest pain. When the father went upstairs to inform her that he was taking her mother to the hospital, he allegedly found the policeman committing the crime, Singh said. He immediately called the police.

The accused was a neighbour of the victim.

The case was registered on the statement of the minor victim's father. The accused was booked for rape, house trespass after preparation of hurt and assault under sections 376 and 452 of the IPC and section 4 and 6 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POSCO) Act.

A local court sent the accused in judicial custody.
