Oshiwara
Jan 18, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			The probe dates back to June 2010, when three women from Oshiwara gave written complaints to Additional Commissioner of Police Amitabh Gupta alleging that assistant police inspector (API) Balasaheb Khade of Local Arms unit in Tardeo, would pass lewd comments and make personal remarks each time they visited the police station to register a complaint.
