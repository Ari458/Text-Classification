Bihar
Dec 12, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A court in Bihar's Munger district sentenced a man to life imprisonment for the murder of his wife two years ago.

The Additional District Judge, I.R.K. Srivastava convicted Md. Ziarul and sentenced him to life imprisonment after finding him guilty in the murder of his wife Sazda Khatun.

According to prosecution, Ziarul had strangled his wife following an argument at his home in Waqarpur village in Mufassil police station area on February 14, 2011.

The victim's father had lodged an FIR against his son in-law charging him with the murder of his daughter.
