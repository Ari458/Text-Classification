Mogappair East
Jan 30, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 35-year-old paedophile, who kidnapped a four-year-old-girl from near her house in Mogappair East was nabbed by the public on Saturday. People, including women of the neighbourhood, assaulted the man and rescued the child from him.  

The accused had sweet talked the child and took her away. As the missing child's image was flashed on news channels the public recognised the girl, said S. Surendran, Inspector of Police, JJ Nagar.

Police sources said Manikandan is a habitual offender, who had previously misbehaved with children in other parts of the city but no formal complaint was lodged against him.
