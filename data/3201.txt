Andhra Pradesh
Sep 22, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman allegedly committed suicide by hanging at her house at Ashoknagar in Kapra on Sunday night even as her parents suspected foul play in the incident alleging that her husband and in-laws were responsible for her death for failing to get additional dowry.

P. Manga (26), hailing from Nalgonda district was married to Prasad, a gardener in a software company, in 2007. A few months after the wedding, Prasad and his family were allegedly harassing her demanding more dowry, the police said.

The couple have two sons.

On Sunday night, she was found hanging under suspicious circumstances in her house.
