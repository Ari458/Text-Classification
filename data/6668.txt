Madhya Pradesh
Mar 22, 2015
Dowry Harassment, Rape
Description:
			A probationary police sub- inspector was today booked here for allegedly raping a woman constable, police said.

Probationary SI Subhash Dwivedi posted at Pachor police station in the district got engaged to his subordinate woman constable after promising to marry her, they said.

Based on complaint filed by the woman constable, Dwivedi allegedly raped her at a lodge here recently, and also demanded dowry for marrying her, inspector at Pachor police station Sunil Sharma said.

On refusal to his dowry demands, Dwivedi's family member called off the marriage, he said.

The cop and his family members are at large and a hunt has been launched to arrest them, Sharma added.
