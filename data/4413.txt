Goa
Mar 18, 2014
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 43-year-old woman was allegedly raped and murdered by an unknown person in Fatorda village.

The body of Antonette Rodrigues was found lying inside her house this afternoon when her daughters returned from school, Margao police told PTI.

"The body is sent for postmortem, but initial indications are that she was raped and strangulated to death," according to a senior police officer.

Husband of the deceased works abroad.

He said Rodrigues was probably murdered when her daughters - a 13-year-old and a 5-year-old - were away for school.

"One of the neighbours had seen Rodrigues at 8.30 am near her house," police said quoting a witnesses.
