Panchavati
Jun 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Rural police have arrested five youths, including a minor for allegedly gangraping a girl who is a resident of Pimpalgaon-Baswant.

The girl was taken to a flat at Nashik's Panchavati locality by one Kiran Patil alias 'Rohit' on his motorcycle, where his four friends were drinking.

After the victim escaped, she reported the incident to her parents, who lodged a complaint at the Pimpalgaon-Baswant police station.

The police have arrested Kiran Patil alias 'Rohit' (22), Umesh Chavan (22), Chetan Sonawane (24), Sagar Aware (23) and a minor Bunty Patil.
