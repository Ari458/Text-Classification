Kerala
Jul 14, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			The Thampanoor police on Monday arrested a man who allegedly molested a 11-year-old boy. 

The police said Rajendran of Kannettumukku, near Thycaud, was arrested after the boy revealed the incident to his sister. The boy, orphaned after his parents passed away, was living with his sister and her husband and used to play with other children of his age near Rajendran's house. Rajendran is accused of molesting the boy at his house for three days. The boy, who first kept silent out of fear, slowly revealed the matter to his sister, after which a complaint was lodged. Rajendran was produced before court and remanded in judicial custody.
