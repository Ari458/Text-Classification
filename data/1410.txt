Odisha

Rape
Description:
			The incident occurred in Ranto village of Odisha's Sundargarh district when the 5 year old girl child along with her grandmother was returning home after attending a birthday party in a nearby village yesterday. 

The old woman, unable to walk fast, requested the accused Manguru Minz to drop the child in her house. On the way, the youth allegedly raped her near a nullah before leaving her at the house.
