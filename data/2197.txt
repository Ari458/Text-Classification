Deesa
May 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a man living in Fanwara locality near the victim's house in Deesa, around 28 Kms from here, the police said.

The incident happened today when the 14-year-old girl was alone at her home and the accused Kishore Panchal (25) lured her, the police said.

According to the complaint filed by the mother of the victim, the accused took her to the bushes near a riverbed and raped her.

"He also threatened the girl not to tell anybody, or else he would kill her," the police said.

Source: Press Trust of India
