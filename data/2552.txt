Manipur
Jul 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man has allegedly raped his minor niece at Tharoijam Maning Leikai village in Imphal West district of Manipur.

After Irom Bobo (40) raped his 12-year-old niece in his house, she came out crying on the road and met women guards ('Meira Paibis' in Manipuri) who took her to the house of local gram panchayat pradhan Dwijamani.

Local residents barged into the house of Bobo, picked him up and handed him over to Patsoi police station.

The girl was taken to Regional Institute of Medical Science and Hospital here where her medical test was conducted.
