West Benagl
Dec 17, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A teenaged girl was allegedly raped by two youths at Nutandanga village under Suri police station in Birbhum district, the police said today.

According to a complaint lodged by the elder brother of the girl, the incident occurred last night when she was returning home from the village church after doing some church-works.

The girl, a student of village school, was admitted to Suri hospital.
