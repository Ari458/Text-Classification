New Delhi
Dec 26, 2013
Murder, Crime
Description:
			An elderly woman was found murdered at her residence in Shahbad Dairy area of outer Delhi with police suspecting it as a robbery-cum-murder case.

Kailasho Devi, lived alone in her Holambi Kalan area residence, as her three sons were settled elsewhere.

On receiving information, the police team rushed to her residence and found the woman lying in a pool of blood.

The body was sent for post mortem.

As the house was found ransacked, the possibility of robbery and the involvement of an insider cannot be ruled out.

Her sons said that she may have been killed for property.

They told police that a theft was also reported from the house in which ornaments and cash worth lakhs had been stolen.
