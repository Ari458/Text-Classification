Uttar Pradesh
Jan 14, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped and gang raped by four people in Chitbaragaon area.

The victim was kidnapped on January 8 and returned recently after which she narrated the story to her family members.

On the basis of a complaint lodged by the victim's mother, an FIR has been lodged against four people, including two brothers, in the case.

Source: Press Trust of India
