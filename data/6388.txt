Tamil Nadu
Feb 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An eight-year-old minor girl was allegedly raped by a 62-year-old man here, police said today.

The accused, identified as Mani, allegedly raped the girl last night, they said, adding, the girl was his neighbour.

He was arrested and remanded to judicial custody today,they said.
