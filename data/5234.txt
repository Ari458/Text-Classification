West Bengal
Aug 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police on Monday arrested a private tutor for allegedly raping his nine-year-old student in Jadavpur.

According to the child, the accused, Madhusudhan Naskar of Jhilmil Colony, abused her between 2pm and 2.30pm. She said the tutor insisted on coming to her house when no one else would be present. The accused was nabbed from his residence.

"We have registered a case under IPC sections of rape and the stringent POCSO Act," said DC (south suburban) Santosh Pandey, adding that a counselling session has been arranged for the girl and that her statement will be recorded in due time.
