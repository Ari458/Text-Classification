Maharashtra
Jun 28, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Mundhwa police on Thursday arrested a 48-year-old man for allegedly raping his 17-year-old daughter on multiple occasions over the last two years. 

Police said that the suspect used to threaten his daughter and rape her inside their house in the Ghorpadi area. When the girl informed her mother about the incidents, the suspect also threatened her and beat her up, police said. 

With the help of her relatives, the girl's mother approached the police and lodged the complaint against her husband. "We have charged the suspect under section 376 (attempt to murder) of the Indian Penal Code and the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act," said H H Sengar of the Mundhwa police station.
