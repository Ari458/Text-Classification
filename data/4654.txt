Jaipur
May 01, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old girl today alleged that she was raped by a youth for over a year on the pretext of marriage in a sub-urban town.

The girl lodged an FIR with the Sanganer-sadar police station after the accused youth, Raju's marriage was fixed elsewhere by his maternal uncle yesterday.

Earlier, parents of both the girl and the youth had agreed on their marriage but later boy's parents and uncle retracted and fixed Raju's marriage with another girl.

The couple were apparently in a live-in relationship for about a year, the police said, adding, the girl's statement was recorded.
