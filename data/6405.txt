Uttar Pradesh
Feb 11, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A local court has awarded 10 years rigorous imprisonment to a man for raping a 13-year-old girl last year.

Additional District and Sessions Judge Amarpal Singh also imposed a fine of Rs 5000 on the convict Sonu Chauhan yesterday.

Chauhan had raped the girl on June 2, 2014 in Bansdeeh area.
