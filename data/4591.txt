Punjab
Apr 19, 2014
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped by a doctor at his clinic. 

In her police complaint, the woman has alleged that she was raped by Jasgir Singh at his clinic yesterday when she visited him for treatment. 

Singh fled after she raised an alarm, she alleged. 

A case of rape has been registered against the accused, police said, adding, the woman has been sent for medical examination.
