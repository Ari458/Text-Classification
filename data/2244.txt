Annaiyur
May 17, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 60-year-old male barber has been arrested in the city for allegedly sexually abusing a nine year old girl, police said..

They said the man had gone to his friend's house at nearby Annaiyur on May 14 and got permission from the girl's mother to take her to a nearby shop to buy her sweets. However he took the minor to an isolated spot and sexually abused her.
