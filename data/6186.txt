Uttar Pradesh
Jan 12, 2015
Molestation, Crime, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 20-year-old sweeper of Ghaziabad's Santosh Medical College was arrested after a doctor alleged he tried to sexually molest her at the hospital early on Monday.

The 24-year-old doctor has said the accused entered the duty room and tried to strangle her and also attempted a sexual assault. The incident was reported at around 3am on Monday morning.

Police arrested Ravi Kumar, a resident of Baghpat, who was recently employed as the maintenance staff by the hospital.

Circle officer Ran Vijay Singh said the woman and the college administration have lodged a complaint into the matter.
