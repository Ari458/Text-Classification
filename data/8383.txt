Punjab
Jan 24, 2016
Rape
Description:
			Police have arrested a man for allegedly raping a woman by marrying her, though he was already in a wedlock. Sukhwinder Singh, a resident of Karol Bagh, Jassiyan Road, has been booked on a complaint by a 23-year-old woman who used to work with him at a factory. She alleged that late they got married on June 18, 2013, after which they started living together in a rented accommodation.

The complainant said that after some time, she asked her husband to take her to his home but he refused to do so. She added that instead he left her and she later came to know that he was already married and had two children.

Ladowal police started a preliminary investigation after the complaint and on Thursday booked the the accused under sections 376 (rape) and 420 (fraud) of IPC.

The investigating officer, Amreek Singh, said the accused was arrested on Friday. He added that the victim's medical examination would be done soon.
