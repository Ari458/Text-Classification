Uttar Pradesh
Mar 04, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her friend's brother in Bahedi area here when she was returning after appearing in an exam, police said today.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl took a lift from her friend's brother Pradeep Kumar, who took her to a secluded place and allegedly raped her.

When the girl resisted the rape bid, she was badly beaten up by him.
