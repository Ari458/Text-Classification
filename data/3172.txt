Maharashtra
Sep 24, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			Three horseriders, two of whom are minors, were detained Monday for allegedly eve teasing and attacking a 25-year-old journalist while she was walking to her office in Andheri (West) Sunday, police said.

The incident took place when the woman was walking past Citi Mall on Link Road around 8.30 am Sunday, police said.

"The trio, who were each riding a horse, passed offensive comments. She shouted at them. One of the suspects hurled a horseshoe at her and all three rode away," said senior inspector Prabhakar Medhe. The woman suffered a minor head injury that she got dressed at a nearby hospital before lodging a complaint. The suspects, Kishor Das (23) and two minors, both aged 17, were remanded in judicial custody.
