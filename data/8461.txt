Maharashtra
Feb 02, 2016
Molestation
Description:
			Lakadganj police have arrested Chandu Vairagde (40) for allegedly molesting a married woman in Lakadganj area. 

The 33-year-old woman claimed that Vairagde was troubling her for the past many days. She told police that he was stalking her on Friday while she was walking home. However, when she confronted him, Vairagde abused her and misbehaved with her. 

On the woman's complaint, police booked Vairagde for molestation and arrested him.
