Odisha
Jan 29, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			A 50-year-old woman from Imphal has lodged a sexual harassment complaint in the Mahila police station here on Thursday against Colonel AN Singh, posted in the NCC Group Headquarters, Sambalpur. She also met Sambalpur SP Akhilesvar Singh and demanded action against A N Singh. 

The woman said she had met the Colonel in Imphal in 2013 and they were in a relationship since then. She alleged that the Colonel kept physical relationship with her with a promise of marriage. He is, however, refusing to marry her now.

Sambalpur Sadar SDPO Gagarin Mohanty said a case has been registered and investigation is on.
