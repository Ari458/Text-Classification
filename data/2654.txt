Uttar Pradesh
Mar 28, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A rail coach attendant was arrested on charges of misbehaving and teasing a woman on the Vikramshila Express train.
	
A 30-year-old woman, native of Patna, filed a complaint with GRP alleging that coach attendant, Sadanand, misbehaved and teased her in B-2 coach while travelling from Delhi to Patna.
