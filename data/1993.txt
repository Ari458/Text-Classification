Thondamuthur
Apr 14, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 22- year-old mill worker allegedly sexually abused a 17-year-old co-worker at Thondamuthur on the outskirts of the city.
     Police said the couple were in love and planned to elope.
A couple of days back he took her to various places and later allegedly raped her.
    Police launched an investigation after her parents complained she had not returned from work. The couple were traced to a bus stand here, they said, and the girl sent back to her parents since she was a minor.
