Puducherry
Jul 12, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The All Women Police on Friday arrested three persons on charges of harassing a woman for dowry. Police said Sangeetha (29) was married to Kavidasan (34) of Kennedy Nagar in Orleanpet for the past one year. Kavidasan and his mother allegedly harassed Sangeetha asking her to bring dowry.

Following alleged serial harassment, Sangeetha lodged a complaint with the All Women Police alleging that she was assaulted by Kavidasan. The police arrested Kavidasan, his brother Kannadasan and mother Indira. A case has been registered.
