Uttar Pradesh
Jul 10, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old married woman was gang-raped in Vibhutikhand area of Gomtinagar. The three accused named in the FIR have been arrested and sent to jail. They have been booked on charges of rape, issuing life threat and harassment.

According to police, the rape survivor lives with her husband in Vibhutikhand. The husband, a daily wager, was suffering from a limb injury amd undergoing treatment in the Balrampur hospital for the past few days. The woman was alone in the house at the time of the incident.

As per the FIR, on July 6, Suresh and his two aides Ravindra and Awdhesh barged into the house of the woman and gang-raped her. The woman informed her husband when he came back on being discharged from the hospital on July 8. The couple lodged a complaint with the police.

The rape survivor told the police that the three accused are her neighbors who used to harass her regularly. The medical examination of the woman has been done and the report is awaited. Station officer, Vibhutikhand police, Devendra Dubey said the couple belong to Lakhimpur-Kheri and migrated to Lucknow in search of job a few months ago.
