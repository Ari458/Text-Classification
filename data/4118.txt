Andhra Pradesh
Feb 09, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a septuagenarian, who resides in her neighbourhood, at LB Nagar. The accused has been taken into custody.

The minor victim said P Buchaiah, a watchman, entered her residence when she was bathing and raped her in the washroom. At the time of the offence, the girl's parents went to work.

However, police said that the culprit was being questioned as there were several contradictions in the complaint of the victim, a class V student.

However, a case has been registered against the watchman under the Nirbhaya Act and Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
