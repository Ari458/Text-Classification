Hosur
Jan 08, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Ms. James Anjela Mary of  the All India Democratic Women's Association (AIDWA) met the collector and handed over the complaint along with a handwritten letter allegedly written by a girl and video recordings on the alleged sexual harassment meted out to the girl students at the home. She alleged that about 25 children below the age of 14 including 10 girls were staying at the Annai Sandhiya Home for Deserted Children.

The couple Mr. Theagarajan and Venkatalakshmi who are are running the home and their son were harassing the girl children studying from fourth standard to ninth standard.
