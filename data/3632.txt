Uttar Pradesh
Dec 03, 2013
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 30-year-old woman and her two sons were killed allegedly by her husband over dowry in Gaurikhalsa village.

Fakurbano and her sons Sayan Abbas(7) and Hamza (2) were found dead in their house.

The woman's father alleged that the three were either strangulated or poisoned by his son-in-law Shakeel Abbas.

In the complaint, he claimed that Abbas had been harassing Fakurbano for dowry since their marriage in 2006.

The father who rushed to his daughter's house locked Abbas in a room and later handed him over to the police.
