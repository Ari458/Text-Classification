Rajasthan
Apr 04, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 25-year old man allegedly tried to rape a minor girl when she was returning home from a nearby school in the district.
The 5-year-old girl was lured by the accused identified as Jagdish who took her to an isolated place and tried to rape her.
He left the girl when she raised an alarm. Her father lodged an FIR against the accused Jagdish who is absconding.
