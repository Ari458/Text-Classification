Santhigiri
Apr 02, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 63-year old migrant labourer from Tamil Nadu was arrested for raping a five-year-old girl at Santhigiri in Kalamassery area on Saturday. 

Chandran was staying next to the girl's house. He first lured the girl with sweets and chocolates. One day, while the girl was playing in the courtyard of her house, the man took her to his house and raped her, police said.

The girl's parents, daily wage labourers, were away when the incident took place. When they returned home, the mother came to know of the incident. She informed her husband and they lodged a police complaint, Latheef said.
