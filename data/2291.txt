Hazratpur
May 25, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			Two men were sentenced to life imprisonment and a woman given seven years jail term by a local court for the kidnapping and rape of a minor girl.

In July 2007, Parmanand along with his wife Bhagwan Devi and two other associates had cropped up for kidnapping the girl in Hazratpur area, prosecution sources said here.

Later, an FIR of kidnapping and rape was lodged against Parmanand, Devi and the third person  Bhure  in this connection, they said.
