Jharkhand
Sep 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a mason at Gobarbandha in Hazaribagh district.

Mohammad Sahazad,who was working at an under-construction building, entered a near-by house and raped the girl when she was alone.

Her mother had gone to a doctor when the man committed the crime.

The girl was sent for medical examination.
