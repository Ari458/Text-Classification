Delhi
Nov 08, 2011
Rape
Description:
			20-year-old Sarwan Kumar was held guilty of raping the three-year-old daughter of his neighbour after taking the child away from her mother on pretext of playing with her in December 2009. Kumar approached the victim's mother at her house on October 3, 2009 and asked her to let the child go along with him. When the victim's mother went to Kumar's house to fetch her daughter, she found the door closed from inside, said the prosecution. When she forced open the door, she saw Kumar raping her daughter.
