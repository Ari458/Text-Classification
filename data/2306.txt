Khajuwala
May 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 14-year old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in a village in Khajuwala area here.

The girl was raped by Babulal in the outskirts of 1 KDM village about 20 days back, SHO, Pugal, Jaiprakash said.

Babulal caught hold of the girl when she was returning home and took the minor to an isolated place, where he raped her, Jaiprakash said.
