Andhra Pradesh
Jan 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by two minor boys at Suraram village in the neighbouring Ranga Reddy district.

The boys forcibly took the victim near an isolated place, when she was going home and allegedly raped her there.

Following a complaint, a case was registered under the Nirbhaya Act, a hunt has been launched to nab the accused and further investigation was underway to ascertain if one more person was involved in the crime.
