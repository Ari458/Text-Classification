Jammu and Kaskmir
May 05, 2014
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped by two jawans of the Indian Reserve Police in Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district last week-end, police said today.

Both of them had been placed under suspension and arrested, they said.

The two barged into the house of the woman in Gambhir Muglaan on Saturday night and allegedly raped her, police said.

Police raided several places and arrested the two jawans last night.

Residents of Kashmir, both are jawans of the IRP 6th Battalion and were deployed in Rajouri.
