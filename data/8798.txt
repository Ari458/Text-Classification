Punjab
Nov 30, 2016
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The women cell of the police have registered a dowry harassment case against a man. On the complaint of a woman named Gurpreet Kaur, a resident of Jhande village near here, the police booked her husband Parminder Singh, a resident of Hathoor village. 

A case has been registered against him. The complainant told the police that she got married to Parminder in 2011, but soon after marriage, the accused started harassing her mentally and physically for bringing meager dowry. The police have started investigation.
