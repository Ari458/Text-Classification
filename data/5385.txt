Uttar Pradesh
Aug 26, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Baghpat district, police said today.

The victim's family lives in a slum in Barouta area of the district.

The incident took place when the family members of the girl were away at work.

Finding the girl alone at home, Abid barged in, beat her up and then raped, police said.
