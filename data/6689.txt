Haryana
Mar 25, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly abducted and raped by a neighborhood youth when she stepped outside her house to answer the call of nature in Ram colony here, police said.

The incident took place late last night when the victim went in the lane oustide her house to answer the call of nature and her neighbour, Pradeep, abducted her on his motocycle and took her to New Krishna colony where he raped her, police said.

Threatening the victim of dire consequences, the accused fled after dropping her near her house, police said, adding, the girl then disclosed her ordeal to to her family following which a complaint was registered with the police.

The girl was sent to the hospital for medical examination while searches are underway to nab the accused, police said.
