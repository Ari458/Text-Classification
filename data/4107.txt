Uttar Pradesh
Feb 07, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly killed by her husband and in-laws for dowry in Hinaula village in Awagarh area here.

Geeta (28), who got married to Gaurishankar last year, died yesterday under mysterious circumstance and the matter came to light when her in-laws wanted to cremate her body.

The police reached the spot and stopped her husband and in-laws from cremating her body and sent it for post mortem.
