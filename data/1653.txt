Goa
May 02, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Other
Description:
			The father of the 14-year-old girl who has been accused of raping her, and his friend, Francis D'Souza, who also sexually abused the minor, were remanded to seven days police custody when produced before the court on Tuesday.

The 14-year old girl was allegedly repeatedly raped by her father since 2009, who also allowed her to be sexually abused by his friend in exchange for a bottle of alcohol.
