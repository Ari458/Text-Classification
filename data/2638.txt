Maharashtra
Jul 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 40-year-old man was arrested for allegedly raping a teenager repeatedly over a period of time.
	
The accused Ramesh Gofnur had lured the 15-year-old victim, a relative of his and had sexually abused her several times while threatening her of dire consequences if she
revealed the abuse.
	
Gofnur was arrested after the tortured girl
registered a complaint with the police.
	
According to the complaint, the accused used to pay her petty sums before taking advantage of her.
