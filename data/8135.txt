Maharashtra
Dec 08, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			A local court in Shahuwadi taluka sentenced a man to two-year rigorous imprisonment (RI) and slapped a fine of Rs 2,000 on him for harassing a woman

The court also awarded two-month RI and slapped a fine of Rs 2,000 on three of his colleagues for helping him in the incident.

The accused, Prakash Dalvi, was booked for eve-teasing and harassing a woman five years ago. His friends Suresh Pandurang Dalvi, Sakharam Namu Dalvi and Tukaram Dalvi had helped him in the incident.

The complaint in this regard was registered with the Shahuwadi police station. After investigations, police had filed a chargesheet against the four accused.

After hearing both sides, junior magistrate first class (JMFC) S M Sarode awarded the punishment to the four suspects.
