Bihar
Nov 27, 2013
Crime, Street Harassment
Description:
			A woman athlete was assaulted by some youths in Jehanabad railway station premises after she resisted harassment attempts.

The woman athlete Ashwani Raj was returning home from Gaya after attending a training programme when 3-4 youths, who also alighted from the same train, accosted her and later started beating.

The woman resisted harassment by them, following which they assaulted her.

The SP who had gone to meet the athlete in the hospital said she was complaining of serious pain in stomach.

The doctors have been directed to monitor her condition and take her to Patna Medical College and Hospital (PMCH) if required.
