Delhi
Jan 28, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman alleged rape by her husband in Nihal Vihar. She told police that she had married her neighbour Santosh Kumar, 29, in a temple on August 29 last year.

They soon started living together in Arjun Nagar, but Santosh left the home on January 20. 

The woman claimed that she has been cheated by Santosh due to which she alleged rape.

According to Delhi Police, the new laws force them to file a case on the basis of the allegations and medical examination.
