Maharashtra
Jul 10, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			An unidentified person gained entry through the open door of a house, attacked 78-year-old woman alone and robbed her gold chain worth Rs20,000. Victim Kamla Sakhare was alone at her Maya Nagar residence on Wednesday afternoon when the thief entered home. Police said that finding the door open, the man entered the house. Before the woman could question him, the thief hit her with a tap spanner on her head and snatched away her gold chain. A case has been registered at Jaripatka police station against the unidentified man
