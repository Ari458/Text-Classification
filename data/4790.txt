East Delhi
May 24, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in East Delhi's Harsh Vihar area.

The incident took place on Thursday evening when the victim went to her neighbour's house in the same building to take her mobile phone which she had put on charging there.

"The accused took advantage of the situation and allegedly raped her and threatened her not to speak about the incident to anyone," said a police official.

The victim revealed her ordeal to her sister who informed the police.

Medical examination of the victim confirmed she was raped.
