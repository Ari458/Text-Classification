Bharabanki
Apr 29, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped by a drunk youth here, police said today.

The incident took place on April 26 when one Rajesh entered the 13-year-old girl's house in an inebriated state and forcibly took her to a field where he raped her, they said.

He left the minor there in an unconscious state. She managed to reach back home later.
