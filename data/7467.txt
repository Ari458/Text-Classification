Tamil Nadu
Jul 22, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old physically challenged girl was sexually abused by her maternal uncle at Anjetti in Denkanikottai.

The girl’s family had hushed up the crime, till they noticed the girl was six months pregnant. The victim, physically challenged with both legs and a hand dysfunctional from polio, was abused by 45-year-old Amasi, her maternal uncle.

According to the District Child Protection Officer D.Saravanan, who took the child’s deposition, the girl was  regularly assaulted by Amasi, while her parents, both labourers, were out most of the time for work.
