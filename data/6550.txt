Haryana
Mar 04, 2015
Abduction
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly abducted by a youth in Palla area who intended to marry her, police said today.

As per the complaint lodged by Vijendra Singh, his 13-year-old daughter went missing yesterday after she left for school but did not return home, a police spokesperson said.

Singh later came to know that she was lured into eloping by one Bunty, a resident of Sarai Khawaja, he added.
