Goa
Aug 01, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Following a complaint from a 25-year-old woman from Bethora-Ponda, police have arrested Shrinivas Acharya, 31, also a resident of Bethora on Thursday, on charges of raping her on several occasions at several places since 2005.

Ponda police said that the woman has lodged a complaint that the alleged accused had promised to marry her and raped her. She has complained that the alleged accused had first raped her in 2005 and later on several other occasions at several places.

Clarifying why she had kept quiet for a long time, she alleged that the accused had threatened her with dire consequences. Police booked the accused under Sections 376 ( rape), 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) and Sections 506 (II) (criminal intimidation) of Indian Penal Code (IPC).

He is in Ponda police's custody and will be produced before a local court on Friday to seek remand.
