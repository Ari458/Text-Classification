Maharashtra
Apr 28, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 23-year-old youth was arrested for allegedly murdering his sister and her husband at their residence near Butibori industrial area.

The accused Ajay Raj allegedly smashed heads of his sister Indu Devi Pandit (31) and her husband Umashankar Pandit (35) while they were fast asleep in wee hours at their home in Takalghat-Bori.

Prima facie, Raj was harbouring a grudge against the deceased for not parting money for his marriage.
