Andhra Pradesh
Jul 26, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			A first year degree student of a private college in the town suffered minor injury on her throat when one of her relatives attacked her with a knife at her house under II town police station limits on Monday. She survived the attack as her father came to her rescue. She was admitted to the district headquarters hospital where she is recovering.

According to the police, the boy went to her house in the afternoon with proposal to marry her which the girl's father rejected. Enraged at it, the youth attacked the girl and escaped.Police are trying to nab him.
