Haryana
Sep 24, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old woman accused a Karnal resident of raping her. 

The victim, a resident of Mansa Devi Complex, told the police that the accused raped her for more than a year on the pretext of marrying her but later refused to do so, said a police official. 

A case has been registered at the All-Woman Police Station in MDC. The accused has not been arrested and investigation is on.
