Maharashtra
Sep 05, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A father has been arrested for allegedly raping his 13-year-old daughter on Saturday. The accused has been carrying out the crime since the last three months.

The accused lived with his wife and daughter in Kalwa. According to the complaint, the accused had touched his daughter inappropriately in the past, especially when everyone was asleep at night. He then raped her on several occasions. Unable to bear the pain and trauma anymore, the girl narrated the entire incident to her mother. When her mother confronted the accused, he threatened them with dire consequences if they narrated the incident to anyone. 

However, the girl's mother lodged a complaint with the police on Friday, after which he was arrested.
