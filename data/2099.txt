Hirapur
Apr 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly gangraped by six men in her house in Hirapur village in the the district.

The victim lodged a police complaint alleging that six men from her village forcibly entered her house on Sunday and raped her, police said today.

In the FIR, the victim alleged that she was beaten up by the accused when she tried to resist the rape.
