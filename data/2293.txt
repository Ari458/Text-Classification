Nagpur
May 25, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A government doctor was arrested in the midst of his wedding here after a Nagpur-based woman alleged that he had physically exploited her on the premise of marrying her and was now reneging on it.

According to the woman's complaint, Dr Dhanendra Gajbhiye promised marriage to her and established physical relations with her for quite some time.

Yesterday, when she came to know that he was marrying some other woman, she approached the police following which the doctor was arrested.

The victim told the police that the accused met her in Nagpur sometime back when he had arrived there to see one of her friends following which the doctor took her (complainant's) number, befriended her and later got intimate.
