Kashmir
Sep 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old boy was injured after he was thrashed by relatives of a minor girl as he allegedly tried to rape her inside her home in Pulwana district.

Omar Ahmad Ganie, a resident of Padgampora village in Awantipora area of the district attempted to rape a 13-year-old girl in his neighbourhood.

Before police reached the spot, the locals thrashed him. He was shifted to local hospital where from he has been referred to Srinagar.

The boy and the girl were having an affair and had visited the girl's home when she was alone.

The relatives of the girl thrashed Ganie after they found him and filed a complaint of attempt to rape against him.

The girl tried to commit suicide, she was admitted to SMHS hospital.

Ganie is younger brother of a wanted militant, who was allegedly involved in the deadly attack on army convoy at Hydepora on June 24 that left eight soldiers dead and 16 others injured.
