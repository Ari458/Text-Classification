Puducherry
Feb 17, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A medical officer of government hospital in Tamil Nadu's Tindivanam and five others were arrested in connection with the alleged murder of his wife who was a junior engineer in a municipal office in Puducherry on February 12.

Both were living with their children in Puducherry.

The woman was knocked down by a car while returning home from her office in her scooter following which she died.

Police said that on a complaint from the parents of the woman, and inquiry was conducted and it was found the doctor allegedly engaged mercenaries to kill.

All the six accused were since remanded by a local court to fifteen days judicial custody.

Already the driver of the car was arrested on Saturday.
