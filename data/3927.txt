Jharkhand
Jan 19, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman has been killed allegedly by her husband following a quarrel over money at Amnari village in Hazaribagh district.

Kiran Devi was in the kitchen when her husband Rajendra Prasad allegedly attacked her with a sharp weapon and slit her throat.

Prasad got angry after his brother-in-law refused to transfer to Prasad's account a fixed deposit amount he deposited in the account of Kiran.
