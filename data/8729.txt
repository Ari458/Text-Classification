Maharashtra
Nov 01, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Nalasopara couple has been arrested for alleged sexual abuse of their 14-year-old neighbour.

In her complaint to the Tuling police station in Nalasopara (east), the girl stated that last week when she dropped in at her neighbour's house, the woman latched the main door from inside.

The woman then dragged her to the bed and held her hands even as her husband undressed the girl and raped her.

Police said that while the couple has been arrested and remanded to police custody, it is not known why the sexual assault took place.

Police said that the accused and the girl's family shared a good relation and were always in and out of each other's house.

The girl has been sent for medical examination to ascertain the sexual abuse.
