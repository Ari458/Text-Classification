Andhra Pradesh
Mar 18, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was strangulated to death after being raped allegedly by her brother-in-law (elder sister's husband) in neighbouring Ranga Reddy district.
	
At around 10.30 PM, the accused - K Raju - allegedly raped the minor girl at their house in Mominpet village and then strangulated her to death before fleeing.
