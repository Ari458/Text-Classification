Maharashtra
Jun 03, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			Gittikhadan police booked Gaurav Masram, 25, of Swawlambi Nagar, on molestation charge on Tuesday.

The complaint was filed by his 21-year-old friend. The girl claimed that Masram has been troubling her for last few days. She claimed he had posted her photo on a social networking site without her permission.
