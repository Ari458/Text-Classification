Odisha
May 28, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			An India Reserve Battalion (IRB) havildar have been arrested on charges of torturing his wife due to dowry demand from her parents, police said on Wednesday.
Incidentally the wife of the havildar Santosh Tripathy is also posted as a wireless constable in police department.
Tripathy, who was arrested by the Mahila police of Cuttack, was on Wednesday forwarded to court.
