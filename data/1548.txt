Bishnupur
Mar 24, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Four persons, including a Constable and a rifleman of India Reserve Battalion (IRB), have been arrested in connection with an alleged gangrape of a 40-year-old woman in Manipur. 

The victim was going to Imphal in a truck with her relatives with to sell firewood and chilly. Suddenly, four persons came in a car and stopped the truck and abducted the woman. She was taken to an isolated place in the district where she was sexually assaulted by two persons.
