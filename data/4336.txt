Jharkhand
Mar 07, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Crime
Description:
			A teacher at a private school in Dhanbad has been accused of sexually assaulting two minor girls, recording obscene MMS videos of them and taking their pictures, which he circulated among people

Accused Shyam Narayan Yadav used to give tuitions to the two minor sisters, one nine-year-old and the other aged 11, who are daughters of a Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL) official.

While being circulated, the pictures reached the girls' parents. After questioning the sisters, the parents lodged an FIR against Yadav.

Police raided Yadav's house and arrested his nephew for his role in circulating the pictures and the videos.

The girls were sent to the Patliputra Medical College and Hospital (PMCH) for a medical examination.
