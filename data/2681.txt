Jammu
Jul 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Three people were arrested in connection with the alleged gang-rape of a 18-year-old girl in the Billawar area of Kathua district.

The incident took place on June 4 in Beull, about seven kms from Billawar, when the accused Mohammad Rafiq, Yasir and Khurshid raped the girl in an under-construction house while she had gone to fetch water.

However, a complaint in this regard was registered on July 6 following which the trio were arrested.
