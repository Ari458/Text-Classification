Tamil Nadu
Dec 31, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The District Mahila Court in Udhagamandalam on Wednesday convicted a 33-year-old man and sentenced him to one year imprisonment for sexual harassment of a third standard student.

The judge M. Sarvamangala also slapped a fine of Rs. 1 lakh on him. It is learnt that the accused M. Kannan (33), a cab driver of Kolacombai, near Coonoor, picked up children from the locality and took them to school. The victim used to go to the school in his vehicle.

On September 9, 2010, he took the girl to his house and sexually abused her. On a complaint lodged by her parents, the Kolacombai police registered a case against the accused and arrested him.
