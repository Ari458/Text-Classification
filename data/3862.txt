Punjab
Jan 08, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Two youths were booked for allegedly raping a minor at the Soos village.

The incident happened when the accused, both in their early 20s, raped a 16-year-old girl while she was on her way back home after visiting a gurdawara.

The victim in her complaint alleged that the two youths, identified as Kinda and Sardool, were on a motorcycle on the roadside. When she reached closer, Kinda allegedly caught hold of her and stuffed her mouth with a cloth and both took her to a nearby sugarcane field and raped her, the miscreants after the act, fled on their motorcycle.

The medical examination of the girl confirmed rape.
