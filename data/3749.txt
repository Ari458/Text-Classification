Rajasthan
Dec 21, 2013
Murder, Crime
Description:
			Two men were arrested for allegedly killing an elderly women to rob her ornaments in the district.

Ayyub Pathan and Ashraf were nabbed for killing the woman with sharp-edged weapons when she was alone at her farm in Pur town and taking away her gold and silver ornaments.

Both the accused are drug addicts, they were being interrogated.
