Thane
Oct 16, 2011
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			31-year-old man and his mother have been arrested on the charge of harassing his wife.

Narendra Laxman Dahinade, a Thane resident, married Rekha (28) about a year ago. According to the complaint filed by Rekha, a few months after the marriage, he began to suspect her character. Sometimes he beat her. The parents-in-law too beat her up on a few occasions and demanded more dowry, police said.
