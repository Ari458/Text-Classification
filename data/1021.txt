Basavanagudi
Dec 28, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Padmapriya said she was kidnapped on Banaswadi Main Road on December 12, when she was going to a relative’s house on her two-wheeler by some men in a car. She was taken to the house of her sisters-in-law in Basavanagudi. She told DNA that her sisters-in-law Mahalakshmi and Monica beat her with slippers and abused her for filing a dowry harassment case against them a month ago. She alleged that her sisters-in-law forced her at knifepoint to withdraw the complaint, filed at Electronic City police station. Padmapriya added that one of them even claimed to have political contacts and threatened that she would be killed if she did not file a divorce case.
