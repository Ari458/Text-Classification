Tripura
Feb 11, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Murder
Description:
			A minor girl who went missing from her home at a village in Tripura's Dhalai district was found raped and killed. 

The girl was watching television with her parents and did not return after going out at Raima village in Gandacherra subdivision of Dhalai district on 8th of February. 

The parents, after finding her missing, informed a nearby camp of the paramilitary Tripura State Rifles who recovered her body next day. 

The sources said that she was murdered after rape.
