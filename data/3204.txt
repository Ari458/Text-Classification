Karnataka
Sep 25, 2013
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A school teacher was arrested for allegedly sexually harassing a minor girl at Suratkal on Tuesday.

The accused is identified as Vikas (37) of Suratkal. Sources in the Suratkal police station said Vikas allegedly harrased a 16-year-old girl, who is studying in 10th standard. The accused has been remanded in judicial custody for three days, sources said.
