Haryana
Jan 10, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A Dalit youth was arrested for allegedly raping a 28-year-old widow at Subasedi village.

Sonu (22), who is distantly related to the victim, forcibly raped her at her residence on the night of January 5 and threatened her with dire consequences if she told about it to anyone.

A case of rape and criminal intimidation has been registered against Sonu on the complaint filed by the victim.
