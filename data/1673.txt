Wazirabad
May 05, 2012
Rape, Abuse
Description:
			The organizer of NGO 'Suparaana Ka Aangan' was detained and an activist from the NGO were arrested for raping and molesting several minor girls at the center, police said.

Police have registered a case under the Indian Penal Code along with sections of the Juvenile Justice act. "Rachit was detained in the afternoon and later arrested late evening. We are questioning Suparaana Sethi, president of the NGO," said a police officer.
 
The NGO Suparaana Ka Aangan arranges education classes for under-privileged children in sector 52 of Wazirabad here. More than 60 students used to attend the classes organized by the NGO.
