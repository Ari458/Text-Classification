Andhra Pradesh
Jan 21, 2012
Crime
Description:
			Bodies of Banothu Buchamma, 35, Jambla, 5, and Navitha, 3, were found by locals in an agricultural well. Buchamma was the third wife of one Lal Singh, who divorced his first two wives. The couple used to quarrel frequently. On Thursday night, there was a heated argument between the couple and, on Friday morning, Buchamma and her daughters were found dead.
