Bangalore
Apr 15, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A top official of Karnataka's child development department has been arrested for allegedly raping a 38-year-old woman, who runs a school for children with special needs, police said.

The victim has alleged in her police complaint that the state child development department's Deputy Director Radhakrishna, had raped her at a hotel on April 12, when she gone to meet him for release of medical grant to her institution, Inspector Kenche Gowda said.

The woman reportedly said that she did not file a complaint with the police earlier due to fear that the aid to her school would be stopped.
