Madipakkam
Mar 06, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 70-year-old man identified as Krishnamurthy was arrested on Sunday on charges of sexually harassing a 12-year-old girl in Madipakkam on Saturday evening. Police said the accused was a staff at an Ayyappan Temple. 

A 35-year-old woman was employed at the temple as a maid. Her 12-year-old daughter went to the temple on Saturday evening in search of her, when Krishnamurthy had reportedly harassed her. The girl returned home crying and narrated the incident to her mother, who lodged a complaint at the police station on Sunday morning.
