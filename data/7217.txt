Punjab
Jun 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			The city police has booked a youth for trying to rape a 14-year-old daughter in his neighborhood on Monday.

The accused, identified as Akhilesh Kumar, 21, was arrested on Tuesday by Basti Jodhewal police following a complaint from mother of the girl. The minor, studying in Class VIII, had raised alarm following which accused fled.

In her complaint, the mother of girl alleged that the accused, who resided near their house, barged in and attempted to rape her when she was alone at home.
