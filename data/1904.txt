Rape
Description:
			Delhi seems to be in the news for the wrong reasons again. While the nation has not yet got over the tragedy of the paramedical student, another gangrape of a 20 year old has been reported. 

The incident reportedly took place on 1 January in Bhaktawarpur in the Alipur area on the outskirts of Delhi when she came to her paternal home from Agra, where she lives with her husband. The report was filed on January 6th.
