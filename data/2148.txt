Sarai Rohilla
May 05, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An eight-year-old girl was raped allegedly by her 40-year-old neighbour in north Delhi, police said today.

The incident was reported from Sarai Rohilla last evening and the suspect, who allegedly lured the victim to his house last evening and raped her, is on the run, they said.

Source: Press Trust of India
