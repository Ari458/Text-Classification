Odisha
Jul 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A beggar was taken into custody by the police on charges of outraging the modesty of a minor girl in Sanagudia village on the outskirts of the township.

The accused Biswajit Nayak committed the offence while he had been to 15-year-old victim's house to beg alms. Finding nobody in the house, he made sexual advances towards her.

The accused, who was later forwarded to court, is also facing trial in a case involving attempt to murder.
