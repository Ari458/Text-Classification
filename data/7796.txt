India
Sep 04, 2015
Rape
Description:
			The police have registered a case under Sections 376 and 34 of the IPC for raping a student of a medical institute in Katar Singh Wala village.

The victim, a 25-year-old girl of Chak Ram Singhwala village, complained that on the Tuesday afternoon, Narinder Singh with the help of his accomplice forcefully took her to a house in Parasram Nagar in a car bearing the registration number of Haryana.

Investigating Officer (IO) in the case, SI Charanjit Kaur said apart from the main accused Narinder Singh, the other accused Surinder Singh of Ablu village and Bhushan of Doda village of Muktsar were also booked in this regard. No arrests have been made so far.
