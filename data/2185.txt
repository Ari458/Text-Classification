Thudiyalur
May 10, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			Apparently depressed over the frequent quarrel with the husband, a 26-year-old woman committed suicide by self immolation in her house in Thudiyalur, on the outskirts, police said today.

The woman and her husband, a stock market broker, used to pick up quarrel frequently and in a fit of rage, she doused with kerosene and set afire herself last night in her house, police said.
