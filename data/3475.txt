Andhra Pradesh
Nov 05, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A government school teacher was booked on the charge of raping a 15-year-old girl student and forcibly getting her pregnancy terminated in Chilkur mandal of the district.

B Vijay Kumar, a physical training instructor at a government school in Ramapuram village had sexual intercourse with a 10th standard girl student for the past one year, according to a complaint lodged by her mother.

The matter came to light only when the girl complained of bleeding and was rushed to a hospital for treatment.

After impregnating the minor girl, the teacher gave her some pills which resulted in an abortion around three days ago.

He has been booked for rape under Sections 312 (causing miscarriage) and 420 (cheating) of the Indian Penal Code and under relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
