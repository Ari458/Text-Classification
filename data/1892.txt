Machanayakanahalli
Jan 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old mentally-challenged teenaged girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour at Machanayakanahalli in Bangalore, police said on Tuesday.

Prakash, a lorry driver, was arrested on Tuesday in connection with the case, DySP R Mallesh said.

Prakash allegedly raped the girl on Monday after taking her out of the house, Mallesh said.
