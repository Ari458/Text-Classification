Delhi Cantt
Dec 02, 2011
Sexual Harassment at Work, Other
Description:
			The Accused (Deepak) was held guilty of trying to rape a fellow worker at a construction site near Delhi Cantt in the wee hours on July 25, 2010. According to the prosecution, Deepak had caught hold of the victim when she had gone to relieve herself and as she put up resistance, Deepak punched her in her eye. He also assaulted the victim's husband, who came to her rescue, with an iron rod. 

The victim's medical report corroborated her claims of assault.
