Madhya Pradesh
Mar 03, 2012
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			The police fished out the 25-day-old new-born baby's body from the septic tank behind the house in Ghoradongri tehsil in Betul district of Madhya Pradesh. 

The family had registered a missing person report with the police. Being suspicious, the SDOP of the area Abhishek Rajan reached the spot and directed that the house including its drainage system be searched. After three hours, the baby's body was fished out of the septic tank. After continuous interrogation of family members and the domestic helps, the mother finally broke down before the police.
