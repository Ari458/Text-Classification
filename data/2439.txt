Andhra Pradesh
Jun 19, 2013
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 17-year-old student today committed suicide due to alleged sexual harassment by her teacher in Andhra Pradesh's Krishna district.

The girl, a student of intermediate second year in A P Social Welfare Residential Junior College at Khambampadu village, was fed up with the lewd messages sent by her lecturer Chandrasekhar, DSP A Shankar Reddy said.

Unable to put up with the indecent messages, the victim ended her life by consuming pesticide, he said.

Students from the college demanded that the accused be booked under provisions of Nirbhaya Act.
