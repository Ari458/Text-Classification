Goa
Aug 01, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			Panaji police have registered a case against Pramod Balekar, resident of Manser-Verem, for raping a minor girl for the last two years.

The mother of the survivor, in her plaint, stated that the accused had been sexually abusing her minor daughter since 2012, and the girl got pregnant recently. The incident came to light when the survivor complained of stomach pain to her mother. When she was taken to the hospital for a check-up, the doctor observed that the foetus had died in the minor girl's womb.

Police have registered the case under IPC Section 376 (rape), Section 8 (2) of Goa Children's Act, 2003, and Section 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012. Sources said police are in search of Balekar. The case is being probed by PSI Sachin Narvekar.
