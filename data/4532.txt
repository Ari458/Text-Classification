Muzaffarnagar
Apr 09, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was allegedly strangulated to death by her husband and in-laws in Ahrara village of the district over dowry issue.

Seema (25) was strangulated to death by her husband Ankur and in-laws, for failing to meet their dowry demands, they said.

A case has been registered against five members of the accused family including the husband, who is absconding, police said adding, body of the victim has been sent for postmortem.

Seema and Ankur got married last year in February.
