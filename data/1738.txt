Jamshedpur
Jun 24, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A girl who came to see her ailing father in a government hospital was raped allegedly by her relative inside the hospital premises here, police said today.
 
The victim was found bleeding profusely near a toilet by the staff of M G M Hospital who admitted her later on. Initially the victim was reluctant to report the incident to the police, leading to delay in lodging the FIR.
 
A medical check-up of the girl has been conducted and she was kept under observation.
