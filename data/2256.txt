Sector-49
May 18, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested today for allegedly raping a domestic help of an officer of a local educational institution at Sector-49 here, police said.

The victim, who had been hired through a placement agency by the officer around a month ago, was raped by his driver Prakash on Wednesday, they said.

Later, after she narrated the whole incident to the agency officials, they took her to police station yesterday.
