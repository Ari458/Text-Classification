Kashmir
Jul 14, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 30-year-old man was arrested on charges of raping a minor girl in north Kashmir's Kupwara district.

Showket Ahmad Malik, a resident of Hatmulla area of Kupwara, was arrested on a complaint by the victim, who alleged that he had raped her.

The 16-year-old victim was medically examined at a hospital, where doctors confirmed rape.
