Mumbai
Oct 28, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A naked corpse of an unidentified woman was found hanging on under-construction building at Nala Sopara. Police think that it is a rape-and-murder case. Police have detained three persons including the site supervisor.

Police immediately rushed to the spot and took possession of the body which was sent for a post-mortem. Speaking to DNA, Shriram Koregaonkar, senior inspector of Nala Sopara police station said it appears that the woman was first raped and murdered before her body was hung from iron rods.
