New Delhi
Mar 13, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A woman has accused her neighbour of allegedly raping her over the past few years in Chanakyapuri area.

One pretext of providing a job, the youth Pradeep, took the woman to a park and raped her in 2007, the woman alleged in the complaint.

She alleged that the accused also took photographs of the act and started blackmailing her. Pradeep allegedly continued raping her by threatening to make the pictures public for years.

Police said that Pradeep had befriended the woman as both lived in the same colony.

Source: Press Trust of India
