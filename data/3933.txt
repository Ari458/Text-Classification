Uttar Pradesh
Jan 21, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A local court has sentenced a youth to ten years imprisonment for abducting and raping a 15-year-old girl at Nirmana village in the district.

While pronouncing the sentence yesterday, District Sessions Judge Jitender Kumar also imposed a fine of Rs. 25,000 on the youth, Firoz, after holding him guilty of the crime.

The judge also directed that Rs. 20,000 out of the fine amount will be given to the victim.

According to prosecution, Firoz abducted the girl and raped her at Nirmana village in the district on January 7, 2011. Later, police rescued the girl.

Source: Press Trust of India
