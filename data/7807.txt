Tamil Nadu
Sep 11, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			The Perur All Woman Police have arrested K. Krishnasamy (72) near Pattanam on the outskirts of the city on Thursday for allegedly sexually abusing a 11-year-old girl of the locality. The police said that the incident had taken place in the late hours of Monday in a place near the girl’s house. On Thursday, the police booked Krishnasamy under Sections 7 and 8 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, based on a complaint lodged by the girl’s father.

The victim was sent to the Coimbatore Medical College Hospital for medical examination.
