Maharashtra
May 13, 2014
Murder
Description:
			In Nandkar village in Bhiwandi taluka where accused Sandeep Dinkar Rawle, 32, allegedly set ablaze his wife Sunanda, 28 using kerosene after she refused bring money from her parents' house to fulfil his demand for buying a three-wheeler.

The woman died on the spot yesterday following which a police complaint was filed by the victim's sister last night.
