Odisha
May 26, 2015
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Laikera police on Monday arrested Biswajit Meher, husband of deceased Susmita (23), of Bhuliadih village under the police limits on dowry death charges.

Susmita was found dead under mysterious circumstances on Sunday. Father of the deceased, DC Meher, alleged that his daughter was poisoned by her in-laws.

Susmita was rushed to Jharsuguda hospital where doctor declared her dead. Biswajit was forwarded to court, said Laikera Inspector-in-Charge S Mishra.
