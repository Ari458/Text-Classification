Tamil Nadu
Jul 06, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The All Women Police on Sunday arrested four persons of a family on the charge of demanding dowry. Sources said Karthi of Chinna Andan Koil was alleged to have harassed his wife Vanitha (27) demanding dowry of Rs.3 lakh. Based on her complaint, the police arrested her husband, his mother Rama (52), father Nagulsamy (54) and brother Gowtham (19).
