Odisha
Feb 16, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a 52-year-old man in Kushunpur village under Marshaghai area of Odisha's Kendrapara district, police said today.

The incident occurred on February 13 and the accused has been arrested, the said.

The child was playing alone on the backyard of her house when the accused induced her by offering chocolate and committed the offence, Sub-Divisional Police Officer, Nrusingha Charan Swain said.

The victim was medically examined after a complaint was lodged in connection with the incident which triggered public outcry in the area.
