Tamil Nadu
Mar 03, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The police have arrested a 26-year-old man on charges of waylaying and sexually assaulting a 13-year-old Dalit girl when she was returning home from school.

Police said the class IX student was walking back home after alighting from a government bus at the Killimulli bus stop on Monday evening, when the accused dragged her to a nearby ‘kanmai’ [waterbody] and assaulted her.

Police identified the accused as S. Balamurugan (26), a class X dropout and native of Thurathiyendal village. He was booked under Section 8 (sexual assault) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012, and 354 (A) (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC). He was arrested on Tuesday and remanded in judicial custody.

The accused, who travelled by the same bus, gagged the victim, dragged her to the waterbody and assaulted her, police said.

The accused fled the scene when a passerby came to her rescue hearing her screams. The girl was admitted to the Government District Headquarters Hospital here for medical examination.
