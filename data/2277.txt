Bishoa
May 25, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A case of molestation and trespassing was registered against the husband of a sarpanch here.

Hoshiar Singh, husband of the sarpanch of Bisoha village, allegedly molested his neighbour's wife after entering her house.

Residents rushed to the scene when the woman raised an alam and overpowered Singh.
