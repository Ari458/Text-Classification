West Bengal
May 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Shobha (name changed), 26 years, a deaf and dumb inmate of Welfare Home in Hooghly district was married to Bhola Moira (35) the deaf and dumb son of Mahabir Moira of Kalyani in July 9, 2012.

After her marriage to Bhola, Shobha alleged that she was repeatedly raped by her father-in-law Mahabir, an employee of the Animal Resources department and his son-in-law Bhola Paul.

Shobha informed the Welfare Home authorities about the matter. They lodged a police complaint on behalf of Shobha.

The police today raided the house of Mahabir and arrested him while Bhola was absconding.
