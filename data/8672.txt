Odisha
Aug 29, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The police on Monday arrested a priest for allegedly sexually assaulting and raping a five-year-old girl. The incident has been reported at Anduti village under Cuttack sadar police limits. The accused priest has been identified as Ratikanta Dash (35). The incident had occurred on Sunday.

Police said the minor visited the village temple every day to offer evening prayers with her grandmother. As the temple was located near her house, she visited the temple alone on Sunday at around 6pm. Finding her alone; the priest lured the girl to his house, located in the backside of the temple premises, and raped her. The priest also threatened the girl not to utter anything about the incident.

However, the girl went back to his home and complained of stomach ache and uneasiness. Family rushed the girl to nearby health centre for treatment, where the victim narrated her ordeal to her parents.

The family approached the police and lodged a complaint in the matter.

"A case was registered under Sec 376 of IPC and section of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. The accused priest was on the run after the incident, but we managed to nab him from a nearby village," said ACP Nimai Sethi.
