Tamil Nadu
Sep 19, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The Mahila Court here on Friday sentenced a 20-year-old polytechnic student to undergo 14-year imprisonment for sexually assaulting a minor girl at Chinnavalayam village near Jayamkondam last month.

The offence took place on August 1. The Jayamkondam All Women Police registered a case under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act on a complaint from the 14-year-old victim, a standard nine student. According to the prosecution, R. Rajiv Gandhi took photographs of the victim's elder sister in his mobile phone while she was taking bath in her house.

He threatened the victim that he would post the pictures of her elder sister on the web, and sexually assaulted the minor in her house. The student and the minor girl were neighbours.
