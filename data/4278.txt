Tamil Nadu
Mar 01, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A first year engineering student was allegedly raped by two of her seniors, who are twins.

The girl in her complaint lodged yesterday alleged that the two brothers, her seniors in college, had raped her on February 23 after taking her to their house at Thudiyalur.

Acting on the complaint, police today took into custody the two students for interrogation in the presence of a senior police official.

The girl's parents are in the Middle East.
