Andhra Pradesh
May 15, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A four years old girl, who had been brutally raped by a man in Santhipuram village of Kodur mandal in the district on May 12, died at Ruya hospital in Tirupati this morning.

Sub-inspector of Police Ramachandra said that Chanbasha (25), a local gangster, allegedly abducted the girl on May 12 and raped her.

Police did not confirm reports of his arrest.
