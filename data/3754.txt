Rajasthan
Dec 22, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly gang raped by three youths in a hotel.

In an FIR moved through a court's order, a woman alleged that on November 23, she was taken by Phoolchand Meena (25) in a car to a hotel in Daulatpura where he and two of his friends raped her.

The accused had threatened the victim of dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anybody.

Meena's two friends are yet to identified.
