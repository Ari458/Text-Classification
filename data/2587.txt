Odisha
Mar 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two people were arrested for allegedly outraging the modesty of a 14-year-old girl at a village in Odisha's Ganjam district.
	
Following a complaint lodged by the minor girl's
father, the two were arrested for the incident at
Katuru village.
	
The accused were identified as Y Nagameya (50) and Y Badudu (25) of the same village.
