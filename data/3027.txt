Assam
Aug 31, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped by her three brothers-in law at Murmuria Tea estate in Jorhat district.

Based on a complaint lodged at the Cinnamora outpost by the alleged victim's father, the three brothers identified as Dipak Phulmali (18), Krishna Phulmali (19) and Sujit Phulmali (22) were arrested.

The FIR claimed that the three committed the crime when their elder brother and the woman's husband was in an inebriated condition in their home at Bahoni labour line of the tea garden.
