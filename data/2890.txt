Andhra Pradesh
Aug 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman accused a man, with whom she was negotiating for a matrimonial alliance, of raping her along with three others.

The alleged incident took place at a lodge in the Shamshabad area and the woman lodged a complaint with the RGIA Shamshabad police.

However, the police refused to divulge further details including names of the accused, saying that further investigations are on.
