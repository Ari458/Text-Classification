Maharashtra
Jun 23, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A Scientific Assistant (SA) of the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC), Tarapur, has been arrested for allegedly raping a woman by promising her marriage.

Anirudh Sehgal (37) met the Malad based woman, aged 35, through a marriage website. He introduced himself as a bachelor, though he is already married. His wife was in Roha, Rajasthan to take care of his ailing parents.

After finding the online profile of Sehgal suitable, the woman met him.

Sehgal met her family and decided to get married soon. They had a sexual relationship.

The woman, however, learnt that Sehgal was already married. She complained to the Tarapur police. Sehgal has been arrested under Section 376 (rape) of the IPC. He has been remanded to police custody till June 27.
