Tamil Nadu
Oct 25, 2015
Domestic Violence
Description:
			The police on Saturday arrested Alexander (48) of Jagir Redipatti on the charge of harassing his wife.

The police said that Alexander was working as senior manager at Aavin while his wife Parithavani (44) was working as professor at Government Medical College in Dharmapuri.

Alexander, in an inebriated condition beat her causing injuries to her.

Based on her complaint, the All Woman Police Station in Suramangalam registered a case under Section 498A (husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty), 506 (i) (criminal intimation) of IPC and Section 4 of Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Harassment of Women Act.
