West Bengal
Jan 07, 2014
Murder
Description:
			Three women of a family were found murdered in their flat in Beharampore town in Murshidabad district.

The local people informed police that the family was not opening the door or responding even after repeated calls.

The police then went to the flat and broke open the door and found the bodies of three women - Bijaya Basu, her aunt Prabha Das and daughter Aishree Basu.

Preliminary investigation reveals that they were strangulated to death.

Local people alleged that the woman and her daughter were raped before murdering them.

Only after we get postmortem report, we can say whether they were raped or not. 

Bijaya Basu's husband has not seen by the local people for last one year.
