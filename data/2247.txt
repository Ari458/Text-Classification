Abdullahpur
May 19, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A teenage girl has alleged she was repeatedly abused sexually by a youth, who used to blackmail her with a video of rape he had committed after abducting her.

The 16-year-old, allegedly abducted three months back, managed to escape from captivity on Friday and narrated the incident to parents.

They alleged that Neeraj, a resident of Abdullahpur locality, abducted the girl three months back, raped her, and made a video of the crime, using which he would blackmail her, he said.
