Maharashtra
Apr 22, 2014
Rape
Description:
			After two years of love affair, a 25-year-old girl alleged rape by her former boyfriend when he decided to marry another girl. Police said the complainant had called off the relationship a few months back.

Wadi police have arrested Jackson Bhaje, 28, a resident of Defence Quarters, Wadi, following the complaint. Police said the complainant developed physical relationship with her boyfriend during their affair. They had decided to marry after finding a good job for both of them. Sources said the complainant had eloped with Bhaje around six months back. Both stayed together for three months but later decided to end their relationship due to some differences.

Few months back, Bhaje got engaged to another girl. It is learnt, Bhaje had no contact with the complainant since three months.
