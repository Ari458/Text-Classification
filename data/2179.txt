Jaunpur
May 10, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A man was sentenced to life imprisonment by a local court here for molesting his seven-year-old grand daughter.

According to prosecution, on June 4, 2008, Shivkumar molested his grand daughter.

On the basis of the evidences, District Judge Rakesh Kumar held Shivkumar guilty yesterday and awarded life imprisonment to him.
