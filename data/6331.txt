Bihar
Jan 31, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth at Subhai village under Hajipur Sadar police station in Vaishali district, Sadar Station House Officer (SHO) Sunil Kumar said.

The traumatised girl has been hospitalised in serious condition in the government hospital there and a medical test will be done on her, Kumar said.

The minor was alone at her house as her mother had gone to cut grass for cattle fodder, when the accused neighbour Krishna Kumar barged into her house and raped her, the SHO said quoting the FIR.

The accused is absconding.
