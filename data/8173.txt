Punjab
Dec 19, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The police have registered a case against a resident of Balla Ram Nagar for demanding dowry.

In a complaint to the police, Daljit Kaur of Balla Ram Nagar said her husband had been demanding dowry ever since their marriage. 

Investigating Officer Balwinder Singh said a case under Section 498-A of the IPC has been registered against Mahinderpal, a resident of Kashmirewali Gali. No arrests have been made so far.
