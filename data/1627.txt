Kanpur Dehat
Apr 20, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was gangraped and later hanged to death in Ramabai Nagar. Indrapal, father of the victim told the police that three men of his Mehtha Mada village, Raju, Ghanshyam and Lalau alias Ram Autar, had abducted his daughter. 

The police found the girl's body hanging inside Raju's house. The autopsy confirmed that she wasgang raped before hanged.
