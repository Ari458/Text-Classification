Karnataka
Jul 26, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Rape
Description:
			Udayagiri police arrested a paedophile for allegedly sexually abusing a minor girl on Sunday.

When the 9-year-old girl had gone to her friend's house to play, the latter's uncle allegedly molested her. The incident came to light after the girl's mother suddenly entered the house in the neighbourhood.

Abdul Rahil, a resident of Udayagiri, has been booked under the provisions of IPC and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (Pocso) Act. He has been remanded to judicial custody.
