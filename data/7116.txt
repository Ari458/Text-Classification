Tamil Nadu
Jun 04, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A. Palani (63) of Fifth Street, VOC Nagar, Kovilpatti in the district, was arrested on Wednesday on the charge of sexually harassing a three-year-old girl.

According to police, the incident occurred on May 28. The Kovilpatti All-Women Police filed a case against the accused under Sections 4, 6 and 8 of Prevention of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 on Tuesday night based on a complaint lodged by Rajeshwari (33), mother of the girl.
