Puducherry
Jul 11, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A doctor has been booked under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO).

P. Rajendran, an Urologist & Laparoscopic surgeon, of Madhava Hospital in Nellithope has been accused of molesting a 14-year-old girl.

Orleanpet police have filed an FIR against him under the POCSO Act on Saturday night.

The 14-year-old girl had gone to the hospital with her cousin to consult a doctor for skin allergy. Dr. Rajendran after asking the girl’s cousin to stay outside allegedly took the girl in the pretext of examining her. The doctor is said to have abused the girl in the room.

The girl started crying and immediately left the hospital and informed her parents.

Outraged over the abuse of their girl, the parents ransacked the hospital on Saturday night. The police arrived at the hospital and took the complaint against the doctor. An FIR was registered on Saturday night. The doctor is said to be absconding.

Child Welfare Committee (CWC) Chairperson Vidyaa Ramkumar said that the girl was taken for medical examination.

She was inconsolably crying out of fear and looked visibly disturbed. She was taken to a hospital for counselling.
