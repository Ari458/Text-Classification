West Bengal
Jul 29, 2014
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A woman employee of a nursing home here has lodged a complaint with the police that she had been raped for the past seven to eight years by its owner. 

The woman filed a complaint with the police yesterday and has made her deposition before the magistrate today. 

Police said prima facie no evidence was found against the owner after going through CCTV footage, which showed no presence of any woman in his chamber.
