Gujarat
Jul 29, 2016
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A primary school principal in Bandarpura village of Narmada district was booked on Thursday for making several attempts to molest a female teacher of his school.

The teacher lodged an FIR at Tilakwada police station against principal Dilip Gohil accusing him of sexual harassment over the past 18 months.

According to her complaint, she rejected his advance a number of times and also complained about his behaviour to district education officer (DEO), but no action was taken against him. Following her complaint to DEO, Gohil started misbehaving with her even in the classroom in the presence of students.
