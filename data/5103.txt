Uttar Pradesh
Jul 16, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly gang-raped in the district, police said.

The first incident took place on Monday night in Sarurpur area when the 14-year old girl had gone to fetch water, following which she was abducted by four youths at gun-point and taken to a jungle where they raped her.

The girl was left in an unconscious state, police said, adding she returned home after gaining consciousness and narrated the incident to her parents who lodged a complaint with the police yesterday.

A case has been lodged against the accused identified as Yogesh, Rahul and their two accomplices after a medical examination of the victim.
