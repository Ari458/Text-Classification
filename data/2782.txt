Bihar
Jul 21, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			The body of a 12-year old girl, suspected to have been sexually assaulted, was recovered near a police station in Bihar's Rohtas district, triggering violent protests in the area.

A mob, angry over recovery of the body of the victim, burnt some police jeeps and jammed Dehri-Nasriganj road for several hours with the body.

The police recovered the body of the girl by the roadside near the police station.

The girl was missing since a few days.

Prima facie it appeared that the girl was sexually assaulted and her body thrown on the roadside by perpetrators of the crime.
