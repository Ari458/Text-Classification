Karnataka
Jul 19, 2014
Rape
Description:
			An 86-year-old man has allegedly raped his daughter-in-law at Mallapura village in the district and is reported absconding, police said.

The incident took place three months ago, but the woman was threatened by her father-in-law not to disclose it, police said, quoting her complaint.

The woman has also named in the complaint her mother-in-law and brother-in-law for having colluded with the accused in suppressing the issue, police said.

Her husband meanwhile had married another woman after having come to know of the incident, police said.
