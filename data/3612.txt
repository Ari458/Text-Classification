Uttar Pradesh
Dec 02, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A girl was kidnapped and gang-raped by two youth of the same village when she was returning from the field.

The police said that a 14-year-old girl of a village in Sirsia went to the field along with her friend. Accused Vishu and Govind of the same village dragged her to the field when she was returning and raped her while her friend ran to the house and narrated about the incident to the family members. A case of gang-rape has been registered with Sirsia police. The accused have been arrested.
