Rajasthan
Aug 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man allegedly raped his 3-year old daughter in Nahargarh road area of the city.

Ganesh, a labourer, took his daughter outside the house while his wife was asleep and then raped the child. After committing the crime, he left his daughter outside the house.

In the wee hours, when some passersby found the injured girl they admitted her to J K Lone hospital.

When the child's mother woke up and did not find her daughter in the house, she approached police.

During questioning, the child's father confessed to his crime.
