Kerala
Jan 26, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			An autorickshaw driver has been booked for allegedly harassing two Russian tourists recently in Varkala here.

The tourists had filed a complaint before the Kerala Women's commission alleging that the auto rickshaw driver had manhandled and harassed them on January 22.

The tourists, Irena Zykina and Natalia, hailing from Siberia, had booked an auto rickshaw but when they were about to board it another driver prevented them from doing so and allegedly manhandled and harassed one of them, a press release from the commission said.
