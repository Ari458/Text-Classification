Marehra
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a 20-year-old youth in Hayatpurmafi village in the district, police said today.

The 15-year-old girl, who had came to the village to attend a marriage ceremony, was raped by the accused, identified as Devendra while she was sleeping on the terrace, they said.

On hearing the cries of the girl, her family members rushed and caught hold of the accused and handed him over to the police.
