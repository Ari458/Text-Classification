Uttar Pradesh
Feb 18, 2014
Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			Two youths in early 20s were booked by police for allegedly harassing two women. Duo from Indiranagar intercepted the girls near Jiyamau petrol pump around 2pm.

The girl alleged that she was walking with her woman friend when Mahendra Singh Rana and Saahil Pratap Singh started stalking them across busy Lohia Path. The complainant alleged that duo held her hand and then slapped her when she asked them to leave her alone.

Later, two girls reached Gautampalli police station and lodged an FIR against the duo. Police pressed IPC sections amounting to sexual harassment and causing hurt violently and detained the two. Mahendra's father works with tourism department while Saahil's father had expired.
