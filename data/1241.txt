Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Doctor Kapil Dere married Shraddha, also a doctor, last October. According to the complaint lodged by Shraddha, her in-laws started harassing her for various reasons within a few days. Her husband allegedly asked her to bring Rs 50 lakh to set up a clinic, and the harassment grew as she could not arrange the money.
