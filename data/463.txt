Delhi
Oct 16, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl alleged that four youths had attempted to rape her after beating her up in the Aman Vihar area of Outer Delhi on Saturday. 

Police officials said that the suspects live in the same neighborhood as the girl. 

The incident took place at around 11pm on Saturday in the Hind Vihar area in Prem Nagar when the victim was standing near her house.
