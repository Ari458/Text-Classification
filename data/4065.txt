Jharkhand
Feb 04, 2014
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman, Annu Devi, and her one-year-old baby girl was reportedly burnt alive by her husband and parents-in-law in Jharkhand's Dumka district.

The incident took place on January 30. The parents of Annu Devi have alleged dowry-related harassment by the deceased woman's in-laws.

On January 30, the three suspects allegedly poured kerosene on Annu Devi and the baby girl and set them on fire. While the infant died on the spot, the mother succumbed to her injures later in a hospital.
