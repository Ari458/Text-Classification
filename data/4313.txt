New Delhi
Mar 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly molested by a temple priest at Bateshwar town.

The girl, who sells wood-apple (belpatra) leaves near the temple, was allegedly lured to a lonely place and molested by the priest.

The priest has been identified as Ajit Goswami.

The girl, however, managed to escape from the clutches of the priest and inform her parents, who along with locals gathered in front of the temple.

Source: Press Trust of India
