Bihar
Oct 24, 2013
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman, identified as Kalpana Devi, was strangulated to death by her husband Naresh Mandal in Rampur Mohanpur village under Bargachi police station reportedly after she refused to meet dowry demands.

The body has been sent for post-mortem.

The victim's father has lodged a named FIR against his son in-law and other members of his family charging them with the murder of his daughter for dowry, the accused persons were absconding.
