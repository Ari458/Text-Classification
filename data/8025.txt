Puducherry
Oct 24, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Villianur all-women police have booked a police constable attached to Indian reserve battalion (IRBn) on charges of harassing his wife demanding dowry on Thursday in the Union territory of Puducherry. 

Police have launched a hunt for the constable who has been identified as R Raja, 29 from Korkadu, who absconded after learning that a case has been registered against him. 

Police registered a case following a direction from the women's commission. R Jayabharathi, 19 in her complaint said she married the constable in January this year. 

She claimed that her husband has been harassing her demanding more dowry. She said Raja brutally assaulted her on Wednesday when she refused to fulfill his demand for more dowry from her parents. 

She also said she approached the police. However police officials held reconciliatory talks and sent her back with her husband. 

Police booked Raja under Sections 498-A (husband or relative of husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty) and 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt) of the Indian penal code and began investigations.
