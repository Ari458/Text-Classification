Delhi
Mar 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a juvenile in Anand Parbat area.

According to the complaint filed by the family members of the girl, the 16-year-old accused lured the victim to his house on the pretext of buying her some candies.

The juvenile was caught red-handed by the girl's family members who rushed to the spot after hearing her cries for help, a police official said.

The juvenile, a school dropout, has been sent to a correction home for boys.
