Uttar Pradesh
Dec 28, 2013
Murder, Crime
Description:
			A man allegedly killed his two daughters and injured his wife over a family dispute in Rewati.

The accused Govardhan attacked his wife Chandramuni with a sharp-edged weapon and when his daughters Rishu (12) and Rinki (10) attempted to intervene he injured them seriously.

The trio were rushed to the district hospital where Rishu and Rinki died, his wife is still battling for her life.
