Jammu
Oct 10, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman in Rajouri district of Jammu and Kashmir has alleged that she was raped by her neighbour a few days back.

In her complaint lodged, she alleged that the man barged into her house in Lam village and after finding her alone, he assaulted her sexually.

A medical examination of the woman was conducted following the complaint, and the report is awaited.
