Punjab
Oct 27, 2013
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman and her son were arrested, while three other family members also booked for alleged dowry harassment.

In her FIR, the victim had accused her husband Vikas, a resident of Patiala, and four members of his family of harassing her for dowry.

While Vikas and his mother Neelam are under custody, the other three accused his father and two sisters, are yet to be arrested.

The woman also accused Vikas of being in an illicit relationship, an allegation he denied.
