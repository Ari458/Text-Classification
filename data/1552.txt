Panaji
Mar 25, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A Police Sub-Inspector (PSI) Terrance Vaz in Goa has been booked for allegedly harassing his wife Evelyn for dowry. 

Victim has alleged that Vaz had been regularly asking for money from her parents ever since they got married five years ago. The PSI would be arrested only after a proper investigation into the complaint, Police added
