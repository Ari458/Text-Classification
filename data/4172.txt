Uttar Pradesh
Feb 17, 2014
Abduction, Other
Description:
			Six school children, who had gone to a marriage function in village Sojru, went missing in the district.

Rizwan (17), Arif (15), Akib (16), Salman (12), Ashif (11), Saleem (12) and Firoz (15) had gone to attend the marriage function yesterday, police said.

A complaint has been registered by the parents of the children, all students of Class X and XII, in this regard, they said.

Source: Press Trust of India
