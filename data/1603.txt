Delhi
Apr 13, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 14-year-old domestic help has alleged that she was beaten up and sexually assaulted by her employer identified as Nadeem (25) in Jamia Nagar area of Delhi.

A senior police official said that she was thrashed by Nadeem and the girl went to the neighbour for help. When the neighbour intervened, there was a fight between him and the girl's employer. The neighbour informed police following which the girl was taken for medical examination which confirmed that she was raped and physically assaulted. 

The official claimed that the girl was raped two years ago for the first time allegedly by Nadeem, who threatened her with dire consequences if she revealed it to anyone. A week ago she was raped again.
