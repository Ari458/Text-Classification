Tamil Nadu
May 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Valliyoor All Women Police have arrested a teenager on the charge of sexually assaulting an eight-year-old girl.

Police said a 14-year-old boy from a village near Valliyoor sexually assaulted his neighbour, an eight-year-old girl, when she was alone in her house on Saturday. As the girl screamed for help, others rushed to the spot, but he escaped. Based on the complaint from the girl’s mother with All Women Police Station at Valliyoor, the boy was arrested.
