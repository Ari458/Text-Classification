Andhra Pradesh
Mar 10, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Perturbed over alleged dowry harassment by her husband, a 30-year-old dalit woman R Dhanamma allegedly committed suicide by jumping into a well at Rajugudem village in Vemsur mandal in Andhra Pradesh.

It is suspected that she resorted to the drastic step unable to cope with the constant harassment by her husband Krishnaiah for additional dowry. 

According to sources, Dhanamma jumped into a well situated in the rear side of their house around midnight on Wednesday. Her body was found in the well in the early hours of Thursday.
