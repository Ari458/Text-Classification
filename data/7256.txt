Tamil Nadu
Jun 29, 2015
Domestic Violence
Description:
			The District Rural Police have charged five persons of a family with harassing and assaulting a 70-year-old woman.

According to the police, T. Rajeswari, a resident of Palapatty near Mettupalayam, had a quarrel with her relative Jayaraj (45) a few days ago.

Following this, Jayaraj and four of his family members had an altercation with Rajeswari on Saturday around 9 a.m. They allegedly abused and assaulted her. Rajeswari is undergoing treatment at a private hospital in Mettupalayam.

Based on her complaint, the Mettupalayam police registered a case and invoked Sections 147 (rioting), 294 (b)(utter obscene words in public place) and 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) of the Indian Penal Code read with Section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Women Harassment Act. Further investigations are on.
