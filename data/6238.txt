Maharashtra
Jan 17, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A Police Constable has been booked for allegedly raping a woman.

Ratnakar Ugalmugle, posted with district police headquarters here, was known to the victim, a widow, and often visited her home, police said today.

Ugalmugle had promised to marry her, but did not keep his word, they said.

According to a complaint lodged by the victim last night, he sexually assaulted her several times during the last about 20-25 days.

An offence of rape has been registered against the Constable, Tarapur Police Station in-charge Durgesh Shelar said.

Ugalmugle is yet to be arrested.
