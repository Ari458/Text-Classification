Uttar Pradesh
Jan 15, 2015
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman was found dead under mysterious circumstances at her residence in Pratap Vihar colony this morning, police said.

The body of Meera was found hanging from the ceiling of her house, police said.

The parents of the victim have filed a complaint with the police, alleging that she was murdered by her husband and in-laws as she did not fulfill their dowry demands, they said.

She was married to Mohit nine months ago, police said, adding, he and his father have been arrested and an FIR under section 304 B (dowry death) of IPC has been registered.

The body has been sent for post-mortem.
