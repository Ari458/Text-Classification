Haryana
Dec 19, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly repeatedly raped by her father for nearly two months in the Tilpat area of the district.

A case in this regard was registered with the Sarai Kwaja police on the complaint of the girl's mother.

In the complaint, the mother has alleged that when she returned from work in the evening, her minor daughter told her that Rajesh had been raping her for two months. Rajesh would allegedly rape the girl in the daytime when her mother would be in office.

The woman claimed when she spoke to Rajesh about it, he beat her up, after which she approached the police.
