Sopore
Mar 27, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			A Jammu and Kashmir Minister's nephew has been arrested on the charges of sexual harassment in Sopore area of Baramulla district. 

Two girls had on Sunday lodged a complaint against Gowhar Habib, the nephew of Minister of State for Roads and Buildings Javed Ahmad Dar, alleging that he had misbehaved with them, police said. 

The allegations by the girls, who are sisters, led to local residents holding protest against Gowhar, demanding his immediate arrest.
