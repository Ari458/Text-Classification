Tamil Nadu
Aug 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old-man was arrested on charges of sexually assaulting a two-and-half-year-old girl at Karimangalam here on Friday.

According to the police, the girl was left to sleep in the house, while her mother, a flower seller, was yet to return home. The neighbours heard the girl raising an alarm. They saw the accused, Manikandan, running out of the house. He was caught by passers-by and handed over to the police.
