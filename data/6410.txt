Rajasthan
Feb 11, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 50-year-old woman was today found dead inside her house in Kanchanpur area here, police said.

Ramdulari Devi was found lying in a pool of blood, they said, adding, prima facie she was shot dead by unknown persons.

The body has been handed over to respective family members after port-mortem, police said.
