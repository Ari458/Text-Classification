Odisha
Jan 31, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Two persons (Md. Arman and Md. Zahid Ansari) were arrested in Sundargarh's Rajgangpur for raping a minor.

The youths had trapped the girl and taken her to a lonely place around two months ago. While one raped her, the other recorded the same in his cellphone. About four days ago, the footage was circulated in the locality giving rise to a hue and cry.
