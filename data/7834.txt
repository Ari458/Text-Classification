Tamil Nadu
Sep 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime
Description:
			Eraniel police registered a case against a Cable TV operator for sexual abusing a 13-year-old girl on Thursday.

According to the police, A. Sathish of Saralvilai area in Villukkuri village took the minor girl from a bus stand while she was waiting to board the bus to her school after lying to her that her mother had fallen sick on August 4. Sathish took the girl to her residence and took away an ATM card and Rs. 20,000 from an almirah.

Then he took her to Tirunelveli, where he kept her in a house for a day and sexually abused her.

Sathish relieved the girl of a two-sovereign gold chain and three gold rings, left her at Villukkuri bus stand and fled the spot on August 5, the police said.

Based on the complaint lodged by the girl’s mother, Eraniel police have registered a case under Sections 8 and 9 (N) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012, and Sections 366 (A), 384 and 506 (ii) of the Indian Penal Code.

The police are on the lookout for the accused.
