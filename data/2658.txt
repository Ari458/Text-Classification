Gujarat
Mar 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 27-year-old martial arts instructor has been arrested for raping two minor girls.
	
Makwana, a resident of Sabarmati locality, has been booked under Section 376 (rape) and 328 (causing hurt by means of poison etc, with intent to commit an offence) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
	
The girls registered a complaint in this regard at Chandkheda police station.  The girls, who were also learning martial arts, were returning from Delhi by train when the accused met them and advised them to take up martial arts. According to police, he also promised to make them instructors soon.
	
While one girl was from Ahmedabad, another one was from Anand district.
	
The girls arrived in the city on March 24 and they were put up at Makwana's friend's flat in Chandkheda area of the city.
	
The duo were then offered cold drinks
laced with sedatives and allegedly raped by the youth.
	
When the girls came to know about the incident, they informed their head coach before proceeding to file a police
complaint.
	
The victims have been sent to Civil Hospital for medical examination.
