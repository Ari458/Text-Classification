Shillong
Jan 25, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			The incident took place when members of a dance troupe comprising five girls and four boys, visited residential quarters inside the Meghalaya Raj Bhavan to discuss a dance programme for Republic Day.

According to the FIR, Gopakumar- the aide-de-camp (ADC) to Meghalaya Governor called her to his room saying that he wanted to give something to her and allegedly molested her while a teacher of the troupe was waiting outside and others were sitting in the dining room.

Gopakumar said the girl lodged the FIR as the troupe was denied participation in the Republic Day function. He said he called the girl inside to to give her his visiting card.
