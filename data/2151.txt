Bandhgachhi
May 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 55-year-old adivasi woman was allegedly gangraped by three youths of her own village in Burdwan district today, Superintendent of Police SMH Mirza said.

The incident occurred when the woman, resident of Bandhgachhi village under Kalna police station, went to a field to relieve herself in the morning.

The three accused dragged her to a bush, raped her one by one and fled.

The two were identified as Sanjib Tudu and Subodh Hembram.
