Uttar Pradesh
Jul 03, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A middle-aged man was arrested for allegedly raping his step-daughter for the last several years. The 17-year-old survivor visited the Chibramau police of Kannauj district along with her maternal uncles and lodged a case against the accused.

"The man's first wife had died 12 months ago. He later married victim's mother, who too died within a year," the police said.

"For few days, the accused behaved decently but later started sexually exploiting her following which she became pregnant. Later, he forced her to undergo abortion at a private nursing home in Chibramau," the officials added.

After she narrated her woes to her maternal uncles, they approached the police. The Chibramau police station registered a case under sections 376, 323 and 506 (2) (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code and under sections 4 (penetrative assault), 8 (sexual assault), 12 (sexual harassment) of the Protection Of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO), 2012.

"We have arrested the accused and two women staff of the private nursing home. The girl's detailed statement has been recorded by women police personnel. The girl's medical test has been conducted," said Chibramau station in-charge Rajnesh Singh.

Investigation officer said, "The accused often used to sexually exploit the girl. He also threatened her with dire consequences if she told anyone about her exploitation."
