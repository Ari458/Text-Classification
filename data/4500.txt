New Delhi
Apr 01, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A 25-year old Somalian woman has accused her compatriot, who was known to her, of trying to rape her in North Delhi's Timarpur area following which he was arrested. 

The incident took place when the victim had gone to meet the accused, whom she knew for quite some time, at his residence. 

As per the victim's statement, the accused Mahad Yusuf Nawale (22) tried to rape her when she was at his residence. She somehow managed to flee from his clutches after hitting him with something. 

She then approached police and filed a case of attempt to rape in this connection at Timarpur police station.
