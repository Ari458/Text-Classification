Maharashtra
Dec 07, 2013
Murder
Description:
			Police are on lookout for a man who allegedly killed his young wife and dumped her body in Malshej ghat in this district.

Santoshsingh Rajput is suspected of murdering his wife Divya (23) between late August and early December and dumping her body in the ghat.

The couple resided in Pragati Nagar in the western part of the district. Divya's family stays in Vashi, Navi Mumbai.

Rajput was apparently annoyed with Divya as she had not bailed him out when he was arrested in a criminal case sometime ago.
