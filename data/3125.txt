Delhi
Sep 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old was raped while she was visiting her aunt's house in Sonia Gandhi camp near Naraina in southwest Delhi on Sunday.

The incident took place around 8.30pm when the child was playing alone outside her aunt's home. A man reportedly lured the girl to a nearby park and raped her. 

When the girl's family did not find her outside the house, they began looking for her frantically. She was later found lying in the park and was rushed to Deen Dayal Upadhyay Hospital. 

Source: Times of India
