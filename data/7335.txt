Haryana
Jul 09, 2015
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 32-year-old woman was killed allegedly by her in-laws over dowry in Sirohi village here, police said today. According to a complaint filed by the victim's father, the woman was killed yesterday by her husband and in-laws.

Police said the woman, who was married to Azharuddinin 2010, was subjected to physical and mental torture by her in-laws who demanded dowry from her. 

A case has been registered against three persons, including the victim's husband, under relevant sections of the IPC, SHO Inspector Saifuddin said, adding, the matter is being investigated.
