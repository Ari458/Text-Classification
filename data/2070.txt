Rajasthan
Apr 27, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			The owner of a private school has been arrested for allegedly raping two minor girls and giving them poison leading to their deaths, police said today.

Rajesh Dhangad was arrested last night for allegedly raping the girls, both students of class VIII at his school, on April 11 and then poisoning the duo when they threatened to disclose the matter to their parents, they said.

The girls succumbed later that day at separate hospitals where they were admitted upon falling unconscious after returning to their respective homes, police said.
