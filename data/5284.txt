Punjab
Aug 12, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A girl was allegedly abducted by two men who tried to rape her in Fathehpur Madar village. 

On the complaint of the 17-year-old girl, police have booked two people, Amarjeet Singh and Jasbir Singh. 

No arrest has been made in this connection so far, police said, adding that they were investigating the matter.
