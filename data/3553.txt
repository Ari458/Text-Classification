Madhya Pradesh
Nov 20, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A court awarded life sentence to a man for the rape of a six-year old tribal girl.

Judge R P Sharma also imposed fine of Rs 7,000 on Mohd Sadik (38), the accused.

The prosecution examined ten witnesses in the case.

The incident had taken place in Badgonda police station limits on June 5, 2011. The girl was raped at her house when her parents were away at work.
