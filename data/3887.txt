Haryana
Jan 12, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man was booked for allegedly raping a 16-year-old girl at knife-point.

Kalicharan had allegedly raped the girl, the daughter of his tenant, at knife-point when she was alone at her house in Parvatiya Colony on January 3.

According to her father, the family had gone out when the Kalicharan committed the crime and threatened the girl with dire consequences if she revealed the matter to anyone.

The police did not file a case immediately, and did so only when they threatened to go to the media.

A case has been filed based on the statement of the girl, and her medical checkup was done.

Source: Press Trust of India
