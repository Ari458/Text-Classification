Karnataka
Oct 15, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The police have taken into custody a security guard of Karnataka Institute of Medical Sciences here, on the charge of molesting a woman on Wednesday.

The Vidyanagar Police who registered a case, said Chandrashekhar Ningappa Hadapad molested a woman who had come to the hospital to stay with a patient.

The accused has been produced before the local court, the police said.
