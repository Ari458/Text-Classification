Ghaziabad
Aug 13, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A self-styled godman was arrested on Wednesday from Ghaziabad for raping a 23-year-old girl on the pretext of curing her of her ailments. The man was identified as Abdulla Musharaf. 

The girl said she had gone to meet Musharaf with a friend who had assured her that he would cure her of her ailments. But she was confined to Musharaf's house for 19 days and raped repeatedly .Police are now looking for the girl's friend. A case of rape has been registered at Shakarpur police station.
