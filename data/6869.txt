Tamil Nadu
May 07, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 45-year-old man was arrested by the police for sexually assaulting a 10-year-old girl in Tharamangalam here on Wednesday.

The police said that the Class V girl was on her way to a relative’s house on Tuesday evening when Vijayakumar of the same area intercepted her and took her to his house. He sexually assaulted the girl and later she escaped from the house. She informed her parents about the incident. Her parents lodged a complaint with the Tharamangalam Police, who forwarded the complaint to All Women Police Station in Omalur. Vijayakumar was arrested and produced in the court and lodged at Salem Central Prison.
