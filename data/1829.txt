Dombivali
Nov 13, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Yatin Patkar (31), along with his mother Nutan (62), was booked under various sections of Indian Penal Code (IPC) including 498-A (husband or relative of husband of a woman subjecting her to cruelty) among others on the complaint of his 25-year-old wife, they said.

The woman, an engineering graduate, was married to Yatin a year ago, Dombivali police sub-inspector Rohini Sonar said.
