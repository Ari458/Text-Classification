Goa
Jul 12, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl, who had been reportedly kidnapped, managed to free herself from the clutches of her abductors, who took her to Hubli in Karnataka.

The Collem police found the girl travelling back to Goa in a Karnataka state transport bus and was later handed over to her parents.

The girl was found in the Karnataka state transport bus near Goa-Karnataka border.

She had been reportedly kidnapped on July 10 by a Panaji-based man, who had taken her to the neighbouring state.
