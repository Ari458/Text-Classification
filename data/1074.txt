Street Harassment
Description:
			A school-going girl was being regularly harassed by a 19-year-old boy. "He used to tease me every now and then whenever I went out of my house," said the victim. The accused humiliated her by singing film songs, passing cheap comments and even chasing her. According to police, when the girl was returning to her house on Tuesday evening, Pawan started teasing again. "The girl grabbed him by the shirt and started beating him," onlookers said. She threw him down on the road and started kicking him.
