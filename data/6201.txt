Uttar Pradesh
Jan 09, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			An Afghan national was today arrested on charges of allegedly eve-teasing a co-passenger in New-Delhi-Howrah Rajdhani express train, police said.

A woman alleged that Izzat Khan, hailing from Kabul in Afghanistan, was travelling with her in coach A-2 and he misbehaved with her and eve-teased her as the train departed from Allahabad Railway station, Mughalsarai GRP Inspector, Ratan Singh Yadav said.

He was arrested when the train reached Mughalsarai station, Yadav said, adding, an FIR has been registered under various IPC sections against Khan and further investigation is underway.
