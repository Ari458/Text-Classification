Khajuwala
May 22, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			The woman, a resident of the district's Khajuwala area, has alleged that her father-in-law, Deeparam, had first raped her two years back when her husband was out of station.

"He had been raping the woman for the last two years and threatened to exclude his son (her husband) from his property if she disclosed (the matter) to anyone," police said.
