Rourkela
Mar 07, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Four persons of a family were arrested for dowry torture and attempt to murder, police said on Tuesday. 

Sasmita's brother Satyanaryan Pradhan in a complaint with police alleged that his sister who had married Balmiki Nayak (32) of sector-6 area of the steel town ship on March 3, 2009 and was mother of a one and half year old daughter was subjected to mental and physical torture by her husband and other in-law's demanding more dowry.
