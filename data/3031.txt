Andhra Pradesh
Sep 01, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Three youths were arrested from Abids area on the charge of allegedly raping a minor girl.

Ravi, Bablu and Khannu, the arrested youths, allegedly raped the girl many times over a period of five months, as per the complaint filed by her family members.

The family also alleged that the girl was impregnated as a result.
