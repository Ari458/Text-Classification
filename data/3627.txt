Maharashtra
Dec 01, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A tribal man allegedly killed his wife as he was miffed with her for not being able to conceive during their 15 years of marriage.

The couple, residing in a hut at a farm in Dahanu taluka of Thane district, had frequent quarrels over the issue and because of it they were not on good terms.

During another quarrel on the issue, Bharat Ladak Bharavi (35) brutally attacked his wife Sangeeta (around 30 years of age) with an axe on her face, stomach and other body parts leading to her death.

The accused then fled from the spot.

Police registered an offence against Bharat under section 302 (murder) of the IPC.
