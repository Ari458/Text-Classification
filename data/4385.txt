New Delhi
Mar 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by two juveniles in her neighbourhood in Kanjhawala area of outer Delhi.

According to the complaint filed by the victim's parents, the incident took place on March 11 when the girl was playing outside her house.

"The victim was playing when the two boys took her to a secluded area where they allegedly raped her," a police officer said.

The girl later narrated her ordeal to her parents, who then approached the police.

Source: Press Trust of India
