Maharashtra
Jun 17, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Samit Satibabne (26), from Paoni village in Bhandara district, who allegedly sexually assaulted a 17-year-old girl between September, 2013, and January, 2014, at Dighori village of Nagpur and refused to marry her when she became pregnant.

Following a complaint, Samit was arrested yesterday.
