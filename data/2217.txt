Qadian
May 14, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl has been abducted and raped allegedly by a youth in Qadian area of Gurdaspur district.

On Sunday night the girl was abducted allegedly by Satnam Singh alias Satta, according to the complaint filed by the victim's mother.

The accused took her to a nearby tube well and repeated raped her.

Seeing the serious condition of her daughter, the victim's mother approached the police, who later rushed the girl to civil hospital, Qadian, where the doctors referred her to Batala.
