Tamil Nadu
Dec 15, 2013
Abduction
Description:
			A seven-member gang was arrested for allegedly kidnapping a woman near.

The 26-year-old woman was allegedly kidnapped by her relative Bhupesh,who wanted to marry her though the woman and her parents were not willing.

She was kidnapped when she returning after visiting a local temple on the Maravamangalam-Ilayankudi road with her friends.

She was forcibly pushed into a van but her friends alerted police who managed to intercept the vehicle at Kaikatti.
