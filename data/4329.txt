Uttar Pradesh
Mar 06, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A college student was allegedly gang-raped by two youths in her neighbourhood at gunpoint in Surana village.

According to a complaint filed by the family members of the girl, who is a student of BTech at a college here, claimed when she was going to purchase something at a local shop, two youths from her neighbourhood abducted her.

After that they allegedly gang raped her at gunpoint and also threatened to kill her if she revealed anything about the incident.

Upon hearing the commotion some locals gathered there and rescued the girl and handed her over to her parents.


Source: Press Trust of India
