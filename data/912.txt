Mehrauli
Dec 13, 2011
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The incident took place at around 3 a.m. Tuesday in Mehrauli area when the victim after finishing her shift in a pub hired the Indigo cab to take her to her residence in south Delhi. However, the driver took her to a deserted spot in Chattarpur area in south Delhi and raped her.He later dropped her at an isolated spot and threatened her with dire consequences if she reported the matter to police" said a police official.
