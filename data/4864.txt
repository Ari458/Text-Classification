Uttar Pradesh
Jun 12, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her mother's paramour in Chandpur area of the district.

The victim was allegedly raped by Ram Vishal at her residence last night, Station House Officer, Chandpur, Angad Pratap Singh, said.

He said the girl narrated the incident to her father this morning, after which a case was lodged and Ram Vishal was arrested.
