Haryana
May 23, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment, Rape
Description:
			A married woman was allegedly raped by her brother-in-law and harassed for dowry by her husband and in-laws here, police said on Friday.
The victim woman, a resident of Ferozpur Kalan, complained that since her marriage to Krishna in 2008, her husband and in-laws used to beat her demanding a car and money, police said. Her brother-in-law even raped her, they said quoting her compliant.

When she complained to her husband about it, he supported his brother's action while her mother-in-law tried to injure her with an electric iron, they said.

An investigation in the case has been initiated, informed the investigating officer ASI Savita.
