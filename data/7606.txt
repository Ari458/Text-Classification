Kerala
Aug 10, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 27-year-old man hailing from Kunnathunadu has been arrested for raping a minor girl, promising marriage. The 16-year-old girl was raped by the accused for two consecutive days at his residence.

The arrested has been identified as Sandeep, son of Sivan from AIkkaranadu near Kunnathunadu.

According to police, Sandeep was in a relationship with the girl hailing from Aluva. "On Wednesday, the accused took the girl to his house at Meenbara near AIkkaranadu where she was forced to stay for two days. Though parents of the girl filed a missing complaint, she returned after two days. While recording her statement on return, the girl said that she was raped by Sandeep," said T B Vijayan, circle inspector, Aluva.

Cases were registered against the accused on charges of rape and wrongful confinement under provisions of The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (Pocso Act). Sandeep was produced before a court and remanded in judicial custody for 14 days.

One arrested for smuggling gold: The air intelligence unit of customs at the Cochin International Airport Ltd (Cial) arrested a passenger while attempting to smuggle 800g gold late on Saturday. Vijesh, a native of Malappuram, was held on arrival at the airport on an Air India flight from Dubai at 10.30pm.

The passenger was intercepted at screening area of the arrival hall based on suspicion. He had concealed seven gold bars in his rectum. The seized gold is valued approximately Rs 20 lakh.
