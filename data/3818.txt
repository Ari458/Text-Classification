West Bengal
Jan 02, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly molested by a man in Birbhum district.

A complaint of molestation has been lodged with Suri police station by the relatives of the girl. 

Angry relatives and neighbours of the girl demonstrated at the Suri police station.

A relative of the girl claimed that they have lodged a complaint of rape but the police did not confirm it.

The girl was playing with a friend in the courtyard of a neighbour when a man from a neighbouring locality allegedly dragged her and assualted her.

The girl complained to her parents that she was feeling unwell. The parents admitted her to Suri hospital the next day.
