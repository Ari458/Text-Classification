Andhra Pradesh
Aug 13, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Locals thrashed an elementary school teacher for sexually abusing a nine-year-old girl student at Vaddepalem village near Udayagiri of Nellore district on Tuesday.

According to reports, the class IV child complained to her parents about the behaviour of the teacher Penchalaraju.

Enraged parents and relatives of the child reached the school on Tuesday and beat him up, following which he fled the school. Police registered a case
