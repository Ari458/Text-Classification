Uttar Pradesh
Jan 05, 2015
Abduction
Description:
			Infuriated over the marriage of his daughter against his wishes, a man along with his brother today allegedly kidnapped her at gun point from her husband's house in Ramgaon area of the district.

Police registered a case against the duo and arrested them today, police said.

However, the whereabout of the girl was not known yet.

Sushila had married Chailu Yadav two years ago against the wishes of her father Nanhe Yadav.

On the complaint of the husband, a case was registered against Nanhe and his brother Mulkraj.
