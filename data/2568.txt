Himachal Pradesh
Mar 19, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Kiratpur village of Sirmaur district.
	
Hasib (22), a resident of Bhagwanpur village in Paonta area, was arrested following the complaint made by the girl's family.
	
According to the complaint, Hasib raped the minor when she was alone in her residence in Kiratpur.
	
The accused had threatened her with dire consequences if she told anyone about the incident or reported the matter to the police.
