Bihar
Nov 29, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly gangraped by two different groups in two different villages on Sunday night.

The 17 yr old victim, who was recovered in an unconscious state from Gangwara village told the police she was first raped at Gangwara by six persons and then taken to nearby Nawtolia village and raped again by four other men.
