Odisha
Jan 16, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by two youths in Kendrapara district, police said today.

The duo had allegedly abducted the girl from her native village in Marsaghai area on January 12 and taken her to Talcher in Angul district and sexually assaulted her there the same day, police officials said.

While one Dinabandhu Mallik was arrested yesterday, search was on to locate the other youth.
