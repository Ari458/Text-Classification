Cidco
Apr 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old married woman has claimed that she was raped here twice, by different men, in the space of three days.

According to the victim, who was visiting her parents' house in Cidco area, a local goon Vinod Salve raped her on Sunday, and threatened her with dire consequences if she approached the police.

The woman, traumatised and scared, left parents' home last night to take an auto rickshaw back to her husband's house.

Sangarsh B Landge and Sagar Dalvi, along with auto driver Manoj Jadhav, forcibly took her to a secluded spot, where Landge and Jadhav raped her, according to the victim.
