Andhra Pradesh
Nov 24, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A middle-aged man was arrested for allegedly raping a five-year-old girl in Mahabubnagar district of Andhra Pradesh in an "inebriated" condition.

The accused B Kurumaiah lured the neighbourhood girl under the pretext of showing her a film on television and allegedly raped her.

The girl was sent for medical examinations.
