Haryana
Oct 01, 2015
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment, Rape
Description:
			A woman today filed a complaint of rape against her brother-in-law and accused her husband and in-laws of torturing her for dowry in Mewat area here.

The woman, married for two years, accused her husband, two elder brothers-in-law, two elder sisters-in-law, and her mother-in-law of subjecting her to mental and physical torture, police said today.

The woman also accused her elder brother-in-law of raping her, they said.

On the basis of her complaint, a case has been registered under various sections of the IPC, Investigating Officer ASI Sushila said, adding, the matter is being investigated.
