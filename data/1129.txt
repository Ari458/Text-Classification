Description:
			The victim 29-year-old J Anitha  met V Ramprasad while working in Chennai. They fell in love and got married on December 30, 2006.

During marriage, Anitha's family gave 80 sovereigns of gold and Rs 1 lakh as dowry to Ramprasad. Further, the family also spent Rs 6 lakh in marriage expenses.

After giving birth to a daughter, Anitha was subjected to continuous dowry harassment by her husband's family. Unable to withstand the physical and mental torture demanding dowry, Anitha returned to her Coimbatore home last month with the daughter.
