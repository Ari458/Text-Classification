Tamil Nadu
Aug 24, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			S. Palani (39), a daily wage earner of Kuddupattti village in Krishnapuram police station limits in Dharmapuri district, was arrested on Friday on charges of allegedly attempting to sexually abuse his 14-year-old daughter on Thursday night. Police sources said the man was under the influence of alcohol when he made the alleged attempt to abuse the girl who was a student of a government school. The man beat up his wife and injured her when she tried to save her daughter.
