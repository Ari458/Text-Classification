Rajasthan
Dec 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A police inspector was booked for allegedly raping a 27-year-old girl in Sriganganagar district in Rajasthan.

The woman alleged that Chug Singh Sodha, SHO Ghadsana, who was investigating a rape case lodged by her against two people in Bikaner earlier, raped her in August.
