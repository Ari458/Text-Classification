Uttar Pradesh
Mar 03, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			A woman constable was allegedly assaulted by four youths, who also took semi-nude pictures of her at her house in Krishna Nagar police station area here.

The accused, including three sons of a police sub- inspector, assaulted the policewoman in her Agrasen Colony- located house and took semi-nude photographs of her last Thursday, Deputy Superintendent of Police Chakrapani Tripathi said.

Tripathi said, "An attempt to nab the absconding accused is on. Nobody is above law and the woman constable has been assured full protection."
