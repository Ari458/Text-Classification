Delhi
Dec 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A rape case was reported from Kirti Nagar in west Delhi when a 12-year-old girl was raped by her paternal uncle. The matter came to light when the girl was rushed to DDU Hospital after she bled continuously from her private parts. The hospital confirmed raped and reported the matter to police who, after interrogation, arrested the girl's uncle.

The victim underwent a surgery and was counselled.
