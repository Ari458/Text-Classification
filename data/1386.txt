Odisha
Feb 26, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Police said one Sachidananda Swain had alleged that his daughter Laxmipriya had gone missing from her in-law's house in Naya bazar area under plant site police limit of Rourkela on Monday. 
  
Swain had filed a complaint alleging that his daughter's husband Sanjay Jena and her elder brother-in-law Bijay Jena and mother-in-law Premalata were involved in the disappearance of his daughter. 

He alleged that his daughter was facing dowry torture by her in-law's family.
