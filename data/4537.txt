Bihar
Apr 11, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A woman was shot dead today by a man in a locality in Bihar's Munger district.

Sunaina Devi (40) was on way home after deposing in property-related case when her relative Shankar Pandit shot her from close range near Safiabad airport area.

She died on the spot, they said, adding the body has been sent for post-mortem.
