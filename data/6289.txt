Jammu
Jan 23, 2015
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 32-year-old 'occultist' was today arrested for allegedly sexually exploiting a woman on the pretext of healing her, police said.

The accused identified as Bal Krishan, a resident of Miran Sahab in Jammu, would promise people they will get rid of their miseries through magic.

"He used to exploit people, especially girls by calling them to a secluded place and sexually exploiting them," said Randhir Singh, Superintendent of Police South.

In her complaint, the woman alleged that she was sexually exploited by the accused, he said.

"He called the victim to a secluded place on the pretext of healing her of sickness and then sexually exploited her," he said.

"We registered a case and arrested the accused," he said.
