New Delhi
Jun 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman has alleged she was raped by her colleague inside a rented flat in R K Puram in south Delhi on Wednesday. The man was arrested on the basis of her complaint. 

The colleague had taken her to his flat in Mohammadpur in RK Puram and had raped her there, the woman alleged. On the basis of her complaint, a case of rape was registered at RK Puram police station. Police said that the accused and the victim are employed as sales executives with a private company in Moti Nagar. 

According to the woman, the incident happened on Wednesday night. The man had allegedly taken her to the flat for her to check it out. Later she approached the RK Puram police and filed a complaint. She claimed she knew him for three months.
