Uttar Pradesh
Mar 25, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A local court has sentenced a man to 10 years rigorous imprisonment for raping a minor girl of his neighbourhood in Sonai village in the district after eloping with her.

Additional District Judge A K Gupta also imposed a fine of Rs 20,000 on the convict, Sattar.

The judge said if Sattar failed to pay the fine, he will have to undergo additional imprisonment of 16 months, assistant government council Nand Kumar Tewari said.

The judge, however, acquitted Sattar's brother, also an accused in the case. He was given benefit of doubt, he said.

According to the prosecution, On October 14 2011, Sattar and his brother lured the girl and Sattar eloped with her.

An FIR was filed by the father of the girl in this regard.

Sattar was caught at Mathura railway station on November 2, 2011 and later the girl was also recovered.

Medical examination of the girl confirmed that she was sexually assaulted.
