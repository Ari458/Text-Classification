Sikar
Apr 05, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly raped at Jasti ka Bagh village in Sikar district of Rajasthan. Following the incident, the girl attempted suicide by setting herself on fire. 

The victim was returning from school when she was abducted by one Rajaram who allegedly raped her and left her at an isolated place. Later, the girl after reaching home set herself on fire.  According to the hospital, the girl has sustained 70 % burn injuries and chances of her survival are very slim.
