Tamil Nadu
Oct 21, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Mahila Court here has sentenced a youth to seven years imprisonment for sexually assaulting a teenaged girl.

According to prosecution, S. Mahendran alias Mahesh (23), a sound system labourer from Govindanpatti under Kadambur police station limits, had abducted and sexually assaulted a 16-year-old girl from the same area when she had gone to witness the temple festival at West Eeraal on May 22, 2014.

Based on the complaint from the girl's father, the Kadambur police arrested Mahesh. Mahila Court Judge J. Flora on Tuesday awarded one year imprisonment and imposed a fine of Rs. 1,000 for abducting the girl and sentenced him to undergo seven years imprisonment for sexually assaulting the girl besides slapping a fine of Rs. 2,000.

However, the sentence would go concurrently, the Judge ordered.
