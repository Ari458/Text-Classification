New Delhi
Jan 02, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Two sisters, six and eight years of age, were allegedly kidnapped and molested by Vanshilal (24) near Navjeevan Camp in the Govindpuri area of southeast Delhi.

Police has arrested the accused, who lives in the area with his family.

Vanshilal lured the two girls to an isolated spot where he then molested them. It was the girls' father who caught the accused while looking for his daughters. He then handed Vanshilal over to the police.
