Punjab
Dec 19, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 27-year-old married woman was allegedly raped inside her residence by an acquaintance, who has been arrested.

Rakesh Kumar, a resident of Dharamkot mohalla, was held for raping the woman, whose husband is settled abroad.

According to the complaint, the victim had recently purchased a new flat and had shifted into it only a week back.

She was alone in the house when Kumar, an acquaintance, entered the house and raped her.

The victim alleged Kumar of physically assaulting her when she tried to resist him.
