Gurgaon
Aug 16, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A three-and-a-half-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour near her house in Gurgaon on Thursday night.

The accused was arrested and produced in a city court on Saturday. The court sent him to judicial custody.

The accused has been identified as 21-year-old from West Bengal. He works as a labourer.

According to police, he took the girl for a walk when their area faced a power cut on Thursday night. He then took her to a deserted spot and raped her. He left there and fled.

The girl returned home by herself and was in tears. When her parents realized what had happened, they called the police.

The girl was admitted to a nearby hospital where her condition is said to be stable. Her medical reports have confirmed rape. The 21-year-old labourer was arrested late on Friday from the outskirts of the village where he lives.
