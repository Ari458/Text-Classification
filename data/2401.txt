Kapashera
Jun 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenaged girl was raped allegedly by two men known to her in the capital.

The incident was reported from Kapashera and the accused Rohit and Gaurav were arrested.

The 14-year-old victim told police that the duo allegedly lured her to an isolated place near her house and raped her two days ago.

They also allegedly threatened her of dire consequences if she informed anyone about the incident.

Source: Press Trust Of India
