Tamil Nadu
Aug 03, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 43-year-old man was arrested by the Thudiyalur All Women police on Saturday for allegedly developing a sexual relationship with a woman after promising to marry her.

The accused, V Ragupathy (43), from Ayyampalayam near Vellamadai, was working as a conductor with the TNSTC. He is married and has two children. Police said that the victim aged 24 years and the accused fell in love last year.

The victim had become pregnant and was forced to undergo an abortion at a private hospital. Ragupathy then started avoiding her. When the victim demanded that he marry her, he is alleged to have threatened her, following which the victim lodged a police complaint.

Ragupathy was arrested and lodged at the Coimbatore Central Prison.
