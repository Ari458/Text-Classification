Uttar Pradesh
Mar 12, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A woman was allegedly beaten to death by her husband in Saiyadraja area after she objected to his illicit relationship.

The 32-year-old woman identified as Poonam Mishra was thrashed by her husband Krishnanand Mishra after she objected to his relationship with another woman.

She was rushed to the hospital in a serious condition, where she died.
