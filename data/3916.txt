Gujarat
Jan 16, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A man allegedly killed his wife for her inability to bear him children in 10 years of their marriage.

Inability of his wife Rekha to conceive for the last 10 years prompted Jagdish Makwana, who happens to be a Vadodara Municipal Corporation employee, to kill his wife who succumbed to her head injuries.

Makwana fled the spot after allegedly killing her in the Baranpura locality city.

Parents and family members of the deceased alleged that their daughter had complained to them that her husband used to torture her for her inability to conceive.
