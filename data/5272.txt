Madhya Pradesh
Aug 11, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl with learning disability was raped by her neighbour for more than 6 months in Madhya Pradesh's Gwalior district. 

The survivor's mother lodged a police complaint after she learnt about it. 

Accused Naresh Rathod who is an auto-driver, lived in the survivor's neighbourhood. He is on the run. A case of rape has been registered against him. 

Police said the matter came to fore on August 9 when Rathod called the survivor to his house. When she did not return for long her mother went looking and saw the accused raping her, police said citing the complaint.
