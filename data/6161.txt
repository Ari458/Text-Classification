Uttar Pradesh
Jan 06, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl was murdered by unidentified persons when she had gone to relieve herself at Malira village in the district today, police said.

The body of the girl was found today, following which a case has been registered against unidentified persons, police said.

The body has been sent for post-mortem examination, they said, adding, according to the villagers the girl is suspected to have been murdered by her family members over a love affair.
