Burla
Dec 22, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The 17-year-old physically disabled girl was raped by a staff of the government-run VSS Medical College and Hospital, Burla, in 2009. The girl was attending her ailing mother at the hospital at that time. Residents had protested against the alleged crime against the deaf and mute girl and had observed a Burla bandh, demanding justice for her.
