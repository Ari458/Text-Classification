Karnataka
Jul 26, 2016
Human Trafficking
Description:
			A girl from Kolkata was rescued from a prostitution ring in Mysore on Monday. Acting on a clue, Udayagiri police raided a house on the first-floor of a building in Ambedkar Nagar and arrested two women for allegedly running the brothel in a residential area. A customer from T Narasipur too was arrested. The girl from Kolkata was in her mid-20s.

Udayagiri police said investigation is under way to know how the girl landed in Mysore. She has been sent to a rehabilitation home here.
