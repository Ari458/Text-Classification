New Delhi
Dec 18, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman, who had played an active role during the India Gate protests in wake of the Nirbhaya gang rape, has allegedly been sedated and raped by the official of an NGO in south Delhi. 

An FIR has been filed based on a complaint filed by National Commission for Women (NCW) to the South district police on the matter.

The woman has said in her complaint to NCW that the official had offered her a soft drink laced with sedatives at a meeting of the NGO she worked in and then forced himself upon her. The date and time of the incident is not clear as police haven't been able to contact her. Police will meet the woman and record her statement before a magistrate under Section 164 of CrPC.
