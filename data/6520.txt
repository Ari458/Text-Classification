Haryana
Feb 27, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in Mewla Maharajpur village here, police said today.

The incident took place yesterday when the victim reached the gate of her school, they said.

The accused, identified as Sharda Soni, had come there to drop his children at the school. After sending his children inside, he grabbed the girl's hand, covered her mouth and dragged her to his room where he raped her, police said.

He even threatened to kill her if she revealed the incident to anyone, they said, adding, he raped her twice again after that.

The girl later narrated her ordeal to her teacher who then informed her parents, police said.
