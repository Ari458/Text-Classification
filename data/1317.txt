Bhavnagar
Feb 19, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl was gang-raped in Gariyadhar town of Bhavnagar district on the intervening night of February 16 and 17. 

She said that three youths broke into her house at Pachegam Road when she was asleep. They gagged her and then raped one after another at knife-point  reportedly after she could not return Rs 8,000 borrowed from one of the suspects. She named Dasrath Patel, Sura Velji and Anil Brahmin, all from Gariadhar, as the accused.
