Jharkhand
Apr 02, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Nawadih village of Palamau district today.

The girl was playing in front of her house when the accused identified as Pradip Rajak lured and then raped her, police officials said.

Following the incident, the girl came home wailing and narrated the incident to her parents, who then informed the police and started searching for the accused.

The accused was later arrested, they said.

The girl was taken to Sadar Hospital here for check-up,they said.
