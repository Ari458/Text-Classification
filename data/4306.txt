Uttar Pradesh
Mar 06, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A girl student has been allegedly harassed and beaten up by two motorcycle-borne youths while she was on way to her school.

The incident took place yesterday at Shahabuddinpur Road under Kotwali police station here, when the victim was on way to her school. Two youths came on a motorcycle and harassed her. They beat her up after she opposed to it.
