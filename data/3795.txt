Gujarat
Dec 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A director of Sabar Dairy in Himmatnagar was arrested for allegedly raping a 27 years old woman.

The victim, a married woman living in Sadarda village in Malpur tehsil, had filed a complaint with the Malpur Police station that Jasubhai Patal (50), director of the Sabar Dairy, and Bipin Patel (40), a lawyer from Helodar village, came to her house when she was alone, and raped her.

They had come to the house on the pretext of helping the woman's husband who was involved in another rape case.

The accused Jasu Patel was absconding since the complaint was filed on November 14.

While the other accused is on the run. 

Source: Press Trust of India
