Allahabad
Oct 19, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Apparently distraught over her strained relations with her husband, a woman in this Uttar Pradesh district has killed her three minor children before committing suicide, police said Wednesday.

Chanda Devi, 35, poisoned four-year-old Vikas, two-year-old Sanjeev and one-year-old Ragini to death and later committed suicide by hanging at her parents' house in Baijra village Tuesday, police said.

"Preliminary investigations indicate the woman took the extreme step following a marital dispute," police inspector Ram Bharose told reporters in Allahabad, 200 km from Lucknow.
