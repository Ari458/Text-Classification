Punjab
Apr 26, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A case was registered today against the husband and father-in-law of an 18-year-old woman over allegations that the duo had raped her, police said today.

Cases were also registered against the victim's mother-in-law and the ex-wife of her husband, who have been accused of beating her and abetting other violations.

In her complaint, the victim alleged that her husband Sulakhan Singh and father-in-law Sohan Singh used to rape her while her mother-in-law used to support them, police said.
