Punjab
Jul 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A labourer has been booked for allegedly attempting rape on an 8-year-old girl in Khati village of the district when her parents were away.

The girl was lured to a secluded place by the accused Sheh Mohan (22), who tried to sexually assault her.

A neighbourer, however, noticed it and raised an alarm following which Mohan escaped from the spot, the girl's parents had gone to market when the incident took place.
