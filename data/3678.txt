Himachal Pradesh
Dec 12, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A person was sentenced to ten years of rigorous imprisonment by a local court for raping an eight-year-old girl in March 2013.

The sentence was handed out by Special District Judge, Una, Rattan Singh Thakur who also imposed a fine of Rs 30,000 on the convict.

In case of default in payment of cash penalty he would undergo imprisonment of another three months.

According to the prosecution, accused Mukesh Kumar, hailing from Nakodar in Punjab had gone to Pirnigaha shrine on March 31, 2013, where he came across a the girl and her family.

Kumar asked the girl's parents to arrange blankets for the night. When the girl was alone, he forcefully took the minor to a near area and raped her.

The minor was found at the entrance of the shrine in the wee hours, following which a case was registered and Kumar arrested.
