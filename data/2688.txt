Haryana
Jul 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Danoda village in the district when she had gone to collect fodder in a field.

Sanjay (24) of the same village raped the girl and threatened her and her family members of dire consequences if they reported the matter to anyone.

The medical examination of the victim, conducted at the general hospital in Narwana, has confirmed rape.
