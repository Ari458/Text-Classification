Hyderabad
Feb 01, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			The 23-year-old woman, who suffered burn injuries after four men allegedly set her on fire when she resisted their rape attempt died.

The victim had suffered 90 per cent burns and died while undergoing treatment.

According to police, the victim, a resident of railway quarters in Chilkalguda here, had discontinued studies after class IV and stayed with her mother and grandmother in the house.

The accused had called the victim on her mobile phone and asked her to come to a particular place on January 29 with malafide intention.

When she reached the spot, they allegedly attempted to rape her.

When she resisted, the four accused allegedly poured kerosene on her and set her ablaze, a police officer said, based on the statement given by the woman.
