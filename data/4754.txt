Bihar
May 15, 2014
Murder
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a woman today set on fire her two minor children in Bihar's Madhubani district.

Sulekha Devi of Bhatgawan village set her two children, Kajal Kumari (4) and Krishna Kumar (2), on fire after pouring kerosene oil on them while they were asleep in a room, Babubarhi police station in-charge B K Singh said.

Both children died on the spot, he said, adding the bodies have been sent for post-mortem to a government hospital.

The woman took the extreme step in a fit of rage following a bitter quarrel with her husband Ayodhya Singh over a family-related matter, Singh said.
