Bihar
Mar 26, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A court in Bihar's Jehanabad district today awarded life imprisonment to a man and imposed Rs 20,000 fine on him in a rape case two years ago.

The Additional District and Session judge - III Shailendra Kumar sent Dheeraj Kumar to jail for life and imposed a fine of Rs 20,000.

The convict had raped a four year-old girl by luring her away to an isolated place at Sonwa village in the Ghosi police station area on March 3, 2012.
