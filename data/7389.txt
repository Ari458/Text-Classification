Karnataka
May 02, 2015
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 35-year-old was molested by the house-keeping staff at her office on Bannerghatta Road in JP Nagar. Praveen, (21), is a native of Mandya and had joined the company just a month ago.

Police said the woman was working on the night-shift on Wednesday. When Praveen noticing that she was alone, went up to her and misbehaved with her. When she tried to raise an alarm he allegedly molested her. She managed to escape and alerted other staff members, who caught him and handed him over to J P Nagar police. He was sent to judicial custody.
