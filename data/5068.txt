Rajasthan
Jul 08, 2014
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A woman employee at a private institute here has alleged that a senior official there had asked for sexual favours in return for offering her money and promotion, police said. 

The victim, who is married and in her 30s, filed a complaint yesterday, against the official of the institute claiming that the man sought sexual favours from her. 

A case under section 354 A, and 354 D for a demand or request for sexual favours was registered against the official, and probe was on, SHO, Sanganer, Sadar Manoj Gupta said. 

The official could not be contacted for comment.
