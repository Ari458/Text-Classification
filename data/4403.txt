Bihar
Mar 16, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Two persons wanted in connection with a gang-rape case were arrested today from a village in Bihar's Aurangabad district.

Acting on a tip-off, the police raided a hideout in Pachokhar village and arrested the accused Mithilesh Paswan and Jaiprakash Paswan in connection with the gang-rape case of a Dalit girl in the village four days ago, Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) Ajay Narayan Yadav said.

Raids were on to nab two other absconding accused, he said.

The teen-aged girl was raped by four men at an isolated place in her village four days ago.
