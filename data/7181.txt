Haryana
Jun 17, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A sarpanch of a village in Panchkula was booked by Mohali police on charges of raping a 21-year-old married woman on Monday night at Raipur Rani.

The absconding accused was identified as Mangat Ram, sarpanch of Chhota Tarlokpur. Sources said he took advantage of a tiff between the victim and her husband. According to the complaint, Mangat who was known to the husband of the victim went to the house of the woman's aunt where she was staying in Derabassi. Her told her that he would take her to meet him. But instead, he took her to Raipurrani and allegedly raped her.
