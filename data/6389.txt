Uttar Pradesh
Feb 08, 2015
Street Harassment
Description:
			A 10-year-old boy was arrested for allegedly eve-teasing a minor girl outside her school premises here, police said today.

According to the complaint filed by girl's family, the boy waited outside her institution and allegedly eve-teased the fourth grade student during the after-hours of school for a period of time.

The accused misbehaved with the other school girls too.

He had been eve-teasing the victim for a long time, Additional Superintendent of Police (ASP), Vidyasagar Mishra said.

A complaint was lodged yesterday, following which the boy was arrested from Badaut area, he said.

Police is investigating the matter, Mishra said.
