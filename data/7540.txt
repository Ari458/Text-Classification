Kerala
Aug 02, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A KSRTC bus conductor was held in connection with molestation attempt on a minor girl. Sunil Kumar, 38, of Kanjhirakkualm, conductor of a KSRTC bus from Kozhikode to Thiruvananthapuam, tried to molest the girl who was travelling with her family to Guruvayur.
