Maharashtra
Jul 02, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A youth, Arshad Qureshi (26), was arrested on rape charges by the police on Monday.

The accused, an Agripada resident, was reportedly in a relationship with survivor (17) three years ago. The 17-year-old severed ties with Qureshi after she moved to Mira Road with her parents.

One June 28, Qureshi took her to a lodge in Kashimira and sexually assaulted her. The accused has been remanded in police custody.
