Uttar Pradesh
Apr 05, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old schoolgirl going for her tuition class  was picked up by three boys in a car and raped by one of them.

A medical examination confirmed rape.

The three boys, who are absconding, are known to the girl.

The girl told the police that while going for her tuition class, three boys of her neighbourhood forced her into a car and took her to a deserted house where one of the boys raped her while the other two kept a watch outside.

She was later thrown near Madhu Nagar crossing from where she walked home.
