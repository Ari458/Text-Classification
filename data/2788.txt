Maharashtra
Jul 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old physically- challenged girl was allegedly raped by a minor boy in Mumbra township in the district.

The accused and the victim lived in the the same building. The victim had gone to the boy's house to return a book when he asked her to come inside and then sexually assaulted her.

The victim later told about the incident to her family members who immediately lodged a complaint at Mumbra police station and the boy was arrested.
