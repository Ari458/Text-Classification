Assam
Sep 05, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A high school student was allegedly gang-raped by her own classmate and his friend last night.

One of the accused is 15 years old and the other is 20.

According to Sonapur police station in-charge Ratneswar Das, the girl was made to sit in a car belonging to the accused and taken to Byrnihat in Meghalaya, 7km from here, where she was allegedly raped through the night.

She was dumped in Ural village, 20km from here, and threatened with dire consequences if she spoke, police said.

On reaching home, she told her parents who informed the police. Both the accused have been arrested. Medical examination at GMCH has confirmed rape, Das said.
