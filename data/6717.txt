Andhra Pradesh
Apr 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl from Darjeeling was allegedly gang-raped by her boyfriend and six of his friends at Himayathnagar locality here, police said today.

According to Narayangunda police, the victim, who works in a beauty parlour, was sexually assaulted last evening.

In her complaint, the girl said the incident took place when she had gone to a park with her boyfriend.

"We have sent her for the medical examination and awaiting reports," Deputy Commissioner of Police (Central Zone) VB Kamalasan Reddy said.

The boyfriend of the victim has been detained and being questioned in this regard, he added.

Further investigation is on.
