Delhi
Oct 12, 2016
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A cabin crew alleged that she was raped by a government official in a five-star hotel in southwest Delhi's Dwarka in September. Police are in the lookout for the accused who worked as patent officer.

"The government official called the woman to a hotel room on the pretext of filling up some job application forms. He tried touching her inappropriately and when she resisted, he raped her," said a senior police officer.
