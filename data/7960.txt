Abuse
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl from the slums in Lokmanyanagar has alleged that a peon who works with her father has been raping her since the last two years. 

The victim said Nitesh Kadam star ted exploiting her in June 2013. He would often take her to his home and sometimes to the hills at Wagle Estate and assault her, the girl told the Vartak Nagar police. 

Kadam also repeatedly threatened to kill the girl, her parents and brother if she made any complaints, she said. He reportedly took an objectionable video of her and often threatened to make it viral. 

The accused was arrested on Friday and has been remanded to police custody till Tuesday .
