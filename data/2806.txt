Uttar Pradesh
Jul 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two brothers were sentenced to life imprisonment by a local court, which also slapped a penalty of Rs 5,000 on each of them for raping a Dalit woman.

The victim was raped by Jai Sigh Yadav and his brother Ajay Singh Yadav in Khaga area in July 2009.

After hearing both sides, special additional district and sessions judge Mohd Zaheeruddin held the duo guilty.
