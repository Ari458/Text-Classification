Uttar Pradesh
Feb 20, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			A woman in Ganga Nagar was set on fire by a distant relative after he tried to rape her but failed late on Thursday night. The incident occurred when the woman's husband, an auto driver, was not at home. The husband later filed a complaint with the police and the woman has been admitted to hospital with 90% burns. The woman's husband filed the case after their son told him about the incident. The accused was sent to jail on Friday.

"The woman's husband out of the house doing his job on Thursday evening. The accused, a distant relative of hers, tried to take advantage of the situation and forced himself on her. When his attempts to rape her failed, he set her ablaze in exasperation. She was badly injured in the fire, but survived. She suffered 90% burns. We managed to record her statement," said Anandpal Singh, station officer at Ganga Nagar police station.

He added, "We have registered a case against the accused under sections 354 (assault on woman with intent to outrage her modesty) and 326 (causing grievous hurt by dangerous means) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) against him. We arrested him on Thursday and sent him to jail on Friday morning. Late on Thursday night, we also managed to record the woman's statement. She has confirmed everything her husband has filed in the report."
