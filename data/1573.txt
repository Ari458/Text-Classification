Thiruvananthapuram
Mar 31, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Thiruvananthapuram Fort police took into custody three youths for allegedly threatening the family of a minor girl for testifying against the person who raped her. 

The police said that the arrested trio were the friends of an accused in the rape case Manoj, who is facing trial on charges of sexually abusing the girl six years ago when she was just nine years old. The trial of the case is going on before the additional assistant sessions court.
