Uttar Pradesh
Mar 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbours in Kulfatehgarh area.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl went to her neighbour's house for some work and was raped by brothers Ankit and Johny.

Later, the girl informed her parents about the incident who then lodged an FIR.

The police have launched a manhunt to nab the accused who are absconding.
