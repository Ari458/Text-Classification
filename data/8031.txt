Tamil Nadu
Oct 24, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The police have arrested P. Muthusamy (39), a labourer, on the charge of sexually assaulting a 7-year-old girl at a village in Kolathur on October 21. When the girl raised her voice, he ran away. Her parents were away for work. 

Based on a complaint from the girl’s mother, the All Women Police Station in Mettur registered a case under Section 363 (kidnapping) of IPC and under Sections 9 and 10 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012. Muthusamy was arrested and produced in the court and lodged at prison.
