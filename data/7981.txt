Madhya Pradesh
Oct 14, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			An FIR was registered on Wednesday against Madhya Pradesh Deputy Director of public relation department, R.S. Meena for allegedly molesting a woman here. 

According to reports, the woman who was molested and filed FIR is missing since last evening. The mother of the victim alleged the involvement of Meena. 

Meanwhile, police has also registered a case of kidnapping.
