Udhampur
Nov 03, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Three men allegedly involved in kidnapping and molesting a minor girl from Rajasthan were arrested in Jammu on Wednesday.
Police told Rising Kashmir that the trio had lured the minor on a social networking site promising her a pilgrimage to Vaishnodevi shrine during the Navratra festival and molested her at Kud in Udhampur district.

The girl was shifted to Womens Police Cell at Udhampur and subsequently handed over to her parents.
