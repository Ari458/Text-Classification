Bihar
Mar 24, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Sengaon tehsil of neighbouring Hingoli district, police said today.

The incident occurred last night when the accused identified as Arun Hingole lured her to accompany her under the pretext of offering her sweets when she was returning her home along with her grandmother, they said.
