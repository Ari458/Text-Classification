Tamil Nadu
Dec 30, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old youth was arrested for allegedly raping a 15-year-old girl of his neighbour in Kottada village in Krishna district.

The girl, a student of class IX, was resting at home as she was feeling unwell while her parents were away. Taking advantage of the situation, the accused identified as P. Sathyanarayana raped her.

Source: Press Trust of India
