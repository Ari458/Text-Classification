Govandi
Apr 11, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Following allegations from a 10-year-old girl of a civic school in Shivaji Nagar in Govandi, the police are on the lookout for a man who had allegedly attempted to molest her in the school toilet. 

The incident had allegedly taken place at the Sanjay Nagar School number 2 in the area on Saturday. Police said the victim, accompanied by her parents, had approached them on Saturday afternoon alleging that an unidentified man tried to remove her clothes in the school bathroom.
