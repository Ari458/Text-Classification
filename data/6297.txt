Uttar Pradesh
Jan 25, 2015
Murder
Description:
			The body of a 21-year-old girl was found hanging from a tree under mysterious circumstances three days after she went missing in Gaisadi area here, police said today.

"The girl was missing since January 21. Last evening, she was found hanging from the tree in Thakurapur village," Station House Officer Arjun Yadav said.

A probe was on and the body has been sent for post mortem examination.
