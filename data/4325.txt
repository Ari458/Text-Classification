Maharashtra
Mar 06, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Yerawada police arrested a 21-year-old student of a city college for allegedly raping a 22-year-old girl after promising her marriage.

The suspect Clement Chaurinathan, a resident of Khadki, was arrested after the girl lodged a complaint.

Both study in the same college and knew each other. He added that the duo fell in love and their family members were aware of their relationship.

Chaurinathan refused to marry her. The girl then alleged that he raped her at various places between October 2012 and October 2013. Chaurinathan was also booked for committing unnatural offence, under sections of the Indian Penal Code, he added. A magisterial court has sent Chaurinathan to police custody remand till March 8.
