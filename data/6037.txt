Uttar Pradesh
Dec 22, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by a tempo driver and his aide in Sahatwar area, a senior officer said here today.

The victim was raped by tempo driver Rahul Yadav and his friend Lalji Yadav on Saturday last while she was going to her sister's house, Additional Superintendent of Police K C Goswami said.

He said that on the complaint of the victim an FIR was lodged against Rahul and Lalji yesterday.

The ASP said that Rahul had been arrested, while effort are on nab Yadav.

Source: Press Trust of India
