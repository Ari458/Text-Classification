Nawada
Mar 14, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			22 year old woman identified as Hema Devi was burnt alive allegedly by her in-laws for dowry at Bari Gulni in Nawada district of Bihar.

It is suspected that kerosene was poured on Hema Devi (22) before she was set ablaze by her in-laws on Monday night for her father's falure to meet their dowry demand.
