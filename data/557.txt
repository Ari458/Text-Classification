Sawai Madhopur
Nov 01, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			In a shameful and shocking act, an eight-month-old girl was allegedly raped by a middle-aged man while she was sleeping alone at her house in Sawai Madhopur district on Sunday evening. 
The 50-year-old accused allegedly visited the victim's house on Saturday evening where he found none except the infant. "Her mother had gone to market to buy groceries. Balmukund was in an inebriated condition, and raped the child," the officer said. The girl's mother saw him lying with the child on the bed, and raised an alarm. "Balmukund pushed the mother aside and ran away. The woman informed her husband following which the girl, who was bleeding, was rushed to hospital," the officer said.
