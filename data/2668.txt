Nellore
Mar 29, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A deputy chief ticket inspector was arrested for allegedly making sexual advances towards a woman software professional on the Chennai-Ahmedabad
Navjeevan Express in Nellore district of Andhra Pradesh.
	
Government Railway Police sources said E Jaya Gopi, the accused, was drunk at the time.
	
He took the woman to an AC compartment on the pretext of collecting the fare difference, and allegedly made a pass at
her.
	
She shouted for help and other passengers came to her rescue.
	
The 23-year-old woman had boarded the train in Chennai with a general ticket and had approached the accused for a reserved berth.
	
Railway authorities suspended him from service.
