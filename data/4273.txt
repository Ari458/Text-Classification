West Bengal
Feb 28, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A primary school teacher was arrested for allegedly trying to rape an 11-year-old student.

The incident took place around 2:30pm. As news spread, locals reportedly manhandled the teacher before Burdwan Sadar Police arrested Swapan Basu. He was later admitted to Burdwan Medical College Hospital.

The accused has been arrested based on the complaint lodged by the Class VI student's father at Burdwan Sadar police station. A case has been registered. The girl will be sent for a medical examination.

Basu will be taken into police remand after being produced in court. The girl though safe is in trauma. The incident has sent shockwaves in the area.
