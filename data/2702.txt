Uttar Pradesh
Jul 11, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			A youth was arrested for allegedly eve-teasing a software engineer in Sector 18 area.

Shamshuddin was chased and arrested in connection with the incident.

Sector 20 SHO Rita Yadav heard a woman's cry for help when she was on duty in the area.

She followed the eve-teaser who tried to escape on a motorcycle and after a brief chase arrested him.
