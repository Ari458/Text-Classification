Maharashtra
Feb 13, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Chatushrungi police have booked a man, a resident of Wakad, for allegedly raping his fiancee and later refusing to marry her. It took place between June and July 2012 in the suspect's flat in Pashan.

The woman, who is completing her education, got engaged with the suspect in 2012. Between June and July 2012, the suspect allegedly raped the woman in his flat in Pashan. The woman lodged a complaint with the police after the suspect refused to marry her.
