Maharashtra
Jun 03, 2015
Molestation, Crime, Street Harassment
Description:
			Three youths Bunty Hiranwar, Shakti Yadav and Karan Thor were booked by Sitabuldi police for molesting a 28-year-old woman. The woman claimed she was heading home on her two-wheeler with her family who were on another vehicle when the trio passed indecent comments on her. However, when she opposed, the trio misbehaved with her and her family and even damaged the vehicle by hitting it with an iron rod.
