Delhi
Sep 11, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A teenager has alleged molestation by her friend's brother and four other youths, all minors, in east Delhi's Pandav Nagar on Thursday. Police have apprehended all the boys.

The incident happened on Monday. The youths study in the same coaching institute as the girl. The girl, a Class IX student, alleged that her friend's brother had come to her house and took her to his own. There, he pushed her inside a room and molested her. She also said she saw someone standing near the window and clicking photographs. The girl then raised an alarm after which neighbours came to her rescue.

The girl later told her father about the matter leading to their apprehension.
