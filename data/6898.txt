Kerala
May 15, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A man attacked his wife with a knife at Kaippangalam at 8 pm on Thursday and later hanged himself to death. Ashok Prajanandan's   wife Priya 38 is reportedly out of danger at the Government Hospital where she was admitted.

The police officials, who rushed to the spot after hearing about the incident, found Ashok hanging at the house. We suspect it is a case of drunken rage resulting in the attack. We will know more details only after further investigations, the police officials said.
