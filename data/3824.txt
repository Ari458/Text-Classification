Madhya Pradesh
Jan 03, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A person was arrested for allegedly killing his wife over a property dispute.

Identified as Dilip Pahwa (54), the accused allegedly killed his wife Santosh Pahwa (50) by attacking her with a sharp-edged weapon on December 19 last year, when she was at her cast iron furniture shop.

Because of a property dispute between them, the woman had forced her husband out of the shop.

The police learnt about the dispute between them through neighbours who revealed that despite living under the same roof, both used to cook their food separately and also filed a number of property-related cases against each other.
