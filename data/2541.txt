Rajasthan
Jul 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			Two teenaged girls were allegedly raped by many youths at Mount Abu, the lone hill station in Rajasthan.

The incident took place on June 27.

Two FIRs were lodged at Jyoti Nagar police station on separate complaints of the girls, aged 15 and 13.

The accused hail from Uttar Pradesh and were working in Jaipur as labourers.
