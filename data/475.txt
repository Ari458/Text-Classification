New delhi
Oct 17, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A newly-married NRI woman was rescued from a locked house in Raj Nagar area, police said.

Mrinalini came in contact with Pulkit through Facebook when she was in Muscat. She came to India in June this year and both Pulkit and Mrinalini got married on August 16 but he nullified the marriage on September 12.

She filed a case against her husband under section 276 (rape) and 498 (physical torture for dowry). Last night, Mrinalini's in laws locked her in their house and cut the water and electricity connections, police said.

When the locals informed them, police reached there and broke open the locks and rescued her in the morning.
