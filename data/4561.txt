Rajasthan
Apr 13, 2014
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A special court sentenced a youth to life imprisonment for rape and murder of a woman police constable in 2010. 

Special Court judge Ajay Singh convicted and awarded life sentence to Tulsiram, who worked as a cook at Chechat police station of the district, for raping and killing the woman constable on the night of September 29, 2010. 

Another accused - Deshraj, constable at the time of the crime, was acquitted due to absence of evidence against him. 

The body of the woman constable was found inside a room in women's guest house of Chechat police station a day after the incident. 

The rape-cum-murder case had triggered a huge protest in Chehchat village three-and-a-half year ago.
