East Delhi
Dec 02, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly molested and assaulted by a group of young men after she protested against one of them for making objectionable gestures at her in Trilokpuri here on Tuesday.

The girl has been hospitalised but a case has been registered against the errant youths, said the police.

The incident took place in an East Delhi locality where the victim stays.

Police said that case under sections of POCSO, and IPC provisions pertaining to assault was registered based on the complaint and efforts were being made to identify the others.
