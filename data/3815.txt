Tamil Nadu
Jan 01, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year old woman was allegedly raped by her neighbour at a village in the district.

The woman, of Katheeranallur, was grazing her sheep in the field when the 23-year-old Suresh committed the crime.

On a complaint from the woman, Suresh was arrested and produced before a magistrate court which remanded him to 15 days judicial custody.
