Madhya Pradesh
Jan 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by four youths in the district, police said today.

The four accused abducted the minor on the intervening night of Monday and Tuesday from outside her home in Kolua village under Shamsabad police station limits in Vidisha district.

They took her to a desolate place near the village and gang-raped her, police said.

While searching for the girl, her family members found her last morning near the village in an unconscious condition.

The victim was admitted to a hospital where a medical examination has confirmed rape, they said.
