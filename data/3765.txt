Jammu
Dec 23, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Class VII student was allegedly abducted and raped by two brothers when she was on her way to school.

The accused -- Joginder Kumar (25) and Sudesh Kumar (28) -- have been arrested.

According to the complaint lodged by the girl's parents, the duo kidnapped the girl from Chinore area of the city.

The accused then forcibly took her into a mini bus and raped her.

The girl told her parents about the incident on reaching home in Bantalab, after which they lodged a police complaint.

Medical examination of the girl was conducted and she was handed over to family members.
