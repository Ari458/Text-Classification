Punjab
Mar 01, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A case of abduction and rape of a minor Dalit girl has come to light here with police registering a complaint in the matter.

A 15-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly abducted by one Shinder Singh from Dharamkot on February 23.

According to the complaint lodged by the victim's parents, Singh took the girl to a secluded place and raped her before dropping her near her house the next morning.

The victim's parents, however, only last night lodged a complaint against the accused, police said. No arrests have been made so far in the case.
