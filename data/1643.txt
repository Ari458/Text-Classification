Kakroli
Apr 28, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old Dalit girl was raped at gunpoint and was found pregnant a month later in Muzaffarnagar district, police said on Saturday. 

A youth identified as Yograj had raped the minor girl at gun-point on March 11 last at Kumhara village, under Kakroli police station, threatening her of dire consequences if she revealed it to anyone.
    
However, the girl told her parents a month later about the incident after she found herself to be pregnant, police said.
