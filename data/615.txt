Ghaziabad
Nov 09, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl, daughter of a widow, was kidnapped and gang-raped by two youths in Ghaziabad. She had gone to relieve herself in fields. 

In her complaint, the girl alleged that the rapists also shot video clips of the incident. The two alleged rapists made a video clip and threatened to circulate it if she told anyone about the incident.
