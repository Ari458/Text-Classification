Rajasthan
Mar 29, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year old girl was allegedly
gang-raped by five people in Hanumangarh district.
	
The accused abducted the girl from her house in Nukera village on the night of March 20.
	
The girl was taken to the house of one of the accused, where she was gang-raped.
	
The girl returned home and narrated the incident to her mother who informed elders of the village.
	
The girl and her mother visited Sangaria police station on March 26 and on their complaint a case was registered against Ranveer, Mukesh, Bansilal, Dalip and Rakesh.
