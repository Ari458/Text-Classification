Uttar Pradesh
May 05, 2012
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Crime
Description:
			A teenaged girl in Banda district committed suicide four days after being assaulted and gang-raped by three local youth. 

Her father lodged a named FIR with the Kotwali police in the Banda on Thursday, alleging that his teenaged daughter had been gang-raped by three local youth and fearing social stigma, she had committed suicide. 

On Wednesday night, the girl attempted suicide by consuming poison. She was rushed to the district hospital, where her condition deteriorated and she died on her way to Lala Lajpat Rai Hospital.
