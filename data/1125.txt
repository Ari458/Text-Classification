Orissa
Jan 12, 2012
Abduction, Murder, Rape
Description:
			The victim a resident of Ranipur had boarded a private bus to return to her village around 5.30 pm last evening. The girl, however, did not reach home. 

Two hours later, two youths reached the government hospital at Agarpada with the girl who was in an unconscious state. "They ran away from the hospital leaving behind their motor bike after doctors declared the girl dead," her father claimed.
