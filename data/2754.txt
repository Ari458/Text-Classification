Tamil Nadu
Jul 18, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 11-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a boy in a hostel.

The girl, a Class VI student at a government school, was staying in the hostel where the 19-year-old boy was doing a teacher training course.

The matter came to light after authorities found the girl writhing in pain and sent her to a doctor for check-up where it was confirmed that she was raped.
