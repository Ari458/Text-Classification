Maharashtra
Feb 28, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A couple has been charged with murder for allegedly killing a woman in Manor village here over a petty rivalry, police said today.

Santosh Dhavla and Geeta Dhavala killed Manju R Rajput (26) by hammering her head with a heavy stone at her house in Manor yesterday, API at Manor police station Maruti Patil said.

The couple and the deceased were neighbours and picked up a quarrel as the duo suspected that Manju had killed one their cocks last year for meal, Patil said, adding that in a fit of rage the couple smashed Manju's head with a heavy stone.

Palghar DSP Suvez Haque today visited the site and ordered a probe into the matter.
