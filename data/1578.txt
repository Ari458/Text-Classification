Jalupura
Apr 02, 2012
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Rajasthan has been in the news due to increasing cases of female foeticide and infanticide. Another foetus was recovered from a dustbin here Saturday taking the total number of foetuses recovered in the city to four over the past five days.

Some passers-by informed the police in the afternoon that a foetus was lying in a dustbin in Jalupura area. "The foetus was not fully developed, so it is difficult to ascertain its sex. However it seems that it is of a girl.
