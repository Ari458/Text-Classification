Tamil Nadu
Oct 06, 2015
Murder
Description:
			The charred body of a 13-year-old girl was found in an agricultural field at Pongaliyur near Coimbatore on Tuesday morning.

Police said the girl was murdered and the body was dumped in the agricultural field after burning it. The whole body was charred beyond recognition.

Police suspect that the girl might have raped and murdered.

The body was sent to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital for postmortem.

The Vadavalli police registered a case and further investigation were on.
