Muzaffarpur
Jan 01, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Police today detained a doctor in Muzaffarpur for allegedly molesting a 17-year-old girl. Indrajeet Kumar was beaten up by a mob after the girl accused him of molesting her at a private nursing home. The victim was admitted to the hospital seven days ago with some ailments in her nose. She accused Kumar of entering her room this morning and molesting her.
