Jabalpur
Mar 14, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Pradeep Singh, a policeman, who was caught on video raping a woman in a slum in Jabalpur district, was arrested and sent to jail on Tuesday. 

The accused was posted in Seoni district and had come to Jabalpur to visit a court. Later, he went to a slum area and entered a hut. When he forced himself on a woman, she cried and local people gathered there. The constable was literally caught on camera with his trousers unfastened.

Police first refused to accept her complaint but soon a person reached there with the video clip and police had to register an FIR against the police constable.
