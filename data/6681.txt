Haryana
Mar 25, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man who allegedly abducted and raped a minor Dalit girl in Ram Colony area here two days ago, was today arrested.

According to police, Pradeep was arrested from Gohana road and presented before a local court which remanded him to judicial custody.

A 13-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly abducted and raped by Pradeep, the neighbourhood youth, when she stepped outside her house to answer the call of nature in Ram colony, police said.

The incident took place on March 23 night when the victim went in the lane outside her house to answer the call of nature and Pradeep abducted her on his motorcycle and took her to New Krishna colony where he raped her, police said.

Threatening the victim of dire consequences, the accused fled after dropping her near her house, police said, adding, the girl then disclosed her ordeal to to her family following which a complaint was registered with the police.
