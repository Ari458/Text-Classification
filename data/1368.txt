Chorwad
Feb 24, 2012
Rape
Description:
			According to police, accused Ashok Solanki, who was serving life term for his wife's murder for last eight years, raped his 17-year-old daughter after being released on parole.

"After his release on parole, Ashok visited Shishu Mangal Sanstha on February 13 and took his two daughters out under the pretext of fixing marriage of the elder daughter. The accused went to his father's place in Gadu Railway Quarter where he allegedly raped his daughter and fled. We arrested Ashok on the complaint of the victim and his sister," said an official from Chorwad police station.
