West Bengal
Mar 06, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A man today killed his wife in of a fit of rage and later on surrendered at a local police station in the Domjur area, the police said.

Senior police officers at Domjur police station said Indrajit Ghosh, aged around 35-years, today surrendered at the police station after stabbing his wife with a knife.

"The man came to us and said he had an old family dispute with his wife and today in a fit of rage, he had stabbed his wife Mala Ghosh and killed her. We arrested the man and recovered the body, which has been sent for post mortem. Investigation is on," said a senior officer.
