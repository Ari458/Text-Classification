Assam
Oct 11, 2013
Crime
Description:
			A woman was allegedly killed by her brother-in-law suspecting her to be a witch in upper Assam's Dhemaji district.

The woamn, identified as Purnima Yein (35), was killed with a sharp weapon by her husband's younger brother at Sili.

The brother-in-law, Bhaba Yein, who has been arrested confessed before police that he killed his sister-in-law as she allegedly practiced witchcraft on his brother and made him sick.

The body has been sent for postmortem.
