Delhi
May 04, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			An 18 year-old girl was allegedly gang raped and then thrown out of a moving car in Delhi on Sunday. The survivor has said she was lured with a promise of a job by a man claiming to own a company in Shahdara.  

She has claimed that the man and his acquaintance then drugged her and then raped her in a moving car. They then allegedly threw her out of the moving car and left her near Vikas Bhawan in an unconscious state.

The survivor was admitted to AIIMS trauma centre and then moved to LNJP hospital. One person has been detained by the police for questioning in the case. They are also awaiting the medical report of the survivor.
