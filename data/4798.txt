Uttar Pradesh
May 24, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A class 9th student was allegedly gang-raped by a neighbour and two others who abducted her in a car while she was returning home from school and later dumped her near her village in Modinagar area of Ghaziabad.

The 16-year-old girl told police that she was returning home yesterday when her neighbour Guddu, his maternal uncle Rajan and friend Lalit stopped her and kidnapped her in car.

Thereafter, they took her to Delhi where they allegedly took turns to rape her, the girl said in the police complaint.

After committing the crime, the three accused dumped her near her village this morning and fled the spot.

"Police have recorded the statements of the girl and sent her for medical examination," a police officer said.
