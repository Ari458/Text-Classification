Madhya Pradesh
Jun 30, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 40-year-old man allegedly raped his 14-year-old would-be daughter-in-law at Chachar village in Singrauli district late on Friday night.

The accused, Rajaram Yadav, a resident of Garhwa, arranged his son's marriage in Chachar village. "On Friday, Rajaram went to the girl's home to perform pre-marriage rituals, where he allegedly raped her," Manda police station in charge Dhaniram Warkade told TOI.

"The girl informed her parents, after which the matter was reported to police on June 28. The accused was arrested on Saturday evening," he said.

Rajaram has been booked under Section 367 (rape) and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act and other relevant sections, Warkade added.
