Tamil Nadu
Jul 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teacher of the Government Higher Secondary School, Bhavanisagar was placed under suspension following a complaint that he had sexually harassed a girl student.

The Chief Educational Officer (CEO) Sridevi ordered for suspending Thiagarajan (33) in the wake of complaint by the parents of Class Seven girl that the accused had sexually harassed her.

The girl informed her parents in the Sri Lankan Refugee camp after the harassment became unbearable.

The Education Department has also ordered an enquiry against the teacher.
