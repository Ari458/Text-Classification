Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman has registered a complaint at the Robersonpete Police Station against her husband and in-laws alleging dowry harassment.

Bhargavi, a resident of KGF married Murlidhar two years ago.  Though it was a love marriage that was arranged, all the demands from Muralidhar's family were fulfilled. She alleged that after marriage, her husband and her in-laws constantly pestering her to get Rs 10 lakh. Muralidhar has not had a glimpse of their new-born baby for the greed for money.

They added that they he forced Bhargavi out of the house and also issued life threat to her and their son.
