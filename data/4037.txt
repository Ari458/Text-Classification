Jharkhand
Feb 02, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A grocery shop owner was booked for attempting to rape a 12-year-old girl at Sindoor village under the Hazaribag sadar police station.

Shashi Mehta allegedly called the girl to his shop when she was going to school. Mehta took her inside the shop and tried to sexually assault her.

The girl, sensing trouble, raised an alarm after which Mehta abandoned her on NH-33, about 10km from Sindoor. He then closed his shop and fled.

The girl has been admitted to Hazaribag sadar hospital for medical examination.
