Punjab
Feb 08, 2016
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A man and his parents were booked by the police following the death of a woman. 

In his complaint to the police, Bala Ram of Faridkot stated that his daughter Parveen Kumari was married to Khem Chand and was expecting a baby. 

The in-laws and the husband of his daughter did not allegedly care for her following which she died. The accused have been named as Khem Chand, Roshan Lal and Gulabo Devi, all residents of Rama Mandi.
