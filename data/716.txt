Mehrauli
Nov 21, 2011
Rape
Description:
			Two persons have been arrested for abducting a 25-year-old woman from the Mehrauli area and raping her in a moving car. 

The incident took place on Saturday when the accused stopped the woman in the Mehrauli area on the "pretext of asking her the way to Jaipur". When the woman stopped to answer the men, they pulled her inside their Alto car. After raping her, the duo dumped the woman in the Bhiwani area in Rajasthan and fled, said the police.
