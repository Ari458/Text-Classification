Rajasthan
Dec 21, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested for allegedly raping a 27-year-old woman in a hotel room on the pretext of discussing his marriage proposal to her.

Vijay, 30, was arrested from a hotel on Ajmer road when he was about to leave the city.

Vijay called the woman, who works as a nurse in Bharatpur, and proposed to marry her. He said he had seen her profile on a matrimonial website.

When the woman arrived from Bharatpur, he took her to a hotel and raped her.

After the incident, the victim lodged an FIR against the accused who was arrested.

The accused, hailing from Barmer district, is being interrogated.
