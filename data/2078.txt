Gautampuri
Apr 28, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			Delhi Police is yet to arrest the person who attacked a girl in a public toilet here slitting her throat and inflicting other injuries even as doctors are yet to confirm whether she was sexually assaulted.

"We are investigating the matter," a senior police official said adding they were also probing whether any minor was involved in the incident.

The girl was attacked in a public toilet in south-east Delhi's Gautampuri on Friday and police are awaiting a medical report to ascertain whether she was sexually assaulted.

Doctors at the AIIMS Trauma Centre where she is being treated said the victim, whose throat was slit, was stable and was having semi-solid food.
