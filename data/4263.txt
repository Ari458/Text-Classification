Maharashtra
Feb 27, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A madrasa teacher was arrested for allegedly molesting two minor girls.

The accused Samiulla Karudddin Shah (25) had offered to teach the girls Arabic at the seminary which falls under Rabodi police station limits.

He allegedly molested them at the Madrasa, police said quoting the complaint filed by the father of one of the girls yesterday.

The girls (both aged 8) hailed from Rehmatnagar area, police said, adding Shah was apprehended last night.
