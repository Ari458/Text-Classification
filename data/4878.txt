Rajasthan
Jun 13, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old married woman was allegedly raped by three youths in the Kanota area.

The woman, who had separated from her husband and is living with her mother and 10-month-old son, has named three persons in the FIR, police said. She alleged that the three accused -- identified as Nawal, Jaswant and Mausam -- had raped her on multiple occasions in the recent past.

Interestingly, the victim's mother had a few days back filed a complaint against her daughter saying she had stolen cash not only from their home but also from other houses in their neighbourhood in the Mali-ki-Kothi-Bagrana area here, police said.
