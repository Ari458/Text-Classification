Maharashtra
Mar 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The headmaster of a government-aided
residential school at Lakahndur taluka in neighbouring Bhandara district, was booked for allegedly sexually exploiting tribal girls studying there.
    
Pralhad Wasudeo Bhusari, 47 was sexually exploiting the girls of his school for sometime in Sarandi Budruk village, following which one of them lodged a complaint against him and his assistant Vivek Atmaram Chawle.
