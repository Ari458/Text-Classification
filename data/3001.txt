New Delhi
Aug 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a middle aged man inside the premises of a temple in South-West Delhi's Bindapur area.

The 52-year-old man lured the girl inside the temple on some pretext and raped her.

The incident came to light when the girl's parents found that she was sexually assaulted following which they took her to Deen Dayal Upadhyay hospital and reported the incident to police.

The girl has been discharged from the hospital. She is doing fine.

Source: Press Trust Of India
