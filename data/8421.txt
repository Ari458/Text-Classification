Telangana
Jan 29, 2016
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			The headmaster of a school was awarded a one-year jail term and Rs. 50,000 penalty in a case related to harassment of a female teacher at the school.

According to Indrakaran Sub-Inspector Praveen Kumar Reddy, Kubya Naik, who was working as the headmaster at Indrakaran High School, harassed a teacher in 2013, and the teacher lodged a complaint with the police. 

Based on the investigation by the police, Justice Venkatram of Excise Court awarded him the punishment.
