Railway colony Chandigarh
Mar 27, 2013
Crime
Description:
			The body of a woman was found lying inside an abandoned car parked near the railway colony in Bathinda.
     
The car's keys were found inside the car along with a sim card and a voter's identity card, both of which belonged to the woman. The deceased has been identified as Reena from Rajpura, who was married to Shivraj of Kotkapura.
	
Initial investigations have revealed that the woman had strained relations with her husband.
