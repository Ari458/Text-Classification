Tamil Nadu
Oct 10, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Medical College Police on Friday arrested a 52-year-old taxi driver for sexually abusing a nine-year-old girl in the parking lot of the Medical College Hospital. The accused has been identified as Murali, a native of Vamanapuram. According to the police, the incident occurred on September 17, when the victim along with her family hired his taxi for taking the victim’s younger brother to the hospital.

When the victim and her relatives reached the hospital, she was asked to remain in the taxi till the rest returned after the consultation. The driver took advantage of it and took the car to a deserted parking lot where he abused her. Though the victim screamed, no one heard as the car windows were closed.

The incident came to the light when the victim  told her parents when the latter hired the same taxi for going to the hospital again. Following this, the parents lodged a complaint and the accused was arrested. The accused has been produced before the magistrate and remanded in judicial custody, the police said.
