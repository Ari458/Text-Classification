Madhya Pradesh
Jul 24, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The district court granted six months of rigorous imprisonment (RI) to three accused who were booked for dowry harassment under Sections of 498 A, 506 and 34 of IPC, here on Thursday. Woman police had booked accused Santosh son of Ambaram Solanki, Ambaram son of Siddhulal Solanki and Ayodhyabai Solanki, all residents of Mahavir Nagar on February 13, 2014 on the complaint of the victim Sanjana Solanki. All three accused had harassed the victim mentally and physically and demanded dowry. Judicial Magistrate Dinesh Kumar convicted the accused and granted them jail of six months each and imposed the fine of Rs 500.
