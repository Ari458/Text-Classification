Uttar Pradesh
Dec 25, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly gang raped by three people in a deserted school building in Hariharpur village.

The girl who was missing since December 20 somehow fled and reached home and told her family as to how she was abducted by three men, who took her to a deserted school building where she was raped.

The victim was also warned of dire consequences if she disclosed the matter to anyone.

On the complaint of the victim's mother all three accused, belonging to the same village, have been arrested.
