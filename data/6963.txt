New Delhi
May 25, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl who came to the city from Dehradun to learn how to become a DJ has alleged she was raped by her cousin's friend in central Delhi's Karol Bagh area. 

Police said the girl has alleged she was raped by a 28-year-old businessman from Sonepat, Haryana after she went to meet him at a hotel in Karol Bagh last week. 

In her complaint, the woman told police she had met the accused at her cousin's house in Baghpat earlier this month and they had exchanged phone numbers. The two remained in contact. 

On Wednesday, she told the accused that she was coming to Delhi for a few days. The accused too came to the capital and took her to the hotel in Karol Bagh on the pretext of having dinner but, instead, he took her to a room and raped her, police said. 

The woman returned to Dehradun and told her parents what had happened. She returned to the city with her parents a few days later after which a case was registered at Karol Bagh police station.
