Mumbai
Nov 17, 2012
Rape
Description:
			An auto-rickshaw driver from Virar has been arrested for allegedly raping a widow at Nalasopara on Wednesday. 

According to Nalasopara police, Raju Pawar (24) is a friend of the victim's son. The victim, who works for a grocery shop in Malad, was watching television when Pawar came to her Nalasopara (E) home. Initially, he sat down to watch television and later raped the widow, who stays alone. 

The victim complained to the police in the wee hours of Thursday. She said that she had entertained Pawar as he was her son's friend. Her son is married and stays in Mumbai.
