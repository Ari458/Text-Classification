Patuli
Jun 25, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by three unidentified men when she left home after a quarrel at a village in Burdwan district.

The teenager left her home at Adivasipara in Jamalpur village due to an altercation with her mother and was picked up by the three men near a pisciculture farm at Patuli on Katwa Road. She was then gang-raped by them.

The girl, with her clothes torn off, was found by a fisherman who was returning home. The girl had fallen ill after the traumatic experience and was hospitalised.
