Kashmir
Aug 21, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was held for allegedly attempting to rape a minor girl after protests broke out demanding his arrest in south Kashmir's Kulgam district

Tariq Ahmad Dar, a resident of Bugam village in Kulgam, was arrested after locals complained that he had tried to molest a minor girl in his neighbourhood.

The incident led to minor protests in the village with locals demanding Dar's arrest.

Locals complained about the incident to the police. A police team was deputed to the hospital where the girl had been admitted for medical check-up.

Based on her complaint, the team raided the house of Dar, who by then had fled from the spot.

Police detained Dar's brother for questioning, but later set him free.
