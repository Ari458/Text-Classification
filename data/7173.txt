Karnataka
Jun 17, 2015
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman committed suicide by hanging at her uncle's residence allegedly due to dowry harassment, on Tuesday. Rekha married Adarsha, a private bank employee, two years ago.

The police said Adarsha and his parents used to harass Rekha for dowry.

On hearing the news, agitated relatives ransacked Adarsha's house. The police arrested the in-laws, Puttappa and Manoharamma and is on the lookout for Adarsha.
