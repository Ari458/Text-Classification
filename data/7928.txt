Punjab
Oct 07, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The Daba police have booked five persons for allegedly assaulting Narinder Kaur (35) of Gurpal Nagar on Monday. The accused have been identified as Inderjeet Singh (son-in-law of the victim's sister), Amritpal Singh (Inderjeet's brother), and three unidentified persons of Sherpur, Sangrur. Kaur, in her complaint, said that on Monday at around 10.30pm, Inderjeet and Amritpal came to her house - along with three unidentified persons - damaged her property, abused and assaulted her under the influence of alcohol.

Inquiry officer ASI Balbir Singh of Daba police station said a few days back, Kaur's niece and her husband had a fight, after which she took her away from her in-laws, which offended Inderjeet. Late on Monday evening, Inderjeet allegedly assaulted Kaur and pelted stones on her car to threaten her. At the time of the incident, Kaur was alone at home with her aged mother-in-law. After her neighbours gathered on the spot, Inderjeet fled.

The case has been registered under sections 452 (house trespass after preparation for hurt, assault, or wrongful restraint), 427 (mischief causing damage), 354 (assault or criminal force on woman with intent to outrage her modesty), 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation), 148 (rioting, armed with a deadly weapon), and 149 (unlawful assembly, guilty of offence committed in pursuance of a common object) of the IPC. The accused are yet to be arrested.
