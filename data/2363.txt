Madhya Pradesh
Jun 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a 15-year-old youth and has been admitted to the district hospital here with severe injuries, police said today.

The incident took place last evening in Ward Number 13 of Samnapura village where the accused took the girl to an isolated place and sexually assaulted her.

As the victim began bleeding, the accused in a bid to wipe out evidence, washed her in a nearby river.

The accused then left the victim at her home in an unconscious state following which her parents filed a police complaint.
