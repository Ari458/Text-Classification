Hyderabad
Dec 16, 2011
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A guest faculty member Rajat Kumar Mohanty of the English and Foreign Languages University (Eflu) was booked for attempting to sexually assault a 26-year-old female Ph D scholar of department of Linguistics and Phonetics. The accused  is also from the Linguistics department of Eflu. He also forcibly kissed her and then the lady pushed him away and escaped from the room.
