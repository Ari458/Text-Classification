Jaipur
Mar 30, 2012
Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			Locals of P&T colony in Shastri Nagar area informed the police about an abandoned infant girl in a dustbin, but it was too late. "We took the girl to Kanwatia hospital and the doctors declared her brought dead. She was probably born on Monday and her body was dumped in the garbage bin in the night," an officer said. It seems she was strangulated as there were marks on her neck.
