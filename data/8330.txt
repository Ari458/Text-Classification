Punjab
Jan 15, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A local court today sentenced a man to 10 years imprisonment for the rape and kidnapping of a 15-year-old girl.

The court sentenced Narinder, alias Golu, a resident of village near Panipat in Haryana, to 10 years imprisonment and imposed a fine of Rs 58,000 on him.

He was convicted under Sections 363, 366 and 376 (ii) of the IPC and Section 8 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The case was registered on December 20, 2014, when the girl was recovered from the accused. She was kidnapped in 2012.

At the time the girl was kidnapped, she was a Class X student. The police had recovered the girl two years after the incident.

The defence counsel stated that the accused had married the girl.
