Assam
Apr 14, 2015
Molestation, Rape
Description:
			An Army jawan, one of the accused in the rape of a woman in Karbi Anlong district, committed suicide on Monday.

Police said the jawan was identified as Satyabir Dara and belonged to the 8 Jat Regiment. Dara shot himself with his rifle. He was rushed to the hospital but died on the way. The body has been sent for post mortem.

Police officials said Dara is from Haryana.

Various organizations in Karbi Anglong protested and called a strike last Wednesday. On Friday, hundreds of protesters clashed with policemen when the mob tried to storm into an Army camp at Dokmoka in protest against the rape of a woman and molestation of two others. Several protestors and police were hurt during the clash.
