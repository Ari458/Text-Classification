Goa
Oct 01, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The mother of a 10-year-old girl, a Class IV student, has filed a police complaint stating that a school teacher from a government primary school in Mapusa allegedly beat up and injured her daughter. 

The victim girl's mother, a resident of Ganganagar in Khorlim-Mapusa, stated that the incident occurred at 11am on Wednesday.

Mapusa police have booked an offence under Section 323 of the Indian Penal Code and Section 8 of the Goa Children's Act.
