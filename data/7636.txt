Kerala
Aug 10, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 40-year-old man, who allegedly has been abusing his minor daughter sexually for the past two years, was arrested by the Aryanad police on Sunday.

The police said the man was arrested following a complaint from Childline, who had come to know of the incident from the girl's school authorities.

The girl, it is learnt, had told her classmate that her father was raping her repeatedly when her mother was not at home. The classmate then passed on the information to their teachers, who alerted Childline.

The girl, aged 13, was subjected to a medical examination and is undergoing counselling now. The arrested will be produced before court on Monday.
