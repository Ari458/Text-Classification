Punjab
Dec 09, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Seven persons, including three women, in Jalalabad town of Fazilka district have been booked on the complaint of a girl who has alleged that she was sexually exploited by a man for two years on the pretext of marriage.

The victim alleged that when she raised the matter with her cousin's family members and Balkar, they all started threatening her following which she approached the police.

Police booked Balkar and Kartaro Bai of Mehmujoiya village in Jalalabad, Rajinder Singh of Abohar and Harinder, Neetu, Swarna Rani and Jaswant Singh, all residents of Ferozepur. However, no arrest has been made.
