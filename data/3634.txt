Madhya Pradesh
Dec 03, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A woman was allegedly murdered by some unidentified people who later stuffed the body in a sack and dumped it in Heera Nagar area of the city.

The sack containing the body of the woman, around 25 years of age, was found by some people, following which they informed the police.

The body, which is yet to be identified, bore injury marks on the neck, suggesting that she might have been strangulated with a rope.

After getting the autopsy report they would be able to ascertain whether she was sexually assaulted before being killed.
