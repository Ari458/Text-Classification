Maharashtra
May 19, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old man was arrested on Friday for allegedly abducting and raping a 14-year-old girl after promising to marry her. The incident took place in Lohegaon on Thursday.

The Airport police said the suspect Omkar Khadke is a resident of Shivnagar in Lohegaon. Khadke has been charged under sections 363, 366 (abduction) and 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code and the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

"On Thursday, Khadke allegedly abducted the girl and raped her. The girl returned home on Friday and told her brother about the incident. He then lodged a complaint against Khadke. According to the girl, Khadke had promised to marry her. We are investigating," assistant inspector C S Kapse said.
