Nashik
Dec 07, 2011
Rape
Description:
			Jalgaon legislator Dilip Wagh, along with his personal assistant Mahesh Mali, was arrested on February 27,2011 after a 20-year-old girl from Aurangabad lodged a complaint, stating that he had allegedly raped her. In her statement, the woman alleged that Wagh and Mali called her to Nashik, promising to give her a job. After this, he was suspended from the party.
