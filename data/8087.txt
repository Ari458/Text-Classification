Maharashtra
Nov 05, 2015
Domestic Violence
Description:
			An engineer was arrested by the Borivli police late on Wednesday after he allegedly attacked his former wife with a sickle following a dispute. The couple divorced last year after 13 years of marriage. They have two children. 

The incident took place in Gorai where the accused, Milind Kamble, resides. The victim, Bhageshwari Chetwani, lives separately with their kids. The victim and her elder son, had come to Kamble's home to pick up belongings when they had a row and Kamble attacked her. Bhageshwari suffered injuries on her back neck and hands. After cops were informed, Kamble was arrested.
