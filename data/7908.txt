Tamil Nadu
Sep 26, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Pollachi All Woman Police arrested A Ramakrishnan (65) on Thursday for allegedly sexually abusing the seven-year-old daughter of his tenant.

The accused lived on the first floor of the building, while the victim and her family lived on the ground floor. 

The man had allegedly raped the girl when her parents had sent her to his house to play. One of the girl’s family members had seen the abuse and reported it to the police. 

Police arrested the man and remanded him to judicial custody. The girl was taken to CMCH for medical examination.
