Uttar Pradesh
Mar 11, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman was shot dead while three of her family members were seriously injured when some assailants opened fire on them over a land dispute at Mutbhar village in the district.

Four assailants barged into the house of the victim and opened fire. While the woman Riya was killed on the spot, her father Suresh, mother Anita, uncle Chandrapal sustained bullet injuries.

They were rushed to a hospital in a serious condition.

The killings were a fallout of a land dispute, security has been tightened in the village.

Police have registered a case against four people identified as Sunil, Rohtash, Lalit Kumar, and ex-village head Parveen Kumar in this connection.
