Rajasthan
Apr 09, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old handicapped woman was allegedly raped by her husband's friend in Paldi-Meena locality. 

The victim has alleged that when her husband was out of town for a couple of days, his friend Udaram entered her house with another friend. He then raped her while his accomplice sat outside. 

The accused has been booked for rape after the court directed the Kho-Nagorian police to lodge a case, Udaram is absconding.
