Telangana
Feb 15, 2016
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman allegedly committed suicide by consuming pesticide in front of a police station in Sircilla town on Sunday.

Police sources said that Potlapalli Harika of Gudem village in Mustabad mandal had registered a dowry harassment case against her husband.  During the counselling taken up by the police, Niharika sought divorce from her husband. Even as the police were trying to settle the issue, Niharika came to the station and threatened to commit suicide. After coming out of police station she whipped out pesticide bottle and consumed it.  She was shifted to hospital where she was declared dead.
