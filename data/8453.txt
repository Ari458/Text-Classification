Maharashtra
Feb 08, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old watchman deployed at a construction site in Hinjewadi was arrested on Sunday for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl at his residence around 3.30pm on Saturday.

"The suspect, identified as Yogesh Zalte, lured the girl into his house and raped her. He also threatened to kill the girl, if she revealed anything to her parents," said S W Waghmare, sub-inspector of the Hinjewadi police station.

The girl is the daughter of a labourer working at the construction site. The incident came to light after the girl informed her mother.
