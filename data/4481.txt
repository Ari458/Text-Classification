Maharashtra
Mar 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Sangvi police have booked a 19-year-old youth for allegedly raping a 14-year-old girl in Pimple Gurav on the pretext of marrying her. Police have also booked the suspect's friend for allegedly helping him.

The incident took place on March 6 in Pimple Gurav. The suspect had threatened to kill her parents if she told anyone about the incident. However, she informed her parents after which her mother approached the Sangvi police and lodged the complaint.

The girl and the suspect knew each other. On March 6, the suspect had taken her to a room in Pimple Gurav, while his friend was waiting outside. The suspect then raped the girl on the promise of marrying her.
