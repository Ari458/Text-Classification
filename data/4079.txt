Haryana
Feb 05, 2014
Abduction, Murder
Description:
			The body of a 5-year-old girl, who was allegedly abducted and strangulated, was found in a field here this evening.

The body was recovered near Thathera Colony on Pataudi Road, they said adding the girl was kidnapped yesterday.

Police said they were also looking into the possibility of the kidnapper sexually assaulting the girl.
