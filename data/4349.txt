Haryana
Mar 10, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl from Rohtak was allegedly gang-raped by three brothers at Bhattu village of district Fatehabad. The police started investigation into the matter after registering a case of gang-rape. No arrest has been made so far.

Accused have been identified as, Mauji, Bhajju and Naresh, who are absconding after the incident. According to the FIR, the victim alleged that she had come to Bhattu to attend a marriage function and when she want to a room to change her clothes, the trio overpowered her and gang-raped her. She reported the incident to her parents, who approached the police with a complaint.
