Bihar
Dec 09, 2013
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was murdered by her husband for dowry in a village in Bihar's Madhubani district.

The incident took place in Pathra village where the woman, identified as Sunita Devi (26), was strangulated to death by her husband Ashok Rai after her family failed to meet their dowry demands.

Th victim's father has lodged an FIR against his son-in-law and his father charging them with the murder of his daughter for dowry.
