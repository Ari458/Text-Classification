Andhra Pradesh
Jun 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted and murdered by a teenager in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh.

The girl, a student of class 7th, was returning home from school at TVNR Puram village in Palasamudra mandal when the 16-year-old boy sexually assaulted her in a field before hitting her with stone, resulting in her death on the spot.
