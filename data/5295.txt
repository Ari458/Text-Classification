New Delhi
Aug 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			An eight-year-old was raped by her neighbour, a juvenile, at Begampur in outer Delhi. Police said the incident took place on Tuesday evening when the 16-year old boy got the girl to accompany him on some pretext to a forest near their house. Her parents went to a police station. The boy was apprehended and sent to a juvenile home.
