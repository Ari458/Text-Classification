Tamil Nadu
Feb 26, 2015
Abduction
Description:
			Three persons were today arrested for allegedly kidnapping a 14-year old girl, police said.

Selvam (25), who had been allegedly luring the girl to come with him, and his friends Muthu and Easwaran had recently kidnapped her girl when she was on way to the workplace at Mettamalai near here.

On a complaint, Police arrested all the three and handed over the girl to her parents.
