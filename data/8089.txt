Tamil Nadu
Nov 05, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A 40-year-old man was arrested on the charge of sexually harassing a five-year-old child at Natham near here on Wednesday. The arrested was Shahjahan, a tea master at Nallakulam in Natham. 

The police said the accused harassed the girl who had come to her grandmother’s house in Natham from Madurai for holidays. The girl was referred to Dindigul GH for tests. The arrested was produced before Natham court which remanded him to 15 days judicial custody.
