Gurgaon
Oct 12, 2012
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by two youths at Laxman Vihar in Sector 5 of Gurgaon, the 17th such incident within a month in Haryana.

She was raped by the two accused on Thursday when she was playing near her house, SHO Harinder Singh told PTI over phone in 
Chandigarh on Friday.

The accused are in the age group of 20-22 years. The medical examination of the girl confirmed that she was raped.
