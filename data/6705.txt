Kolkata
Mar 30, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped in the Garden Reach area of Kolkata by her boyfriend, who is also a minor, the police said today.

The girl yesterday lodged a complaint with the Garden Reach police station, alleging that her boyfriend had raped her on the night March 25, the police said.

The police has launched an investigation into the case and is searching for the accused who is absconding.
