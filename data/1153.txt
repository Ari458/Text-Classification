Patna
Jan 16, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			The residents saw Upendra Kumar, who was on a motorcycle, trying to tease a girl walking past along with her sister. The youth anticipated the victim and asked her to identify the picture of another woman on his cellphone. When the girl looked at the mobile handset, she was shocked to see pornographic content.

On hearing her screaming for help, the residents rushed to the spot, caught the accused and handed him over to the police, the source added.
