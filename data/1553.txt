Ranchi
Mar 25, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Indian Police Service (IPS) officer P.S. Natarajan who was suspended from service on charges of sexually exploiting a tribal woman, has been dismissed from service.
 
Natrajan, then inspector general of police of Ranchi, remained suspended since August 2005 when a television news channel showed him in a compromising position with a tribal woman.

In her complaint, the victim has said that she was sexually exploited by Natarajan who assured her justice against another IPS official. Following the scandal, the Jharkhand government had initiated the process for Natarajan's dismissal.
