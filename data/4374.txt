Tamil Nadu
Mar 12, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A nine-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by her relative at her residence in Ammapet.

The incident occurred while her mother was away at work in a quarry. Her father had passed away some time back.

The woman returned home to find her daughter in tears, questioned her and later lodged a complaint with police, following which the 23-year-old man was arrested. 

Source: Press Trust of India
