Uttar Pradesh
Feb 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A youth has been sentenced to seven years in jail by a court here which found him guilty of the attempted rape of a seven-year-old girl. 

The court of the Additional District Sessions Judge passed the order against Ashok Yadav and also imposed a fine of Rs 5,000 after convicting him of the charges of attempting to rape the girl at Gopalpur village of Shamli district on May 5, 2013.
