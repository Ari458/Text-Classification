Tamil Nadu
Sep 17, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			Tirunelveli Town police arrested a youth for allegedly sexually harassing a nine-year-old girl on Tuesday. Police said Altaf Hussein (33) of Tirunelveli Town, who was working in Chennai, had come to his native place recently. The girl’s mother rescued her when he had attempted to sexually assault the girl from the same area in a building under construction on September 6.

When he issued a death threat to the woman and the girl, she filed a case with Tirunelveli Town police, who arrested him.
