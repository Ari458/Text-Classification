Maharashtra
Jul 09, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old man has been sentenced to 10 years rigorous imprisonment by the Vasai sessions court for raping a teenager in 2013. 

Sessions judge S G Mehere on Wednesday found Manoj Patara guilty of raping the 13-year-old school-going girl on July 10, 2013. The girl was returning home from school when Patara forcibly took away her to a nearby jungle, gagged her mouth and raped her. 

He left her unconscious in the jungle and returned to his home. The girl regained consciousness and found her way home. She confided in her parents who registered a case under sections 376 (rape), 341 (wrongful constraint), 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) of the IPC and under sections 4 and 8 of the Protection of Children Against Sexual Offences Act. 

Patara who was hiding in a relative's home was arrested two days later. He was lodged in the Thane jail and was out on bail. He has now been arrested.
