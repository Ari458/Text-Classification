Jaipur
Aug 15, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Crime
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped here by two men who also made a video of the act and used it to blackmail the victim for assaulting her repeatedly, police said today.

An FIR was lodged in this regard with Bhankhrota police station here yesterday following a court's directive on a complaint of the victim's father, they said.

Two months ago, the girl was allegedly kidnapped by Raju Mehra and Omprakash from near her house who took her to a room and made her unconsciousness, police said, quoting the FIR.

The accused then raped her and made a clip of the act.

They raped the girl several times later by blackmailing her with this clip.

No arrest has been made in the case and the matter is being probed, Investigating Officer Mohesh Chaudhary said.
