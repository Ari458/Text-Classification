Jharkhand
Jan 08, 2015
Murder
Description:
			The body of a eight-year-old boy who was missing since January five was found from Majarkhoa village in Palamau district, police said today.

The body was found after leads provided by four youths of the village, who were taken into custody for interrogation after the missing boy's father Mahendra Mehta lodged a FIR expressing apprehension of abduction, said Deputy Superintendent of Police, Nazrulla Khan.

All the four have been arrested following the recovery of the body, the DSP said.

Khan said police have launched a hunt to apprehend other culprits, who were at large.

Meanwhile, the villagers gheraoed the police station alleging negligence on the part of police to act in time and demanded action against erring police personnel.
