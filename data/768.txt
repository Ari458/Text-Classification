Jaipur
Nov 26, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The station house officer (SHO) of Kathumar police station has been suspended over allegations that he kept a woman in illegal confinement at the station and molested her. The victim charged the SHO with forcing her to come along with him to the police station from her marriage mandap and later molesting her in station premises and kept her in detention illegally.

Note:
The incident has occurred despite strict directions from police headquarters in Jaipur that woman suspects and witnesses should be accompanied only by policewomen and they should not be kept detained illegally in the police stations across the state.
