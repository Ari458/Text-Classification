Kolkata
Nov 08, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A nursery student of an English medium school in central Kolkata has became the latest victim of molestation. The accused Ricky Singh, 25, is the security guard of the school at R.N. Mukherjee Road, the police said.

The incident came to light on Wednesday when the student of the schools primary section complained to her parents against the guard. Her family lodged a complaint with the Hare Street police station alleging that Ricky molested her after the school hours on November 5, sources revealed.
