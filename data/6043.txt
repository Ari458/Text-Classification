Tamil Nadu
Dec 22, 2014
Murder, Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A 35-year-old man was today arrested for allegedly murdering his 40-day-old boy near here following a family dispute, police said.

The man allegedly murdered the infant by strangling him at his house in Kaikalathur early this morning after a quarrel with his wife, they said.

He was arrested following a complaint lodged by his wife, police added.
