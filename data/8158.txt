Delhi
Dec 12, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested for raping his 12-year-old daughter in a hotel at Paharganj in central Delhi. His family came from Bihar and booked a hotel room for a night on Thursday. 

"The man raped his daughter when the girl's mother was away. He lured her with gifts and asked her to remain quiet about the incident. However, she narrated her ordeal when her mother returned. The woman went to Paharganj police station and got a complaint registered. The man was arrested from the hotel after an hour," said a senior police official.
