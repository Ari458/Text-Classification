Maharashtra
Feb 18, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The five accused arrested by the Khadakpada police officials for allegedly raping a 13-year-old in Kalyan have confessed to the crime.

They are identified as Sameep Patil, 19, Nilesh Kale, 25, Prathmesh Bhoir, 21, Vinay Patil, 20, and Mayuresh Patil, 25. The five are college students and are residents of Kalyan. The cops said one of them is pursuing ITI and they hail from a well-to-do family.

On February 15, between 7.30pm and 8pm, the class IX victim, received a call from an unknown number on her mother's cell and Sameep asked her to join them for a ride near the pipeline area. When the victim came out of her house, Sameep, who was on his two-wheeler, along with one another accused, abducted her. They took her to a nearby field and allegedly raped her. When she returned home, she narrated her ordeal to her parents, who lodged a police case.

Investigation officer from Khadakpada police station, Dinesh Sonawane, said, "The accused have confessed to their crime. The accused were produced in court. Further probe is on."
