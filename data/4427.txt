Uttar Pradesh
Mar 20, 2014
Dowry Harassment, Other
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman succumbed to burn injuries at BRD Medical College. She was brought to hospital with 80% burn injuries. The family members have alleged her in-laws for setting Suman, wife of Bajilal, a resident of Muhammadpur village in Guleria police station, ablaze for dowry. They have lodged a written complaint against Bajilal and his father Shyam Bahal.

Suman, a resident of Madhuban Parsia village in Guleria, got married to Bajilal on May 9, 2014.

The body was sent for postmortem examination.
