Uttar Pradesh
Jun 05, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped at gunpoint by a youth at her house in Khubbapur village in the district.

The girl's mother lodged a complaint with the police alleging that the youth Pankaj Kumar raped the girl when she was alone in the house and threatened to kill her if she reported the matter to police.
