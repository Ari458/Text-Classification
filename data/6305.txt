Rajasthan
Jan 26, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 50-year-old woman guest house owner was found murdered with her throat slit in a room in Malviya Nagar area of the city, police said today.

Anuradha Sethi was found lying in a pool of blood and her throat was slit by some unknown persons yesterday, SHO of Jawhar Circle police station Mukesh Choudhary said.

Forensic experts have been called and a probe was on into the matter, he said.

Sethi was running a guest house and a restaurant and her daughter spotted the murder and raised an alarm.

"At the time of the murder, neither any guest nor staff of the guest house were present," the SHO said.
