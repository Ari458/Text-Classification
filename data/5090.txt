Uttar Pradesh
Jul 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was waylaid on her way to a computer coaching centre and allegedly gang-raped by three men in Khandauli town.

According to a police complaint, the incident took place on July 11 when the girl was going to a computer training centre. The three youths, two of whom hail from the girl's Nauki Sarai locality, waylaid and allegedly gang-raped her.

They then dumped the girl near her house. She was found late on Saturday night in an unconscious state.
