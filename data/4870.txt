Rajasthan
Jun 14, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by three men on knife point at Chhetra village of Pali district.

The victim alleged in her complaint filed at Raipur police station yesterday that Ishwar (30) and his two friends gang-raped her on June 8 while she was returning home, and threatened to stab her when she protested, police said.

The victim was sent for a medical examination earlier today.
