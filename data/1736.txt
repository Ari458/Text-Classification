Tripura
Jun 24, 2012
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A girl from Tripura bound for Guwahati to face a railway recruitment interview was allegedly gangraped by three youths at Byrnihat on the Assam-Meghalaya border early yesterday. 

The trio allegedly kidnapped her from an eatery at Byrnihat around 4.30am yesterday and raped her near the Bees Mile MVI weighbridge.

Talking to The Telegraph over phone, Ri Bhoi superintendent of police C.A. Lyngwa said the police had already arrested the three culprits. We will do everything possible to have the culprits punished.
