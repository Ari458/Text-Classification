Haryana
Jun 20, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old married woman was allegedly gang-raped by three persons in a field of Bibipur village yesterday.

One of the accused identified as Ram Niwas offered a lift to the victim for going to the city market but took her to a field where she was allegedly gang-raped by the accused and two of his accomplices.

The trio have been booked for rape. Ram Niwas and one Neeraj have been arrested in the connection, police said adding, efforts are on to nab the accused.
