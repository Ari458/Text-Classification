Andhra Pradesh
Feb 04, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 22-year-old married woman was allegedly raped by two youth at Ganeshwaram village in Raghunadhapalem mandal on Monday afternoon. The incident, which occurred in broad daylight, triggered outrage among the relatives of the victim. They attacked the house of one of the accused and ransacked the furniture.

Sources said that the tribal woman, a farm labourer, was waylaid by two motorcycle-borne youth - Ajmeera Rama (28) and Malothu Mangilal (21) - near an agriculture field on the outskirts of the village. The duo allegedly gagged her mouth and dragged her to a secluded place adjacent to a cotton field and raped her.
