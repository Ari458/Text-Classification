Uttar Pradesh
Jul 16, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year old girl was allegedly gang-raped by two youths in a moving car at Mawana town, 25 kms from the district headquarters.

The incident took place on Monday night when the girl was on her way to the market and two youths abducted her, following which she was gang-raped in a moving car.

The victim was later thrown outside the Inter College on the Meerut-Mawana road, police said, adding a case has been lodged against one Shekhu alias Bittu who belongs to Mavana town and Saifal, a resident of Amroha.
