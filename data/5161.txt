Maharashtra
Jul 24, 2014
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old woman has filed a case of alleged abduction and rape at gun-point, at Nandanvan police station. She told the police that the incident took place on July 14. She claimed that she was abducted from Krida Square in a car by five youths who took her to an abandoned room in Nandanvan area and one of them raped her at gun-point. She has also alleged that the accused had even filmed her. She has named Amit Murkute, Avinash Murkute and three others identified as Suyog, Vishal and Vijendra as the culprits. Police have registered a case.
