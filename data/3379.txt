Arunachal Pradesh
Oct 20, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			An assistant teacher has been arrested in East Siang district on charges of sexual assault on a minor girl student.

The teacher allegedly took the 10-year-old girl to the market on the pretext of taking photograph, enticed her by offering sweets and allegedly raped her in the backyard of the industrial complex at Niglok.

The teacher of Niglok Upper Primary School under Ruksin block in the district was arrested following an FIR lodged by the victim's parents.
