West Bengal
Dec 08, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A man allegedly hacked his wife to death following an altercation over a family problem at Basirhat in North 24 Parganas district.

During an altercation, Sukumar Mondal attacked his wife Rekha (31) with a chopper and hit her on the head killing her on the spot.

Sukumar, a father of two sons and a daughter, was arrested.
