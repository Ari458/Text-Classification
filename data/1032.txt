Bengaluru
Dec 29, 2011
Rape, Street Harassment
Description:
			The woman had gone to a church along with a friend. While returning near Berlin Street, the duo was waylaid by an 11-member gang. The gang allegedly assaulted the man and dragged the woman to a cemetery that is close by, and reportedly raped her. Before fleeing, the miscreants allegedly robbed the duo of their vehicle, two mobile phones and a guitar.

"Earlier, She did not file rape case against the accused fearing social stigma.," sources said.
