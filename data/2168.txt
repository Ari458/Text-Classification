Malviya Nagar
May 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was raped allegedly by her neighbour here, police said today.

The incident was reported from Malviya Nagar and the accused Vijay, a labourer, has been arrested.

The girl was alone when the incident took place. Her parents had gone out for work.

The incident came to light when a neighbour spotted the victim crying outside the house.

Source: Press Trust of India
