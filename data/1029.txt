Kannur
Dec 29, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A young woman from West Bengal, who reportedly came in search of her lover, a migrant worker in Kerala, was gang-raped and abandoned naked in Kannur district. The four assailants had assaulted the woman after locking her sister's husband and his friend, both migrant workers, inside a truck, which the victim and the two others were travelling in.
