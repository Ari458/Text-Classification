Kanpur
Nov 04, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A 27-year-old unemployed man killed his wife and 18-month-old son at Nai Basti in Macharia locality on Wednesday night. He had entered into an altercation with his wife after the dinner. On early Thursday morning, his father inquired about his health while he was sitting dull in front of his room. He called him to say that he had killed his family members.

Anil was jobless since the past six months and was depending on his father and other members in the family besides relatives for money. This probably led to frequent quarrels between the couple.
