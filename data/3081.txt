Chhattisgarh
Sep 09, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman was allegedly gangraped by her husband and in-laws in Janjgir-Champa district of Chhattisgarh.

The woman's husband, brother-in-law and father-in-law were arrested after the victim, a resident of Barpali village in Janjgir, filed a police complaint that she was raped by them.

The victim had filed a complaint on Sunday that she was sexually abused by her husband and in-laws.

In her complaint, she also said her in-laws had molested her several times earlier.
