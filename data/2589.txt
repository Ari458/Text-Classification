Uttar Pradesh
Mar 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly gangraped by three men in Haliyapur village.
	
The girl was returning home when she was forcibly taken to the house of Dadai, a local, with the help of Naumilal of Barabanki and Jugnu of Bakshi ka Talab, Lucknow. She was allegedly raped at gun-point by the three.
	
The family reported the incident to police following which the girl was sent for medical examination.
