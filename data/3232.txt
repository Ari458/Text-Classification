Haryana
Oct 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly gang raped by three youths at Rajana Khurd village of Jind district.

The incident took place when the 15-year-old victim had gone out to answer nature's call. She was overpowered by three youths, who grabbed her and took her to a nearby field and gang raped her.

The accused have been identified as Pawan, Narender and Dalsher, all residents of the same village.

The victim's medical examination has confirmed rape.
