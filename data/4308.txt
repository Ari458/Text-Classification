Uttar Pradesh
Mar 06, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A local court here has sentenced three persons to 10 years of imprisonment for gang-raping a minor girl.

Additional District and Sessions Judge Davendra Singh also imposed a fine of Rs 20,000 each on the three convicts Arvind Yadav, Manoj Kumar and Sanoj Yadav, the prosecution said.

The trio abducted the 17-year-old girl in Gudaripur village of the district on July 15, 2011 and gang-raped her.

She was rescued after one day of captivity and her medical examination confirmed rape.
