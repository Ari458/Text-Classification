Gujarat
Jun 01, 2015
Molestation
Description:
			Police on Monday arrested a 24-year -old man for allegedly attempting to rape a 35-year-old nurse at the civil hospital in Rajkot.

Assistant sub-inspector Rekha Savaliya of Women police station said the alleged incident took place last night.

The accused was identified as Suresh Rathwa, native of Chhota Udepur district, Savaliya said.

Last night, Rahtwa followed the woman into hospital's restroom, and allegedly tried to rape her. Hearing her shouts for help, some other staff members came and caught Rahtwa, said the police officer.
