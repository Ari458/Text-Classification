Haryana
Aug 20, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in the city, police said today. 

According to a complaint filed by the girl's mother, her 16-year-old daughter was getting trained to become a beautician at a parlor in the city. 

On August 3, while the girl was returning home, she met Suraj, one of her neighbors, following which he asked the girl to accompany him on his bike saying he had something important to discuss with her. 

The girl, believing him, accompanied the man to his house after which Suraj allegedly raped her. 

The girl narrated the incident to her mother yesterday and a complaint was filed with the police. A case was registered under section 363 (kidnapping), 366A (procuration of minor girl), 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of IPC and section 6 of POCSO (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences) Act. 

The police have started investigations in the case, said Investigating officer ASI Sunita Devi.
