Tamil Nadu
Jan 23, 2016
Abuse
Description:
			R.S. Puram Police have arrested T. Manoj (33) of Seeranaickenpalayam on charges of assaulting his house owner S. Jayalakshmi on Wednesday.

The police said that when Ms. Jayalakshmi questioned Manoj for frequently quarrelling with his wife, the later grew angry, abused, harassed and also assaulted her. He also criminally intimidated her.
