Banswara
Mar 22, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A tribal woman was allegedly stripped and molested amid threats of killing her husband in Banswara district in Rajasthan.

The couple had gone to the wife's maternal home in Nathpura village to attend a function. The husband, Prem Singh, alleged in his complaint that at the function, Kunji, Hariya and Ekan, his wife Kali's relatives, threatened to kill him to settle an old dispute. They tied him to a chair and beat him up. When his wife came to his rescue, she was asked to strip if she wanted to save her husband.
