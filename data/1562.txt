Masakkalipalayam
Mar 27, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 37-year-old lecturer identified as A Senthilnathan was arrested for allegedly entering into his neighbour woman's house and attempting to molest her at Masakkalipalayam near Singanallur.

Police said the accused is working as commerce department lecturer in a private college at Kalapatti. On Friday evening, he came to his house in a drunken mood and trespassed into his neighbour woman P Tamilselvi's house. She was alone at the home and he attempted to outrage her modesty. He also criminally intimated her.
