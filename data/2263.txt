Rajasthan
May 21, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A teenage girl committed suicide after she was unable to withstand the harassment by a youth who insisted on marrying her.

The 17-year old girl had set herself ablaze on Saturday night. She was rushed to a local government hospital, where she succumbed to her injuries last night.

The girl's father has alleged that Sonu Kumar (21), who is a distant relative and hails from Haryana, had been pressuring her to marry him.

"Primary investigation revealed that the accused had been threatening her to accept his proposal of marriage. The girl was suffering depression for some time due to this," police said.
