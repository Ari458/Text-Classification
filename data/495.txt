Pune
Oct 21, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A city court on Friday awarded life imprisonment to three men for gangraping a 25-year-old management graduate. 

Subhash Bhosle and two other convicts in the case had raped the victim inside a moving car. 

The victim was waiting for a bus when she got into their car, mistaking it for a shuttle service. The trio then drove around the city for eight hours, and raped the victim repeatedly.
