Rajasthan
May 17, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A Sub-Inspector has been suspended following allegations that he molested a woman in Rajasthan's Tonk district, police said today.

Narendra Parik, who was posted as the in-charge of Dooni police station, had allegedly molested a woman from Gopipura village a few days back when she had gone to see her plot located near his house.

District SP S Parimala said that Parik had been suspended pending investigation into the complaint filed earlier this week by the woman.
