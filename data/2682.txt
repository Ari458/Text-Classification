Lamlai. Manipur
Jul 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man allegedly raped his niece at an interior village in Manipur's Imphal East district.

A complaint was lodged about the 50-year-old man Kangujam Loken allegedly raping his 11-year-old niece taking advantage that the family of the victim was working in the fields, at Phaknung village, under Lamlai police station on July 5.

The alleged rapist, who was a father of two children, was absconding.

The girl, a student of class five in a local school was sent for medical check up at the Regional Institute of Medical Science and Hospital.

Enraged local people demanded that the man be handed over to them after immediate arrest.
