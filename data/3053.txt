New Delhi
Sep 04, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 40-year-old nurse was allegedly raped by a 45-year-old man on the pretext of marriage in Pandav Nagar area of East Delhi.

According to police, Beenu has been arrested after the woman's complaint.

Beenu and the victim both are residents of Kerala. As per the woman's complaint, she is a widow and knew Beenu since 2005 who has been exploiting her physically on the pretext of marriage since the last eight years.

Whenever, she asked Beenu to marry her he made one excuse after the other. She approached the police when she got to know that Beenu was already married and her family lived in Kotla.
