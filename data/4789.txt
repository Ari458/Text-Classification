Uttar Pradesh
May 25, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and gang-raped at gun point by three youths in Kheri Firozabad village of the district.

The girl was yesterday abducted from outside her house by three youths, who took her to a nearby jungle and gang-raped her. The accused also threatened her with dire consequences if she told anyone about it.

The victim later narrated the incident to her father, who then lodged a complaint with police.
