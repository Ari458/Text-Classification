Uttarakhand
Sep 08, 2013
Crime
Description:
			Wife of a homeopathic doctor was found dead with her throat slit at her residence in Patel Nagar area.

Body of the 70-year-old woman was found lying in a pool of blood by her husband when he returned from clinic.

Police suspect the woman might have been murdered over a personal enmity as the assailants did not appear to have entered with an intention of loot because all the valuables were found intact.
