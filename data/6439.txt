Uttar Pradesh
Feb 14, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A 12-year-old boy was sodomized by three youths under New Mandi police station area.

As per the complaint lodged yesterday by the victim's father, his son had gone to play cricket in Kambalwala locality when he was sodomized and threatened by the accused, they said.

The accused, who are also minors, were known to the victim, police added.

A case has been registered in this connection, they said, adding, further investigation is on.
