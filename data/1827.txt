Chamba
Nov 11, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A dalit woman from Churah tehsil of Chamba district was gang-raped by five youths, including a minor, on Thursday night in Chamba town while she was heading towards a hospital for treatment.

According to police, the victim was overpowered by the accused neat the court complex before being raped. Hearing her cries, a police patrolling party rescued her from the clutches of the accused.
