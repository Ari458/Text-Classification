Andhra Pradesh
Dec 15, 2013
Molestation
Description:
			A photo studio owner was arrested for allegedly trying to rape a 19-year-old girl in Vuyyur town in Krishna district.

V. Kumar allegedly lured the girl, who works at a mobile phone shop near his studio, to come to his studio on some pretext. He then rolled down the shutter and tried to rape her.

However, an alert owner of a cyber cafe witnessed the incident and with the help of locals rescued the teenager.
