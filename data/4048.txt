Tamil Nadu
Feb 04, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Police arrested Praveen (21) of U. Mangalam near Neyveli on Monday in connection with alleged gang-rape of a differently-abled woman. They are also on the look out for five more persons in this regard. Police sources said that when the 20-year-old woman, daughter of Chidambaram, was returning home, the gang, lying in wait, gagged her and took her to a secluded spot and outraged her modesty. Police have booked a case.
