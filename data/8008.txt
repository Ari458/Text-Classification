Delhi
Oct 25, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 37-year-old television news channel producer has alleged that a 27-year-old man raped her several times.

The accused identified by the Delhi Police as Harkeerat Singh was arrested from Ludhiana. The victim had registered the case against Singh at the Vasant Vihar police station in south Delhi in March. A manhunt was launched to find him.
The victim told the police that Singh raped her in September 2014 in Manesar and later in Jaipur, Ludhiana and Vasant Vihar. 

She also claimed that Singh had promised to marry her, but for the past few months, he had stopped receiving her phone calls. 

"Singh has been arrested from Ludhiana and booked under the relevant Sections of the Indian Penal Code," said a police officer.
