Chandigarh
Nov 26, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			In an over four-year-old rape case, a local court on Friday acquitted a CBI inspector and a Chandigarh police head constable.

The victim, 40-year-old Hisar resident had moved an application before the Chandigarh SSP alleging that she was raped by Kundu and Singh.
