Maharashtra
Apr 29, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Sangvi police on Sunday arrested a middle-aged man for allegedly raping a three-year-old daughter of his tenant in Pimple Gurav on April 24. 

Following a complaint lodged by the girl's mother, the police have charged the man under section 376 (rape) of the Indian Penal Code and sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act. 

He was remanded in police custody till May 3. 

Archana Patil, sub-inspector of the Sangvi police station, told TOI that the incident took place around 9.30am on Thursday. 

The complainant's husband, who is a truck driver, had rented a room in a building owned by the suspect. "The three-year-old girl was playing with the grand daughter of the suspect in front of their house. When the complainant came out to check on her daughter, she found her missing," Patil said. 

When the complainant went inside the suspect's house, she found him molesting her daughter. "She raised an alarm and rescued the girl," Patil said.
