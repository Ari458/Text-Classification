Andhra Pradesh
Nov 18, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A headmaster of a private school in Saidabad was arrested on charge of harassing a class IX girl student.

Accused Yogeshwar Rao was allegedly misbehaving with the girl for the past few months.

Though the girl apparently kept the matter to herself, her parents grew suspicious as she was not attending the school for the last four days.

A complaint was lodged with Saidabad police after the girl confided in her parents and revealed the matter to them.
