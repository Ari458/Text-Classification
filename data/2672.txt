Rajasthan
Mar 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by a youth from her neighbourhood in Shivbari area in the
district.
	
The accused, identified as Sanjay, a college student, forcibly took the 16-year-old girl to his house and raped her.
	
Later, the victim informed her family members about the incident following which they lodged an FIR against Sanjay.
