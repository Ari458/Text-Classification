Punjab
Jul 14, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police arrested a man for raping his minor daughter in city late on Sunday night.

In her police complaint, the seven-year-old's mother said, "I, along with my son, had gone to a market at 6.30 pm on July 6. When we returned home after an hour, my daughter started crying. Later, she told me that her father raped her while we were away."

Investigating officer Harbans Singh said, "The accused, who was arrested from outside his house, was booked under the Protection of Children from Sexual Abuse Act and the IPC. Doctors at civil hospital conducted the girl's medical examination. The report is awaited." The 28-year-old accused was produced in a local court, which sent him in one-day police remand.
