Chandigarh
Mar 25, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A woman was allegedly raped by her co-passenger who also robbed her of gold ear rings and a mobile phone in Pinjore town in Panchkula district.
     
The alleged rapist Sukhwinder Singh alias Sukha, hailing from Patiala district in Punjab, had been arrested.
     
The alleged incident happened in Khera Basola area when the woman and Sukhwinder alighted from a
vehicle.
     
After getting down, Sukhwinder allegedly raped her, snatched her mobile phone and robbed her of gold ear rings before fleeing.
