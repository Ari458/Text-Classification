Punjab
May 20, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl has accused her stepfather and his three relatives of repeatedly raping her for last one year.

The victim in her police complaint alleged that she was raped by her stepfather identified as Kashmir Singh, his father Guljar Singh and his brothers Kuldip Singh and Amrik Singh.

The girl alleged that she had been sexually exploited by the accused for the last one year.

The victim's mother had married Kashmir in 2012 and had been living with him since then.
