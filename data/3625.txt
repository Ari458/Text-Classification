Uttar Pradesh
Dec 01, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A woman and her two children have allegedly been strangled to death by unidentified people in Jalaun district.

The bodies of Shakeela Begum (35), Yaseen (10) and Muskaan (7) were found at their residence in Abdullahpur village of Gohan.

Prima facie it seemed that they were strangulated.
