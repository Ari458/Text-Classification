Goa
Mar 30, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			A Goa Police constable has been arrested for allegedly molesting a 22-year-old girl near a picnic spot.

As per the complaint filed with Verna police, constable Azad Ahmad with the escort cell, molested the girl, who was holidaying with her boyfriend at Verna spring, a known tourist attraction. The girl has alleged that the constable, who was accompanied by his friends, molested her in an inebriated condition and pushed her boyfriend aside.
