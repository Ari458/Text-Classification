Rajasthan
Aug 19, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 50-year-old man has been booked on the charge of attempting to rape his daughter-in-law in Sanganer Sadar.

Anandi Lal, a labourer, was booked after his 20-year-old daughter-in-law lodged an FIR against him for attempting to rape her.

According to her complaint, Lal tried to rape her when his son was out for work, but she managed to escape.
