Andhra Pradesh
Jan 10, 2014
Murder
Description:
			K. Meenakshi (28) was murdered allegedly by her husband Srinivas at their house at KPHB Colony. The couple got married seven months ago.

Srinivas returned home in an inebriated condition and picked up an argument with Meenakshi over a family dispute.

An enraged Srinivas attacked her with a stick resulting in grievous head injuries.

Death was instant for Meenakshi, the accused escaped after committing the crime.
