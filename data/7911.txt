Tamil Nadu
Sep 23, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			Two persons  S. Mahesh (29) and S. Vinod Kumar (28) of Melpatti colony in Gudiyatham taluk  were arrested by the All Women Police Station, Gudiyatham on Tuesday on charges of cheating, rape and criminal intimidation of a 22-year-old woman of Melpatti village who was in love with the former.

According to the police, Mahesh and the woman were in love for the last one year, but the former not only refused to marry her but also threatened to kill her if she insisted on marriage.

A few days ago, the woman was caught on camera in a compromising position with Mahesh by his friend Vinod Kumar.

He threatened to post the picture, in his mobile phone, on WhatsApp.

On a complaint from the woman, Mahesh and Vinod Kumar were arrested under sections 417 (cheating), 376 (rape), 506 (1) (criminal intimidation) and 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) of the Indian Penal Code and section 4 of The Protection of Women against Sexual Harassment Act.
