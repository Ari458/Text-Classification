Goa
Nov 04, 2011
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			The state government has issued a notification transferring the investigation in a case - wherein a doctor's wife in Margao had gone missing after she was allegedly abused by her husband - over to the jurisdiction of CBI.

The woman, Archana Dessai, has been missing since the last 11 months. CBI will study the matter and then decide on whether to accept it or not.
