West Bengal
Jul 04, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 35-year-old married woman has been allegedly gang-raped in West Bengal's 24 North Paraganas district, a senior police official said.

The woman was kidnapped by four persons in a vehicle on June 26 and allegedly gang-raped by them at Garapotha area, according to additional superintendent of police Bhaskar Mukherjee.

The accused left the victim in an unconscious state and fled after committing the crime.

The police started investigating the matter after the victim lodged an FIR with the Bonga police station yesterday.

The woman was subsequently admitted to the Bonga hospital and later discharged.
