Delhi
May 05, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A four-year-old girl, who was allegedly molested by her 20-year-old neighbour, was brought to AIIMS Trauma Centre on Thursday evening with minor injuries. 

According to sources at the hospital, The girl was brought to the hospital in an unconscious state with abrasion wounds and swelling on her right cheek. She was bleeding from her ear and nose. She also sustained minor head injuries.The girl was made to undergo a medical examination which confirmed molestation. According to police, the youth allegedly molested the child who was playing in the area. Hearing the girl scream, her mother went to the youth's house and tried to nab him but he fled.
