Uttar Pradesh
Feb 05, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 27-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by two youths in Piplheda village of the district.

The accused barged into her house in the village under Mussorie police station and raped her at gunpoint, SHO Virendra Yadav said.

An FIR has been registered against accused Shakeel (24) and Tasleem (23 yrs) under section 376(2)(g) of Indian Penal Code.

The victim has been sent for medical examination, the SHO said, adding that a manhunt has been launched to nab the absconding duo.
