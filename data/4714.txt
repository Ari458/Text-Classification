Jammu
May 09, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Police based on the rape complaint arrested a youth from Jammu and are further investigating the matter. 

A police officer said, "The girl who is from Kishtwar reached her relatives house in Jammu and fell unconscious. Family members shifted her to hospital." 

"After two days she gained her conscious and told police that a youth Jatinder Singh from the Chinor area lured her to accompany him and then committed rape on her. She also alleged the accused gave her some substance which knocked her out by the time she reached home and had to be hospitalized," he added.
