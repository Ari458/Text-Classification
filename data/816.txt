Kalikapur
Dec 01, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Other
Description:
			Sunil Meher from Odisha, the alleged kingpin, of a racket that lured attractive young girls with promises of jobs, gangraped them and forced them into the sex trade, has been arrested by the Police in a raid in Kalikapur.
He used the same modus operandi in Kolkata and Bhubaneswar and made at least `10 lakh a month. 
 
The Garfa police swooped down on him while he was trying to transport some call girls from Kolkata to Bhubaneswar.
