Hyderabad
Jul 28, 2015
Murder
Description:
			An 18-year-old girl was found murdered on the street at Shanthinagar in Lallaguda police station limits on Monday morning. Police are yet to identify the girl and have registered a case of murder. Locals who saw the body on the roadside had informed police.

According to Karan Kumar Singh, inspector, Lallaguda, the location where the body was found is not the crime scene. He said that the murder could have happened between 12pm and 6am.

The cause of death is a head injury and there are no marks of physical assault on her body. She seems to be a Lambada girl from a poor family, he added.

The body has been sent to Gandhi Hospital for autopsy. The inspector said that they are investigating and denied possibility of a rape attempt.
