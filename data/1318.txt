Betavad village
Feb 19, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A godman (Sadhu) Ramdas Charandas Jat has been arrested from Betavad village of Maharashtra for allegedly kidnapping the 20-year-old mentally challenged girl in October 2011 and raping her three times when she was in his custody. Jat was in Surat's Chimni Tekra area on October 7, 2011, when he spotted the girl, daughter of a labourer. He lured her and took her to Udhna railway station in an autorickshaw. He then took the girl to different locations in Gujarat and Maharashtra and on Thursday she was spotted by her relatives at Betavad village with Jat.
