Kerala
Jun 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Mother of an eight-year-old girl has approached the Chottanikkara police against an 80-year-old man for allegedly sexually abusing her daughter. 

Following the complaint, the girl was produced before the Ernakulam district child welfare committee (CWC) for counselling. Members of the CWC found the allegations correct. 

As per the direction of the CWC, a woman sub-inspector from Aluva police station will record the statement of the girl on Saturday. "Once the statement is recorded, we will get a clear picture about the incident. A case will be registered against the elderly man, if required," said a source.
