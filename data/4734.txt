Uttar Pradesh
May 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Inchauli area here following which irate people pelted stones at the accused's house leading to tension.

The accused identified as Sonu, lured the girl on Monday evening to accompany him to the forest area and raped her. She was spotted by her family members in an unconscious state yesterday morning.

An angered mob pelted stones at the accused's house yesterday leading to tension in the area as the accused and victim belong to separate communities, police said adding, the situation was under control now.
