Maharashtra
May 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Sarkarwada police on Tuesday arrested three people in connection with the alleged gang-rape of a 14-year-old girl in the city's Old Gangapur Naka area.

Raju Dassappa Mala (22), Naresh Bhandari (22) and Prabhakar Harijan (22), all residents of the same locality, produced before a local court, that remanded them in police custody till May 19. One more accused in the case is the survivor's brother, who is on the run.

A complaint was lodged by the survivor's father, a labourer by profession, who said his daughter was raped several times by the four from April 27 to May 8 at his residence.

The complainant alleged that the four threatened her daughter of dire consequences if she spoke about the incident. However, the girl later narrated the matter to her father, who lodged a police complaint.
