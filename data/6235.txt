Tripura
Jan 17, 2015
Crime
Description:
			A man was arrested on the charge of his attempt to bury alive his nine-year old daughter at Putia in Sipahijala district of Tripura, police today said.

When Abul Hussain was forcibly trying to bury her daughter Rukshena in a pit in the backyard of his residence, a neighbour saw it and raised an alarm following which locals rescued the girl yesterday, SP (Control), Uttam Bhaumick said.

The incident took place when her mother was not at home.

The girl was admitted to a hospital where her condition is stated to be serious.
