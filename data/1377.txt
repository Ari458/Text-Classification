Madhya Pradesh
Feb 25, 2012
Rape
Description:
			The 36-year-old woman victim who cannot speak or hear was gang raped by the people who had their office near the victim's residence. The victim was gang raped for a week by three persons in Depalpur area of Indore, Madhya Pradesh, police said yesterday. The three used the woman's two-year-old kid to blackmail her. They held a knife to the child's neck every time they raped her, said an official.
