Rajasthan
Dec 23, 2013
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A middle-aged woman railway employee has accused a Senior Divisional Commercial Manager of North Western Railway of harassing her for a period of over one year.

The woman, a reservation booking clerk of the Jodhpur Division, lodged a complaint with police  and sought action against the accused.

He had been trying every move to get close to the victim and also sent objectionable SMSs and MMSs, which she tolerated for long as she could not muster the courage to file a police complaint against him.

The employee alleged that she had been constantly harassed by Meena for about a year.

The railway authority, following a departmental inquiry, has sent Meena on leave for 15 days.
