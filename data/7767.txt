Uttarakhand
Sep 07, 2015
Rape
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a 30-year-old man was brutally murdered in his residence in Ansal Plaza in Dehradun.

The murderer allegedly wrote a note on the wall claiming that the man he murdered, had raped his sister. He stated that he murdered the man to take revenge for the same. Dehradun police is currently investigating the case.

The owner of the house in which the man stayed said, "he was like family and used to be regular to our house. He was very sincere in studies and had come here for a job. Since he had no place to stay, I asked him to stay in my house".
