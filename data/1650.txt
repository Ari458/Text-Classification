Gurgaon
May 02, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			A 23-year-old girl from Manipur, working in a shopping mall in Gurgaon, says she has been molested at her home, allegedly by her neighbour. She says she forgot to lock her apartment door at night.

The alleged victim lives with two young men who she says are her cousins.

Students and young people working in Delhi who belong to the North East complain of regular discrimination.
