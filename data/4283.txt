Andhra Pradesh
Mar 01, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Murder
Description:
			A paedophile was arrested by the Chandanagar police for the kidnap and murder of an eight-year-old boy. Raman Patel, a labourer, was nabbed based on the information given by some construction workers employed at Sri Sai Developers.

The boy went missing on February 18, following which his mother lodged a complaint with the Chandanagar police. On February 22, cops found a dead body amid some bushes in a park at Huda layout, Nallagandla village. Locals identified the body as that of the missing boy.

Based on information given by local construction workers, cops arrested Raman Patel. During interrogation, it came to light that the accused had sexually assaulted the boy before strangulating him to death.
