Jajpur
Jun 08, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An eight-year old girl was sexually exploited allegedly by a teacher who gave her private tuition in Urdu in Vyas Nagar town in Odisha's Jajpur district.

According to a complaint filed with the police by the girls father, his daughter, a student of class III in an English medium school at Vyas Nagar took private tuitions from Muzafar Housen, a teacher at a madrasa at Ahmadnagar.

The girl told her mother about her teacher's alleged exploitation last Wednesday.

When the father of the girl sought action against the teacher from the madrasa management, he absconded.
