Outer Delhi
Oct 23, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Deshraj (20) has been arrested for allegedly raping a 15-year-old student in Sultanpuri area of outer district, said police. Deshraj is a school dropout," said a senior cop. The accused lured the victim while she was coming back from school and raped her after taking her to an isolated spot, said police. 

The girl reported the matter to her parents who in turn informed cops. "The girl would sometimes talk to Deshraj on her way back from school," said cops.
