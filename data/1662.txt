Rewari
May 03, 2012
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A youth, who allegedly raped a 19-year-old girl repeatedly for nearly three weeks, has been arrested, police said today. The girl, hailing from Kanhora village in Rewari, was enticed by Krishan Kumar and taken to Bathinda in Punjab on April nine, they said.

However, the girl, a student of B.A (Ist year), managed to escape and returned here on April 30. The girl's statement was recorded in a court and after her medical test, a case of abduction and rape was registered against Kumar.
