Tamil Nadu
Oct 15, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Two sexual harassment cases, including one involving a minor, were reported in Coimbatore district on Tuesday. 

A 38-year-old woman registered a complaint of rape, confinement and criminal intimidation against S Vignesh, a farmer in whose house she worked as a maidservant. 

In her complaint, the woman, who has been married for 18 years and has a 17-year-old daughter, stated that Vignesh had been sexually assaulting and raping her for more than five years. She told police officials that he prevented her from speaking to her husband, locking her up in a room. 

The Pollachi all-women police have booked a case under Sections 376 (rape), 506 (i) (criminal intimidation) and 342 (punishment for wrongful confinement) against Vignesh. Vignesh's wife and seven-year-old daughter left him two years ago.
