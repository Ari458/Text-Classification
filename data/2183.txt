Himachal Pradesh
May 10, 2013
Rape
Description:
			As per the complaint of the girl, she was abducted and raped in Biar Khurd village on March 26 but she did not report the matter as the accused had threatened to kill her.

As per the statement of the girl, she was abducted by three persons and raped by one of them in Biar Khurd while the two others assisted him in the crime, Hamirpur district police chief, Jagat Ram said.
