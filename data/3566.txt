Gujarat
Nov 23, 2013
Abduction, Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old woman was allegedly abducted and gang-raped by two men near Khatva village in Banaskantha district.

The incident occurred when the woman was on her way to home in Sotmala village, 20 kms from Bhildi taluka.

An FIR lodged by the victim said two motorcycle-borne youths, identified as Vishnuji Thakore and Hakaji Thakore of Khatva (aged around 24), abducted and molested her when she was heading to home.

According to police, the accused forced her to sit pillion and took her to a hut in a field where they repeatedly raped her overnight.

The victim was sent for medical check-up.
