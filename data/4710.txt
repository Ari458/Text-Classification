Rajasthan
May 12, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old nursing student was allegedly raped by a youth on pretext of marriage in Nayapura area.

The victim has alleged in her complaint that 26-year-old Premchand Mali befriended her about two years ago when she came to Kota for nursing course, SHO Ramkhiladi Meena said.

The accused raped her several times on pretext of marriage, Meena said adding, when the victim pressurised him for marriage, he turned out to be already married.
