Tamil Nadu
Apr 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old man, working as an assistant manager in a private financial firm, was arrested on charges of raping a woman, running a self-help group, on the promise of extending loans to its member 

The 38-year-old woman, who is unmarried and working as a tailor, had lodged a complaint with All Woman Police Station that one Paramasivam used to visit her house frequently in Ondipudur on the pretext of discussing lending loans to the members of the SHG. 

During such a visit, he outraged her modesty and raped her. 

Based on the complaint, police nabbed Paramasivam, who reportedly told them that since the woman was elder to him, he was not willing to marry her, following which he was arrested and remanded to custody.
