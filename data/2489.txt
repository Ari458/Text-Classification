Chiloda
Jun 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) constable was arrested today on charges of allegedly raping a minor girl in Gandhinagar.

The incident took place at a CRPF camp in Chiloda late last night.

The police said that he would be produced in court to seek further remand.

The girl informed her relative about the incident who then registered a complaint with the police.

Source: Press Trust Of India
