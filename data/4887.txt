Rajasthan
Jun 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old widow in the city has accused a man of raping her on the pretext of marrying her. The victim also alleged that the accused, Subhas, a photographer, made a video clipping of the crime and blackmailed her, police said.
An FIR was registered against Subhash and five others, who allegedly helped him in committing the crime, at Jhotwara police station after the woman moved a local court with her complaint.
She alleged that Subhash after promising to marry her, raped her several times, police said on Saturday.

The allegation is being probed, the police said.
