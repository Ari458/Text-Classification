Assam
Jan 08, 2014
Abduction, Crime
Description:
			A minor school girl was allegedly pulled into a car by four young men, but a young woman who was also on board helped her to escape in Lakhimpur town in Assam.

The 14-year old girl was going to school when the four young men and the woman pulled her inside the car and drove off.

After travelling a short distance, the car stopped and when the young men alighted the woman inside asked the girl to escape, which she did.

After she escaped, the men and the woman sped off in the car.

The girl told local people of her ordeal and was escorted to the local police station.
