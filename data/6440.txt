Delhi
Feb 15, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Police has arrested a 24-year-old youth, wanted in a rape case since last year, from near AIIMS hospital here.

Harender Kumar alias Harinder Nirala, a resident of Dabri area, was arrested yesterday after police laid a trap for him based on a tip off, police said today.

"Yesterday, a tip off was received by the Special Cell about him following which a trap was laid near AIIMS Hospital and Harender Kumar was apprehended in afternoon," said Deputy Commissioner of Police (Special Cell) Sanjeev Kumar Yadav.

Harinder carried a reward of Rs 20,000 on his head after evading arrest in a rape case last year.

Source: Press Trust of India
