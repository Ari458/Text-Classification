New Delhi
Jul 05, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman who worked as an actor in a theatre group has alleged that she was raped for the past two years by her male friend on the pretext of marriage. The man has been arrested.

Police said the 28-year-old man used to stay with the woman at a rented house in east Delhi's Jagatpuri area. The woman said that she had gone to Mumbai for a job and had met the man there. They later shifted to Delhi and started staying together at the flat after the man promised to marry her.

She alleged that the man kept on making excuses of not marrying her for the past few months and had even planned to run away. Police said a case of rape has been registered.
