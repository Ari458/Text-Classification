Rajasthan
Apr 27, 2013
Street Harassment
Description:
			Locals today saved a 10-year-old girl from possible assault after they caught and thrashed a man, who was taking the child along with him, police said today 

The incident occurred at Banjara basti in Ramganj area.

The man, identified as Shahadat Khan, stopped the girl who was going to a nearby place and started talking with her. Moments later, he took the girl and started walking.

Some locals, who were watching this, caught the man and thrashed him before informing police.
