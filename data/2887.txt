Rajasthan
Aug 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A teenage girl was allegedly raped by a neighbour who barged into her house while her parents slept in another room in Chilpili village.

A 14-year-old girl was raped by Ramprasad Gurjar (22) after barging into her house.

The victim's parents were sleeping in another room, Gurjar fled the scene after the crime.

The victim was sent for medical examination after her mother filed an FIR with the Jamwaramgarh police station.

Gurjar's father has been arrested for disturbing the peace of the village after he raised a hue and cry that his son was being implicated in the case.
