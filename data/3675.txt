Uttar Pradesh
Dec 11, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in a village in Tambaur.

The incident took place when the minor girl was grazing goats in fields. She was raped by Mathura Prasad (30).

The accused fled from the spot after the incident.

The police was informed when family members came to know about the incident.

The accused was later arrested by the police.
