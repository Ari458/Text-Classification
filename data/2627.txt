Haryana
Jul 06, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 23-year-old married woman was allegedly raped in Morkhi village of Safidon subdivision.

The woman was raped while she was alone in her house.

The accused identified as Sleem had threatened her of dire consequences if she told about the incident to any one, the woman said in her complaint.

The medical examination of the victim also confirmed that she was raped, police added.
