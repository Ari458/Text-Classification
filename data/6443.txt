Haryana
Feb 15, 2015
Rape, Other
Description:
			A 21-year-old girl who was allegedly raped by a youth attempted suicide.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl had gone to collect water from a public hand-pump following which one Ankit came and took her to a nearby place. He then raped the girl at gun-point, Station House Officer Dhananjay Mishra said.

Later, the girl attempted suicide by jumping in Ganga canal at Jansath town in the district, he said.

The victim's father has lodged an FIR against the accused.

A case has been registered against the absconding accused, police said, adding that the girl has been sent for medical examination.
