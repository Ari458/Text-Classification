Haryana
Dec 24, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old woman was allegedly gang-raped by two youths yesterday. 

In her complaint, the victim, resident of Palwal area here, said that the two youths - Ankit Tyagi and Tarun Tyagi - took her to a secluded place and raped her. 

The duo threatened the woman with dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anyone, police said.
