Maharashtra
Jan 21, 2016
Domestic Violence, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			An eight-months pregnant Nalasopara woman died after she allegedly set herself ablaze over dowry harassment.

Sanjeevani Jadhav poured kerosene on herself at her Santosh Bhuvan home in Nalasopara (east). She succumbed to the burns. Sanjeevani was to deliver next month. The couple who had a love marriage in 2014 have a year old daughter.

Sanjeevani's parents alleged that her husband Abhijeet used to torture her for dowry. He sustained around 10 per cent burns while trying to rescue Sanjeevani. The victim's parents have alleged that Abhijeet may have killed his wife and later set her body on fire. Abhijeet has been arrested by the Tuling police. He is being treated for burn injuries.

The suspect has been held under sections 304 (culpable homicide not amounting to murder) and 306 (abetment of suicide) of the IPC.
The body has been send to J J Hospital for autopsy.
