Maharashtra
Jul 22, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Anurag Misale has been booked for rape following complaint of a 19-year-old woman at Katol police station. Misale, a resident of Tukdoji Putla Square, is learnt to have sexually exploited the woman on the pretext of first helping her get admission in a college and later on the promise of job. She was also lured with promise of marriage. The woman approached police after Misale did not keep any of his promises and started ignoring her. The woman even tried to convince Misale to get married but he refused.
