Tamil Nadu
Jan 06, 2015
Murder, Crime
Description:
			A man stabbed his wife to death and seriously injured her paramour at nearby Pallapatti, police said.

They said the 34-year-old man had gone out of his house for some work and returned around midnight to reportedly find his wife with another man.

He stabbed her to death and seriously injured her paramour, who has been hospitalised, police said.

The man fled after the incident and a search was on to nab him, they said.
