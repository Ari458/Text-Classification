VISAKHAPATNAM
Nov 04, 2011
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A junior assistant of Andhra Univeristy employees' union cooperative society on Thursday lodged a complaint against four of its directors on an allegation of sexual harassment and abuse in the name of caste.

The junior assistant, a resident of Madhavadhara, lodged the complaint at the III Town police station, 24 hours after she was suspended.
