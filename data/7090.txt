Telangana
Jun 02, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A lecturer Manjula (25) was stabbed to death while her 60-year-old mother Kalavati sustained serious injuries when two unknown assailants attacked them at their house in Tappachabutra on Monday. According to the police, Manjula was living in Vishveshwarnagar in Karwan along with her four-year-old daughter. Her husband, Yeshwanth, was unemployed and often quarrelled with his wife. Police suspect Yeshwanth to be behind the attack.
