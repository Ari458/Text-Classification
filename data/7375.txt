Tamil Nadu
Jul 14, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 25-year-old man was arrested for sexual assault of a nine-year-old girl here in Gurubalaralli on Monday. 

The accused Saravanakumar, a diploma holder on the lookout for a job, had picked up the fourth standard girl, who was playing outside her house on Friday. Saravanakumar allegedly took the girl to an abandoned temple and assaulted her. The girl’s mother had sighted the bruises on the girl’s body the next day while she was bathing.

The victim was taken to Krishnagiri government hospital. A complaint was lodged with Krishnagiri AWPS. The accused Saravanakumar was arrested and remanded.
