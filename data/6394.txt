Punjab
Feb 09, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Crime, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old school girl was allegedly gang-raped by three youths identified as - Sagar, Mangu and Bansi - at Maujgarh village. They videographed the incident and uploaded it online, police said.

The accused threatened her with dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anyone. However, she disclosed it to her parents after the video was uploaded on the web, they said.
