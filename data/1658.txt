Manonmanium Sundaranar University
May 02, 2012
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A professor at the Manonmanium Sundaranar University in Tirunelveli against whom charges of sexual allegations were levelled, was suspended on Sunday.

The students had alleged that A Chellamani, professor and head, Department of Chemistry was sexually harassing girl students for many years. On a sit-in protest in the varsity campus since Friday, the students alleged that they had lodged a complaint with the registrar on April 19 itself but no action was taken. The students alleged that the registrar was indifferent to the plight of the girls and only attempted to turn away the students.
