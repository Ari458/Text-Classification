Andhra Pradesh
Jan 02, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A youth was arrested for allegedly raping a six-year-old girl in her house.

The incident occurred on December 31 night when the accused, identified only as Valli, was returning home after partying with his friends.

On his way home he saw three children including the victim girl sleeping alone inside their house. Valli then went inside and allegedly raped the girl.

The officer said the girl's parents were away at temple on the night of the incident. The girl confided in her mother who lodged an FIR with police.Valli was apprehended on the description given by the girl.
