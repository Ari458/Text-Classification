Mumbai

Abuse
Description:
			Somnath Sambha Tayde (27) was living with his wife Veena (22) and son Vikas. On Saturday evening, the couple had a fight over a domestic issue. During the quarrel, Somnath flew into rage and set his wife and son on fire. Neighbours broke open the door of Tayde's house and doused the fire by pouring water.
