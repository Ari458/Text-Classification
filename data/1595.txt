Pragati Maidan Metro station
Apr 11, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			Two women in their early twenties were allegedly eve teased and later molested by an inebriated man outside Pragati Maidan Metro station on Saturday evening, police said. The accused, Mohit Kumar, works as a software engineer in a Noida MNC and lives in Dwarka in southwest Delhi. 

The incident took place around 6.30pm on Saturday when the victims were going home after work and were on their way to catch the Metro. When they reached the temple near ITO, a man in his twenties made catcalls at them, police said. When the girls objected, he began to misbehave with them. The women raised an alarm following which passersby dialled 100 to inform police.
