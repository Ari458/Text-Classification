Tamil Nadu
Dec 13, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A man from Odisha was taken into custody for questioning in connection with the murder of his wife and son to a week ago in the city outskirts.

The woman's body was recovered near a poultry farm in Appanaickenpatti in Sulur limits and the body of the boy, aged about eight years, was found after residents complained about bad smell emanating from the area.

The needle of suspicion pointed to Ishantkumar, a daily wage earner, hailing from Odisha.
