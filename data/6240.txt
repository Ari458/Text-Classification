Haryana
Jan 18, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was allegedly sexually abused by her tuition teacher in Ballabhgarh area here, police said today.

According to the complaint filed yesterday, the victim's tuition teacher, identified as Rahul, would sexually abuse her after sending other students back home early after class.

The accused threatened to kill her if she told anyone about the abuse, police said, adding, further investigations into the case are on.
