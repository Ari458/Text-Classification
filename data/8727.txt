Madhya Pradesh
Nov 03, 2016
Domestic Violence, Murder
Description:
			A three-year-old girl was beaten to death by her stepfather for her failure to count numbers in Khudel area on Tuesday night.

Bhuri was beaten to death by her stepfather Mangu Bheel in an agriculture field in Pedami village after she failed to count numbers, Khudel police station in-charge Avinash Singh said.

Once he found that Bhuri had died, he fled the spot, he said adding Bhuri's mother held onto the girl during the night and informed the owner of the agriculture fields on Wednesday morning. The police was immediately informed.

The body was sent for a post-mortem.
