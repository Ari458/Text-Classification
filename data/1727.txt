Dehradun
Jun 18, 2012
Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			An Army officer has been arrested on charges of repeatedly raping a woman officer of the same rank after falsely entering into a marriage in Dehradun.

The woman officer, who is also of the Lieutenant Colonel rank, has alleged in her complaint that the officer entered into a marriage with her but later did not accept her as his wife.

In the FIR she alleged the officer raped her during the past few months in a military hospital.

The woman also alleged that the officer was already married and had not revealed his marital status to her.
