Uttar Pradesh
Jul 07, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped and gang-raped by three youth of the same village in Uttar Pradesh's Bahraich district. 

The girl was gang-raped on July 2. The three accused took her to Bareilly from where the Railway Protection Force men rescued her. They handed her over to her family members. The girl's mother lodged a case of rape against the accused on Saturday. They are absconding. 

According to reports, she went out of the house to the bathroom when Fida Hussain overpowered her at gun-point. He and his accomplices Jamal and Shareef took the girl to an isolated spot where they allegedly raped her. 

On July 3, the accused allegedly took her to Bareilly by train. Next day some RPF men spotted the girl in an unconscious state. The cops interrogated the girl and the accused separately. 

The girl's and the accused the family members of the youth were informed. The girl's family reached Bareilly and brought the girl back on Saturday. 

Hussain managed to escape. The girl's mother lodged an FIR against all three. The police said that a case had been registered. The accused are missing.
