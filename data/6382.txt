West Bengal
Feb 07, 2015
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A woman and her associates today allegedly assaulted a policemen after he made an indecent proposal to her when she came to file a complaint in Malda district.

The woman had gone to Bhaluka police outpost under Harischandrapur police station to lodge a complaint, police said.

However, Bhaluka outpost in-charge Sanat Kumar Biswas allegedly did not attend to her complaint and made an indecent proposal to her.

The woman went away and returned with a group of people, police said, adding that she hit Biswas with a shoe while others too assaulted him.

No arrests have been made in this connection so far.
