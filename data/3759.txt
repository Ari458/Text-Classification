Punjab
Dec 22, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			Police booked a youth on the charge of attempting to outrage the modesty of a minor at Bhagike village in the district.

On the complaint of the 13-year-old victim, police registered a case against Parvinder Singh (23), a neighbour of the complainant, for allegedly entering her house and using force in a bid to outrage her modesty.

When she raised an alarm her father, who was in another room of the house, arrived at the spot, as per the complaint.

The accused managed to escape, a search is on for him.
