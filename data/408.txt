Tamil Nadu
Oct 08, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 51-year-old man was arrested on Thursday for raping a 25-year-old married woman in his neighborhood. According to the police, the victim is residing at Pillaiyarpuram near Sundarapuram on the outskirts of the city. 

Her husband is an auto driver and the couple has a four-year-old son. On Thursday , the victim's husband took his auto and left home. The woman was alone at home. M Selvaraj, a neighbour, entered her house and raped her. When she shouted for help. neighbours rushed to her house.
