Uttar Pradesh
Feb 28, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A girl was raped in a field near her house at a gun point by a youth identified as Arvind on February 24 in a village under Incholi police station, police spokesperson said.

When the victim complained about the ordeal to Arvind's uncle Mohanlal, she was beaten by him, police said.

Yesterday, the victim's family met the SSP Subhash Singh Baghel, after which a case under section 376 (rape) of IPC was lodged against the absconding accused today and efforts are on nab him, they said, adding the girl has been sent for medical examination.
