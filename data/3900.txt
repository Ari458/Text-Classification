Jammu
Jan 14, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A school security guard, have been arrested for alleged rape and attempt to outrage the modesty of  a minor girl.

An 8-year-old girl, who was playing outside her house, was kidnapped by Sansar Chand, the security guard of a private school in Greater Kailash area.

The guard took her to an isolated area and committed rape, the victim after reaching home narrated the incident to her family members, who lodged a complaint with police against the guard.
