Bihar
Jun 22, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 11-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by two men at a village in Bihar's Madhubani district today, police said.

The minor girl was playing outside her house at Jaram village when two youths Mohammad Sadiq and Mohammad Israel lured her to bushes at some distance from the house and raped her.

"Hearing the cries of the girl, villagers rushed to the scene, but by that time the culprits had fled the spot," said Phulparas DSP Kishore Kumar.

The girl has been sent to Madhubani town for medical examination.
