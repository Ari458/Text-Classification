Telangana
Jun 05, 2015
Murder
Description:
			In a shocking incident, a woman allegedly killed her five-month-old daughter by pushing her into a well, unable to bear constant harassment by her husband at Narsimhapuram in Chintakani mandal on Thursday.

According to police sources, P. Rajeshwari and her husband Ravi, a farmer, allegedly had a verbal duel at an open well near their house in the afternoon.

Vexed over her husband's behaviour towards her and their baby, Rajeshwari allegedly threw her baby into the well in a fit of rage.

The local police retrieved the body of the baby from the well later in the day.

The Chintakani police registered a case against the couple and investigation is on.
