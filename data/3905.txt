Tamil Nadu
Jan 15, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A youth has been arrested for allegedly sexually harassing an 18-year-old German girl on a city-bound train.

The incident happened on the West Coast Express on January 11 and the youth has been identified as 22-year-old Chandan Kumar, a native of Bihar.

Kumar, a carpenter by profession and working at a furniture store in suburban Kundrathur, was returning after an official visit to Mangalore.

Soon after the train reached Chennai, the victim lodged a complaint and the perpetrator of the alleged crime was arrested, he was remanded in judicial custody.

The victim was on a visit to the heritage town of Mahabalipuram near Chennai.
