sarojini nagar delhi
Apr 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her three neighbours, including her cousin, after she was kidnapped while on way to school here this morning, police said.
The class X student was kidnapped in a car allegedly by the trio from Ashok Nagar in East Delhi when she was on way to school and taken to Sarojini Nagar in South Delhi. She was raped allegedly by the trio in Sarojini Nagar.
"One of the accused is her cousin," a senior police official said.

Source: Press Trust Of India
