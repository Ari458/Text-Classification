Villupuram
Dec 19, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			An American woman in her early twenties, who was staying in a farmhouse near Auroville, was allegedly molested by a gang of youths near Morattandi in Villupuram district, while she was returning after visiting Puducherry on Saturday evening. 

Police said the victim, who was travelling on her moped, was waylaid at an isolated area about one km from the farmhouse around 5pm. The miscreants, who were reportedly in an inebriated condition, attacked her and dragged her to the nearby bushes where she was reportedly molested.
