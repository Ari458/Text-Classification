Bikaner
Jun 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old girl was allegedly molested by a tantrik in a hotel room where she had gone for seeking a solution to her problems.

In a complaint to police, the girl said the tantrik asked her to meet him in a hotel room for redressal of her problems.

When she went to his hotel room, he molested her, the girl alleged.

The girl approached the police after the incident.
