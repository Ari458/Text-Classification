Andhra Pradesh
Mar 21, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 20 years old man was today arrested for allegedly raping his seven years old niece in Juttada area here.

The girl, a standard third student, was admitted to a hospital.

The incident happened yesterday, police said.

The accused, whose sister is the victim's mother, lives next-door. Yesterday evening he invited the girl to his house by promising her ice cream and raped her, police said.
