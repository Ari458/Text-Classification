Haryana
Mar 21, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour twice in Lakkatpur area here, police said today.

The victim, in her complaint, alleged that when she went to get some vegetables from a neighbour's house, the accused, Pawan, who stayed nearby, forcefully dragged her to his room and raped her, they said.

The accused then fled to his village. After four months, he returned and rented a separate room, few blocks away from the victim's residence. The girl was raped again by Pawan when she bumped into him at the market, police said.

Source: Press Trust of India
