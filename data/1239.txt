Madhya Pradesh
Jan 30, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			The 16 year old girl from Salakhedi village in Khargone district, studying in Class X, was allegedly raped and set ablaze by one Rajendra Yadav. In her dying declaration, the victim said the accused raped her, promising to marry her. Later, he went back on his promise, poured kerosene over her and set her on fire.
