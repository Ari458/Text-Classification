Tamil Nadu
Dec 06, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A six-year-old girl was allegedly sexually abused by a 16-year-old boy at her house at Kottur near Pollachi on Friday night.

Police arrested the accused, who is working in textile unit at Sundarapuri, and a neighbour of the victim. The girl is a first standard student.

The accused was booked under sections 354A (punishment for sexual harassment), 323 (voluntarily causing hurt) of the Indian Penal Code read with section 3(b) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

He was lodged at the Juvenile Home in Coimbatore. The victim was undergoing treatment and counselling.
