Hisar
May 03, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A local Court here today held four persons guilty for gang rape of a minor dalit girl while four others were acquitted.

Pawan, Vikas, Baljeet alias Teetu and Raj Kumar, all residents of village Dabra were held guilty while four others have been acquitted, officials said.

Victim's father Krishan had committed suicide by consuming poison on September 18, 2012.
