Maharashtra
Jul 21, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 24-year-old youth was arrested by the Dindoshi police on Saturday on charges of raping a minor. The accused was known to the 16-year-old victim.

On Saturday, the girl stepped out of her house at Santosh Nagar. But when she did not return for a long time, her family got worried. They started to look for her. Eventually, they found her at the accused's house. She told them that the accused had sexually assaulted her. There was an altercation between her family and the accused. Thereafter, her family approached the Dindoshi police station and lodged a complaint.

The girl was sent for a medical examination. Reports confirmed that she had been sexually assaulted. A case of rape, under section 376 of IPC, was registered. Provisions of 'Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act' were also applied and the accused was taken into custody on Saturday.

The accused works in a security agency. He has been remanded to police custody till July 24.
