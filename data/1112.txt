Gujarat
Jan 10, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			As per the victim's statement, she was taken to the first floor of Ratnamani Market by the accused (Chauhan). He opened an empty shop and after giving her a couple of kites, started harassing her. Feeling uncomfortable, she first asked him to leave her, but when he became persistent she started shouting for help. Hearing her cries, shopkeepers on the ground floor rushed to the scene. Seeing them approach, Chauhan fled the spot," said a Karanj police official.
