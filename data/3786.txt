Andhra Pradesh
Dec 26, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man was arrested for allegedly raping a 14-year-old girl in Machilipatnam town of Krishna district.

The victim was raped by her neighbour M Mahesh on the pretext of marriage in the Godugupet area of the town.

Later, her parents requested him to marry the girl, but he refused, after which they lodged a complaint.
