Jammu
May 09, 2014
Molestation, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			Police also arrested a contractual lecturer of Government Degree College Nowshera for harassing a girl student of his class. 

The survivor who is married reported the matter to her family members. She alleged that Sanjay Kumar was passing obscene remarks and harassing her for the last several weeks. The police registered an FIR against Kumar.
