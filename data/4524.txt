Uttar Pradesh
Apr 07, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl has allegedly been raped by two youth in the Itmad Ud Daulah in Agra.
According to a complaint filed by the victim's relatives, the accused used to frequent the locality and picked up the girl when she had gone to buy vegetables, and later allegedly raped her at the nearby Murshi forest.
The girl was later found in a distraught condition at around 8:30 pm that day, at some distance away from her house.

In the complaint, they also said that the girl was threatened by the accused of dire consequences if she reveled about the incident to anyone.
The parents of the victim have also alleged that when they went to lodge a report at the Itmad Ud Daulah police station, they were turned away. Later they met the Deputy Inspector General of Police (DIG), Vijay Singh Meena, on whose order, a case was registered against the youths, who are absconding.
The police have ordered medical examination of the victim.
