Jaipur
Nov 20, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A young married woman was allegedly raped by her acquaintance and five of his friends after he took her to a city hotel in Jaipur on pretext of giving her a lift to a nearby market.

Montu met the victim yesterday and offered her lift to the market, SHO Shastri Nagar police station, Nathu Lal, said.

However, instead of the market, she was taken to a hotel where she was gangraped by him and his friends, the SHO said.

When the victim returned home, she told her parents about the incident, after which they informed the police station, Lal said.
