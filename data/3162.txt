Rajasthan
Sep 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A jewellery designer has been booked for allegedly raping a 19-year-old girl over a period of eight years in Sundernagar.

A case was lodged against Sultan Singh Jangid after the victim's father filed an FIR.

The accused runs a jewellery designing unit and was hiring employees for his firm, the victim was working for him.
