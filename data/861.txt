Baktawng
Dec 06, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 66-year-old widow (Tawnluii),was allegedly raped before getting killed, at Baktawng village, about 80 kilometres from Aizwal.

Tawnluii was found lying in a pool of blood with her throat slit at her home around 2030 hours last night, police said.
