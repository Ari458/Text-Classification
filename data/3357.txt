Assam
Oct 17, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A school girl was gang raped allegedly by four men in Assam's Dhubri district, two of whom were arrested after being identified by the victim.

The 17-year-old girl was returning home from Rail Path area in South Tukrerchar Part 4 village on October 15 night when two young men accosted her and was joined by two others, according to the FIR filed by the victim.

The FIR, filed at the Golakganj police station said that the four dragged her to a paddy field and gang raped her.

The victim identified two of the attackers as 19-year-old Alokesh Roy and 21-year-old Bhutto Ali from her village, who were arrested.
