Uttar Pradesh
Jan 04, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly raped after being held captive for two days in Maheshganj area nere here, police said today.

The minor girl was held captive by one Surendra Yadav and was raped on December 23 last in Maheshganj area, Additional Superintendent of Police Tariq Mohammad said here today.

Police said the medical confirmation of rape is still awaited, however, an FIR was lodged against Yadav yesterday on the complaint of the family members of the girl.

The family members of the victim alleged that they had approached the local police when the girl had gone missing but they did not register the matter.
