Kamanpalli
Jun 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was raped allegedly by a tractor driver in Andhra Pradesh's Adilabad district.

The incident occurred late last night at Kamanpalli village.

The driver, identified as Rajesh, sexually assaulted the girl and fled from the spot. The child has been admitted to a hospital in Mancherial and her condition is stable.
