Indore
Mar 16, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Anita Singh lodged a dowry harassment complaint against her husband Rakesh Singh and his mother Shefu Bai accusing them of harassing her demanding a dowry amount of Rs 7 lakh. 

The victim is a resident of Bijwan village coming under the jurisdiction of Khudail police station. In her complaint, Anita claimed that she has been harassed by her in-laws demanding Rs 7 lakh dowry.
