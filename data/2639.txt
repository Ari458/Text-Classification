Madhya Pradesh
Mar 21, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old dalit girl was allegedly raped by a history-sheeter at Patharia Jaigan village in the district.
	
The incident occurred on March 19 when the accused, identified as Mahendra Lodhi allegedly, lured the victim, playing near her house under a pretext to a secluded place and sexually assaulted her.
	
The complaint was filed by the victim's parents, following which police apprehended Lodhi who has 18 cases registered against him in various police stations.
