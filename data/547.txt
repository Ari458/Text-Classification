South Delhi
Oct 31, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 31-year-old man identified as Vijay Kumar, a rickshaw puller has been arrested by the Delhi police for allegedly raping a 12-year-old girl on Saturday. The accused was reportedly a family friend of the victim. 

On Friday evening, at around 8.45 pm, the girl went out to the local market to get some food, where she met the accused. He took her to an isolated place and raped her.
