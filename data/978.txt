Udaipur
Dec 21, 2011
Molestation
Description:
			According to police, the woman was walking in the Lal Gate area in Udaipur town on Tuesday evening when Pushpendra Singh, the owner of Massala restaurant, asked her to come inside for some food. "The woman alleged that the accused offered her drinks, which she had. Later, she realized that she was drugged through the drinks. Then the accused allegedly attempted rape. The woman however pushed the man away and fled," an investigating officer from Ghanta Ghar police staton said.
