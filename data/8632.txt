Haryana
Jul 27, 2016
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 22-year-old woman allegedly hanged herself after killing her one-year-old daughter in Pataudi on Monday morning. Her father, however, alleged she was killed by her husband and in-laws for dowry. An FIR has been lodged against the woman's husband and in-laws, following a complaint registered by her father. 

According to police, the woman, identified as Neeru, killed her daughter Sakshi by immersing her in a bucket of water, and then hanged herself from a ceiling fan. Her husband spotted them and informed the cops . Later, police reached the spot and recovered the bodies. 

Neeru, who hailed from Alwar in Rajasthan, got married to Govind, resident of Daultabad Kuni village in Pataudi, around two-and-a-half years ago. Govind works at a private company. 

Later in the day, Neeru's father Mukesh Kumar approached police. In his complaint, he said his daughter was regularly harassed for dowry by Govind and his parents. He further alleged the family had killed Neeru and Sakshi, and later created a sequence which indicates otherwise. 

As per complaint, an FIR has been filed against Govind and his parents, under sections 304B (dowry death), 302 (murder), 498A (dowry harassment), 34 (acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of IPC.

The post mortem report established the cause of death of the woman as strangulation, while that of her daughter as drowning, said Dr Deepak Mathur, forensic expert. 

"We've lodged an FIR against three people. Post mortems have been conducted and we have sent the viscera for examination. A probe is on," said Heera Singh, SHO, Pataudi.
