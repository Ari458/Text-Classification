Tamil Nadu
Mar 27, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A local court sentenced a man to ten years rigorous imprisonment for raping a
19-year-old girl in 2008.
     
The 27-year-old man had an affair with the girl and promised to marry her, but later reneged on his promise.
     
The girl delivered a baby boy but the man refused to marry her following which she lodged a complaint with the Salem all woman police station.
