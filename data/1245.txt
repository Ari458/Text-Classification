Khallikote
Jan 31, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Police said the accused identified as Tulu Behera (23), owner of a fast food centre had allegedly kidnapped a 15-year-old girl from a different caste. He had kept her in his house and sexually exploitated her promising to marry her. The girl, however, escaped and returned home in Khallikote. Though the incident happened 3 years ago, the matter came to light when the girl lodged an FIR recently.
