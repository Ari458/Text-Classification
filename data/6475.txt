Haryana
Feb 20, 2015
Abduction
Description:
			A 40-year-old woman and her infant son have gone missing in the district, police said today.

One Sanjay, resident of Kheri Neharpar village, in his complaint to the police has alleged that his wife and 3-year-old son Kartik went missing and may have been abducted, a police spokesperson said.
