Bengusarai
May 14, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was today sentenced to life imprisonment by a court here for raping a five-year-old girl at Singhaul village in Begusarai district last month.

Begusarai Sessions Judge Parshuram Shukla sentenced Pappu Tanti to life imprisonment and imposed Rs 20,000 as fine on him for raping the minor on April 10.

The accused was produced before the Begusarai court on May 3 and charge sheet against him was submitted the very next day.
