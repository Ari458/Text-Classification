Uttar Pradesh
Dec 19, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor allegedly raped a five-year-old girl at the Khora Colony.

The accused, a 14-year-old boy, was taken into custody and the report of the medical test on the girl was awaited.

The incident happened when the girl, daughter of the owner of the house where the boy was staying with his family, was alone in a room with her mother on the terrace.

The teenager took the advantage and allegedly raped the girl.

Hearing her daughter's scream, the mother rushed to the room and caught the boy red-handed. The boy tried to run away from the spot but was nabbed by the neighbours, because the accused is a minor his identity has not been disclosed.
