Tamil Nadu
Jul 06, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			The City police are investigating a complaint of harassment filed by a 27-year-old domestic help. The complainant said her employers, a government doctor and his wife at Madhavaram milk colony, had been physically assaulting her. She was working for them for the past 17 years. Officials of the Labour and Revenue Departments inspected the doctor's house on Sunday. The complainant was taken to a hospital for a complete medical check-up, sources said.
