West Bengal
Jan 11, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old girl was raped allegedly by the son of her landlord at Sainthia area of Birbhum district.

The girl's family was living as a tenant in the Suryasen Palli house of the accused's father.

When the father of the girl was not at home, he allegedly called the her to a room and raped the minor.

The girl was admitted to Suri hospital.

A protest rally was held at Sainthia.
