Haryana
Jun 22, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Two people were arrested on Saturday for allegedly abducting and gang-raping a 14-year-old Dalit girl in a railway cabin in Haily Mandi railway station near Pataudi on Friday night. The rape survivor was found near the railway cabin in the early hours of Saturday. 

According to police, the girl was attending a marriage in the neigbhourhood when around 8.30pm, the two accused abducted her from the function. The two then took her to the railway station's cabin where they took turns to rape her. They then fled, leaving the girl inside the cabin, police said. 

On realizing that she was missing, the girl's father started searching for her and informed police. It was around 4.30am on Saturday when police found the girl. She was immediately taken to a hospital where a medical examination confirmed rape. 


An FIR has been registered against the accused under sections 363, 366A and 376D of the Indian Penal Code, sections 4/8 of the POCSO Act and the SC/ST Act. The two accused have been identified as 20-year-old Vikki, a resident of Jatauli village and 19-year-old Sanjay, who lives in Haily Mandi. 

"Sanjay is a neighbour of the victim while Vikki, who is an auto driver, is a friend of Sanjay. We will produce both the accused in a city court tomorrow" said Ranbir Yadav, SHO, Pataudi police station.
