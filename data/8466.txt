Rajasthan
Feb 09, 2016
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A BSP leader and a member of zila parishad in Dholpur was arrested on Monday for kidnapping and raping a 15-year-old girl. The accused man was on the run since an FIR was registered against him two months ago.

An FIR had been registered against the accused Karvendra Singh, who is a local BSP leader, on December 2. A man registered an FIR under sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act with Kotwali police station, accusing Singh of kidnapping and raping her daughter. The police recorded the minor girl's statements and then launched a search for the accused.

"We got a tip-off that Singh had come to a place in Dholpur to meet some friends. We rushed a team and arrested him. He will be produced in a court on Tuesday," said a police officer.
