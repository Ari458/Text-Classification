Bihar
Feb 27, 2016
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman was strangled to death by her husband at Mothabadi village under Katoria police station area in the district when her family members failed to meet dowry demand. 

Police said here that Rajesh Yadav used to demand dowry from his in-laws but when his demand was not met, he and his family members thrashed Savita Devi (30) last night.

Later, her husband strangled Savita to death. Rajesh and his family members managed to escape after committing the crime. The body had been sent for post-mortem. A search operation is on to nab culprits.
