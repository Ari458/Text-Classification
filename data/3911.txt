West Bengal
Jan 16, 2014
Molestation
Description:
			A woman was allegedly molested by a man in Hooghly district.

The woman a nurse in the Bandel Thermal Power Station (BTPS) hospital was hanging her wet clothes in front of her house when a man allegedly molested.

A complaint has been lodged with the Mogra police station.

The Hooghly Superintendent of Police Sunil Chowdhury visited the area and said police has started an investigation into the matter.

Patrolling in the area has been beefed up.
