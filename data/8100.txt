Punjab
Dec 01, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The court of Additional District and Sessions Judge, Anshu Shukla, today sentenced a 29-year-old man to 20 years imprisonment for the rape of a four-year-old girl. The court imposed a fine of Rs 2.08 lakh on her.

The court sentenced Shanker Singh, a resident of Uttarakhand, to 20 years in jail. He has been convicted of the charges of rape, kidnapping and various Sections of the POCSO Act.

As per the prosecution case, the FIR was registered on December 8, 2014, when the victim's mother reported to the police that she left her four-year-old daughter playing on the rooftop of their residence in Kishangarh.

When she returned, she found her daughter missing and they began looking for her.
She then saw her daughter on the Saketri road with the accused. Her daughter was crying loudly and had injuries on her genitals.

The police then arrested the accused in a case under Sections 363, 366, 376 of the IPC and Section 6 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.
