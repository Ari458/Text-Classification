Punjab
Oct 01, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A woman, from the north Indian city of Ludhiana, has filed a complaint against her husband alleging that he raped their minor daughter.

The incident came to light when the mother caught the girl crying. On repeated enquiry, she explained the ordeal her father put her through.

He had been doing the same for the previous four months, the child revealed.

She also claimed that she was afraid of her father because he threatened her with dire consequences, if she revealed it to anyone.
