Andhra Pradesh
Dec 21, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The accused had entered the hostel on December 15 midnight. He then forced open the door of one room. Armed with a knife, he was reportedly wearing only his undergarments. Threatening girls, he made them cover their faces with blankets. He proceeded to consume liquor and then molested a girl. When one of the knives carried by him slipped from his grasp, the victim picked it up and stabbed him. Using another knife, the accused injured the girl before fleeing.

A spurned lover, he had been targeting girls in hostels for some time and had committed such breakins in Badvel, Mydukur and Vemula mandals in Kadapa
