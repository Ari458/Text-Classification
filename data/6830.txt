Andhra Pradesh
May 03, 2015
Crime, Rape
Description:
			Two teenagers, including a minor allegedly raped a pregnant woman while another minor boy filmed an obscene video of the victim at a village in Andhra Pradesh's West Godavari district, police said on Sunday.

The incident took place yesterday when the 18-year-old victim was alone at her house in Ramachandrapuram village of the district and the three boys broke into her house, sub-inspector at Pedapadu police station S Vijay Kumar said.

After the incident, the woman lodged a complaint with Pedapadu police station in the evening. Eluru DSP, Saritha told reporters today that a case in this regard has been lodged under relevant sections of Nirbhaya Act and a manhunt has been launched to nab the three boys who are at large.
