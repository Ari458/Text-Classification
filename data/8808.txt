Telangana
Dec 04, 2016
Domestic Violence
Description:
			A woman was attacked by her estranged husband at her house on Saturday evening. 

The victim, Mamta sustained minor injuries, said a police official from the Saroornagar police station. The accused, Ramesh and his wife were having issues from a long time, he added.

On Saturday, Ramesh had come to Mamta's house at Kothapet and attacked her with a pocket knife after an argument, said the official. Ramesh (21), is a B.Com student at Sangareddy and Mamta is a nursing student in the city.
