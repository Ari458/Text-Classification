Karnataka
Apr 23, 2015
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A woman has filed a dowry harassment complaint against her husband and in-laws with the Andersonpet police.

Robertsonpet Circle Inspector of Police, Lakshminarayan, told The Hindu on Wednesday that Lakshmi, a resident of Buchepalli near KGF, married Nagaraj of Hosapalya in Bengaluru around four years ago. Nagaraj works as an accountant with a private company.

In the complaint, Lakshmi alleged that during the wedding, apart from the expenses, her family gave 180 gm of gold. However, she alleged that Nagaraj and his family members continued to pester her for dowry. They demanded a car and Rs. 2 lakh, she alleged.
