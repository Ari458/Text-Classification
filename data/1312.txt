New Delhi
Feb 19, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A seven-year-old resisted a rape attempt by a close relative at a wedding. She was able escape from his grip by biting him and informed her parents about the incident. 

The girl's father told TOI: "My daughter had finished her dinner and had gone to her room adjoining the marriage hall. It was then the accused attacked her. She thought on her feet and bit the accused on the hand and ran from the room."
