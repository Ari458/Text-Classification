Andhra Pradesh
Jun 28, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An engineering student allegedly outraged the modesty of a minor tribal girl at Godavarikhani in Karimnagar district.

The accused, Gandra Amar (22), a B.Tech student tried to allegedly rape a minor girl (11) near a school in Godavarikhani town.

The girl is an anganwadi school student, which is allegedly run by the mother of the accused.

The police registered a case under Section 354 (b) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) against the accused for outraging the modesty of minor girl (attempting to commit rape on the minor girl), after a complaint by the minor girl's parents.
