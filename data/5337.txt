Rajasthan
Aug 21, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Two sisters on Wednesday accused three men of raping them when they were returning home in Rajasthan's Barmer district, police said on Thursday.

On August 17, the girls, aged 18 and 20, in a complaint to Gidan Police Station had alleged the men had tried to rape them last Saturday.

But, on Wednesday they lodged another complaint to police accusing the three men of raping them, SP Hemant Sharma said.

"The victims on Wednesday lodged a complaint alleging they were raped," he said.

Medical examination of the girls was conducted on Wednesday, the SP said.

"It is a matter of investigation why they first gave a complaint of attempt to rape and later alleged the men raped them," he said.
