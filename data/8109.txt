Telangana
Dec 03, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Dundigal police arrested a 27-year-old cabbie, Nagaraj of Suraram Colony, for allegedly raping a 23 year-old tailor at a common friend's house on November 27. The victim lodged a complaint with the police on Tuesday. 

After registering a case, police arrested Nagaraj on Wednesday. He was remanded in judicial custody.
