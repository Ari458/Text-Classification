Tikamgarh
Dec 22, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A man raped a 19-year-old girl when she was working in a field in Tikamgarh district's Sendhri village last evening.

The accused Naresh Yadav threw the girl's body in a secluded place after committing the crime. The girl sustained injuries and was provided first aid, police said, adding that the accused is absconding.
