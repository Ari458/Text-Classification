Uttar Pradesh
Dec 18, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was kidnapped and raped allegedly by a youth in Kursi area here, police said today.

The minor girl was raped by accused Vimlesh when she was going to the fields yesterday evening, Station Officer Brijesh Singh said.

A case has been registered by the victim's father and efforts are on to nab the accused, he said.
