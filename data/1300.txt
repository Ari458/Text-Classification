Jaipur
Feb 17, 2012
Rape
Description:
			Mother of an 18-year old girl registered an FIR alleging that her daughter was abducted and raped in a hotel in Chandpol area on Tuesday. According to the police, the victim is a resident of Samod area near Chomu in Jaipur.

"The mother registered the FIR on Wednesday saying that one Ramanuj, a resident of the same village, forced the girl into a car on Tuesday and took her to a hotel in Chandpol area," said sub-inspector Data Ram. 


"The mother also says that Ramanuj threatened the girl not to tell anyone about the incident. However, she told her mother after returning home," said the officer.
