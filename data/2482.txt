West Bengal
Jun 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The principal of a well-known school on Park street in the city, was arrested for allegedly molesting a class XII student and charged under Section 354A of the IPC.

Fifty-year-old George Bernard Shaw Halder, principal of the English medium school, was arrested for allegedly molesting a Class XII student of his school in his chamber.

A preliminary investigation has revealed that Halder earlier too harassed some girl students, but the victims had kept their mouths shut because of threat by the principal.

This time the victim summoned up courage to reveal her ordeal to her parents who promptly filed a police complaint with the Shakespeare Sarani police station.
