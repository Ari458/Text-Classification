Maharashtra
Feb 28, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The Pimpri police have booked a man from Morwadi in Pimpri for allegedly raping his 11-year-old daughter and threatening to kill her.

The suspect, who works as a labourer, has been accused of raping his daughter since last 20 days. The girl kept mum because she was afraid. The incident came to light when the suspect was caught red-handed by his wife.

The suspect also threatened to kill his wife, but she approached the police station and lodged a complaint. The suspect is untraceable.
