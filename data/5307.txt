Jaipur
Aug 16, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A newly-married woman was stabbed to death allegedly by her husband and his friend in Phagi town.

Body of Manisha, aged around 18, was found near her parent's house at Madhorajpura late last night, SHO (Phagi) Srimohan said.

"Her husband Ajay was interrogated and he confessed to have murdered his wife with the help of his friend Rakesh," the SHO said.

Both of them had been arrested, he said.

Ajay told his interrogators that he disliked his wife and so he murdered her, the SHO said.

The two entered into wedlock in May this year.

The body had been handed over to family members after post mortem, the SHO said.
