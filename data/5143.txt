Assam
Jul 20, 2014
Abduction
Description:
			One persons was arrested today after kidnapping a boy from Nellie in Morigaon district.

According to police official, the child lifter has been identified as Nagen Das (52) and was arrested from Jagiroad.

The kidnapper picked up 8-year old Rafikul Islam from near his house this morning and was on way to Guwahati.

The abductor was brought to Jagiroad police station and is being interrogated currently.
