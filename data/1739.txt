Bisharpara near Kolkata Airport
Jun 24, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			Two miscreants allegedly fired at the house of a molestation victim at Bisharpara near Kolkata airport early on Friday. The victim is the niece of Ashim Dam, the Kolkata Police constable who was beaten to death on March 8, for trying to protect his niece from being molested. 

The girl lodged a complaint at Airport police station. "It was the third attack after the bail petition of the prime accused, Panka Dutta was rejected on Thursday. I woke up after hearing the gun shots and found two locals, who are associates of Dutta Kamal Das and Babu Ghosh escaping," she said.
