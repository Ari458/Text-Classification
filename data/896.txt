Delhi
Dec 10, 2011
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			The victim went missing on December 3. She said she was taken to Mathura by the accused and raped. "We had registered a missing complaint and began investigations. On December 6, we received inputs that she was last seen with a man in Mathura. A team was sent to Mathura and the girl was rescued," said a police officer.
