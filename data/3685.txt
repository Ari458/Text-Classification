Punjab
Dec 13, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A youth has been sentenced to 10 years rigorous imprisonment for abducting and raping a minor girl.

He also imposed a fine of Rs 6,000 on him.

In his complaint, the girl's father had alleged that the accused had abducted his 16-year-old daughter on April 8.

Police managed to rescue the girl after two months.
