Rajasthan
Feb 17, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 25-year old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Harmada area in the district.

The girl was raped on Saturday.

The accused, identified as Kailash Raigar, has been arrested, police said. The victim's mother yesterday lodged an FIR against Raigar.

The girl's family lives in a rented accommodation in Harmada area, investigating officer Mukesh said.

He said the statement of the girl has been recorded and the accused was being interrogated.
