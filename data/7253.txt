New Delhi
Jun 25, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Human Trafficking, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl has alleged that her father is forcing her into prostitution. The minor has lodged a complaint with the help of the family of her father's elder brother. "The girl has claimed that her father used to take her to hotels in Paharganj area and force her to sleep with random people. The minor was taken for medical examination last night which confirmed rape. A case has been registered against her father, mother, sister and brother-in-law," police said. The 43-year-old accused is a tailor in Brahmpuri area.

"She came to our house and revealed that her father uses her for engaging in illegitimate relationships for money and beats her up for disobeying. She told us that these things had happened over the last 3-4 months. They also forced her to quit school after 9th standard,"her cousin said.
