Uttar Pradesh
Dec 26, 2013
Murder
Description:
			Suspecting his wife to be in an illicit relationship, a man allegedly shot her dead at Sarthana.

After the incident, the accused surrendered himself to the police. They also recovered a sharp weapon from him.

According to the police, 39-year-old Sanjay, a resident of Mulheda village, suspected his wife Jogindiri of having an illicit relationship.

Sanjay shot Jogindiri in her chest after a both of them quarreled over her alleged affair.

Meanwhile, family members of the victim informed police that Sanjay killed their daughter in an inebriated condition as he is an alcoholic and she opposed it.
