Andhra Pradesh
Jun 03, 2015
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A five-year-old girl of Pedararmpalle village of V.Kota mandal was allegedly sexually assaulted by a daily wager on Tuesday.

Police said that the girl, whose parents work as labourers in Bengaluru, is under the care of her grandparents. The accused, identified as Vinod (22), took the girl to some isolated place, saying that he would buy her some eatables and forced him on her. The locals rushed to girl a hospital. The accused was taken into custody and a case was registered on charges of rape and abduction, police said.
