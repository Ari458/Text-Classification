Uttar Pradesh
Sep 24, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in a park located right in front of a police station in Vijay Nagar.

25-year-old Amit was arrested after locals nabbed him on hearing the girl's cry for help.

The girl and accused's families lived together in rented accommodation few years ago. When the girl was standing outside her house, Amit took her to Bhimabai park and raped her.

On hearing the girl's cries for help, locals rushed to the spot and nabbed the accused who was trying to flee.

The locals handed him over to police, who then arrested him.
