Rajasthan
Mar 01, 2015
Murder
Description:
			Bodies of two minor girls were today found in a well in Alwar gate area of Ajmer, police said.

One of the deceased has been identified as Sonu (2) while another deceased, a six-month-old baby girl, is yet to be identified, they said.

The reason of the murder is yet to be ascertained, police said.

The deceased belonged to a poor family, they said, adding, the bodies were spotted in a well which is located 50 metres away from their house.

Police said the bodies have been sent to a government hospital for post-mortem.
