Chandigarh
Feb 01, 2012
Crime, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			According to the police, the deceased Monika(22) from Chandigarh got married in December last year. Her father stated that she was being compelled and abused by her in-laws for dowry and finally she was choked to death by them. The police officials have revealed that there were rope marks of around her neck and some bruises on her arm. These marks reveal that some kind of struggle was involved in the incident, police said.
