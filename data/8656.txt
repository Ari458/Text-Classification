Gujarat
Jul 30, 2016
Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A 48-year-old computer engineer of urban development department in Surat Municipal Corporation (SMC) was booked on Thursday for allegedly harassing a senior woman clerk in his office between 2014 and 2015. The complaint was registered following an inquiry by sexual harassment committee of SMC.

Rakesh Rana, an officer in town planning department, was booked following a complaint by Bhamini Mahida, member of the committee formed by SMC to investigate the allegations made by the woman employee. 

The woman had earlier alleged that she was harassed by the accused and some other officers of her department by passing lewd comments and sending obscene messages on her mobile phone.

While SMC had formed a committee to investigate her allegations, the woman had approached high court seeking police action. Following inquiry of the committee the complaint was lodged on Thursday.
