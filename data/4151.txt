Uttar Pradesh
Feb 14, 2014
Rape, Other
Description:
			A case of suicide by a BA student has come to light in the district over her alleged rape by a youth. 

The student in Khatoli town died on January 9 after consuming poison. 

35 days after her death, a suicide note was found in which she has stated that she was raped by a youth from her neighbourhood when she had been alone at home. 

The parents of the girl have registered a complaint and submitted the alleged suicide note and a sample of her handwriting with police, who said that they were looking into the matter.
