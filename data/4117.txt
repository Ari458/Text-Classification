Gujarat
Feb 08, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 60-year-old man was arrested on the charge that he had allegedly raped a 26-year-old married woman for three years after promising her a house and a powerloom unit for her husband.

Ashok Mishra of Biliyanagar, Navagam Dindoli, told the rape survivor that he would transfer his flat in her name and that he would also set up a powerloom unit for her husband if she keeps relation with him. The woman, who lived with her husband in a rented house at Kailash Manasarovar Apartment in Limbayat, in her complaint, stated that Mishra raped her several times between 2010 and 2013.

Police have registered a complaint and would get the accused and the rape survivor medically examined. They will also get their DNA test done together with that of the woman's child to ascertain the latter's parentage.
