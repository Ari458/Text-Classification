Mumbra
Jun 13, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 11-year-old student from Mumbra township here has been booked for allegedly raping a four-year-old girl.

PI Usha Suradkar of Mumbra police station said that the victim and the accused were neighbours in Kausa and the incident took place on Tuesday evening when the boy locked the girl in his house and sexually assaulted her.

The victim later narrated the incident to her mother following which a complaint was registered against him.
