Rohtak
May 03, 2015
Abuse
Description:
			A 26-year-old woman suffered 35% burns and her six-year-old daughter suffered injuries when her husband threw acid on them following an argument at Shivpur Colony of Rohtak on Friday night. The accused identified as Vishvajit was arrested and sent to judicial custody by the court on Saturday. The woman and her daughter were rushed to PGIMS, Rohtak, for treatment.

According to information, Vishvajit belongs to Hardoi district of Uttar Pradesh and was a daily wager. The couple had shifted to their current residence two years ago. According to police, the couple had strained relations over the alleged extramarital affair of Vishvajit.
