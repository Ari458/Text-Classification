Haryana
Jan 05, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A 20-year-old married girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Dumerkha village of the district here, police said today.

The victim was working in the fields yesterday when two youths forcibly took her to another field in the village and then one of them allegedly raped her, they said.

The accused have been identified as Sonu, a resident of Dumerkha village, and Shila of Dhamtan village.

While accused Shila has been nabbed, Sonu still remains at large, police said, adding, further probe in the case is underway.
