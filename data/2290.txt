Hauz Khas
May 26, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A man was today arrested in South Delhi for allegedly raping a 43-year-old woman for the last 19 years on the pretext of marrying her.

The woman filed a complaint at Hauz Khas police station alleging that the accused, Ramesh Pahalwan, had duped her by making false promises of marriage and raped her since 1994.

Source: Press Trust of India
