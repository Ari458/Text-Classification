Karnataka
Apr 12, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			Khatija, 22, a homemaker, hung herself at her residence in Pillaganahalli following alleged dowry harassment by her husband and mother-in-law.

According to the Hulimavu police, Khatija, a native of Tamil Nadu, was married to Afzal, a painter, around two years ago and the couple have a one-year-old daughter.

The incident came to light when her mother-in-law Pyarima went to check on her at around 2.30 pm.

Afzal and Pyarima have been arrested based on the complaint lodged by Khatija's parents that they were allegedly torturing their daughter for dowry.
