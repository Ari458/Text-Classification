Tamil Nadu
Dec 20, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A 23-year-old drunk man was today arrested for allegedly misbehaving with a three-year-old girl here, police said.

The accused, a painter by profession, allegedly under the influence of liquor, lured the girl to the terrace and started biting her cheeks and lips when she was alone in her house, they said.

As the girl raised alarm, neighbours came to her rescue and informed police, who arrested him and registered a case under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, police said.

Source: Press Trust of India
