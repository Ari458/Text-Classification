Assam
Nov 25, 2013
Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A pregnant woman was killed allegedly by her in-laws because of dowry.

Akhitaj Parbim alias Pinky was found dead in Boragaon near Barpeta town.

According to the complaint by Parbim's family, she was married to Shahjahan Ali in Bongaigaon district around one and half years ago.

While her in-laws claimed that she was killed by some unknown people at their home, the woman's family charged the in-laws of killing her with sharp weapons because of dowry.
