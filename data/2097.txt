Rajasthan
Apr 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			An 8-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in Karauli district when she had gone to attend a marriage function, police said today.

The girl was sleeping with her father and other family members where the function took place when the accused, who was staying in the same house, took her to a spot late last night and raped her, they said.
