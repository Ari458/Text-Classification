Madhya Pradesh
May 19, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 44-year-old man was arrested for allegedly kidnapping and attempting to rape a two-and-a-half year old girl, police said today.

The accused, Genda Lal, had kidnapped the little girl two days back after which he tried to sexually assault her, they said.

The matter came to light when the parents of the missing girl filed a report with the Baghsevania police station following which a hunt was launched to trace her.

After a search, she was found lying near a colony with injury marks over her body.

Police rushed her to a government hospital for treatment where her medical examination was conducted which confirmed that she was assaulted and had injury marks on her body including private parts, police said.

Police identified and nabbed the accused last night after examining the footage of a security camera installed in the colony, Deputy Inspector General of Police (DIG) D Sriniwas Varma said.
