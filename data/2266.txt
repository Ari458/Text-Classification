Gumla
May 21, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A woman panchayat ward member was beaten to death and three of her family members were injured when three persons attacked them in Gumla district, the police said today.

The ward member died on the spot while her husband, brother-in-law and four-year-old granddaughter sustained injuries in the attack.

All the injured were admitted to the Sadar Hospital.

The attackers barged into their house at Murkunda Purna Toli in the intervening night of Sunday and Monday and beat them up with sticks.
