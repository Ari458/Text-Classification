Jharkhand
May 10, 2014
Rape
Description:
			Two sisters, who are minor, were allegedly raped by two persons at Jarda panchayat in Koderma district.

Two accused, identified as Raju and Pitu of the same panchayat, went to their house on the pretext of asking water and allegedly raped them last night, the officer-in-charge of Tilaya police station Harendra Tiwari told reporters here.

Quoting the girls? complaint registered with the police station, the police officer said they were aged between 15 and 16 years.

While the girls have been admitted to a hospital for medical tests, the accused were detained, Tiwari added.
