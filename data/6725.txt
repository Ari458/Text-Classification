Delhi
Apr 03, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 42-year-old man was today arrested for attempting to rape his neighbour's one-and-a half-year-old daughter in south west Delhi's Sagarpur area.

According to police, the incident took place in the afternoon today when one Ram Swaroop went to the child's house and after taking her parents' permission, he took the girl to his house for allegedly playing with her.

The incident came to light when Swaroop was trying to rape the child and she started crying. Her mother rushed to the man's house after hearing her cries and saved.

Police said the woman immediately took the child to her house and made a call to the police control room and a team of officials reached the spot.

Raids were conducted to nab Swaroop and after few hours of search operation, he was nabbed by the police.

Swaroop is a vegetable vendor in the area. According to the police, he confessed his involvement in the crime and has been booked under the relevant sections of the POCSO Act. A case has been registered at Sagarpur police station.
