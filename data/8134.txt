Kerala
Dec 08, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 21-year-old man was arrested in Parassala on Monday for alleged sexual abuse of a minor.

The Parassala police said the accused has been identified as Tony, from Erichaloor in Parassala. He is accused of having sexually exploited a school girl several times in the past three months. This had come to light when the school authorities questioned her regular absence.

It is believed that the accused had befriended the victim at the bus stop after classes. He has been charged under Sections 3 and 4 of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, an official said.
