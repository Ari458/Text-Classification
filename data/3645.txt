Odisha
Dec 05, 2013
Murder
Description:
			A woman was found dead from a half-constructed house in Sector 8 of Abhinav Bidanasi under Markat Nagar police station.

A man with his tongue severed was also found lying unconscious nearby.

The body of the woman bore multiple injuries and it lying in a pool of blood.

The man was sent to hospital, the identity of the man and the woman was not yet known.

Help of forensic experts were being taken in the investigation.
