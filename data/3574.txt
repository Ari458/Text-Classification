Uttar Pradesh
Nov 24, 2013
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Crime
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped by four youths at gun point in Puthi village in the district following which they took her to a jungle and gang raped her. She was found in an unconscious state by her family members.

Two of the four accused have been identified as Vicky and Dinesh.

Source: Press Trust Of India
