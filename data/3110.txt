Rajasthan
Sep 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A married woman, working as manual labourer, was allegedly gang-raped by four people in Banipark area.

The 30-year-old woman, a mother of five, was raped by the four men.

The woman alleged in her complaint to police that a person known to her called on phone claiming that her husband had met with an accident.

The man, later, on pretext of taking her to the spot of the accident, took her to a deserted spot on Benar Road where three others also joined him in the alleged sexual assault.
