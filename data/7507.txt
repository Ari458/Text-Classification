Punjab
Jul 17, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Haibowal police have arrested a 24-year-old youth of Pratap Singh Wala village for allegedly raping a girl for one year on the pretext of marriage.

The accused and victim lived in the same area and both have good family relations since their mothers were friends since long.

The survivor, 26, in her complaint, alleged that on January 2014, the accused called her at his place and made physical relations with her on the pretext of marriage. However, he refused to marry her later.

A case under section 376 (rape) of IPC has been registered.

Sub-inspector Varinder Kumar, investigating officer, said ACP Atam Nagar had marked an inquiry in the matter. Further he said the survivor has also alleged that the youth has captured his pictures in compromising condition in his phone. However, no such pictures were found during the investigation.

The police arrested the accused on Thursday.
