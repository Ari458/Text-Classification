New Delhi
Jan 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 28-year-old married woman was raped by her friend in a moving car.

The woman was found weeping sitting along the road near the Anand Vihar Bus Terminal in east Delhi.

She was taken to a police station where she said that she was raped by her friend in a car, in front of his two friends. Police took her to the hospital where a medical examination confirmed rape.

The woman also told police that the three beat her up when she resisted.

She had met her friend in the evening, and he allured her on the pretext of getting her a job in a factory.
