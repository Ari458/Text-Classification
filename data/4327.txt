Maharashtra
Mar 06, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A thirty-year-old man was arrested for raping a minor girl at village Girjapur in Akola district.

The incident took place in the afternoon.

The girl, who is 12 years old, works at a brick kiln in the village. Accused Mangesh Palaspagar, who lives in the neighboring village, raped her, she told her father.

Following which police arrested Palaspagar.
