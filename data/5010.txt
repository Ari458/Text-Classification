Gujarat
Jun 27, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 32-year-old married woman has registered case against six persons for allegedly raping her Chamardi village of Surendranagar district on June 22. The woman approached Pansina police station on Wednesday night.

The rape survivor has named Gopal Koli, Vasram Koli, Shamji Koli, Dhaval Koli, Dharmendra Solanki and Ghanshyam Koli and alleged that they forcibly took her to a farm near the village and took turns to rape her.

The six persons threatened her with dire consequences if she revealed anything to her family members or police. However, she gathered courage and told her husband about the crime. The woman's family members are farm labourers in the village.
