Uttar Pradesh
Jul 03, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by two youths at Rasoolpur village in Shamli district.

The girl was on her way to the market when the two accused Kurban and Waseem forcibly caught hold of her and raped her in nearby fields yesterday, according to the FIR registered by the girl's father.
