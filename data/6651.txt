Uttar Pradesh
Mar 19, 2015
Molestation, Crime
Description:
			A 27-year-old woman belonging to Palpur in Amethi district was allegedly poisoned by a youth of the same village today when the latter failed to molest the woman, police said.

17-year-old Afzal allegedly tried to molest the woman and when he failed to do so, he poisoned her, SHO Jagdishpur Shiv Muni Yadav said.

The woman was admitted to Community Health Centre Jagdishpur, but after her serious condition doctors referred her to Trauma Centre in Lucknow, police added.

Police is looking seriously into the matter, they said.
