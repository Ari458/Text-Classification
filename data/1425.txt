Jamnagar
Mar 06, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A 17 year old girl has accused three people of raping and looting her at knife point in Gulabnagar area of Jamnagar.

She alleged that while she was riding pillion on a motorcycle with two friends Kiran Vaghela and Dhaval Mochi, three unidentified assailants accosted them. "Vaghela and Mochi fled the spot after the accused beat them up. Then the trio took turns raping the girl. Before leaving her, the accused robbed her of Rs 800 cash and her cellphone worth Rs 9,000. They also demanded Rs 20,000 from her," said an investigating official.
