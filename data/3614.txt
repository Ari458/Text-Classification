Andhra Pradesh
Nov 29, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Molestation
Description:
			A local court sentenced a middle-aged man to seven years' rigorous imprisonment on the charge of attempting to rape his 12-year-old daughter.

According to the police, the accused, a cook by profession, took his daughter to a lodge in Narayanguda on November 21, 2012 and tried to rape her while in an inebriated condition.
