Bhojipura
May 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old girl was raped by Pappu and his domestic help Prem Shanker yesterday while she was grazing cattle along with her mother in Bhojipura area.

Shanker was nabbed by local residents when the girl's mother raised an alarm and was thrashed before being handed over to the police. Pappu is absconding.
