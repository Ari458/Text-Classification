Madhya Pradesh
Mar 20, 2013
Rape
Description:
			In a shocking incident, two minor daughters of a farmer were raped in front of family members by four people at Gadhai village in Madhya Pradesh.
	
The incident occurred when Jitendra Rawat,
Mahipal Rawat, Rajesh Rawat and Kuldeep Rawat of Adhak village thrashed the farmer at his residence and held his family members hostage at knife-point.
	
According to police, Jitendra and the farmer were engaged in a land dispute. The accused dragged the minor sisters to some distance and took turns to rape them.
	
The sisters were admitted in the district hospital, police said.
