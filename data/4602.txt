Jammu and Kashmir
Apr 23, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape, Sexual Harassment at Work
Description:
			A government teacher was today arrested for allegedly raping a teenage student in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

The family of the girl had lodged a complaint with the police that she was raped by her school teacher, Vilayat Ullah, in Dera Darsi area of Poonch.

After registration of the case, a manhunt was launched and the teacher was arrested today, they said, adding the medical examination of the girl had been conducted and the report was awaited.

The teacher has been suspended by the Chief Education officer (CEO), Poonch.

People of the village had held a protest over the alleged incident.
