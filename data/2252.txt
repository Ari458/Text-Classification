Usmanpur
May 20, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly raped by three persons, including two minors, after being given sedatives.

The incident took place in New Usmanpur on May 17.

One of the accused, Ajay, has been arrested in this connection, while his younger brother, a minor, was detained. The third is on the run.

Two of them are the victim's neighbours.

The girl was found in an unconscious condition in a street and after she regained consciousness, she narrated the incident to her family.
