Rajasthan
Jun 19, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A 45-year-old woman was today found murdered in Viratnagar, a sub-urban town.

The body of Koilee Kumawat was found in a half-naked state and the killers had hammered her head.

She was a widow and her only son used to live in Mumbai.

The body was sent for postmortem and forensic experts were called to investigate, they said, adding that the reason of the murder was yet to be ascertained.

The incident took place in Saraimohalla area of the town.
