mapusa town
Apr 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man in Mapusa town, 8 kms from here, police said.
According to police, the accused Mahendra Kauthankar, who works as a bus conductor on private buses, took the victim
to a nearby hill at Cansabhat in Thivim locality yesterday and sexually assaulted her.
The complaint was lodged by the victim's sister in this regard, police said.
