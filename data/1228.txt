Chennai
Jan 29, 2012
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			Gayatri (22) married one Sonai Yadav alias Seenu Ganesh on September 7, 2009. At the time of marriage, they had given 75 sovereigns of gold, five kg of silver articles and Rs 10 lakh cash. 

Gayatri moved to her husband's house in Anna Nagar,Chennai but soon her husband and in-laws began demanding more dowry, particularly a car costing Rs 15 lakh. They even scissored her five-ft-long hair, her parents said.
