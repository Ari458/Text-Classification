Andhra Pradesh
Dec 19, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was allegedly raped by three minor boys and a man in the old city area.

A complaint was received on December 14 about a missing girl and she turned up along with her parents recently to complain about the rape.

As per the girl's statement, a minor boy, known to her, called her to Chandrayanagutta locality from where she was taken to another place by him and his friend. The two allegedly outraged her modesty.

They dropped her at Chandrayanagutta junction, where two others, including another minor boy, raped her.
