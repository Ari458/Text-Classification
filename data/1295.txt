India
Feb 13, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			Gopal Adhikari, a resident of Salaidanga village in West Bengal's Malda district, had been critically injured after the youth attacked his house Wednesday and hit him and his wife Kalyani with rods and sticks.
The youths had been regularly sending MMSs to Adhikari's college-going daughter's mobile, police said. After getting to know the identity of the youths, he made a complaint to the Gajol police station which led to the youths attacking his house.
