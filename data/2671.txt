Patiala
Mar 30, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a cook, working with the National Institute of Sports.
	
45-year-old Ram Verma raped the victim, a relative, at his official residence outside the NIS campus.
	
Few days ago, the girl's family had gone to their village leaving her under Verma's care. Verma reached home drunk and allegedly raped the minor girl.
	 
The girl approached the neighbours, who
took her to senior NIS officials.
	
NIS authorities lodged a complaint with police and Verma was arrested.
