Delhi
Mar 06, 2012
Rape
Description:
			A man identified as Dal Chand has been arrested for allegedly raping his 17 year old step-daughter over the last three years at their Pul Prahladpur residence.

During investigations, it turned out that the accused had married the mother of the victim some years back after he got separated from his first wife. “On many occasions, he forcibly confined her in a room where he used to rape her. He threatened to kill her if she disclosed her ordeal to anyone,” a senior police official said.
