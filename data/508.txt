Chandigarh
Oct 23, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl studying in Government Model School was allegedly abducted and molested in a moving car after the accused administered her some intoxicants.

She claimed that a youngster named Tarun was the culprit. She added the incident took place outside her school when she met Tarun, whom she knew. She alleged that the youth asked her to accompany him. When she refused, Tarun dragged her inside the car and forced a laced drink into her mouth, the girl said.

Sources said the girl told police she did not remember how she ended up in the park, where some passersby noticed her. The girl was found heavily intoxicated and was taken to GMH-16 in a police car.
