Andhra Pradesh
Jun 05, 2014
Domestic Violence, Murder, Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A 24-year-old housewife was allegedly burnt to death by her husband and in-laws at RC Puram on Wednesday.
The victim, A Swati, was the wife of a private employee Naveen Kumar from Marx Society in RC Puram. The couple got married in 2011 and, at the time of the wedding, Swati's parents gave Rs 7 lakh cash, 5 tola gold and other articles as dowry. For the past few months, Naveen and his family members began harassing Swati to get Rs 5 lakh more dowry.

On Tuesday night, Naveen along with his parents allegedly set ablaze Swati using kerosene. Neighbours rushed her to Gandhi Hospital, where she died while undergoing treatment on Wednesday. Based on the complaint lodged by the parents of Swati, police registered a dowry death case against Naveen and five of his family members under section 304-B of the IPC.
