Uttar Pradesh
Mar 12, 2014
Abduction
Description:
			A girl was allegedly kidnapped by some youths in a car near Aurangabad in Sadar Bazar police station area while she was coming home after attending a coaching class.

The girl, daughter of a junior engineer in Electricity Department, was abducted by the youths when she was on her way home from Awa coaching centre.

The car used by the kidnappers was later found abandoned on the outskirts of Jagdishpura village in Mahaban police station area of the district.


Source: Press Trust of India
