Haryana
Dec 25, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A Class 9 student was allegedly gang raped by three youths at Mohana village in Sonepat district.

According to her relatives, the youths had lured her to at a nearby deserted cattle shed and gang raped her. When the relatives reached the shed, the youths managed to flee.

The doctor who conducted the victim's medical test did not rule out gang rape but said a confirmation is only possible after the reports are available.

Source: Press Trust of India
