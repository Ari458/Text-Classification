Odisha
Jan 20, 2015
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A three-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a man at Rambha in Ganjam district, police said today.

The victim disclosed the matter to her mother after two days of the incident on January 10 and later an FIR was lodged, police officials said.

On the basis of the complaint, Dhirendra Nahak(27) was arrested yesterday.
