New Delhi
Nov 24, 2013
Murder
Description:
			With the arrest of a 24-year-old youth, police claimed to have cracked the murder case involving the recovery of a headless and decomposed body of a woman from Karawal Nagar area of North East Delhi.

According to police, Rahul had killed his wife Khusbu (22) to marry another woman.

The accused was nabbed from his residence in Ghaziabad's Loni area after the decomposed body was found from a rented accommodation in Karawal Nagar.

The accused killed his wife on November 19 to marry an another girl Pinki (20) at his native village.

He works with a private company in Gurgaon.

Rahul had married Khusbu in April against the wishes of his family members. He agreed to marry another woman when pressurised by his family, but he did not tell Khusbu about it.

The incident came to light after foul smell starting emanating of the rented house and neighbours called police.


Source: Press Trust Of India
