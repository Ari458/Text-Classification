Uttarkhand
Jul 02, 2015
Molestation, Street Harassment
Description:
			A man was arrested for allegedly stalking and trying to molest a 20-year-girl in full public view in Chakrata area on Thursday. Timely intervention by the Kotwali police averted any untoward incident. 

The accused identified as Firoz Khan, alias Sonu, hails from Muzaffarnagar district of UP and was currently residing in Machchili Bazaar area of the city. Both the victim and the accused belong to different religions. 

Giving details, circle officer Kotwali Manoj Kumar Katyal told TOI, "Sonu approached the girl on several occasions but she repeatedly turned down his advances." 

Sonu allegedly stalked the victim and tried to molest her at Chakrata road, said the official adding, "The girl shouted for help following which passersby came to her rescue." 

Local residents thrashed Sonu and later handed him over to the Kotwali police. 

Meanwhile, acting on the victim's complaint, a case has been registered against Sonu under section 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with the intent to outrage her modesty), 504 (intentional insult) and 506 (criminal intimidation), IPC. 

Katyal said Sonu will be produced in the court on Friday.
