Goa
Feb 24, 2014
Rape
Description:
			The Maina-Curtorim police arrested Aiyaz Shaikh, a resident of Velim, on charges of raping a 23-year-old woman at Betul. The crime took place in September 2013. Acting on a complaint lodged by the victim, the police booked an offence against the accused and arrested him under Section 376 ( rape) of the Indian Penal Code.
