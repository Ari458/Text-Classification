Uttar Pradesh
Jan 25, 2015
Murder
Description:
			A 16-year-old girl was today allegedly hacked to death by a man in Asiwan area here, police said.

The girl, identified as Puja, was hacked to death with a sharp-edged weapon allegedly by Chattrapal in Gauriya Kalan village this morning, they said.

A case has been lodged against the accused on the basis of a complaint by the deceased's father, police said.
