Punjab
Mar 24, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old girl was allegedly kidnapped and raped by three youths at Badhni Kalan village in this district.

A case has been registered against the youths on charges of kidnapping the student of a government school.

The three accused, including Sukhjiwan Singh and Jagseer Singh, who had come in a car, forcibly took the girl to a secluded place where she was raped on Saturday, police said.

After committing the crime, the accused, all in their early 20s, escaped leaving behind the victim, police said.

On reaching home, she narrated her ordeal to her parents who reported the matter to the Police.

The statement of the girl has been recorded and three youths have been booked.

No arrest has been made, police said, adding that raids were being conducted to arrest the youths.
