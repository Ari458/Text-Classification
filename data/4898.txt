Chhattisgarh
Jun 11, 2014
Rape
Description:
			A 19-year-old woman was raped by a village kotwar and his associate in Chhattisgarh's Bemetara district on Monday. 

The recently married woman was living in Pirha village to earn a livelihood with her husband. 

They were working as labourers in a brick kiln there, police said. The incident happened on the intervening night of Sunday when the accused in inebriated condition started shouting in front of her house. 

As per the victim's statement to police, her husband had a heated argument with the accused after which he was asked to leave the place and return to his native village. 

Her husband went to look for the contractor. While the woman was alone at home, the kotwar Chandrika Chouhan (38) and his accomplice Ramswaroop (33) forced themselves inside and raped her.

The accused were booked under IPC's Sections- 376(d) (committing gang rape), 450 (house trespass to commit an offence), 506(b) (criminal intimidation) and 34 (common intention to commit an offence), police said. The duo is on the run.
