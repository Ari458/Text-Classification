Uttarakhand
Oct 20, 2013
Crime, Foeticide/Infanticide
Description:
			A man allegedly killed his newborn girl apparently upset over not having a boy in Laksar area of Haridwar district, with police initiating a probe into the matter.

After reports about the incident on October 17, a team of officials has been sent to Habibpur Kundi village to investigate the case and establish its authenticity.

The man expected his third baby to be a boy, but upset over it being a baby girl he reportedly smacked the newborn to death at this house on October 17.
