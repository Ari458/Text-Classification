Uttar Pradesh
Feb 18, 2014
Abuse
Description:
			A one-and-a-half-year-old child died after being hit by his uncle during a fight in Ramkot area.

The boy was struck by his maternal uncle Haripal while the latter was having a fight with his brother Tarachand in Beehat village last evening.

The boy was seriously injured in the incident and died later.
