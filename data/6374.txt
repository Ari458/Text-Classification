Uttar Pradesh
Feb 06, 2015
Rape
Description:
			A local court has sentenced two people to ten years rigrous imprisonment in a 2012 gang rape case.

Additional sessions judge, Rajni Singh yesterday sentenced Sant Ram and Anuj to ten years imprisonment for gang-raping a girl in Naurozpur Gurjar village.

The victim's family had registered an FIR on June 12, 2012 against three persons but one of them was acquitted due to lack of evidence, government counsel Chashmvir said.

A fine of Rs 15,000 each has been imposed on both of them, he added.
