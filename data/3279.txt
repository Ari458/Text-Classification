Maharashtra
Oct 07, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A minor girl was raped allegedly by her neighbour in a village in the adjoining Buldhana district of Maharashtra.

The incident took place in Warwat Bakal. The accused, Sanghpal Ingle, has been arrested.

The nine-year-old victim's mother sent her daughter to Ingle's house to get Rs 100 from him. Once the girl entered his house, Ingle locked it from inside and sexually assaulted the minor, he also threatened and asked her not to tell anyone about the incident.

Once back home, the girl narrated the incident to her mother, who lodged a police complaint.
