Mettupalayam
Jan 30, 2012
Abuse
Description:
			Jaipunisha (23) of Mettupalayam, said that she was married to A. Kuthupdeen (35) of Salem for seven years. Her parents had given 12 sovereign gold jewellery and Rs. 40,000 as dowry during the marriage. But said that her husband, father-in-law and mother-in-law had taken the jewellery from her and sent her out of house.
