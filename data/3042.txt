New Delhi
Sep 02, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old minor girl was sexually assaulted by her teenaged neighbour in South Delhi's R K Puram area.

According to the police, the 16-year-old has been apprehended and sent to juvenile home after being produced before the Juvenile Justice Board.

The victim and the juvenile offender live in immediate neighbourhood in Janta Flats of R K Puram area. The incident took place inside the toilet of the victim's house.When the girl started crying, he fled from the spot.

The girl, who was bleeding, was taken to Safdurjung hospital by her family.

A minor surgery was performed on the victim to repair the injury and the girl is stable and out of danger but in shock. She was moved to a hospital ward.

Source: Press Trust Of India
