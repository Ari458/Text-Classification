Bihar
Jan 08, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A minor girl lodged an FIR against a fellow villager at Benipatti police station in Bihar's Madhubani district charging him with raping her for months by promising to marry her.

The 14-year-old girl, a native of Sarso village, in the FIR charged the man with raping her for over six months by making a promise to marry her.

The victim has been sent to a government hospital for medical examination.
