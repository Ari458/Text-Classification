Uttar Pradesh
Mar 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 14-year-old Dalit girl was allegedly raped by a Class X student in Bhavanpur.
	
The Class VIII student on was taken to nearby
forest area by her neighbour, where she was raped by him.
	
The accused also threatened her of dire consequences if she revealed the incident to anyone.
	
The girl, however, narrated the incident to her parents the next day, following which they filed a case at the Bhavanpur police station.
	
A medical test conducted on the girl has confirmed rape.
