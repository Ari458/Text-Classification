Tamil Nadu
Jul 29, 2015
Rape
Description:
			Four persons were arrested in connection with a sexual harassment case here on Tuesday.

The arrested were M. Prasanna (30) of Amarapoondi, his friend Manickam (40), S, Jegannathan (25) of Kalikanaickenpatti, and P. Krishnan (38) of Palani.

The police said that a 24-year-old woman from Kerala was admitted to Palani Government Hospital for treatment as she was allegedly sexually assaulted by a gang on Monday.

Acting on a complaint, the team arrested and produced them before the Mahila Court. They were remanded to 15 days judicial custody.
