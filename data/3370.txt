Andhra Pradesh
Oct 19, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			A client lodged a complaint against her Advocate Kaja Moinuddin accused him of raping her.

It was six months ago the 23-year-old victim approached Kaja, to help her secure divorce from her husband Srinivas, who is a resident of VAMBAY colony, Domalguda. But she accused that the Advocate allegedly raped her during the one of her visits to him. She lodged a complaint with Langar Houz police.
