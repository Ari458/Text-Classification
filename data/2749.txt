Odisha
Jul 17, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 58-year-old man was today taken into custody on charge of physically abusing his 15-year-old grand-daughter in Odisha's Jagatsinghpur district.

The accused Dukhishyam Mallick from Tantia village was arrested by Ersama police after victim's mother lodged an FIR alleging sexual assault.
