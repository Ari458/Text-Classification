Kashmir
May 19, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A four-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her neighbour in Pampore area of Pulwama district of Kashmir.

The 22-year-old accused identified as Gowhar Ahmad Dar, sexually assaulted the minor yesterday.

"The child is critical and has been admitted to a hospital in Srinagar for treatment," police said.
