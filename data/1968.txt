Utter Pradesh
Apr 22, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A 10-year-old minor girl was allegedly raped by a youth at Jasala village in the Shamli district, police said today.

Accused, Baljora Kashyap, allegedly abducted the victim who was playing near her house and assaulted her last evening, police said.

The victim has been admitted to a hospital.

Angry villagers protested against the incident and demanded immediate arrest of the accused.
