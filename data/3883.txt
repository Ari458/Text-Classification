Madhya Pradesh
Jan 11, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A youth allegedly beheaded his 62-year-old aunt whom he suspected was practising witchcraft against his family.

The accused murdered her and appeared before the police with the victim's head.

Harishanker Singh Gond (35) of Pand village suspected his aunt Meeravati Gond had been harassing his family since long through witchcraft.

Disturbed over the issue, Harishankar took her to a secluded place and beheaded her with a sharp weapon.

He surrendered before the Majhauli police and admitted his crime.

The victim's torso was later recovered and sent for post-mortem.
