Jammu
Jan 03, 2014
Rape
Description:
			An FIR has been registered against a Sarpanch for allegedly raping a married woman in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

The FIR was registered on the complaint of the woman of Qasba village.

The woman claimed that she had left her house after a quarrel with her husband 10 days ago, following which she was taken to meet the Sarpanch by two people to complain against him.

She claimed that the Sarpanch raped her.
