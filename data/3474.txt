Andhra Pradesh
Nov 05, 2013
Crime, Rape
Description:
			An auto-rickshaw driver was arrested for allegedly raping an eight-year-old girl in L B Nagar area of the city.

Accused Jawed took the minor girl, studying in class II, and her brother to an open land on the pretext of buying firecrackers for them.

The accused then gave the victim's brother a Rs 100 note asking him to get change and in the meanwhile took the girl in the nearby bushes and allegedly raped her.
