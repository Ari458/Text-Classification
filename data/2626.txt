Rape
Description:
			A case was filed against an assistant sub-inspector of Haryana police for allegedly raping a 40-year-old woman for last seven years on the pretext of marriage.

The woman, a divorcee and a resident of Raghu colony area, also alleged that ASI Balraj had duped her a sum of Rs four lakh on the pretext of purchasing a plot in her name.

The police have started the investigation against the ASI and further action will be taken after the completion of the same.

The woman said that seven years back she got divorce from her husband due to personal feuds and after that she was staying with her daughter alone.

Later, the accused, a friend of her husband, used to frequently visit her residence and raped her on several occasions in the last seven years.
