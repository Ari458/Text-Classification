Rajasthan
Dec 07, 2013
Child Sexual Abuse
Description:
			A-53-year-old teacher of a government middle school was arrested for the alleged sexual harassment of a tribal minor girl.

The parents of the 16-year-old girl, a class VI student of the institute, lodged a case against Abdul Hammid Siddique at Nayapura police station.

According to the complaint, Siddique on the pretext of having a discussion about her studies, called the teenager at an secluded location in the school building this afternoon.

Following which, the teacher launched a sexual assault on the girl.

The girl reached home and informed her parents about the incident.

The accused would be produced before the magistrate.
