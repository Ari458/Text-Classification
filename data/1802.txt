Kolkata
Oct 28, 2012
Street Harassment
Description:
			Two youth were arrested for allegedly eve-teasing a housewife in central Kolkata on Friday late evening. The 27-year-old woman was returning home from a hospital at around 9 pm. The duo, Rabi Das (21) and his associate Babai Hazra (22), stopped her at Amherst Street-Keshab Chandra Sen Street crossing and behaved indecently with her, according to preliminary investigations.

After managing to escape from the spot, the woman narrated the incident to her husband.
