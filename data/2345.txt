Ahmedabad
Jun 05, 2013
Rape
Description:
			The girl was asleep on the terrace of her house on Sunday night when Manish Parmar (25), the accused, forcibly took her to his terrace and raped her. Parmar had threatened her with dire consequences if she disclosed the incident to anyone.Her elder sister approached the police yesterday.

"Parmar, was arrested late last night. He has confessed to his crime and has been sent to judicial custody," Odhav Police Inspector V D Brahmbhatt said.
