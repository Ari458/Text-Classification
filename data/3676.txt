West Bengal
Dec 12, 2013
Rape
Description:
			A housewife was allegedly gang raped and left tied to a tree trunk when she went to ask for the money lent to the accused at Falakata.

An FIR has been filed against Rabi Sarkar and five others of the same family for raping the woman at Kathalbari.

She has been admitted to a local hospital and the accused have been absconding.

Locals informed the police after seeing the woman tied to the tree and the police rescued her in the evening.
