Uttar Pradesh
Aug 13, 2014
Abduction, Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 15-year-old girl was allegedly gang-raped by two youths in Bhari village of Shamli district.

The girl was returning from a shop when the two youth identified as Javed and Mukaram kidnapped her and took her to a vacant house where they allegedly raped her yesterday.

The girl's brother lodged a complaint against the two following which a case was registered.
