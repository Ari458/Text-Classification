WB
Jan 30, 2014
Murder
Description:
			A tribal woman was allegedly burnt to death after she was accused of practising witchcraft at Jambani in West Midnapore district.

The hut of 65-year-old Srimati Sabar was burnt last night when she was asleep at her hut at Barsol village.

Bimala Sabar and her husband Subol Sabar were arrested on the basis of a complaint lodged by Srimati Sabar's son.

Bimala and Subol had complained the elderly woman of having done black magic which was the cause of their grandson's sickness.
