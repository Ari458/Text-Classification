Punjab
May 28, 2013
Abuse
Description:
			Punjab Education Minister Sikander Singh Maluka today suspended a government school teacher after parents alleged that he had harassed girl students.

Maluka had earlier directed the Circle Education Officer, Nabha to enquire into the complaints and after submission of the inquiry report, ordered the suspension of Mahesh Kumar, who taught in a government school in Mahinderganj at Rajpura.
