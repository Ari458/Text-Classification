Mumbai
Oct 12, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The Mulund police arrested Hanif Shaikh (38) for molesting his 15-year-old daughter at their Mulund Colony home on Monday night. The accused , who is a skilled labourer, was caught red-handed while misbehaving with his elder daughter and was arrested the next day.

The victims mother told the police that her husband had threatened her to keep quiet while he misbehaved with his daughter. On Monday morning the victimas mother lodged a complaint with the police.
