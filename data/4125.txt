Uttar Pradesh
Feb 10, 2014
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A 13-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a youth in Salempur area, police said here today.

The victim was raped by Manoj (22) when she had gone out to graze her cattle yesterday, Superintendent of Police S Channapa said.

A case has been lodged in this regard, he said.
