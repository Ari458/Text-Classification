Tamil Nadu
Nov 15, 2016
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			Police arrested an eatery owner for sexually abusing a 13-year-old girl at Guduvanchery on the outskirts of Chennai on Monday.

Police said the girl, who is a Class 7 student at a private school in the area, went to the eatery to buy biryani. The eatery owner, identified as Sheik, 28, allegedly sexually abused her, they said. There was no one else at the eatery. 

Hearing the girl's cries, people rushed to the spot and rescued her. They thrashed Sheik and handed him over to the Guduvanchery police.
